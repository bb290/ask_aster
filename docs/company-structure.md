:::info
Sagareus has 4 employment levels:

* Director Level — direct reports include up to 2 Department Managers
* Manager Level — direct reports include up to 4 Team Leads
* Team Lead Level — direct reports include up to 4 Team Members
* Team Member — direct reports include themselves

:::

## Dept Director


1. Establish and maintain Role Responsibilities + Priorities (KPIs).

   
   1. [Career Paths](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/182dQb-VpplBazFHsHrf9y-p3gJCT7V4Msfby5PdPH1s/edit?usp=sharing)
2. Establish, Receive & Evaluate Dept KPIs.
3. Evaluate, develop and/or modify SOPs to improve Dept KPIs
4. Collaborate with Directors to develop and/or modify cross dept SOPs & initiatives
5. Supervise & Develop Dept Managers

   
   1. Business Development Director

      
      1. Leasing Manager
      2. Biz Dev Manager
   2. Accounting Director

      
      1. Accounting Manager
      2. HR Manager
   3. Operations Director

      
      1. Operations Manager
      2. Customer Service Manager

## Dept Manager


1. Execute up to 4 High Level Functions
2. Track & prepare Monthly KPI Reports
3. Identify issues / make SOP improvement recommendations
4. Implement / execute / enforce SOP adherence
5. Train, Develop & Support Team + Team Leads

   
   1. Leasing Manager

      
      1. Leasing Team Leads
      2. Leasing Assistants
   2. Operations Manager

      
      1. Field Team Lead
      2. Turn Over Team Lead
      3. Maintenance Team Lead
   3. Accounting Manager

      
      1. Accounting Team Lead

## Team Lead


1. Senior Team Member
2. Execute Day to Day Operations
3. Assigned mentorship / development of 1 to 4 assigned team members
4. Ensure Team Members & self hit individual KPIs

   
   1. Leasing Team Lead

      
      1. Up to 4 Leasing Agents
   2. Field Services Team Lead

      
      1. Up to 4 Field Services Technicians
   3. Turn Over Team Lead

      
      1. Up to 4 Turn Over Coordinators
   4. Maintenance Team Lead

      
      1. Up to 4 Maintenance Coordinators
   5. Accounting Team Lead

      
      1. Up to 4 Accounting Team Staff

         
         1. A/R Bookkeeper
         2. A/P Bookkeeper
         3. Resident Account Manager
         4. Utility Manager

## Team Member


1. Front line staff executing day to day operations
2. Lead themselves to achieve individual KPIs

   
   1. Leasing Agents
   2. TurnOver Coordinators
   3. Maintenance Coordinators
   4. Property Field Services Technicians
   5. Accounts Managers
