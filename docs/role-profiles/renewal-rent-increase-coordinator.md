## Role Description

Manages the full lease renewal and rent increase cycle. Coordinates notice serving, mailing, and resident communication.

## Key Responsibilities

* Prepare lease renewals and rent increases
* Resident communication
* Coordinate notice serving and mailing
* Renewal follow-ups and escalations
* Manages Notice Servicing task list
* Pet additions / removals on leases
* Rent increase appeals
* Unit transfer requests
* Determines notice-to-vacate type (PreMove Out)
* Creates move-out tasks
* Domestic violence early termination processing
