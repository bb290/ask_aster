## Leasing Agent

### Recruiting


1. Entry Level Job
2. 1 year customer service experience required
3. Extroverted
4. Busy bodies, like to be on the go
5. Tech Savvy

### Not a Fit


1. Resume is poorly presented

   
   1. Typos / grammar mistakes
   2. Hard to read layout, no bullets, lack of punctuation
   3. Overly designed, we're looking for simple
2. Resume makes no sense

   
   1. Programmer seeking Leasing Agent role?

### Good Fit


1. Cover Letter or Objective communicates clear objective

   
   1. Indicates they are intentionally seeking Leasing Agent role, entry level into PM.
2. Experience in Real Estate / Leasing
