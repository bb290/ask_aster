## Role Description

Oversees the accounting and finance team. Manages payroll, reimbursements, and financial operations.

## Key Responsibilities

* Hires, trains and supports Finance team
* Processes payroll, reimbursements, bonuses and mileage
* Oversees accounting team
* Charges management fees (1st–5th of each month)
* Handles late fee disputes and utility shutoff escalations
* Supply order approvals
* Manages funding deficits
* Vendor refund check voiding
