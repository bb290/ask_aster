## Role Description

Manages office operations, deposit tracking, and bill pay. Handles document requests and key management. Also serves as [Utility Coordinator](utility-coordinator.md).

## Key Responsibilities

* Office management
* Deposit tracking
* Bill pay
* Document requests for rental assistance / non-profits
* Key management (1Password updates)
* Unmatched bank deposits
* Charge backs and refunds
* Housing Authority change of rent
* Accounts payable vendor portal issues
* [Utility Coordinator](utility-coordinator.md) — utility account takeovers, audits, and utility company communications
