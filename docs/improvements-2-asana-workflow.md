## Improvement 2.0 Task Template


1. New issue input as task in Improvement project
2. Reviewed by Sales & Marketing Director

   
   1. Details issue in task description

      
      1. Assigns subtasks for clarity / examples as needed
   2. Assigns priority
   3. Assigns to appropriate Director or Manager to investigate + complete task description

## Investigate + Complete Task Description


1. To fully solve a problem, the assigned Director, Manager or Team must fully understand the problem:

### Describe Issue in Detail

* What is the problem? Like a detective building a case, find evidence of the culprit(s) causing the problem
  * Link, screenshot, copy/paste examples into Improvement Parent task
* Is the current procedure ineffective or less efficient than possible?

### Root Causes

* What is the root cause? Or root causes?
* Is the current issue due to lack of follow through on existing procedure?

### Proposed Solution / Ideas

* Listed Ideas to improve the SOPs to resolve the issue
* How will proposed changes affect each department / individual team members?
  * What is the reality of this change?

### Link to Relevant SOPs

* Include links to related SOPs doc for reference

## Single Department

Director determines the issue can be resolved within their department without effecting work flow of other departments:


1. Update SOP doc
2. Update Asana Task Template, if needed
3. Set Implementation Date
4. Schedule Team Meeting or One on Ones, as appropriate
5. Update team member who suggested the idea
6. Follow-Up on Result / Modify until satisfied

   
   1. This task is often missed
   2. Set a due date to check in on progress / result of new procedure at regular intervals
   3. Continue to modify the SOP & re-train team until the issue is **FULLY resolved**, consistently.
7. Once procedure is fully tested & working properly

   
   1. Add to Process Training Slide, as appropriate
8. Mark task complete

## Multi-Department

Director determines the issue will require 2 or more department participation or will otherwise disrupt current work flow:


1. Assigned Director schedules Directors Meeting

### Directors Meetings


1. Are designed to address ONE issue at a time
2. Are scheduled by the assigned Director **AFTER** he/she has

   
   1. Investigated the issue thoroughly
   2. Drafted proposed solutions
   3. Identified **specific** questions / areas for leadership discussion + collaboration

### Post Meeting

Once Directors have agreed on proposed changes (this may take multiple meetings – depending on the complexity of the issue), the assigned Director or Manager completes the remaining tasks:


1. Update SOP doc
2. Update Asana Task Template, if needed
3. Set Implementation Date
4. Schedule Team Meeting or One on Ones, as appropriate
5. Update team member who suggested the idea
6. Follow-Up on Result / Modify until satisfied

   
   1. This task is often missed
   2. Set a due date to check in on progress / result of new procedure at regular intervals
   3. Continue to modify the SOP & re-train team until the issue is **FULLY resolved**, consistently.
7. Once procedure is fully tested & working properly

   
   1. Add to Process Training Slide, as appropriate
8. Mark task complete
