Sagareus invests in leadership at every level. Each Director, Manager, Team Lead, and Team Member is expected to coach, develop, and hold themselves accountable to clear KPIs — and to be coached, developed, and held accountable in return.

This section covers the leadership philosophy that underpins how we build teams, the four-tier company structure (Director / Manager / Team Lead / Team Member), the Team Development Asana project that tracks each employee, and the weekly 1-on-1 cadence that keeps development on the rails.


:::info
Every Team Lead and Manager meets with each direct report **weekly** via Google Meet. The 1-on-1 is the unit of leadership at Sagareus.

:::

## Children

* Leadership Concepts — what leadership is, strong-leader characteristics, the Learning Pyramid, Employee Drift, Feedback Sandwich
* Company Structure — the four employment levels and their responsibilities (Dept Director, Dept Manager, Team Lead, Team Member)
* Team Development Asana Project — task template "US Based PM Staff // Employee Name", subtasks for onboarding, reviews, incidents, and the shared weekly 1-on-1 subtask
* Weekly 1-on-1 — cadence, Gemini recording, SOP-of-the-week training pattern, summary capture in Asana
