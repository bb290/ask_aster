:::tip
Maintenance 2.0 has 3 objectives:


1. Preserve property value
2. Provide quick & cost effective repairs
3. Support Resident + Client Satisfaction

:::

Maintenance 2.0 procedures flow through 4 stages:


:::info
**Stage 1, New**

Incoming Tickets are input by [Customer Service Specialist](../role-profiles/customer-service-specialist.md); Including High Priority and Normal / Low Priority.

:::


:::info
**Stage 2, Investigate / Self Repair**

Once tickets have been responded to by Maintenance Coordinator, they move into Investigate / Self Repair if further information is needed.

:::


:::info
**Stage 3, Dispatched / In Progress**

Once enough information is gathered to dispatch vendor, Maintenance Coordinator obtains Owner Approval (if needed), dispatches, and moves task to Dispatched / In Progress. High Priority issues skip investigate and move directly to Dispatched / In Progress.

:::


:::info
**Stage 4, Quality Control + Close Out**

Once vendor and/or Resident reports the issue is resolved, task moves to Quality Control + Close Out.

:::

## Maintenance Lifecycle Overview


1. **New**
   * Incoming Tickets are input by [Customer Service Specialist](../role-profiles/customer-service-specialist.md); Including:
     * High Priority
     * Normal / Low Priority
   * High Priority tickets are addressed right away
   * Normal / Low Priority tickets are addressed same or next day
2. **Investigate / Self Repair**
3. **Dispatched / In Progress**
4. **Quality Control + Close Out**

## Maintenance Policies

### Electrical & Plumbing Repairs


:::warning
Sagareus prioritizes life-safety, legal compliance, and protection of owner assets. Electrical and plumbing failures fall into high-risk categories with the potential to cause fire, flooding, personal injury, or significant property damage.

**Default Dispatch**

Sagareus will dispatch only licensed electricians and licensed plumbers for all electrical and plumbing repairs, regardless of perceived severity.

:::

#### Owner-Requested Exceptions

Owners may request use of an unlicensed or lower-cost vendor **only by written instruction** (email is sufficient).
