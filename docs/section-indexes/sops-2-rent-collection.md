## Policies


:::info
* **Payment plan deadline:** All payment plans must be established by the **3rd Friday** of each month
* **Eviction start:** Evictions start on the **22nd** of every month
* **After eviction starts, only 2 options:** Early Move Out or Pay in Full
* **Waiving fees:** Late fees or reversed payment fees waived once per Resident as a courtesy

:::


---


:::warning
Rent collection is the core revenue function. Unrecovered balances directly impact owner distributions and property operations.

:::

* **Recover 100% of balances** owed to property
* **Prevent Residents from experiencing eviction** through proactive communication and resolution options

## Collections Lifecycle Overview

* **Days 1-5: Rent Due** - Rent due on or before 5th of each month
* **Days 6-14: Eviction Prevention**
  * 6th of month, Auto reminder email goes out via Buildium
  * 7th thru ~14th, Email sequence (HubSpot), calls/texts (Asana), educating Residents on options
  * Resolutions available: Pay in Full | Payment Plan | Early Termination
* **Days 14-21: Tenant Not Responding**
  * Day 12, Call, text & email emergency contact
  * Day 15, Start abandonment procedure if still no response
* **Days 21+: Start Evictions**
  * 21st, Verify payment plan payments, send Default Notifications
  * 22nd, Start Eviction Paperwork
