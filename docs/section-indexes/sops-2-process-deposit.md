:::warning
Security deposit processing is governed by Washington State law ([RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280)). Landlords must return deposits or provide an itemized withholding statement within 30 days of move-out. Failure to comply results in liability for the full deposit amount, and courts may award up to double the deposit for intentional refusal.

:::

* Deposit processing protects both the owner's property investment and the resident's legal right to a fair accounting of their security deposit.
* A legally defensible deposit statement requires documented prior condition (move-in), after condition (move-out), and itemized vendor invoices for every withheld amount.
* Sagareus targets a **25-day turnaround** — 5 days before the legal deadline — to provide buffer for mailing and any complications.
* Deposit withholding is a common source of resident disputes and Google reviews. A well-documented, transparent process reduces appeals and protects Sagareus's reputation.


---

## Policies


:::warning
* All withholding must meet the four-part test: move-in report, prior condition documentation, after condition documentation, and itemized vendor invoice. See [Deposit Withholding Requirements](../../sops/deposit return/deposit-withholding-requirements.md).
* Per [RCW 59.18.595](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.595) (effective 7/23/2023), landlords cannot charge for professional carpet cleaning at move-out unless the carpet is damaged beyond ordinary use.
* Wear resulting from ordinary use ([RCW 59.18.030(39)](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.030)) is never chargeable.

:::
