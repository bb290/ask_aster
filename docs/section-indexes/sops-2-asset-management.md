:::info
Sagareus maintains a stockpile of field assets to support incident response. These include:

* Oil heaters for loss of heat
* Dehumidifiers for water damage control
* Temp fridge for broken refrigeration
* Portable AC unit for heat wave

The goal of asset management is to inventory, track, and secure these assets across all storage locations and deployments.

:::

* **Property preservation** — Assets enable rapid response to habitability issues (heat, water damage, refrigeration)
* **Accountability** — Every asset is labeled, tracked, and assigned to a responsible party
* **Cost control** — Centralized inventory prevents duplicate purchases and lost equipment

## Policies


:::warning
* All asset checkouts must go through [Assistant Property Manager (Asset)](../role-profiles/assistant-property-manager-asset.md)
* Staff may temporarily hold assets, but not exceeding 2x of each item
* During deployment, a subtask must be created to track pickup, assigned to [Assistant Property Manager (Asset)](../role-profiles/assistant-property-manager-asset.md)
* All assets must be labeled with a unique Item ID before entering inventory

:::
