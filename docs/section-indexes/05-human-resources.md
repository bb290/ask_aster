Human Resources at Sagareus is responsible for the Employee Handbook, employee classification, garnishments and wage verifications, I-9 compliance, timekeeping, and all of the state and federal forms that flow through Payroll/HR.

The HR Manager is the primary owner of these processes; the Accounting Director provides backup oversight on anything that touches benefits, payroll, or financial policy.


:::warning
Most HR responses to state and federal agencies have **statutory deadlines**. When a notice arrives — child-support order, unemployment claim, ESD wage verification — log the receipt date and respond by the deadline on the form. Missing these deadlines exposes Sagareus to penalties.

:::

The child docs below cover each HR-owned process in detail, including the Employee Handbook update flow, the FLSA exempt vs. non-exempt classification rules for 2025, and the timekeeping options available to staff.
