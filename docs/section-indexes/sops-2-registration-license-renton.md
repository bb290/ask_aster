:::info
Renton has a city-administered **rental registration** program (not a general business license). Registration is filed online and renewed annually; registrations always expire **December 31** of the renewal year. VPN is required for remote staff to access the form.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Program type:** City of Renton Rental Registration
* **Portal:** [Renton Rental Registration Form](https://forms.rentonwa.gov/Forms/RentalRegistration)
* **Renewal cadence:** Annual, always expiring **December 31** of the current renewal year
* **City contact:** [rentalregistration@rentonwa.gov](mailto:rentalregistration@rentonwa.gov)
* **Administering agency:** City of Renton

## Remote Access


:::warning
Remote team members need a VPN to access the registration form at forms.rentonwa.gov. Confirm VPN connection before starting any Setup or Update procedure.

:::

## Compliance Declaration

On the Renton form, complete the `Checklist of Compliance` answering **NO** to each item, followed by the `Declaration of Compliance`.

## Tracking

* Update `Rental Registration Expiration` on [Property Settings](sops-2-property-settings.md) to **12/31 of the current renewal year** after every Setup or Update.
* See [Update | Property Settings](../../sops/property onboarding/update-property-settings.md).
