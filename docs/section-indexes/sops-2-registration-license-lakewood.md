:::info
Lakewood operates a **Rental Housing Safety Program** requiring annual registration and a 5-year inspection cycle.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Cadence:** Annual registration
* **Fees:** Single-family license `$50`; Multifamily `$50` base + `$20` per additional unit
* **Inspection cadence:** Property inspection required every 5 years
* **Inspector selection:** City inspector or qualified private inspector
* **Administering agency:** City of Lakewood
* **Authoritative reference:** [Lakewood Rental Housing Safety Program](https://cityoflakewood.us/rental-housing-safety-program/)
