:::info
Burien requires an **Annual Housing Business License** for each rental property, with 20% of units inspected every 3 years by an owner-hired inspector from a city-approved list.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Cadence:** Annual Housing Business License required per property
* **Fee:** `$315.29 – $945.87` per property, scaled by number of units
* **Inspection cadence:** At least 20% of a property's rental units inspected every 3 years
* **Inspector selection:** Owner must hire a qualified inspector from a list provided by the City
* **Administering agency:** City of Burien
* **Authoritative reference:** [Burien Rental Housing Inspection Program](https://www.burienwa.gov/city_hall/laws_regulations/renting_in_burien/rental_housing_inspection_program)
