Accounting and Banking covers the day-to-day financial plumbing of Sagareus: bank account structure, ePay setup through Buildium, who has access to which platforms, and how new properties get attached to the right banking infrastructure during onboarding.

Bank accounts are a mirror of the recorded accounts housed in Buildium. Bank of America and Chase platforms are both used. All bank accounts are identified by their three-digit numerical code; Chase accounts are designated with a **C** at the end of the code.


:::info
Newly acquired properties are initially allocated to **901. TEMP/ONBOARDING ACCOUNT**. The lead accountant is responsible for daily monitoring of this account — receipt of new property funds triggers the comprehensive onboarding process.

:::


:::warning
Admin, Controller, and Lead Accountant have access to all necessary banking accounts for day-to-day operations. Admin accounts are limited to Zelle and view access on Certificate of Deposit accounts that house liability funds. **No banking passwords can be stored** — access routes through 2FA on the credentialed phone. In a worst-case scenario, company owners (and signers) must contact bank representatives directly to re-establish Admin access.

:::
