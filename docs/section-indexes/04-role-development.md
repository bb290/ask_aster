Leasing Agents are expected to operate independently and consistently meet Leasing Agent KPIs. By month 6, Agents are expected to manage a Sagareus-grade rental listing from **Pre-Move-Out through Move-In** while minimizing vacancy, maximizing rental income, and maintaining high standards for applicant quality and resident experience.

Leasing Agents may continue their career as **individual contributors** or choose to pursue a **Team Lead** role, overseeing a pod of 1–4 Leasing Agents. Compensation for these two paths is intentionally comparable. Advancement into leadership is driven by a desire to coach, develop, and support others, not by compensation.


:::info
Sagareus values high-performing individual contributors equally to team leaders. Both paths represent meaningful, respected ways to grow within the organization, based on strengths, interests, and long-term career goals.

:::

This section covers role-specific KPIs and training tracks. Use it as the source of truth for what each role is accountable for and what training is required at each stage of development.

## Children

* Leasing Agent KPIs + Training — KPIs (move-out inspections, activity reports, monthly move-ins, customer ratings, Google reviews) plus the seven-deck training track from Lease Up Basics through Lease Up Advanced
* Leasing Team Lead KPIs + Training — Team Lead KPIs (personal listing performance, weekly coaching, team KPI attainment, SOP adherence, escalation quality) plus Team Leadership + Development and Leasing Team Lead training decks
* Additional Trainings — Time Management Workshop, Mold Remediation OSHA Certification
