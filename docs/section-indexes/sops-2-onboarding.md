Onboarding is the efficient, seamless, brand-consistent handoff from Business Development to Sagareus' Teams: Leasing, Accounting, and Operations.

**Exceptional Onboarding** requires accurate data input, proactive communication, and detailed, excessive documentation.


---

## Asana SOP

Start with the [Onboarding Asana SOP](sops-2-onboarding-asana-sop.md) — 29 numbered step docs that mirror the `(CLIENT)` task template in Client Relations 2.0. Work the Asana subtasks in order; each links to the step doc for the detailed procedure.

### Variants

* [**Adding Property**](sops-2-onboarding-asana-sop-adding-property.md) — existing client adding a property to their portfolio. Same lifecycle, minus steps 9, 10, 11.
* [**Adding Unit**](sops-2-onboarding-asana-sop-adding-unit.md) — existing client adding a unit to an existing property (ADU, DADU, conversion). Shorter mini template.

## Scenarios

| Scenario | Template | When to use |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **New Client** | `(CLIENT) <Address> / <Owner Name>` | Brand new client signing PMA for the first time |
| **Adding Property** | `(PROPERTY) <Address> / <Owner Name>` | Existing client adding a property to their portfolio |
| **Adding Unit** | `(UNIT) <Address> / <Owner Name>` | Existing client adding a unit to an existing property |

All templates live in the **Client Relations 2.0** project, Onboarding section.

## KPI Targets

| Metric | Target | Tracked By |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Initial Onboarding Complete | 2 days | `SEND \| Onboarding Progress Update & UPDATE \| Initial Onboard Complete` |
| Initial Reports Sent | 21 days | `SEND \| Initial Owner Reports & UPDATE \| Initial Reports Sent` |
| Onboarding Finalized | 60 days | `SEND \| Onboard Complete! & UPDATE \| Onboarding Finalized` |
| Owner Sentiment | 100% Happy | Post-onboarding follow-up |
