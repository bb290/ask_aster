* Unauthorized Vehicle in Assigned Space, Identify owner, 24-hour removal

## Parking + Storage Policies

### Parking Rules / Regulations


:::warning
Below rules & regulations are listed in the Parking Addendum included in standard Sagareus Lease.

:::

Resident(s) understand and agree to the following expectations:

* To pay all rent and other charges promptly when due or assessed
* To keep the Premises, parking areas, in good order and in clean and sanitary condition
* Maintain full responsibility of the vehicle security
* Performing vehicle maintenance or repairs in parking space is prohibited
* Landlord not responsible for vandalism, theft or damage to vehicles
* Resident agrees to park in assigned space
* Vehicles parked in spaces should have current registration and insurance
* Vehicles must be in working order
* Additional gasoline or other flammables should not be stored in space
* Vehicle parking only, no storage of personal items in space
* The Landlord may regulate the time, manner, and place of parking by the Resident
* Unauthorized or illegally parked vehicles may be towed at the vehicle owner's expense
