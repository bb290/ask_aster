## Preparing Files for Reporting


:::info
The following steps must be completed prior to initiating account reconciliation:

:::

**Management Fees**, Cooper is responsible for charging management fees. This task is typically completed between the 1st and 5th of each month.

**Payment of Sagareus Invoices**, Collection of funds for the corresponding reporting month:

* Lease-Up Fees
* Renewal Fees
* Operating Income Fees
* Onboarding Fees

**Outstanding Invoices/Bills**, Verify the presence of any outstanding bills or invoices for each portfolio.

**Return of Temporary Loans**

**Payout of Management Income**, This is an ongoing process that requires attention every few days throughout the reporting period.

**Adjustments to Autopay Bills**

* Homeowners Association (HOA) Fees
* Mortgage Payments
* Insurance Premiums

## Generate Owner Reports


1. Buildium, Reports, Open the following reports in separate window tabs
   * Rental Owner Statement
   * Income Statement > Consolidated
   * Rent Roll
   * Bank Reconciliation
2. Refer to Accounts in Google Drive to generate reports
3. Cross reference Banking in Buildium with Bank statement

## Owner Distribution


1. Buildium, From shortcut menu select Record Owner Draw by EFT
2. Select 'All Rental' Owners with both Pending and No available Cash
3. Highlight each owner to receive payments
4. Click 'Prepare Payment' and authorize EFT withdrawal from default properties
5. Click Submit
