This step covers serving and mailing **both** rent increase notices and renewal offers. The same process applies to each — only the document type differs.

## Serve Notice // [Address] // deadline [Date]

* Serve Notice tasks are assigned to Leasing and Operations team staff
* There are two important dates:
  * **Task Due Date** — This is the first day, when the notice can be served
  * **Deadline Date** (In the task name) — This is the last day the notice must be posted on the door
* Follow the instructions under [Serving Notice - In Person](../../sops/resident relations/notices.md)
* Track progress on the [Renewal // Dashboard and Metrics](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/renewal-dashboard-and-metrics.md)


---

## Mail Notice // [Address] // deadline [Date]

* Mail notice tasks are assigned to [Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator](sops-2-staff-directory.md)
* Follow the instructions under [Service Notice - Mailing](../../sops/resident relations/notices.md)

## Notice Types

| Notice | When | Document |
|--------|------|----------|
| Rent Increase | 180 days (Seattle/Tacoma) or 120 days (other WA) before increase date | Rent increase notice PDF |
| Tacoma 90-day | 90 days before increase date (Tacoma only, 2nd required notice) | Rent increase notice PDF |
| Renewal Offer | 60–90 days before lease end | Renewal package from Buildium |

See [Renewal // Important Deadlines](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/renewal-important-deadlines.md) for full deadline details by jurisdiction.
