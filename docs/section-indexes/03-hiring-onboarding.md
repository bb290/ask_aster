New Hire Onboarding takes a new team member from offer acceptance through Day 1 orientation, Week 1 Sagareus fundamentals, and Week 2 role-specific training.

The objective of the program is to ensure every new hire:

* Understands the full scope of work occurring across the organization
* Recognizes how their individual role contributes to shared outcomes
* Gains context for the functions, dependencies, and responsibilities of adjacent teams
* Operates with greater accountability, efficiency, and cross-departmental awareness


:::info
Week 1 is intentionally broad-stroke. It is not a replacement for role-specific SOPs — it is the foundation that allows staff to work effectively within a complex, interconnected operation. Role-specific training begins in Week 2.

:::

This section also covers the IT setup checklist for every new hire, the recruiting funnel and resume-review process, role profiles for the two most-hired positions (Field Service Technician and Leasing Agent), and the 90-day / annual performance review cadence.

## Children

* Day 1: New Hire Orientation — orientation slides, Employee Handbook walkthrough, IT setup, ADP setup
* Week 1: Sagareus Fundamentals — Office Tour, Field Day, Core Processes (Leasing, Screening, Communications, Maintenance, Inspections, Accounting), Asana Basics
* Week 2: Role-Specific Training — handoff to the role-specific development tracks
* New Hire IT Setup — Ring Central setup, 1Password, ADP, Asana, Google Workspace, MilesIQ
* Recruiting Funnel — capacity planning, reviewing resumes, screening calls, common applicant questions
* Role Profile: Property Field Service Technician
* Role Profile: Leasing Agent — recruiting, interview, 90-day review, 360 evaluation, self-evaluation
* Performance Reviews — 90-day, annual, and 360-degree review process
