## Management Fees

### Pay Out Management Income Accounts


:::warning
This process is to be done before the owner reporting process has started.

:::


 1. Buildium > Shortcuts > Pay out management income accounts
 2. Exclude all checks, this will be the starting point to track transfers
 3. Open BofA or Chase
    * Make a transfer
    * From: owner's operating account
    * To: receivables account
    * Reflect amount noted in Buildium
 4. "Restore Check" in Buildium, this will track that the transfer has happened with the bank accounts
 5. Click "Record Checks"
    * Do not check the "Queue Checks for Printing"
 6. Go back to the Banking page in Buildium
 7. Record other transaction
 8. Select "Deposit"
 9. Property: Sagareus Real Estate
10. Deposit to: 100(c): Sagareus | operating
11. Offsetting Account: Admin Fee CC
12. Deposit Amount: The total for the pay out of management fee you recorded
13. Go back to BofA or Chase
    * Transfer from Receivables to Sagareus account the amount recorded from deposit

## Lease Up Fees


1. Banking > Sagareus Operating Account (Depending on Chase or BofA)
   * Record other transaction
   * Deposit
   * Property: Sagareus
   * Offset Account: Lease Up Income
   * Memo: Property Address including unit
   * Click Save
2. Lease Up Bill
   * Make a payment
   * Pay as a "Check" payment since we are making the payment directly
3. Record in bank
   * Transfer funds from owner's operating account to sagareus operating account
