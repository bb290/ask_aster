:::info
Follow these steps in order from top to bottom — they map to the subtasks in the Asana renewal template.

:::


1. [1 — Owner Approval // Rent Increase](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/1-owner-approval-rent-increase.md) — Get owner approval for rent increase amount
2. [2 — Serve & Mail Notice](sops-2-renewal-asana-sop-2-serve-mail-notice.md) — Serve notice in person + mail
3. [3 — Notify Rental Assistance of Rent Increase](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/3-notify-rental-assistance-of-rent-increase.md) — Update Housing Authority / Section 8 if applicable
4. [4 — Owner Approval // Renewal](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/4-owner-approval-renewal.md) — Get owner approval for lease renewal
5. [5 — Offer Renewal & Prepare Package in Buildium](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/5-offer-renewal-prepare-package-in-buildium.md) — Create renewal in BD + prepare documents
6. [6 — Resident Follow Up & Lease Termination](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/6-resident-follow-up-lease-termination.md) — Follow up at 60+, 60-45, 45-0 days + terminate at 40 days
7. [7 — Renewal Negotiation](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/7-renewal-negotiation.md) — Handle lease end date requests, MTM requests, approval/rejection
8. [8 — Lease Renewed — Notify Owner & Rental Assistance](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/8-lease-renewed-notify-owner-rental-assistance.md) — Confirm completion to owner + update rental assistance
9. [9 — Charge Renewal Fee](../../sops/renewal & rent increase/9-charge-renewal-fee.md) — MTM, termed, and short-term renewal fee rules
