# Billing Email

The accounting team monitors this inbox. Every email must be reviewed and processed. **billing@sagareus.com** should be monitored from Google Group instead of Gmail, to ensure activities are visible to all members.

All escalations need to be done in Asana with corresponding assignment and due date.
