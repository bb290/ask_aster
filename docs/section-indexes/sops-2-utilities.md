Sagareus proactively **takes over utility management by default** for all properties. This streamlines operations and protects both owners and Residents:

* **Resident Compliance** — Utilities stay active; no risk of Residents neglecting to sign up or causing shutoffs.
* **Billing Control** — All bills received and paid on time, eliminating late fees and enabling consistent pass-through.
* **Leak Detection** — Direct access to usage data lets us flag abnormal consumption early.
* **Payment Revenue** — Company credit card payments yield cash-back rewards; online portal convenience fees offset admin costs.


---

## Policies


:::warning
**Flat-Fee Utility Billing** — Sagareus requires a fixed monthly utility fee for properties with shared/master meters where submetering is not feasible. The fee is disclosed in the listing and lease, charged as a rent component.

:::

* **Predictable & Autopay-Friendly** — Same amount each month; fewer missed payments.
* **Fewer Disputes** — No splitting bills between roommates or arguing over usage.
* **Simple & Clear** — Fee covers water/sewer/garbage (and master-metered gas/electric if applicable). Separately metered utilities remain the Resident's responsibility.
* **Proration & Notice** — Prorated at move-in/out; adjustments are prospective with written notice. No monthly true-ups.
* **Periodic Review** — Fees reviewed at turnover and lease renewal.
* **Sewer Capacity Charges (SCC)** — Owner responsibility. Never billed to Residents. See [Sewer Capacity Charge Policy](../../sops/utilities/utilities-sewer-capacity-charge-policy.md).
* **Goal** — Convert 100% of managed properties to flat fee billing. See [Flat Fee Renegotiation](../../sops/utilities/utilities-flat-fee-renegotiation.md).

## Utility Lifecycle


 1. **Onboarding & Account Setup** — Transfer utilities into Sagareus's name. See [Onboarding](../../sops/property onboarding/8-utility-onboarding.md).
 2. **Configure Lease Utility** — Determine pass-through vs. flat fee per utility. See [Configure Lease Utility](../../sops/utilities/utilities-configure-lease-utility.md).
 3. **Tenant Account Setup** — Set up gas/electric in tenant's name when applicable. See [Tenant Account Setup](../../sops/utilities/utilities-tenant-account-setup.md).
 4. **Bill Receipt** — Collect incoming bills (paper or electronic) and log into Buildium.
 5. **Bill Processing & Payment** — Enter details and pay promptly. See [Paying Utility Bills](../../sops/utilities/utilities-paying-utility-bills.md).
 6. **Automated Pass-Through** — Daily automation posts utility charges to tenant ledgers. See [Pass Through Automation](../../sops/utilities/utilities-pass-through-automation.md).
 7. **Manual Pass-Through** — For exceptions flagged by automation. See [Pass Through to Resident Ledger](../../sops/utilities/utilities-pass-through-to-resident-ledger.md).
 8. **Flat Fee Cost Recovery** — Monthly reconciliation for flat fee tenants. See [Flat Fee Cost Recovery](/doc/utilities-flat-fee-cost-recovery-WparbiZHbZ)<!-- unresolved: doc not migrated -->.
 9. **Monitoring & Exceptions** — Review usage, handle anomalies. See [Increased Water Usage & Leak Adjustments](../../sops/utilities/utilities-increased-water-usage-leak-adjustments.md).
10. **Move-Out Settlement** — Settle final utility charges at move-out. See [Move-Out Settlement](../../sops/utilities/utilities-move-out-settlement.md).
11. **Offboarding** — Transition accounts out without service disruption.

## High Priority Utility Issue


:::warning
High priority issues require immediate action. Check the utility company for unpaid accounts and audit all utilities against Buildium notes.

:::


1. Check utility company for unpaid accounts.
   * If found, email accounting@sagareus.com with **EMERGENCY** in subject line to make payment and restart service.
2. Audit utilities to confirm accounts match Buildium notes.
