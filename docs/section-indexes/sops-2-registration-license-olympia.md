:::info
Olympia requires **both** annual rental registration AND an annual business license as of March 1, 2024. Inspection program started January 1, 2025 — every 5 years.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Cadence:** Annual registration + annual business license (both required)
* **Registration fee:** `$35` per unit per year
* **Business License fees:** `$50` per business entity (one-time) + `$30` annual endorsement + `$5` annual license renewal
* **Inspection cadence:** Every 5 years (effective since January 1, 2025)
* **Administering agency:** City of Olympia
* **Authoritative reference:** [Olympia Rental Registry](https://www.olympiawa.gov/community/housing___homelessness/rental_registry.php)

## Dual Requirement


:::warning
Olympia requires both annual rental registration AND an annual business license. Track both renewal cycles. The `Rental Registration Expiration` custom field covers the registration side; business license tracking conventions are TBD until Sagareus actually holds one.

:::
