## Grading Scales


:::tip
**Purpose of the grading scales:**


1. Standardize how we assess and report unit condition during inspections.
2. Ensures consistent communication between Leasing, Operations, Vendors, Residents, and Owners.

:::

### Cleanliness

#### A+ – Immaculate / Move-In Ready

* Entire unit is spotless and odor-free
* All appliances clean inside and out, including microwave, oven, fridge, and dishwasher
* All kitchen and bathroom cabinets/drawers fully cleaned inside and out
* Surfaces, floors, and fixtures are visually flawless and clean to the touch
  * *"The unit is fully ready for move-in. No further cleaning needed."*

#### B+ – Clean / Light Touch-Up Needed

* Unit is mostly clean with minor dust or smudges
* Appliances deep cleaned
* Cabinets/drawers appear clean but may benefit from a quick check and wipe-down
  * *"A quick clean will bring this to full move-in condition."*

#### C+ – Fair / Needs Standard Cleaning

* Mostly cleared out, but with surface dust, smudges, or pet hair
* Appliances appear used / not clean
* Cabinets and drawers empty but may have visible crumbs, spills, residue or debris
  * *"No major mess, but a full move out clean is needed to meet move-in standards."*

#### D+ – Poor / Needs Deep Cleaning

* Heavy **dirt, stains, and residue** on floors, counters, and major surfaces
* Trash, pet hair, or left-behind items present
* Appliances greasy, stained, or with food debris inside
* Cabinets and drawers contain crumbs, spills, or personal items
  * *"The unit is not ready for any turnover work. Deep clean needed before repairs or photos."*

#### F+ – Unacceptable / Hazardous

* Major health hazards present (e.g. pest infestation, mold, spoiled food, biohazards)
* Severe filth, trash, or odors throughout the unit
* Appliances caked with grime or rotting food, posing health concerns
* Kitchen/bath cabinets and drawers heavily soiled, sticky, or containing trash/personal items
* Immediate deep cleaning and possibly specialty remediation required


:::warning
*"The unit is unsafe or unsanitary—protective gear may be needed to enter."*

:::

### Yard Condition

#### A+ – Immaculate / Curb Appeal Ready

* Lawn, plants, and landscaping are freshly maintained and vibrant
* All hardscape surfaces swept, pressure-washed, and debris-free
* Flowerbeds neatly edged, mulched, and weed-free
* No trash, clutter, or hazards; overall appearance is polished and inviting
  * *"The yard is fully ready for showing or move-in—no further work needed."*

#### B+ – Maintained / Light Touch-Up Needed

* Grass is recently mowed and bushes/hedges trimmed, with only minor uneven growth
* Flowerbeds and planters mostly weed-free but could use light grooming
* Driveways, walkways, and patios mostly clear with minimal debris
* No trash or hazards present
  * *"A quick mow, trim, and sweep will make the yard photo-ready."*

#### C+ – Fair / Needs Standard Maintenance

* Yard is mostly free of trash but has visible weeds, uneven mowing, or patchy grass
* Bushes, hedges, or trees slightly overgrown but not blocking access
* Hardscape areas (patio, driveway, walkways) may have dirt, leaves, or light moss buildup
* Minor clutter or personal items present
  * *"The yard is functional but not at curb appeal standard—basic mowing, weeding, and tidying are needed."*

#### D+ – Poor / Needs Major Cleanup

* Excessive weeds, tall grass, or untrimmed bushes overtaking yard
* Large amounts of trash, furniture, or non-operable vehicles present
* Plant beds or lawns neglected, with bare patches or excessive dead vegetation
* Driveways, sidewalks, or patios heavily soiled, moss-covered, or obstructed
  * *"The yard requires a full cleanup and significant landscaping work before it is functional or presentable."*

#### F+ – Unacceptable / Hazardous

* Major safety or health hazards present (e.g., exposed nails, broken glass, standing water, pest infestation)
* Significant trash, debris, or hazardous waste scattered throughout
* Overgrown vegetation obstructing access or visibility; dead trees or limbs posing a danger
* Structures (fences, sheds, retaining walls) unstable or collapsing
* Immediate professional cleanup and hazard removal required


:::warning
*"The yard is unsafe to enter without protective gear or equipment."*

:::
