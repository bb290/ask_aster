Customer Service scenarios for handling Notice to Vacate requests.

## Scenarios

* [CS // End of Lease, Proper Notice](../../sops/premove out/cs-end-of-lease-proper-notice.md): Standard move-out with proper notice
* [CS // Lease Break, Early Termination](../../sops/premove out/cs-lease-break-early-termination.md): 2x rent early termination fee
* [CS // Military Transfer](../../sops/premove out/cs-military-transfer.md): Protected under RCW 59.18.220; 20 days notice required
* [CS // Domestic Violence, Early Termination](../../sops/premove out/cs-domestic-violence-early-termination.md): Always allowed under RCW 59.18.575; no penalty
