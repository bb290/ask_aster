## Exceptional Property Management

Sagareus Property Management strives to provide an Exceptional Property Management Service to all stakeholders – Clients (property owners); Current Residents; Prospective Residents; and Vendors.

**Exceptional** — Refers to something that is unusual or out of the ordinary in a positive way. It often describes someone or something that stands out due to high quality, skill, or excellence. It can also mean rare or uncommon.

We achieve Exceptional status with our commitment to ongoing improvement structured around 6 Core Processes:


:::tip
**6 Core Processes**

Screening, Leasing, Accounting, Maintenance, Inspections, Communication

:::
