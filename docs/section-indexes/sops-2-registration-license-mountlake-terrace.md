:::info
Mountlake Terrace requires annual **Rental Business Licensing** for all residential rental properties. City or county inspectors conduct inspections every 3 years at no charge to the owner.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Cadence:** Annual Rental Business License renewal
* **Fees:** `$40` + `$1.50` per additional unit
* **Inspection cadence:** Every 3 years
* **Inspector selection:** City or county inspectors — **no charge to the owner**
* **Administering agency:** City of Mountlake Terrace
* **Authoritative reference:** [Mountlake Terrace Business Licenses](https://www.cityofmlt.com/158/Business-Licenses)

## City Portal
