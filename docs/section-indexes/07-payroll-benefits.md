Sagareus runs **semi-monthly payroll** through ADP. Employees are paid on the **16th** and the **1st** of each month, reflecting the preceding two weeks of work. The two payroll runs differ slightly: mid-month is straight-through; month-end adds reimbursements, bonuses, and MilesIQ mileage.

This section covers everything that flows through the payroll cycle — time tracking, missed-punch resolution, the payroll run itself, PTO approval and cash-out, benefits administration (KAVI healthcare), leaves of absence, year-end tasks, and workers comp.


:::info
Sagareus initially covers the full cost of the employee healthcare benefits package, paid directly to KAVI. Employee contributions are recouped via payroll deductions; employer contributions are reflected on the pay stub as pay but are not disbursed to the employee (the funds were already paid to KAVI). See the Benefits Administration child doc for the full deduction-and-reconciliation flow.

:::


:::warning
Payroll deductions for benefits are reconciled annually during the November enrollment period and updated in each employee's ADP settings. Off-cycle changes are only made on a Qualifying Life Event.

:::
