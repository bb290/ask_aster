# Resident Refund

All refunds for credit balances on Resident ledgers will be issued back to the **original source of payment**. This policy ensures proper accounting practices and prevents disputes regarding fund disbursement.

## Refund Policy

* **Identify credit balance** — When a credit balance is found, accounting determines the source of the original payment(s).
* **Verify payment source** — Verification through transaction records, bank statements, or other documentation.
* **Issue to original source** — Refunds are processed and issued directly to the verified payment source.
* **Document everything** — All refund transactions are documented in the Resident ledger including refund amount, payment method, and recipient.

## Issuing a Refund in Buildium


1. Navigate to Resident ledger
2. Select the three-dot shortcut → Issue Refund
3. Select the appropriate bank account
   * Paper check refunds → use BXN account
   * Zelle refunds → can issue from property operating account
4. Select the subject refund person
5. Enter the amount
6. Memo: RENT OVERPAYMENT REFUND
7. Confirm only income accounts are being refunded (Rent or Utility Recovery) — do NOT issue liability refunds this way
8. Save refund
