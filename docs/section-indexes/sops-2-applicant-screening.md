## Applicant Screening Lifecycle Overview


:::info
**Step 1 — Prospect Submits Application**

* Prospect submits an online application

:::


:::info
**Step 2 — Initial Review**

* Screen for any 'Flags'
  * Credit, background & Eviction History
  * Verify Income
  * Verify renter's history

:::


:::info
**Step 3 — Enhanced Review (optional)**

* Verify Identity

:::


:::info
**Step 4 — Approved or Declined**

* Send Lease or Send Adverse Action

:::

## Initial Application Checklist


1. Send "Application Received" Email
2. Charge App Fee
3. Run Credit / eviction / criminal background
4. Request Landlord Reference
5. Any flags on the Credit Report? **If yes →** Enhanced Applicant Review
6. Income Verification | Last 2 Paystubs; Does paystub match employer listed on application? **If No →** Enhanced Applicant Review
7. Review Rental History Ledger


---

## Enhanced Applicant Review


1. Send email to applicant requesting additional documents
2. Upload copy of photo ID
3. Upload copy of last 2 month bank statement
4. Social Media Audit

## Section 8 / Government Housing Program

For Section 8 and other government housing program applicants, follow the same screening criteria above, then complete the additional steps in the [Government Housing Program](sops-2-government-housing-program.md) SOP:


1. Complete [RFTA paperwork](../../sops/applicant screening/section-8-request-for-tenancy-approval-rfta.md)
2. Schedule [Housing Authority Inspection](../../sops/applicant screening/section-8-inspection.md)
3. Assign additional move-in paperwork for Leasing Agent
4. Add case worker contact to BD notes under lease profile
5. Upload Final HAP document
6. Reach out to case worker for 1st payment confirmation
7. Apply for mitigation funds
