:::info
Tukwila requires an annual **Rental Business License** and mandates that every unit (including single-family, ADUs, short-term and long-term rentals) be inspected **before renting** and every 4 years thereafter.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Cadence:** Annual Rental Business License renewal
* **Fee:** `$88` – `$357` per property, scaled by unit count
* **Inspection cadence:** Every unit inspected **before renting** and every 4 years thereafter
* **Inspection fees (city inspector):** `$83` per unit for units 1-4; `$39` per unit for units 5-12; properties with 13+ units must use a private inspector
* **Inspector selection:** City inspector or qualified private inspector; all fees paid by owner
* **Administering agency:** City of Tukwila
* **Authoritative reference:** [Tukwila Rental Housing](https://www.tukwilawa.gov/departments/community-development/rental-housing/)

## Pre-Rent Inspection Requirement


:::warning
Tukwila requires each unit to be inspected BEFORE renting. This affects onboarding: a new-to-Sagareus property cannot be rented (or, if vacated, cannot be re-rented) without a current inspection on file. Factor this into Setup timing.

:::
