## Asana Project | Improvements 2.0

Improvement 2.0 has 2 objectives:


1. Maintain a structured process for all team members to suggest ideas, give feedback, or report issues.
2. Provide clear procedures to analyze, implement, and modify company wide changes until the problem is **fully resolved.**

Improvement 2.0 procedures flow through 3 stages:


1. New Input
2. Current
3. Review
