:::info
Kent's rental housing program is inspection-centered; registration has no fee, but any rental property with 2+ units is subject to mandatory inspection every 3 years.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Cadence:** Annual registration required for properties with **2 or more units**. Single-family homes are exempt from both registration and inspection.
* **Registration fee:** None.
* **Inspection report fee:** `$15` per online inspection report submission.
* **Inspection cadence:** Every 3 years for properties subject to the program.
* **Administering agency:** City of Kent Economic & Community Development.
* **Authoritative reference:** [Kent Rental Housing Inspection Program](https://www.kentwa.gov/departments/econ-community-dev/rental-housing-inspection-program)

## City Portal

The Kent city program page: [Kent Rental Housing Inspection Program](https://www.kentwa.gov/departments/econ-community-dev/rental-housing-inspection-program). Portal credentials stored in 1Password when/if Sagareus has an account.

## Inspector Rules

Owner must hire a **qualified rental housing inspector** (private, city-approved). List of qualified inspectors on the city program page.

## Tracking

The `Rental Registration Expiration` custom field on the [Property Settings](sops-2-property-settings.md) Asana project must be updated after every Setup / Update / Cancel. See [Update | Property Settings](../../sops/property onboarding/update-property-settings.md).

## Single-Family Exemption


:::tip
Kent's program applies only to properties with 2 or more units. Confirm unit count before starting any Setup or Update procedure. Single-family homes and detached ADUs-without-main-residence should skip this topic entirely.

:::
