The Utility Company Playbook centralizes contact information, authorization requirements, and billing procedures for every utility provider serving Sagareus-managed properties.

## Purpose

* **Single source of truth** — One place to look up how to work with any utility company
* **Consistent execution** — Same process regardless of who handles the call
* **Faster onboarding** — New staff or new properties don't start from scratch
* **Prevent errors** — No guessing on transfer requirements, forms, or contacts


---

## Ownership & Responsibility

**Primary Owner:** Utility Coordinator 

The Utility Coordinator is responsible for ensuring all utility company entries are accurate, complete, and up to date. The Office Manager provides oversight on billing-related processes and serves as backup.


---

## When to Update

Updates are required when:

* A new utility company is encountered during property onboarding
* A provider changes their rules, fees, forms, or approval requirements
* Move-in procedures or account setup processes have changed
* An issue reveals outdated or incorrect information
