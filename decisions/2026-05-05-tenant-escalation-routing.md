# Decision — Tenant Escalation Requests Routed to Designated Broker

## Decision

Any tenant request to speak with a "manager," "supervisor," or "corporate" is routed through a structured escalation process to **Brittany F., Designated Broker / Leadership** via standardized email + scheduling link.

## Why this decision

Escalation requests have been handled ad hoc, leading to inconsistent responses and missed opportunities to surface pattern issues to leadership. The 2301 NE 125th rat infestation incident underscored that situations involving prolonged habitability concerns need a clear leadership-touchpoint path so leadership can intervene at scale rather than discovering issues months in.

## What this changes operationally

When a tenant requests to speak with a manager / supervisor / corporate:

- Customer Service Specialist responds with a standardized email
- Email includes a scheduling link for direct communication with Brittany F.
- All such requests are logged as Asana tasks so escalation patterns are visible to leadership
