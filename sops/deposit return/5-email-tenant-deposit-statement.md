Email the final package to the Resident using the template below:


:::info
This template contains ==highlighted sections== that are optional or need customization. Review and remove highlighted sections that don't apply before sending.

:::

### Email Template — Deposit Refund Package

**Subject:** Deposit Refund Package // \[Property Address\]

> Thank you for being our resident. We appreciate you choosing Sagareus and wish you all the best in your next chapter.
> 
> **Attached:**
> 
> • Deposit Refund Statement
> • Withholding Details
> • Full Resident Ledger
> • Vendor Invoice(s)
> 
> **Move-in/Move-out Report & Additional Documentation** (Link)
> 
> ==\[Remove below if no refund owed\]==
> 
> ==Refund Check==
> 
> **==If your statement shows a refund due, a check for the refund amount will be mailed to the forwarding address you provided together with a printed copy of the statement. Please allow standard postal time.==**
> 
> **==(If no forwarding address was provided, we will mail it to the lease address and have USPS forward it.)==**
> 
> ==\[End optional section\]==
> 
> Thank you again for your tenancy. We hope your new home treats you well.
