:::warning
**Deadline reminder:** `deposit_return_date` must fall **within 25 days of move-out** (Sagareus target — 5 days before WA State's 30-day legal deadline per [RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280)). Missing the legal deadline creates liability for the full deposit amount.

:::
