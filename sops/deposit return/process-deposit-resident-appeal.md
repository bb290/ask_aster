> **Situation:** A resident contacts Sagareus to dispute or appeal the security deposit withholding amount, citing damage claims, documentation gaps, or disagreement with specific charges.


---

## Key Points

* **Sagareus will review the appeal in detail.** Each line item is evaluated against the four-part [Deposit Withholding Requirements](deposit-withholding-requirements.md): move-in report, prior condition, after condition, and vendor invoice.
* **Appeals are tracked in Asana.** A subtask is created under the Process Deposit task, assigned to [Operations Manager](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md).
* **Response within 5 business days.** Acknowledge the appeal immediately, then provide findings within 5 business days.
* **Withholding reductions are possible** if any withheld item fails the four-part test. If the resident has a credit balance after adjustments, refunds can be issued via Zelle (fastest) or check (\~10 days).


---

## Resident Inquiry

Acknowledge the appeal, then evaluate each item and respond with findings.


---

## Procedure


1. Acknowledge Resident's appeal -- use Email Template: Acknowledge below
2. Create tracking task for Resident's appeal in the Process Deposit task, assigned to [Operations Manager](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md)
3. In the task body, itemize the list of issues raised by the Resident
4. Evaluate each item, and document the following:
   * Reduced withholding: \[Approved/Denied\] -- $\[Amount\]
   * Supporting notes: \[Basis for Decision\]
5. Respond to Resident's appeal using the appropriate Email Template below
6. **If appeal approved (full or partial):** [Lead Accountant](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md) adjusts the ledger in Buildium to reflect reduced withholding
7. Regenerate the deposit refund package, rename to **Deposit Refund Package - \[Address\]_V2.pdf**
8. Send updated statement + fund (if applicable) to the resident

## Email Template — Resident Inquiring Before Deposit Processed

**CC:** accounting@sagareus.com

> Thank you for reaching out about your deposit refund. We are actively processing your funds. In accordance with [RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280), your deposit will be refunded within 30 days of your move-out, and any outstanding balances or credits on your ledger will be resolved during this process. We appreciate your patience. I have included our accounting team to help expedite your request.
