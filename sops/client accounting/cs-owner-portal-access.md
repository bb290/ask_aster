> **Situation:** Owner is inquiring about how to access their portal, or has forgotten their login credentials.


---

## Key Points

* **Self-service resolution** — Reset password or send welcome email directly from Buildium
* **Navigate to owner profile** — Search by email, scroll to "Rental Owner Website" section
* **Portal URL** — [Owner Portal](https://sagareus.managebuilding.com/manager/public/authentication/login)


---

## Procedure


1. Navigate to the owner's profile by searching their email in Buildium
2. Scroll down to "Rental Owner Website"
3. Click on "Reset Password" or "Send Welcome Email"
4. Send response below to owner

## Response

> Hi \[Owner Name\],
>
> A \[Password Reset/Welcome Email\] has been sent to you from our portal system. Once you activate your account, you'll have full access to your [Owner Portal](https://sagareus.managebuilding.com/manager/public/authentication/login).
>
> Please watch for an email from Buildium and check your spam or junk folder if you don't see it in your inbox. If the activation link expires, let us know your preferred time and we'll resend it for you.

### Example Owner Questions

* "How do I log into my owner portal?"
* "I forgot my password"
* "I never received a welcome email"
