:::info
[Landlord Survivor Relief Program](https://www.commerce.wa.gov/landlord-fund/landlord-survivor-relief/)

:::

The Landlord Survivor Relief program can award payments of up to $5,000 for damages directly to the landlord when the conditions below are met. Any landlord is eligible if:

* their Washington rental property has unpaid charges for damages;
* the Resident(s) are fleeing domestic violence, sexual assault, unlawful harassment or stalking;
* the Resident(s) have been permitted to vacate with no notice; and
* the Resident(s) have received a full refund of their security deposit.


---

# Eligibility Checklist


:::warning
We can only apply for the LSRP program if ALL of the following are satisfied:

:::

* We have signed 3rd party affidavit and/or police report
* Resident is allowed to terminate with no penalty
* Sagareus refunded the security deposit in full
* Move-in inspection is signed by the Resident
