> **Situation:** Owner asks how to send funds (owner contribution) to their property account.


---

## Key Points

* **Self-service via portal** — Owner can initiate EFT transfer directly through the Owner Portal
* **"Send Funds" button** — Located in the upper right corner of the portal
* **No action needed** — Response only; no Asana task required


---

>

> • Initiate transfer with EFT
