Owner Reports FAQs

# Management Fees

## Why is Utility Recovery included in Management Fee calculation?

Utility reimbursements collected from Residents are reported as income to ensure IRS compliance. This is also the industry standard.

## Why is my Management Fee higher than usual this month?

Management fees are determined on a monthly cash basis. The management fee for any given month is calculated based on the total income received in the preceding month.

# Rent Payments

## Why hasn't a Resident made their rent payment yet?

Provide latest ledger and assigned Resident Account Manager will follow up.

## Why did we receive multiple rent payments in a one month period?

Tenants may occasionally make multiple rent payments within a single month, leading to increased income recorded for that period.

## Is my property set up for rent increases on the next renewal, how does this process work?

Our leasing team initiates the lease renewal review process approximately 60 days before the lease agreements are set to expire.

## What is the process for Residents that are late paying their rent?


:::warning
Our collection procedure:


1. Resident is enrolled in eviction prevention (3x comm with text/email/call), they are offered three options:
   * Move-out
   * Pay the full balance
   * Enter payment plan
2. IF no response, or resident fails to execute payment plan, we initiate eviction by having attorney serve Pay or Vacate
3. Owner will receive additional communication
4. Payment portal disabled
5. Once eviction starts, residents only have two options: Move-out, or Pay the full balance

:::

# Disbursements

## When can we expect our reports/disbursements to be received every month?

All reports and disbursements are sent out by the 20th of each month.

## Can I lower my authorization amount?

Sagareus requires a minimum authorization amount of $1,000.

## Why did I receive multiple disbursements instead of one?

Owner has multiple properties OR available funds exceeded withdrawal amount.

## Why is the disbursement amount smaller than the rent amount?

Owner draws are determined by the property's current cash balance.

## What form of payment is the owner disbursement sent as?

EFT, typically requires 2-3 business days to clear.

# Utility Recovery

## Who is responsible for paying utility bills?

Utility bills are received by Sagareus and paid through your operating account. Charges are added to Resident's ledger as Utility Recovery.

## I received a utility bill that has not been paid yet. Are you managing this account?

Pay outstanding balance immediately, audit utility accounts, pass-through Resident-responsible portions.

## Why do my utilities need to be managed by Sagareus?

For accurate billing on flat fee pass-throughs, property monitoring (leaks, abnormal usage), and tenant communication.

# Vendor Bills

## Can I have more details regarding what this repair was?

Review the work order details and provide owner with summary.

## Why am I being charged for invoices that were not approved by me?

Owners are typically not informed of maintenance charges that fall within their property authorization limit.

## Can I be notified of all maintenance going forward?

Update "Maintenance dispatch" setting to "Owner Provide Instruction" in Asana Property Settings.

Provide tenant's Deposit Refund Package with itemized list.

# Miscellaneous

## What is the RRIO charge for?

City of Seattle's Rental Registration & Inspection Ordinance (RRIO) fee. Registration/Renewal every 2 years.

## How do I access my property listing photos?

Send owner Google Drive link to their listing photos.

## Why does the security deposit show up as an expense line item?

The security deposit is held in trust and recorded as a liability.

## What is a temp loan?

Sagareus issues temporary loans to operating accounts to cover expenses between rent payments and owner disbursements.

## What is the property reserve?

The property reserve is the amount withheld from monthly draw to maintain a baseline balance in your operating account.
