> **Situation:** Sagareus is reimbursing or giving a credit to a Resident (management credit, goodwill, error correction, etc.).


---

## Key Points

* **Management credit** — Used when Sagareus is absorbing the cost (not the owner).
* **Funds come from Sagareus** — Withdrawal from Sagareus account, deposit to owner's operating account.
* **Zelle option available** — If resident requests credit via Zelle instead of ledger credit, follow the Zelle procedure below.


---

## Procedure — Ledger Credit


1. Go to the Resident's ledger
2. Click on "Receive Payment"
   * Select date as the day of entry
   * Payment Method: Direct Deposit
   * Received From: Select Resident's name
   * Memo: MANAGEMENT CREDIT
   * Account: RENT
   * Click "Save Payment"
3. Withdrawal money from Sagareus
   * Accounting > Banking > 100. Operating
   * Select "Record Other Transaction"
   * Check "Withdrawal"
   * Property: Sagareus
   * Offsetting Account: Resident/Owner Credit
   * Memo: "Reason of payment" | "Property house number"
4. Record deposit on Owner's account
   * Accounting > Banking > "Owner's Operating Account"
   * Click on "Record Deposit"
   * Check the credit made on the Resident's ledger
   * Click "Save"
5. Transfer funds in the corresponding banks


---

## Procedure — Zelle Refund

If tenant requests their approved credit via Zelle instead of toward their outstanding balance:


1. Issue Credit on resident's ledger
   * Credit Action: Issue credit to waive unpaid charges
   * Account: RENT
   * Memo: brief description of credit — if provided by Sagareus enter "MANAGEMENT CREDIT"
2. Issue Refund
   * Normal process for Zelle refunds
   * Must be completed by accounting team
