> **Situation:** Housing authority sends a change of rent notice for a Section 8 or voucher-holding Resident.


---

## Key Points

* **Save to Buildium** — File the change of rent notice/PDF to the BD lease
* **Notify Resident** — Email a copy to the Resident
* **Accounting review** — [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) verifies if recurring credits on Resident ledger need updating


---

## Action


1. Save the change of rent notice or PDF to BD lease
2. Email a copy of the file to the Residents
3. Create Asana task in [Finance & Office](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1206627942064331/list/1206627972779236) project assigned to [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md)
   * [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) to verify if there are recurring credits on the Resident ledger needing updated
