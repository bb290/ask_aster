> **Situation:** Resident and owner agree to split the cost of a property upgrade that is not required by law but substantially improves the property.


---

## Key Points

* **Common split items** — A/C installation, EV charger, oversized toilet, specialized appliances, or other upgrades beyond standard habitability requirements.
* **Signed agreement required** — Sagareus must obtain a signed General Addendum formalizing the cost-split arrangement before work begins.
* **Sagareus pays vendor directly** — Sagareus pays the full vendor invoice and bills the resident's share via Resident Ledger.


---

## Procedure


1. Draft General Addendum in Buildium
   * Buildium → Lease Profile → Click Add Addendum Button → Choose "General Addendum"
2. Upload copy of invoice or estimate, if available
3. Fill in "General Addendum Terms" section:
   * "Residents and Property Owner have agreed to split the cost of \[Upgrade Description\] 50/50. Attached is the current estimate, subject to change per vendor. Sagareus will pay the vendor directly & collect 50% payment from residents via Resident Portal."
4. Send for signature
5. Add 50% charge to Resident Ledger
