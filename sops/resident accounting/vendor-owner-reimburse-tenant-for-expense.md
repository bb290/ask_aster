> **Situation:** Owner is reimbursing or giving a credit to a Resident (utility credit, goodwill gesture, negotiated concession, etc.).


---

## Key Points

* **Owner absorbs the cost** — Credit is issued from the owner's account, not Sagareus.
* **Notify resident** — Send confirmation email with updated ledger attached.


---

## Procedure


1. Go to the Resident's ledger
2. Click on "Issue Credit"
   * Select date as the day of entry
   * Amount: Amount of Credit
   * Credit Action: Issue Credit to Waive Unpaid Charges
   * Memo: Brief description of reason for credit (e.g., "Utility credit")
   * Account: RENT
   * Click "Save Credit"
3. Communicate to the Resident
