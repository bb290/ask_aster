> **Situation:** Resident has overpaid rent and requests a refund of the credit balance.


---

## Key Points

* **Refund to original source** — All refunds are issued back to the original payment source per [Resident Refund](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-resident-refund.md) policy.
* **4 refund options** — Apply to next rent, check by mail, in-person pickup, or Zelle.
* **Zelle requires test payment** — A test payment is sent first, then full credit once confirmed.


---

## Response

### Email Template — Rent Overpayment Refund Options

> **Subject: Rent Overpayment Refund Options // \[Property Address\]**
>
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
>
> We have received your request for a refund. You have the following options to handle this overpayment:
>
> **Apply Credit to Next Rent Payment:** The credit can be automatically applied to your next month's rent, reducing the amount due. No action is required from you for this option.
>
> **Receive a Check by Mail:** We can mail a refund check to your address on file. Please allow approximately 10-15 business days for processing and mail delivery. If you choose this option, please confirm your current mailing address.
>
> **Pick Up a Check In-Person:** You can schedule an appointment to pick up a refund check from our office. Please reply to this email to coordinate a suitable date and time.
>
> **We can issue funds via Zelle:** The credit can be issued back to you via Zelle. Please provide your Zelle information. A test payment will be issued and once confirmed the full credit will be transferred to your account.
>
> Thank you,
