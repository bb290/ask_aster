> **Situation:** Resident paid rent using a credit card and was charged an unnecessary convenience fee. They may not be aware that EFT (electronic transfer) is free.


---

## Key Points

* **EFT has no cost** — inform Resident they can switch to EFT in the Resident Portal to avoid fees
* **Assist with setup** — some Residents need help setting up the portal. Only do this over the phone with verbal authorization.


---

## Resident Inquiry

Call or email Resident to inform them EFT is an option.

### Email Template — Credit Card Fee Notice

> Thank you for your payment; I noticed you used a credit card, which charged you a \[Fee Amount\] credit card convenience fee.
>
> If you are able in the future, you can set up payment via EFT in the same Resident Portal. This payment method has no additional cost.
>
> Please let me know if you'd like assistance setting this up.

## Procedure — Assist Resident Set Up EFT Payment

**Only do this with Resident over the phone & verbal authorization.**


1. Navigate to Buildium Resident Profile → Click Resident name
2. Scroll down to "Resident Center" section
3. Click "Sign in as User" — now you are in the Resident's profile
4. Set up their account as if you are them
5. Ask Resident to read Routing / Account number to you
6. Type in & repeat back to ensure accuracy
7. Ask if they'd like their rent paid automatically
8. Save!

### Example Resident Questions

* "Why was I charged an extra fee on my rent payment?"
* "How do I avoid the credit card fee?"
* "Is there a way to pay rent without the processing fee?"
* "I didn't know there was a fee for using my debit card"
* "Can you help me set up a different payment method?"
