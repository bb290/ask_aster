> **Situation:** Resident has overpaid rent and is requesting a refund for the credit on their account.


---

## Key Points

* **Four refund options** — Apply to next month's rent, check by mail (10–15 business days), in-person check pickup, or Zelle transfer
* **Accounting review** — [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) reviews whether a credit is actually due before processing
* **Loop in accounting** — Add accounting@sagareus.com to the Resident's request


---

## Response

> We have received your request for a refund. You have the following options to handle this overpayment:
>
> **Apply Credit to Next Rent Payment:** The credit can be automatically applied to your next month's rent, reducing the amount due. No action is required from you for this option.
>
> **Receive a Check by Mail:** We can mail a refund check to your address on file. Please allow approximately 10-15 business days for processing and mail delivery. If you choose this option, please confirm your current mailing address.
>
> **Pick Up a Check In-Person:** You can schedule an appointment to pick up a refund check from our office. Please reply to this email to coordinate a suitable date and time.
>
> **We can issue funds via Zelle:** The credit can be issued back to you Zelle. Please provide your Zelle information. A test payment will be issued and once confirmed the full credit will be transferred to your account.

## Action


1. Add accounting@sagareus.com to the Resident request
2. Create tracking task in [Finance & Office project](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1206627942064331/list/1206627972779236) and assign to [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md)
3. [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) to review if any credit is actually due
4. If Yes, coordinate with the Resident on their preferred refund method
