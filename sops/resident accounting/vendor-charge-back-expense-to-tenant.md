> **Situation:** Maintenance team requests to pass through a repair or maintenance expense to the tenant's ledger due to tenant-caused damage.


---

## Key Points

* **Lease liability must be confirmed** — Review the lease to verify the repair falls under resident responsibility (negligence, misuse, pet damage, no-show fees, pest issues from housekeeping).
* **Itemized invoice required** — Vendor invoice must clearly outline the nature of the repair.
* **Payment plans available** — Under $150: due next rent cycle. $150-$600: up to 3 installments. Over $600: up to 12 installments. Beyond that, escalate to Operations Manager.

### Example Requests

* "Please pass through this expense to the tenant"
* "Tenant caused the clog, charge them for the rooter"
* "Pet damage repair — bill to resident"


---

## Procedure

### Maintenance Team Action


1. Initiate Asana Task — Template — Finance & Office — Passing Maintenance/Repair Expense to Resident Ledger
2. Project: Office & Finance
3. Section: Untitled
4. Assigned to: Lead Accountant (Ana) (Automatic)
5. Due: Same Day / Next Day (Automatic)
6. Link associated maintenance task(s) for reference

### Accounting Manager Action


1. **Confirm Lease Liability**
   * Review the lease agreement to confirm the repair falls under Resident responsibility
   * Common examples: damage caused by negligence (clogged drains, broken windows, pet damage), missed vendor appointment/no-show fees, pest issues caused by Resident housekeeping
   * Verify lease — Sagareus Lease outlines Resident responsibilities under "Rules + Regulations" or "Third Party Vendor Access" sections
2. **Obtain and Upload Invoice**
   * Ensure the vendor invoice is itemized and clearly outlines the nature of the repair
   * Upload invoice to Chargeback Task
3. **Add Charge to Resident Ledger**
   * Enter charge in the Resident ledger via Buildium
   * Income Account: Misc Income
   * Include clear description (e.g., "Invoice for drain clearing — Resident Chargeback")
4. **Notify Resident via email**
5. **Document Communication**
   * Screenshot or upload copy of email sent to Resident to Charge Back task
6. **Set Up Payment Plan (if Resident responds)**
   * Under $150: Due in full next rent cycle
   * $150–$600: Up to 3 monthly installments
   * Over $600: Up to 12 monthly installments
   * If these options do not work → Escalate to Operations Manager
   * Once agreed via email, update Resident ledger with payment amounts & due dates
