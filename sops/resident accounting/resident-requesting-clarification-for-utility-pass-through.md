> **Situation:** Resident questions a utility pass-through charge on their ledger.


---

## Key Points

* **Single inquiry vs. multiple** — Different workflows depending on whether the resident is asking about one charge or several.
* **Verify before responding** — Confirm the lease specifies utility pass-through and the amount matches the backing bill.
* **Multiple inquiries → audit** — Use the Audit Utility Pass Through templated task.


---

## Procedure — Single Pass Through Inquiry


1. Verify:
   * Does the lease specify utility pass through?
   * Is the amount passed through accurate and matches the backing bill?
2. **IF YES** — Send Resident the backing bill:

### Email Template — Utility Pass Through Verification

> **Subject: Utility Pass Through Verification // \[Property Address\]**
> **Attachment:** Backing bill, renamed to \[Utility\] - \[Start Date\] to \[End Date\]
>
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
>
> We have verified the utility pass through:
>
> **Utility Company:** \[Utility Company\]
> **Service Period:** \[Start Date\] to \[End Date\]
> **Amount:** $\[Amount\]
>
> The backing bill is attached.
>
> Thank you,


3. **IF NO** — Correct the pass through:

### Email Template — Utility Pass Through Correction

> **Subject: Utility Pass Through Correction // \[Property Address\]**
>
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
>
> Thank you for checking, we identified an issue with the pass through and made corrections: \[What Correction Was Made\]
>
> Thank you,


---

## Procedure — Multiple Pass Through Inquiries


1. Use the **Audit Utility Pass Through** templated task in the Finance & Office project
2. Assign to [Utility Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/utility-coordinator.md)
3. Add [Utility Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/utility-coordinator.md) to the email thread
4. Accounting team to follow Audit Utility Pass Through SOP
