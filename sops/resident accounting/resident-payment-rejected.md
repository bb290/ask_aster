> **Situation:** A resident's rent payment is rejected by Buildium due to transaction limit exceeded on the property's bank account.


---

## Key Points

* **Buildium per-deposit limits** — Each property bank account has a per-deposit limit in Buildium. When a payment exceeds this, it is rejected.
* **Fix is immediate** — Low limit increases take effect immediately in most cases.
* **Temp account fallback** — If limit increase doesn't take effect immediately, temporarily switch property bank account to 901 Temp and track the change.


---

## Procedure


 1. Notification is received

    ![image](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXclzglz-2AX6Kn1tSnEWoNbhhdb0vw6eNV--hlH1Y-gczRCl3or6bbPwgZSdmxAHWrtpCIY6YBoaMjL9qRfGw3pc1kY1Pw1JmDACf00DvMa3npBfw4On6s976YxIbGlWyuLW5qu40D_IvDgt4DiPsGavA?key=nnhKEmcP1_mvGVmGiinqRQ)
 2. In Buildium, navigate to subject property
 3. Click through to subject operating account
 4. Select payment settings
 5. On Bank Account, click "Request" change for income payment limits

    ![image](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdvBeQg0H2AyahkbyrcsAUaSF6rjG02P-mLHMkQ9-Hsu840eqLauQmE1c55SV4ini8kuPjMn7nssgV2lUXskGZnPiUxEpYaOVdx-VCJuMA9zMAaj_f2qtuXS6Swxn2Qb5icicQq_3wsErwdn7EI3nzoykbN?key=nnhKEmcP1_mvGVmGiinqRQ)
 6. Adjust the per-deposit limit to exceed the current transaction and submit changes
 7. Low limit increases take effect immediately — review and confirm

    ![image](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXesvnWPDOdU7mXHEJKhUmGCGQ2KoZXMqTjMARqkp-B8YChSA94KIey8VyHkamGg83x27YRlUC_SySrASOmJyTz_FbHPlsPpCINJQIHAD2CY1j8EJnQSSDYd2oQqafutEu1Wg3Tc_UU7VxDTAIHxafIYsg?key=nnhKEmcP1_mvGVmGiinqRQ)
 8. Communicate to Resident
 9. If limit did not take immediate effect, adjust the property bank account to 901 Temp and communicate to the Resident that the issue has been resolved
10. Create an Asana task to track and assign to Lead Accountant noting change to operating account
