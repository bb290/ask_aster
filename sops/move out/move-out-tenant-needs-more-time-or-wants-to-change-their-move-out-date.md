**When to Use This Page**

> *Use this page if:*
>
> * *a tenant reached out asking to push back their move-out date*
> * *the move-out date needs to change for any reason*
> * *you need to know how to update dates across all related tasks*
> * *you need to add a prorated rent charge after the actual move-out*

**Situation Summary** 

When a resident requests a modified move-out date, the change needs to be communicated, documented, and reflected across all downstream tasks — including TurnOver, Lease Up, and Security Deposit. Prorated rent must be added to the lease ledger once the actual move-out is complete.


---

## How to Respond to the Resident


1. Reply to the resident using this template:

> *Hi \[Resident Name\], Thanks for letting us know. We can accommodate an updated move-out date — please confirm the new date you intend to fully vacate and return keys so we can notify scheduled vendors. Please note that rent will be prorated through your actual move-out date and added to your ledger. Best,*


---

## How to Escalate and Document


2. Create a subtask inside the Move-Out task in Asana:
   * Title: **Modified Move Out Date**
   * Assigned to: Leasing Team Lead 
   * Due: Today
   * Attach a copy or screenshot of the original message and your response for context.


---

## What the Leasing Manager Does Next


3. Leasing Team Lead updates the Move-Out date across all related tasks:
   * Parent Task, Move Out, Turn Over, Lease Up, Security Deposit
   * Move Out subtask → new move-out date
   * Turn Over task → 1 day after new move-out date
4. Keep the Modified Move Out Date subtask open until the actual move-out inspection is complete — residents frequently revise the date again.


---

## How to Add Prorated Rent After Move-Out


5. Once the move-out inspection is complete, add the prorated rent charge to the lease ledger:
   * Buildium → Rentals → Properties → Unit Profile → Lease Ledger → Click **Enter Charge**
   * Amount: Prorated Rent
   * Memo: Prorated Rent \[X days\]
   * Account: RENT
   * Click **Save Charge**


---

## Escalation

Assign a Modified Move Out Date subtask to Leasing Team Lead due today with the full communication thread attached.
