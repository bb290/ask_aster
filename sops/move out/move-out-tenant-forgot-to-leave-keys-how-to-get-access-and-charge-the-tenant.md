**When to Use This Page**

> *Use this page if:*
>
> * *the tenant confirmed they've moved out but keys aren't in the lockbox*
> * *you need to get into the unit but have no key*
> * *you need to know how to charge the tenant for a locksmith*
> * *the tenant left without returning keys or garage openers*

**Situation Summary** 

Occasionally a tenant vacates but forgets to leave their keys in the lockbox. Once you've confirmed the tenant has fully moved out, you can proceed — but you'll need a locksmith to gain access. The cost of the locksmith is charged back to the tenant and deducted from their security deposit.


---

## How to Gain Access and Document the Charge


1. Confirm with the tenant via text or call that they have fully vacated the property and will not be returning.
   * Do not proceed until vacancy is confirmed. If you cannot reach them or are unsure, treat the unit as occupied and follow the \[Move-Out: Tenant Hasn't Left SOP\].
2. Document the situation in Asana on the Move-Out task.
   * Example: *"Arrived on \[date\] — keys not in lockbox. Contacted resident, confirmed fully vacated. Proceeding with locksmith for access."*
3. Contact the locksmith. Locksmith contact info is stored in 1Password.
4. Wait for the locksmith to arrive and gain entry. Do not attempt to force entry yourself.
5. Once inside, complete the full Move-Out Inspection per the standard process — \[Move-Out: How to Complete a Move-Out Inspection\].
6. After the inspection, change the lockbox code to the internal code (found in 1Password) and update 1Password.
7. Create a subtask on the Move-Out task in Asana:
   * Title: **Locksmith Charge — Keys Not Returned**
   * Assigned to: Turn Over Coordinator
   * Due: Today
   * Include the locksmith invoice or cost confirmation as an attachment
   * Note: *"Tenant confirmed vacated but did not leave keys. Locksmith required for access. Cost to be deducted from security deposit."*


---

## Decision Points ⚠️

* If tenant **cannot be reached** or vacancy is **unconfirmed** → Do not enter. Follow \[Move-Out: Tenant Hasn't Left SOP\].
* If tenant confirms vacated → Proceed with locksmith and charge back per this SOP.


---

## Escalation

Assign the Locksmith Charge subtask to Turn Over Coordinator today with the invoice attached so the cost can be properly deducted from the security deposit.


---

## Required Documentation

* Asana comment confirming tenant vacancy and reason for locksmith
* Locksmith invoice attached to Asana subtask
* 1Password updated with new lockbox code after inspection
