:::warning
If anyone on Sagareus Team suspects a property has been trespassed or potentially are still in the property, **they do not enter for personal safety**.

:::


1. Vacant properties are targets for the unhoused or squatters to break in
   * Often they will leave on their own
2. If anyone on Sagareus Team suspects a property has been trespassed or potentially are still in the property, **they do not enter for personal safety**
3. Take these steps instead:
   * Call Police to report Trespass
     * Police will enter unit to ensure no longer occupied
   * Call Locksmith to replace locks
   * Create Asana Task
     * Leasing Project
     * Task Template: Possible Trespass // \[Address\]
     * Due Date = Today or following day
     * Assigned to [Turn Over Coordinator Lead](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md)
     * Document with photos & comments on suspicious activity
