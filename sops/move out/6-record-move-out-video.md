**When to Use This Page**

> *Use this page if:*
>
> * *you've been assigned the* `*Record | Move-out video*` *subtask*
> * *you're on-site for the move-out inspection and want to record the video in the same trip*

**Situation Summary**

The Move-Out Video is a continuous walkthrough that captures the condition of the property on move-out day. It doubles as (a) documentation for security-deposit disputes and (b) before/after marketing content once Turn Over is complete. Record it the same day as the inspection so condition is consistent across both artifacts.


---

## Procedure


1. Walk the property in a continuous sweep — enter each room, pan slowly, capture floors, walls, ceilings, fixtures, and appliances.
2. Call out each room as you enter it so the audio track labels the video.
3. End the walkthrough outside with a shot of the exterior and any outdoor spaces (yard, garage, patio).
4. Upload the video to the link in the Move Out subtask.
5. Mark this subtask complete once the upload finishes.
