When a Resident stops communicating with the landlord and stops rent payment, there is a chance they have abandoned the premise. Follow the abandonment procedure to establish without doubt the Resident has vacated so the landlord can retain possession of the property.


:::warning
**Any documented signs of Residents still there will immediately stop the whole process.**

:::


:::info
* Abandonment procedures are tracked in the Abandonment project in Asana
* Use the **Abandonment // {Address}** templated task

:::


---

## Does the Resident still manage electricity?


1. Assign task to Utility Coordinator
2. Check if electricity account is still managed by the Resident?


---

## Document attempt to contact Resident


1. Copy the Resident's contact to the task body
2. Do two follow ups with Residents, 48 hours apart:
   * Call/Text/Email with the following template and document their response:

> <Resident>, can you confirm if you are still occupying the home at <Address>?


---

## Abandonment Notice // Create

* Assign the task to "Asana Bot", and the file will be created within 24 hours
* If notice needs to be prepared immediately, download Abandonment Notice template


---

## Abandonment Notice // Certified Mail


1. Send certified mail from Mailform.io to the Resident:
   * Include Electronic Return Receipt
2. Set due date to 10 days, confirm if we receive electronic return receipt indicating the Resident received the mail


---

## Abandonment Notice // Serve


1. Follow Servicing Notice - In Person procedures
2. Fill out Declaration of Service - Abandonment
3. Knock loudly on the door and see if anyone answers
4. Document signs of abandonment:
   * Mailbox full?
   * Can you see inside the window?
   * Did anyone answer the door?
   * Signs of pets?


---

## Establish Abandonment


:::warning
Wait 5 days after posting the notice to allow for the certified mail to arrive.

Resident has abandoned IF **all** of the following are true:

* Residents have not responded to all attempts to communicate
* Landlord has not received rent payment
* Resident's mail has not been collected
* Resident's utilities have been disconnected

:::


---

## Enter property & Confirm abandonment


1. Schedule locksmith to meet you at the property
2. Knock loudly first, to ensure no one is inside
3. Locksmith to open the door
4. Make a determination of whether the Resident has abandoned


---

## IF personal properties are left


:::warning
Landlord must provide Resident notice of how we are disposing the personal items. By default, we recommend storing the items in the home for 45 days vs relocating to 3rd party storage.

:::
