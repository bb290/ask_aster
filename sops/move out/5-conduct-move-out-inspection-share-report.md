> * *the resident has vacated and the property is ready to inspect*

**Situation Summary**

The Move-Out Inspection is the single most consequential artifact of the entire move-out. It feeds Turn Over (scope of work), Security Deposit (damage deductions), and Lease Up (marketing). It must be completed on or within 48 hours of the move-out date and emailed to leasing@sagareus.com the same day it's completed.


---

## Procedure


1. Conduct the inspection independently — without the outgoing resident or owner present — so you can work efficiently and thoroughly.
2. Open the HappyCo Inspector App and create a new **Move-Out Inspection** for the property.
   * If the address isn't showing, switch to **Edge** and type the address in manually.
3. Complete every checklist item. Take photos of all condition issues, especially anything that could be a security-deposit deduction.
4. When done, immediately email the completed inspection to [leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:leasing@sagareus.com) from the Inspector App.
5. Mark this subtask complete.
