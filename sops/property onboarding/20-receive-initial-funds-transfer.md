## When This Runs

Runs during the Messy Middle phase, immediately after the Onboarding Progress Update email requests the owner's initial funds transfer. Tracks receipt of the property reserve and any other initial contributions.

## Procedure

Monitor Owner Account 2x week to ensure Initial Funds are transferred, as requested. Once funds are received, mark task complete. Make a comment every time you check the funds. After 2 weeks of non-payment, escalate.
