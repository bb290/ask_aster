:::info
The Messy Middle is the transitional phase after Initial Onboarding completes and before Finalize Onboarding can begin. Multiple tasks are in progress simultaneously, timelines vary, and sequencing is not always linear.

:::

## Cadence

The Onboarding Team reviews and updates task due dates **every Tuesday and Friday** to confirm progress and address blockers.

Each property is unique — tasks may be completed in different orders and at different speeds depending on circumstances.

## Vacant Properties

Vacant properties require a Turnover and Lease-Up process. During the Messy Middle:

* Turnover and Lease-Up tasks are reviewed twice per week
* The team confirms tasks are actively being worked
* Timelines vary based on property condition, scope of turnover work, and responsiveness / execution by Sagareus staff and vendors

## Occupied Properties

Occupied properties focus on transition and verification:

* Receiving and auditing all documentation and ledgers
* Transferring funds
* Assessing current property condition
* Timelines vary based on availability and completeness of records, quality of the prior management company, and responsiveness of Sagareus staff

## Why This Is Normal

The Messy Middle is expected and temporary. Consistent check-ins and structured follow-ups ensure each property moves steadily toward stabilization.
