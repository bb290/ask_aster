## When This Runs

Runs during the Finalize Onboarding stage alongside the W-9 update. Configures the outgoing EFT account that will receive owner draws from Buildium. Required before the Test Draw can be sent.

## Procedure

Enter Owner Distribution Account number from Accounting Only Form into Buildium.

Buildium → Owner Profile → **Financial Tab** → **Owner Draw ePay settings** subtab → **Outgoing EFT "Add EFT Account"** → Copy / paste **Routing & Account Number** → Check authorization box & save.
