## When This Runs

New Client only — the fee is waived for existing clients. Charged immediately during Initial Onboarding to ensure timely payment; early payment behavior also surfaces clients who may be difficult to work with so we can terminate agreements early if needed.

## Procedure

Charge the Onboarding Fee immediately to ensure timely payment. This allows us to flag clients who will be difficult and terminate agreements early.

Buildium → Accounting → Bills → Record Bill:

* **Date:** Today
* **Due:** 3 days
* **Pay to:** Sagareus
* **Memo:** Onboarding Fee
* **Property or Company:** Subject property address
* **Account:** Onboarding Fee Expense (not Onboarding Income)
* **Description:** Onboarding Fee
* **Amount:** Onboarding Fee listed on PM Agreement
