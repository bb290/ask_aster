## When This Runs

Final step of the onboarding lifecycle. Runs after all tasks are verified complete. This is a KPI-triggering step — the email send date is recorded on the parent task and drives the **Onboarding Finalized** metric on the Growth Dashboard. Target: within 60 days of PMA signing.

## Procedure

Send the Onboarding Complete email to the owner. **Update the Onboarding Finalized date field** on the parent task. This drives KPI tracking on the Growth Dashboard.
