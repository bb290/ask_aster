## When This Runs

Runs during Initial Onboarding for any property with existing occupants at onboarding. Notifies the previous management firm that the property is transferring to Sagareus and initiates document and funds handover. Skip for fully vacant properties.

## Procedure

Send the appropriate email to the previous firm to begin transfer. Use the correct variant:

* Email Template: Previous Firm Management Transfer Notification — **Fully Vacant**
* Email Template: Previous Firm Management Transfer Notification — **Fully Occupied**
* Email Template: Previous Firm Management Transfer Notification — **Vacant AND Occupied**
