## When This Runs

Runs during the Finalize Onboarding stage once the Accounting Only Onboarding Form is received. Enters the owner's W-9 tax identity info into Buildium's 1099-MISC tax filing section and sets the mailing address for year-end tax reporting.

## Procedure

Enter W-9 Information from Accounting Only Form into Buildium 1099-MISC tax filing section.

Buildium → Owner Profile → **1099-MISC tax filing section** → Click **Add**:

* **Tax Identity Type:** EIN or SSN — Copy / paste
* **Update Mailing Address** — Check box **"Use a Different Address"**:
  * C/O Sagareus
  * 2265 116th Ave NE
  * Bellevue, WA 98004
* Click **Save**
