## When This Runs

Runs at the end of Initial Onboarding, once all preceding setup subtasks are complete or assigned/in progress. This is a KPI-triggering step — the email send date is recorded on the parent task and drives the **Initial Onboard Complete** metric on the Growth Dashboard. Target: within 2 days of Onboarding Form receipt.

## Procedure

Once all of the above tasks are complete or assigned/in progress, send the appropriate email to the property owner in order to:

* Update on Progress
* Clarify any confusion on Onboarding Form (delete this section if unnecessary)
* Initiate Funds Transfer

Once sent → Print email to PDF & upload to onboarding parent task. **Update the Initial Onboard Complete date field** — this drives KPI tracking on the Growth Dashboard.

Use the correct variant:

* Email Template: Onboarding Progress Update — **Fully Vacant**
* Email Template: Onboarding Progress Update — **Fully Occupied**
* Email Template: Onboarding Progress Update — **Vacant AND Occupied**
