:::info
Onboarding is the back half of a brand-consistent client journey. To keep the experience seamless, internal operations should closely match the external sales process.

:::

## Before Onboarding

Clients flow through some variation of this sequence before they reach the Onboarding Team:

* **Online Research** — Website, social media
* **Request PM Proposal** — Prospect fills out [Proposal Request](https://www.sagareus.com/pm-proposal) (counts as SQL); directed to Thank You Page. Client / broker referrals get a personal response from Biz Dev.
* **Custom Proposal** — 4-email educational follow-up sequence with CTA: Schedule a call. Prospect books with Biz Dev Manager.
* **PMA Signed** — Once signed, client is automatically redirected to the Onboarding Page.

## PMA Signed → Onboarding Triggered

Sale is closed, onboarding begins. Biz Dev Manager:

* Updates HubSpot Deal
* Moves Deal to **Onboarding / PMA Signed** stage
* Team is notified via email
* Asana Onboarding task is auto-created

## Why This Matters

Our internal operations must match the polished external sales experience the client just went through. Accurate data input, proactive communication, and detailed documentation during onboarding are what deliver on the brand promise.
