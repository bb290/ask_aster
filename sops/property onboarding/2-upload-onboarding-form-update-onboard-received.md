## When This Runs

After the PMA is signed and the client is redirected to the Onboarding Form. This step captures the form on the Asana task, stamps the **Onboard Received** KPI date, and unblocks all downstream data entry.

## Procedure

* Once the PM Agreement is signed, the client is automatically re-directed to the Onboarding Form.
* Onboarding Forms are automatically converted to PDF.
* The Customer Service Specialist receives an email when the Onboarding Form is complete, and the PDF automatically saves to the **Onboarding Forms 2.0** Google file.
* Once received, the Onboarding Team uploads the Onboarding Form to the Asana task and completes initial data entry tasks.
* **Update the** `**Onboard Received**` **date field** on the parent task.

**Follow up until form is received** — Onboarding is stalled until this form is received. Owners are operating on their own timeline, so follow up weekly until the form is received.
