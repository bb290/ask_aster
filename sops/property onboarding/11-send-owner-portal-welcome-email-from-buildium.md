## When This Runs

New Client only — existing clients already have portal access. Runs during Initial Onboarding once the owner profile exists in Buildium so the client can log in and view reports, contributions, and draws.

## Procedure

Buildium → Owner Profile → Scroll to **Rental Owner Website** section → Click **Send welcome email**.

Repeat for all Owners on the Account.
