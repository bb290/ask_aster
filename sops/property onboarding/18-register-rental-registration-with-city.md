:::info
This document is the Outline-side hook for the Asana onboarding template subtask `REGISTER | Rental Registration with City`. It routes to the topic tree for city registration and business license procedures.

:::

## Trigger

This step runs during property onboarding in the Client Relations 2.0 onboarding templates:

* `[Converted to template] [NEW TEMPLATE] (New Client) <Address> / <Owner Name>` subtask `REGISTER | Rental Registration with City`
* `[Converted to template] [NEW TEMPLATE] (Adding Property) <Address> / <Owner Name>` subtask `REGISTER | Rental Registration with City`

## Procedure


1. Identify the jurisdiction for the new property (city).
2. Open [Registration & License](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-registration-license.md) and find the matching `Registration & License // <City>` doc.
3. Follow the `Setup` subdoc for that city.
4. If the city does not yet have a Setup doc, document the procedure as you execute it (first-execution transcription) and update the city's Setup doc accordingly.

## Next

* Back to the onboarding Asana template: continue with the next subtask after Setup is complete.
