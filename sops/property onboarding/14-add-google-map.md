## When This Runs

During Initial Onboarding once the property address is confirmed. Adds the property to the Sagareus AUM Google Map so it shows up in our internal portfolio view.

## Procedure

Open **Sagareus AUM Google Map** → Click the **3 dots** next to **MultiFamily Properties** or **Single Family Homes**, as appropriate → **Open Data Table** → **Add Row** (very bottom) → Enter **street address**, **city**, and **unit count**. Property should populate onto the map.
