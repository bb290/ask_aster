# Wasps


:::warning
Wasps are a safety hazard. Sagareus policy is to dispatch vendor within 24 hours of Resident report.

:::


---

# Ants/Spider/Mice (Single Family, Non-Seattle)


:::warning
For Non-Seattle single family homes, Residents are responsible for one-time and seasonal services.

:::


---

# Ants/Spider/Mice (Single Family, Seattle)


:::warning
Seattle requires housing provider to be responsible for pest control regardless of property type.

[SDCI - Pests](https://www.seattle.gov/sdci/codes/common-code-questions/pests)

:::


---

# Ants/Spider/Mice (Multi-Family)


:::warning
RCW 59.18.060 (4) states that it is the duty of a landlord to provide a reasonable program to control pests and infestations and to control infestations in multifamily buildings.

:::


---

# Mole or Animal Digging Up Lawn (Single Family)


:::info
For a single family home, tenants are responsible for yard maintenance. Provide best practice tips to the residents by spraying grub killer.

:::
