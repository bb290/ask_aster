> **Situation:** Resident reports a maintenance issue classified as High Priority — immediate safety, habitability, or major property damage risk.


---

## Key Points

* **High Priority issues** — Major water leak, mold, heat failure, fridge failure, A/C failure (vulnerable Residents), structural damage (roof, exterior door/window), or any situation where safety or security is compromised
* **Immediate escalation** — Tag both [Maintenance Coordination Lead](../../docs/role-profiles/maintenance-coordination-lead.md) and [Operations Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md); create URGENT REPAIR subtask
* **Same-day response** — Due date is always today
* **When in doubt, escalate** — If the issue feels incredibly urgent, call any/all leadership to assist


---


:::info
Any request related to work needed, repairs, landscaping, pests, etc. Create or Update Asana task per usual. Assess priority: High, Normal, Low.

:::

## Response

> Received—thank you for reporting this. Your request has been marked as High Priority. Our team has been alerted and is actively working to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. You'll receive an update shortly.

## Action


1. Create Asana Task
   * Maintenance Project; New Section
2. Update Fields
   * PRIORITY Field to HIGH
   * Ticket Submitted Date: Today
3. Assign to [Maintenance Coordination Lead](../../docs/role-profiles/maintenance-coordination-lead.md)
   * Due Date: Today
4. @ Tag both [Maintenance Coordination Lead](../../docs/role-profiles/maintenance-coordination-lead.md) and [Operations Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md)
5. Create subtask titled "URGENT REPAIR"
   * Assign to [Operations Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md)
   * Due Today
6. If you feel the issue is incredibly urgent — Error on the side of caution, Call any / all leadership to assist

### Example Resident Questions

* "There's water flooding my unit"
* "My heat isn't working and it's freezing"
* "I found mold in the bathroom"
* "My fridge stopped working and all my food is spoiling"
* "The front door won't lock"
