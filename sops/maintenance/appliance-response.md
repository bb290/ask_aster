### Initial Investigation


1. Before responding to Resident, research Buildium records to locate appliance details we have on record:
   * Reports > Appliances
   * Recent Inspections (Property > Unit > Files; Annual Inspection; Move In Inspection; Move Out Inspection)
   * Download / Screenshot photos of subject appliance to assess condition
   * If possible, extract make / model # of inspection report; Update Appliance Log
   * Determine if any recent repairs have been made

### US Brands vs Imported Brands (Maytag/GE vs Samsung/LG)

**Do**: Stick with US brands like Maytag and GE. Their parts are widely available and repair techs are easy to find.

**Don't**: Rely on Samsung or LG for rental properties. Parts take longer to get and require brand-certified techs to order parts.

### Coil Top vs Glass Top Stoves

**Do**: Choose coil tops for rentals. They're durable, cheaper, and easier to repair.

**Don't**: Install glass top stoves. They crack easily.

### Side-by-Side vs Stacked Washer/Dryer

**Do**: Stick with side-by-side setups when you can.

**Don't**: Rely on stacked washer/dryer combos.
