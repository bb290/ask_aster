# Responding to Resident request

## Triage


:::info

1. Confirm this is not an issue with the entry door from the house to the garage
2. Collect the following information:
   * Picture of the door or impacted area
   * Is the issue with the door or with the motor?
   * Was it working before and it no longer works?
3. Get repair history
   * **IF previous vendor worked on it**, dispatch to previous vendor

:::


---

## Life & Safety


:::warning
* Garage doors are one of the home features that can cause serious injury or death for Residents if not operated by a professional.
* Sagareus policy is to hire licensed professional garage door company to look at garage doors, general handyman are NOT allowed to work on garage doors

:::


---

## Garage malfunction (Single Family)


:::info

1. Dispatch Approved Garage Vendor
   * NTE - $400, ok to approve all repairs under this amount
   * Work exceeding this require estimate and send to owner for approval

:::


---

## Garage malfunction (Multi-Family)


:::info

1. Multi-family usually have custom garage instructions, look up the appropriate SOP for the building to determine next step
   * Assign sub task for field team to unblock Resident

:::
