> **Situation:** Owner requests to update how maintenance is dispatched for their property — whether Sagareus should get approval first, notify and dispatch, or dispatch automatically.


---

## Key Points

* **Three dispatch modes:**
  * **Owner Provide Instruction** — Sagareus reaches out for approval before dispatching any vendors (essential repairs may still proceed)
  * **Notify + Dispatch** — Sagareus dispatches a vendor and notifies the owner simultaneously
  * **Dispatch** — Sagareus dispatches automatically without prior notification for fastest resolution
* **Updated in Asana** — Change is made in the [Property Settings](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1209863830500678/list/1209867411652141) project under the "Maintenance Dispatch" field


---


:::info
This procedure ensures the correct and timely update of a property's maintenance dispatch setting within Asana, based on the owner's request.

:::

## Procedure


1. Go to Asana [Property Settings](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1209863830500678/list/1209867411652141) project
2. Locate the owner's property
3. Under the "Maintenance Dispatch" field, select the owner's preferred dispatch setting:

 ![Maintenance dispatch settings](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcoszdZpGlviX9dBPWCaEC5YVWfS-M3gIsAJIHMxlt8-ovOJ-LvP3a4pOo31adZ_cZWlplfCYy0BradSwgj-r0nfb3hHCZPcMY3HTlrQyIc3uowg6cXAqSbZraLyaRR7ihwZsf8w7RZaNcDbp3X694UIg?key=nnhKEmcP1_mvGVmGiinqRQ)

## Response

Use the template matching the owner's chosen setting:

**Owner Provide Instruction:**

> Hi \[Owner\], We've updated your maintenance settings, and we will reach out for approval before dispatching any vendors. Please note that our team may still proceed with certain essential repairs that tenants require.

**Notify + Dispatch:**

> Hi \[Owner\], We've updated your maintenance settings, and we will dispatch a vendor to resolve the issue, and you will be notified simultaneously.

**Dispatch:**

> Hi \[Owner\], We've updated your maintenance settings, and we will automatically dispatch a vendor without prior notification to ensure the quickest resolution for your tenant.

### Example Owner Questions

* "I want to approve maintenance before you send anyone out"
* "Can you just handle maintenance without asking me every time?"
* "I'd like to be notified when you dispatch a vendor"
