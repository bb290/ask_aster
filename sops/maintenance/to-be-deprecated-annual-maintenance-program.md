# Program Overview

## Purpose / Why We Do It

* Protect tenant safety and comfort
* Preserve and extend the lifespan of the owner's asset
* Prevent costly repairs from deferred maintenance
* Improve property performance and reduce turnover risk

## Frequency

* Conducted annually, authorization in Spring
* Execution in Late Fall & Winter before raining season

## Participation


:::warning
All single-family home owners are opted in by default.

:::

* At program launch each year, send a confirmation notice to owners:
  * Notify owner of anticipated scope of work
  * Allow opt-out or additional work requests

## Standard Annual Maintenance Items

* Dryer Vent Cleaning
* Roof & Gutter Cleaning
* Furnace Servicing (if applicable)
* Winterization

# Tracking Progress


:::info
Annual maintenance for all properties are tracked in the Property Maintenance project in Asana under the "Annual Maintenance" field.

| Field | Values |
|-------|--------|
| State | Not needed -> Pending Approval -> Approved -> Dispatched -> Completed <YYYY> |
