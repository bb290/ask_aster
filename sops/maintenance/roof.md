:::warning
Roof maintenance is a top priority for asset preservation and habitability. Roof leaks result in significant interior damage, mold growth, and potential resident displacement due to water damage. Proactive maintenance and immediate response to leaks are mandatory.

:::


---

# Roof Types & Maintenance Expectations

* **Composite Shingle**: Most common residential roof. Asphalt/fiberglass shingles. Lifespan: 20-30 years.
* **Flat Roof (Torch Down / TPO)**: Common on multifamily or modern structures. Lifespan: 15-25 years.


---

# Safety & Vendor Dispatch Policy


:::warning
Roofing work carries high liability and safety risks. Dispatch decisions must factor in **height**, **pitch**, **access**, and **weather**.

:::

## Sagareus Policy

* **High Risk (Licensed Roofing Company Required):**
  * Any roof exceeding **1 story** in height.
  * Any roof with a **steep pitch** (unsafe to walk without gear).
  * Work occurring during **rain, snow, or ice**.
* **Low Risk (Licensed General Contractor Permitted):**
  * Single-story roofs.
  * Flat or low-pitch roofs accessible via safe ladder placement.
