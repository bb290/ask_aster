:::warning
Loss of heat is considered a **habitability emergency**. Upon report of a heating failure, Sagareus is required to take action within **24 hours**, regardless of whether the resident responds to initial troubleshooting communication. A field response team member is assigned as the incident manager to ensure enhanced oversight, thorough documentation, and expedited, ongoing communication with all stakeholders, including the resident, vendors, and the property owner.

:::


---

## Triage & Incident Response

* **Identify heating type & attach picture if available**
* **Send Ack & Triage** email based on the heating type
* **IF** resident is **NOT** able to self resolve **OR** resident does not respond within 4 hours:
  * Dispatch to approved vendor
  * Confirm if the vendor can be there the same day or within 24 hours. Let them know this is an emergency
  * NTE $1000


---

## Field Service

* Call resident with the incident call script
* Determine if **temp heating** are needed, deliver temporary heaters
* Follow up on the ticket **twice a day**
* IF additional estimates exceeding $650 are needed
* Send Email - Approval for additional repair (Heating) to the owners


:::tip
**Field Service Checklist**: Incident call made, vendor scheduled within 24 hours, temporary heater provided if needed, vendor triage result followed up on.

:::
