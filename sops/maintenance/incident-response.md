:::warning
Incident response is a standardized process for any Sagareus staff to handle issues reported by residents or property owners. The same process applies equally to routine maintenance requests and high-stake emergencies.

:::

Our goals are:

* Ensure well-being of residents and preservation of client's asset
* Consistent and repeatable response to avoid re-inventing the wheels each time
* Make good judgement call
* Good stewardship of owner's fund


---

# Incident Severity

* Each incident must be classified after we assess the situation
* An incident can move up or down in severity depending on the situation

| **Level** | **Criteria** | **Response** |
|-------|----------|----------|
| Emergency | Imminent harm to resident or damage to the property. Loss of Heat, Loss of Electricity, Loss of Hot water | 1-2 Hourly follow up until harm/damage mitigated |
| High  | High level of resident inconvenience | Daily follow up until mitigation in place |
| Medium | Average level of resident inconvenience | 2-3 day follow up |

# Communicating with owners

Owners often react negatively to these reports because:

* They are surprised
* They don't understand the urgency
* They don't know WHY they are being contacted
* They question our capability
* They question the estimate
* They question if the cost should be covered by the Resident
