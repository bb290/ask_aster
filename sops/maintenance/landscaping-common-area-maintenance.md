# Moss / Dirty Exterior / Exterior Bio Hazard / Graffiti


:::info
See Approved Tree & Landscaping Vendors for pressure washing, arborist, and tree vendor contact information by region.

:::

## Triage

**Safety**

* Excessive moss on stairs and walkways that can be trip hazard
* Bio hazard waste (puke, human/pet waste)
* Graffiti

**Cosmetic**

* Grime, dirt, other cosmetic issues that can be fixed with pressure washing


:::warning
**Decision Tree**

* IF **Safety**: Dispatch to Approved Pressure Washing Vendor
* IF **Cosmetic**: Check if any cosmetic work was done in the last 12 months. If no other pressure wash done, schedule Approved Pressure Washing Vendor (NTE $350, or get estimate)

:::


---

# Fallen Branch / Tree Related Safety

## Triage

An arborist is needed to assess the safety of a tree when:

* Large branch is reported to have fallen in the vicinity of the home
* Resident reports concern about tree (leaning, falling debris, at risk of falling on the house)


---

# Tree Removal / Trim High Branches


:::warning
**Tree Removal**

* Must be approved by the owner
* Must have confirmation from arborist whether permit is needed

**Trim High Branches**

* IF branches are at risk of damaging the house
  * IF Single Family - get approval from owner
  * IF Multifamily - OK to approve if under $600

:::

## Tree Touching Power Line


:::warning
Contact electricity provider and let them know that overgrowth is touching power line.

:::

# Garbage Overflow (Single Family)


1. Confirm if the extra garbage is from the Resident? Or illegal dumping?
2. Contact garbage utility company and schedule:
   * One off pick up
   * Or Large item pick up


---

# Garbage Overflow (Multi-Family)

### Loose Trash

* Check if there is existing cleaner for monthly common area cleaning
* Dispatch to a Cleaning Vendor if needed

### Significant Trash


:::warning
This type of scope of work exceeds the responsibility of common area cleaning vendors. Dispatch to an Approved Trashout Company.

:::


---

## Moss Slip Hazard (Single Family)


1. Use Google street view to identify area needing pressure washed
2. Send to approved Pressure Wash Vendor for estimate
3. Send to owner for approval


---

## Moss Slip Hazard (Multi-Family)


1. Use Google street view to identify area needing pressure washed
2. Send to Pressure Wash Vendor with NTE of $350
