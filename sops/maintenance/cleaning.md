## Email Templates


:::info
Use the templates below when dispatching cleaning jobs to vendors. Replace all bracketed placeholders with actual property details before sending.

:::

##### Template, Dispatch Deep Cleaning (Vacant)

> Are you available to complete a **deep** cleaning for the property at **\[Property Address\]** by **\[Date\]**?
> 
> **Property Details:**
> **Status:** The home is vacant.
> **Access:** A keybox is located at \[Keybox Location\].
> **Code:** The keybox code is \[Code\].
> 
> **Instructions:**
> Please provide before and after pictures for documentation.
> Send the final invoice to billing@sagareus.com.
> 
> **Scope of Work:**
> 
> **1. Carpet Cleaning:** \[Yes/No\]
> **2. Interior Deep Clean:** General, Kitchen, Bathrooms, Floors
> **3. Exterior:** Wipe down the front door, Clear all exterior cobwebs, Pick up any loose garbage immediately outside the unit.

##### Template, Light Cleaning

> Are you available to complete a **light** cleaning for the property at **\[Property Address\]** by **\[Date\]**?
> The home should be cleaned already, please let us know if deeper cleaning is needed.
> 
> **Scope of Work:**
> Wipe down all countertops and surfaces in kitchen and bathrooms.
> Clean and sanitize all sinks and toilets.
> Sweep and/or vacuum all main living areas and entryways.
> Mop hard surface floors as needed.
> Spot-clean any visible marks, footprints, or tracked-in dirt.
> Remove any trash or debris left.
