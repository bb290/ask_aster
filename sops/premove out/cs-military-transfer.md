> **Situation:** Resident is terminating their lease due to a military transfer or deployment order.


---

## Key Points

* **No early termination fee** — Military transfers are protected under [RCW 59.18.220](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.220)
* **20 days notice required** — If move-out date is less than 20 days from notice, Resident is still liable for rent until the 20-day mark
* **Documentation required** — Resident must provide official military transfer or deployment orders
* **Deposit refund timeline** — Per [RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280), deposit refund package sent within 30 days


---


:::warning
**IF** military transfer is involved, Resident must provide 20 days notice. **IF** move-out date is less than 20 days, they still need to pay rent until the 20 day mark.

:::

> • Confirm your move out date has not changed

> • Provide you with the key box code
>
> To make your move as easy as possible, we ask you to place keys in the lock box upon move out. The following day, Sagareus staff will complete the Move Out Inspection.
>
> **3-4 weeks after move out** In accordance with [RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280), you will receive a complete deposit refund package within 30 days of the official move out date. Our Deposit Refund Package includes:
> • Original Move In Inspection
> • Move Out Inspection
> • Deposit Refund Statement
> • Withholding Details, if any
> • Full Resident Ledger
> • Vendor Invoice(s)
>
> For additional details, please see complete [Move Out Procedures Here](https://www.sagareus.com/move-out-procedures).
>
> We appreciate your tenancy and will work to make the transition to your new home as seamless as possible.
