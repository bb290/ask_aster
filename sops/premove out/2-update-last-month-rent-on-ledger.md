:::info
**Task assignment:**

* Due 4 days prior to the first day of the last month
* Assigned to [Lead Accountant](../../docs/role-profiles/lead-accountant.md)

:::

* Update **lease end date**.
* Update lease type to **Fixed**.
* **IF** lease ends prior to end of month:
  * Delete rent charge.
  * Calculate pro-rated rent from the 1st to the move-out date.
* **IF** last month rent is held — apply the last month rent deposit to the ledger and update the lease end date in Buildium:

  
  1. Navigate to subject Lease Ledger.
  2. Click the shortcut (three dots) on top right.
  3. Select **Withhold deposit**.
  4. Preview if Last Month's rent is being held by selecting the Deposit drop-down.
  5. Last Month's Rent and Prepaid rent can be applied.
  6. **DO NOT** apply security deposit for last month's rent.
  7. Select either Prepaid Rent or Last Month's rent from the drop-down.
  8. Apply the available balance to the outstanding RENT line item.
