:::warning
If the tenant is moving out before their lease end date or did not provide proper notice, a lease break fee must be added to their ledger.

:::


1. Navigate to the copy of the tenant's lease.
2. The lease break fee is notated under the "Early Termination / Lease Break" subtitle.
   * Typically 2x Month Rent.
3. Once the amount is determined, navigate back to the tenant's ledger in Buildium.
4. Select **Add charge**:
   * Line item: `Lease Break Fee`
   * Amount: as determined in the lease
   * Click **Save**.
