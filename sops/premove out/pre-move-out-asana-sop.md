**Asana Project:** [Leasing 2.0 // Pre Move-Out](https://app.asana.com/)


:::info
Follow these steps in order from top to bottom -- they map to the subtasks in the Asana Pre Move-Out template. Steps 1-6 are the **Outgoing Resident** group (skip for `Leasing Type = New Property`); steps 7-11 are the **Leasing Team Prep** group (always applies).

:::

### Outgoing Resident


1. [Email | Owner - Notice to Vacate](1-email-owner-notice-to-vacate.md) -- Notify owner with the correct notice variant (Proper Notice, Early Termination, DV, Military)
2. [Update | Last Month Rent on Ledger](2-update-last-month-rent-on-ledger.md) -- Apply last month rent / prepaid rent to the ledger and update lease end date
3. [Update | Lease Break Fee on Ledger (if early termination)](3-update-lease-break-fee-on-ledger-if-early-termination.md) -- Add lease break fee charge when tenant terminates early
4. [Take Over | Utilities on move-out date](4-take-over-utilities-on-move-out-date.md) -- Take over Electricity/Gas/Oil on move-out day
5. [Review | Utility Pass Through & Last Month Estimate](5-review-utility-pass-through-last-month-estimate.md) -- Run Audit Utility Pass Through (Resident INITIATED)
6. [Check | Assets to retrieve](6-check-assets-to-retrieve.md) -- Confirm deployed field assets and reassign retrieval to Leasing Agent

### Leasing Team Prep


 7. [Calculate | New utility for listing](7-calculate-new-utility-for-listing.md) -- Determine Pass Through rules, calculate Flat Fee and Estimated Utility Cost
 8. [Update | Utility Settings](8-update-utility-settings.md) -- Apply shared Update | Utility Settings procedure and populate Buildium listing copy
 9. [Add | Property to 1Password](9-add-property-to-1password.md) -- Duplicate the 00 key box template and enter property access info
10. [Update | Parking & Storage Log](10-update-parking-storage-log.md) -- Remove outgoing resident from the Master Parking & Storage log
11. [Prepare | Google File Link & Move Out/Listing Video Links](11-prepare-google-file-link-move-out-listing-video-links.md) -- Create Rental Listing Marketing Google folder and share link
