> **Situation:** Resident provides proper notice to vacate at the end of their lease term or with the required notice period.


---

## Key Points

* **No penalties** — Standard move-out with proper notice; no early termination fees apply
* **Forwarding address required** — Resident must submit forwarding address via Google Form for timely deposit return
* **Key box procedure** — Sagareus installs a secure key box on-site 1–2 weeks before move-out; Resident places keys in the box upon vacating
* **Deposit refund timeline** — Per [RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280), a complete deposit refund package is sent within 30 days of the official move-out date


---

## Response

> **Notice to Vacate Received | \[Property Address\]**
>
> \[Tenant Name\]
>
> Thank you for providing proper Notice to Vacate. Move-out is scheduled for \[Move-Out Date\] by 9:00 PM.
>
> To ensure timely deposit return, [please submit your forwarding address here.](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdDZBjXMh0KQLWy38NBIJsfrVv3F_bGxdvPj5A2uybbZ9-TEw/viewform?usp=header)
>
> **1-2 weeks prior to move out** Sagareus staff will contact you to:
> • Confirm your move out date has not changed
> • Install a secure key box on-site
> • Provide you with the key box code
>
> To make your move as easy as possible, we ask you to place keys in the lock box upon move out. The following day, Sagareus staff will complete the Move Out Inspection.
>
> **3-4 weeks after move out** In accordance with [RCW 59.18.280](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.280), you will receive a complete deposit refund package within 30 days of the official move out date. Our Deposit Refund Package includes:
> • Original Move In Inspection
> • Move Out Inspection
> • Deposit Refund Statement
> • Withholding Details, if any
> • Full Resident Ledger
> • Vendor Invoice(s)
>
> For additional details, please see complete [Move Out Procedures Here](https://www.sagareus.com/move-out-procedures).
>
> We appreciate your tenancy and will work to make the transition to your new home as seamless as possible.
>
> Best,
