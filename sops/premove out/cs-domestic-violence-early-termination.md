> **Situation:** Resident wants to terminate their lease early due to domestic violence.


---

## Key Points

* **Always allowed** — Under [RCW 59.18.575](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.575), tenants may terminate without penalty in DV situations
* **No early termination fee** — Victim is entitled to full refund of all security deposits
* **Multi-Resident lease** — If multiple Residents, the victim may leave the lease; remaining Resident(s) stay on the lease
* **Burden of proof on Resident** — They must provide one of: police report, restraining/court order, signed and notarized self-affidavit, or [qualified third-party form](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1PDNHUlvrutXUT--5GklTE2XF5Qc-W0-k/view?usp=drive_link)
* **Handle with care** — Safety and well-being are the top priority; make the process as supportive as possible


---


:::warning
When Residents communicate they want to terminate their lease early due to domestic violence, this is always allowed - see [RCW 59.18.575](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.575).

* If the tenancy has multiple Residents, the victim is allowed to leave the lease. The remaining Resident shall remain on the lease.
* The victim is entitled to full refund of all security deposits.

:::

> **Notice to Vacate - DV | \[Property Address\]**
>
> \[Tenant Name\]
>
> I'm truly sorry to hear about your situation. Your safety and well-being are our top priority, and our team will make this process as supportive and straightforward as possible.
>
> Under Washington State law ([RCW 59.18.575](https://app.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=59.18.575)), tenants may terminate their lease without penalty in certain circumstances related to safety or domestic violence. To qualify for this exemption, provide **any 1 of the following** documents:
> • Qualified Third Party Statement form (attached)
> • A police report
> • A restraining or court order
> • A signed and notarized self-affidavit
>
> Once documentation is received, we'll finalize the lease termination and will expedite deposit return to aid in your transition.
>
> When available, please confirm Move Out Date & [submit your forwarding address here.](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdDZBjXMh0KQLWy38NBIJsfrVv3F_bGxdvPj5A2uybbZ9-TEw/viewform?usp=header)
>
> Once move out date is confirmed, Sagareus staff will contact you to:
> • Install a secure key box on-site
> • Provide you with the key box code
>
> To make your move as easy as possible, we ask you to place keys in the lock box upon move out.
>
> If there's anything we can do to support your move-out, please let us know. Our team is here to help you navigate this transition safely and respectfully.
>
> Best,
