> **Situation:** Prospective property owner inquires about Sagareus property management services and wants a proposal.


---

## Key Points

* **Three pieces of info needed** — First name, email address, and property address
* **Submit via website** — Once info is received, complete the form at sagareus.com/pm-proposal
* **Referral code** — Write your name and how the inquiry contacted Sagareus (Called Main Line, Emailed mgmt@sagareus.com)
* **Text/phone callers** — Direct to website for fastest response


---

## Email Response

> Thank you so much for reaching out about property management services—I'd be happy to get you connected with the right person on our team.
>
> To prepare a full proposal, please provide:
> • Your first name
> • Email address
> • The address of the property you're looking to have managed
>
> Once I have that info, our Team Lead will follow up directly with a full proposal. It will include:
> • A rental estimate based on market comps
> • Our scope of service
> • Transparent pricing details
>
> Thanks again!

## Text / Phone Response

> Thank you so much for reaching out about property management services—the fastest way to get your Property Management Proposal is to request via our website: https://www.sagareus.com/pm-proposal
>
> This will connect you directly with our Business Development Manager.

## Action


1. Once the 3 required pieces of information are received:


   1. Complete the Form at https://www.sagareus.com/pm-proposal
   2. In Referral Code — Write your name & how the inquiry contacted Sagareus:
      * Called Main Line
      * Emailed mgmt@sagareus.com

### Example Owner Questions

* "I'm looking for a property manager"
* "How much do you charge for property management?"
* "Can you manage my rental property?"
