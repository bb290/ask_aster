### Procedure


1. Read the move-out inspection report
2. Fill out the "To Be Completed" section per the Property Settings in Asana
3. Confirm if the following are needed in the [Property Maintenance project](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1211134623744906/list/1211134624931958) **AND** **NOT already tracked by Annual Inspection**:
   * **HVAC Servicing**
   * **Roof Cleaning**
   * **Dryer Vent Cleaning**
4. IF last service date > 1 year, **get estimate and send to owner for approval**
