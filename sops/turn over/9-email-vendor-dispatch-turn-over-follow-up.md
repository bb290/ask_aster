Once the owner has approved the Turn Over estimate:


1. **IF approved work exceeds $2,000** -- see [Turn Over -- Step 2: Turn Over Estimate](turn-over-step-2-turn-over-estimate.md) for the deposit collection and in-person verification requirements.
2. Approve and dispatch vendor with approved scope of work
3. **Follow up** with vendor every day until a firm ETA is confirmed, then every two days until completion
4. If there is significant delay:
   * Escalate to [Turn Over Coordinator Lead](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md)
   * If unresolved, escalate to [Director of Operations](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md)
