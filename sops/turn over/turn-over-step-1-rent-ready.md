## Rent Ready (Immediate Dispatch)

Rent Ready is dispatched immediately after move-out, without waiting for owner approval.

### Scope

* Standard checklist, pre-negotiated at **$280 + materials**
* **No owner approval required** -- the owner is notified via the Move Out Notification email and we proceed
* While completing Rent Ready, the vendor prepares an **estimate for remaining turn over work** (painting, larger repairs, etc.)

### What Rent Ready Covers

The [Rent Ready Checklist](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1TJSoXMIFhblz8hujJDZ9lM1mLtVTGxf8/view?usp=sharing) includes items Sagareus uniquely needs for every property:

* **Rekeying** all exterior doors and mailbox (as specified)
* **Smoke alarm verification** in each bedroom and common area per floor, including battery replacement and installation at height
* **CO detector verification** on each floor
* **Appliance condition check** and cleaning (dishwasher, washer, garbage disposal)
* **Garage door remote programming** (if applicable)
* General handyman items from the move-out inspection
