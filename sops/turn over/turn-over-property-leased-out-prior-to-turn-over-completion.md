:::warning
**THIS HAS HIGH LIABILITY for Sagareus, manage turn over and cleaning closely to ensure it is done ASAP.**

:::

* When a property is leased out, the [Turn Over Coordinator Lead](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md) will be included in the "Home Leased Out" email notification with the move-in date
* The move-in date needs to be communicated to vendors:
  * Turn over vendor:
    * Get confirmation if the vendor can complete the work prior to the move-in date. Reserve time for cleaning
  * Cleaner:
    * Immediately assign cleaning task to schedule cleaning prior to move-in
* IF vendor cannot complete the turn over prior to move-in:
  * Update the Move-in task due date to today, so leasing agent is immediately tagged to look at the update
  * Notify the leasing agent the turnover cannot be complete on time. Ask the leasing agent to let incoming Residents know that the turn over vendor will schedule with them directly to complete the work.
    * IF turn over scope is high, or cleaning cannot be completed on time, we will offer pro-rated rent credit until cleaning is complete
  * Notify vendor Residents are moving in, provide Resident contacts to the vendor so they can schedule with the Residents directly
