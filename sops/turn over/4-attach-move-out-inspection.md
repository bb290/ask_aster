### How the Inspection Arrives

[Leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:Leasing@sagareus.com) ([Leasing Team](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md)) receives the Move Out Inspection via email sent directly from the HappyCo Inspector App, initiated by the Leasing Agent in the field immediately following inspection completion. ### Processing Steps

When the email is received, [Leasing Team](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md):


1. Download the Move Out Inspection by clicking the link
2. Upload to the Turn Over Parent Task
3. Mark task complete
