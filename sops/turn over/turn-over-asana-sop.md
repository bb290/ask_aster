**Asana Project:** [Leasing 3.0 // Turn Over](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1213171760535170)


:::info
Follow these steps in order from top to bottom -- they map to the subtasks in the Asana Turn Over template.

:::


 1. [Review | Manager Turnover Assessment](1-review-manager-turnover-assessment.md) -- Review parent turn over task for delays or escalation needs
 2. [Update | Property Settings](2-update-property-settings.md) -- Refresh property, unit, and multifamily maintenance & compliance fields
 3. [Review | Annual Maintenance](3-review-annual-maintenance.md) -- Check HVAC, Roof Cleaning, Dryer Vent needs
 4. [Attach | Move Out Inspection](4-attach-move-out-inspection.md) -- Upload move-out inspection report to the parent task
 5. [Email | Owner - Move Out Notification](5-email-owner-move-out-notification.md) -- Notify owner of move-out and keybox access
 6. [Email | Owner - Turn Over Communication](6-email-owner-turn-over-communication.md) -- Send Turn Over Plan to owner
 7. [Email | Vendor - Dispatch Rent Ready](7-email-vendor-dispatch-rent-ready.md) -- Dispatch vendor with Rent Ready Checklist
 8. [Email | Owner - Estimate Approval](8-email-owner-estimate-approval.md) -- Send estimate for owner review
 9. [Email | Vendor - Dispatch Turn Over & Follow Up](9-email-vendor-dispatch-turn-over-follow-up.md) -- Approve and dispatch Turn Over work, follow up
10. [Update | turn_over_completion, Turn Over Vendor, Turn Over Cost metrics](10-update-turnovercompletion-turn-over-vendor-turn-over-cost-metrics.md) -- Set Turn Over Vendor, Cost, and Completion fields
11. [Confirm | Turn Over Complete](11-confirm-turn-over-complete.md) -- Verify Rent Ready Standard is met
12. [Schedule | Move Out Cleaning](12-schedule-move-out-cleaning.md) -- Dispatch cleaner
