### Standard Verification

Once Turn Over is reported complete by the vendor or owner:


1. [Leasing Lead](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md) assigns the appropriate Leasing Agent to complete the Turn Over Confirmation
   * **NOTE:** If the Turn Over Coordinator is confident in who should be assigned, skip the Leasing Lead and assign directly to the Leasing Agent to save time
2. Leasing Agent visits the property as soon as possible
3. Leasing Agent completes the Turn Over Confirmation Inspection in the Inspector App
4. Leasing Agent confirms the [Sagareus Rent Ready Standard](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1TJSoXMIFhblz8hujJDZ9lM1mLtVTGxf8/view?usp=sharing) checklist is complete

### $2,000+ In-Person Verification


:::warning
**When approved work exceeds $2,000**, the [Turn Over Coordinator](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md) must also inspect the property in person. This is **in addition to** the standard Leasing Agent verification. The Turn Over Coordinator Lead verifies work line-by-line against the approved scope.

:::


:::warning
**Vendor Accountability**

Vendors are required to complete the checklist fully. If work is marked complete but issues are later found, this is a problem. Occasional mistakes may occur, but repeated patterns = vendor will no longer be dispatched.

:::


:::info
**Special Note**

Final cleaning may still be pending. All repairs must be complete.

:::


---

### Actions After Inspection

* **No issues:** Mark task complete.
* **Issues found:** The person conducting the inspection updates the task with the punch list, then assigns to the Turn Over Coordinator to resolve.


---

### Punch List Resolution

The Turn Over Coordinator dispatches vendors to resolve punch list items:


1. **Warranty issue** -- Communicate with the original vendor and ask them to complete the work under warranty
2. **New issue** -- Assign a vendor to complete the repair. Preference is to use the original turn over vendor
3. **Appliance issue:**


:::warning
**TODO:** [Operations Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md) to document the appliance resolution process.

:::


:::info
**Expensive Punch List Items**

If a punch list item is potentially expensive, owner approval is needed before proceeding. Be mindful -- this can feel like a negative interaction since the owner may perceive it as something that should have been caught earlier. Be thoughtful and transparent in your communication. Explain what was found, why it was not in the original scope, and provide a clear cost estimate.

:::


---

### Repeat Until Complete

The task is reassigned to the Leasing Agent repeatedly until the Turn Over is fully complete and verified.
