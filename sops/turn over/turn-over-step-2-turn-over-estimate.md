## Step 2 -- Turn Over Estimate (Owner Approval)

After Rent Ready is complete, the vendor submits an estimate for remaining turn over work (painting, flooring, appliances, etc.). This estimate requires owner approval based on the property's communication settings.

### Approval Thresholds

Approval requirements depend on the **Maintenance Communication Settings** field on the Asana task:

| Setting | Approval Threshold |
|---------|--------------------|
| **Approval Needed** | Owner approval required for **all work** |
| **Notify & Dispatch** | Owner approval required for work **above $800** |
| **No Owner Communication** | Owner approval required for work **above $3,000** |

### Estimate Submission

See [SOP 6 -- EMAIL | Owner - Estimate Approval](8-email-owner-estimate-approval.md) for the estimate email template.

### When Approved Work Exceeds $2,000

Two additional requirements apply:


1. **Collect funds first:** Ask the vendor to send an invoice for deposit payment. Forward the invoice to [billing@sagareus.com](mailto:billing@sagareus.com) -- this triggers the bill approval procedure, which auto-collects from the owner.
2. **In-person verification required:** When the job is complete, the [Turn Over Coordinator Lead](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md) (or closest ops team member) must inspect the property in person and verify work line-by-line.
