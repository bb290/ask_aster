## Average Turn Over Time

**How it's calculated:** Turn Over Completion date minus Move-Out date.

**Current SLA:** 21 days

The Turn Over Completion date is set in [Step 8 — Update Metrics](10-update-turnovercompletion-turn-over-vendor-turn-over-cost-metrics.md) when the vendor first reports work is complete (before punch list resolution).


---

## Metric Exclusion

Turn overs that are excessively long (e.g., large remodels, owner-caused delays) can be excluded from the average calculation.

To exclude a turn over, set the **Metric Exclusion** field on the task in the [Leasing 3.0 // Turn Over](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1213171760535170) Asana project.
