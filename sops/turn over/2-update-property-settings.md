Run this step immediately after the Manager Turnover Assessment (step 1). Keeping property settings current ensures accurate dispatches, compliance deadlines, and maintenance scheduling for the incoming Turn Over cycle.


---

### How To Update

See [Update | Property Settings](../property onboarding/update-property-settings.md) for the full procedure. Update the fields at every relevant level:

* Property level
* Unit level
* Multifamily level (when applicable)
