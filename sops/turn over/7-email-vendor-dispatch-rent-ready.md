* **Assignment:** Turn Over Coordinator

### Overview

This step dispatches the vendor for [Step 1: Rent Ready](turn-over-step-1-rent-ready.md). Rent Ready is the standard work required for all properties. For full details on what Rent Ready covers and the approval thresholds for [Step 2: Turn Over Estimate](turn-over-step-2-turn-over-estimate.md), see the linked sub-docs.

### Turn Over Coordinator Actions

* Fill out the rekey and garage door remote specification for the vendor
* If keys are not made, create a subtask for a locksmith to fill the gap
* Dispatch the **Rent Ready Checklist** using the **email template** AND **text the Vendor** with template below
* Follow up with vendor every day until a firm ETA is confirmed, then every two days until completion
* If there are discrete work items needing a plumber, glass repair, etc.:
  * Create a subtask to track each one separately

:::info
#### Quick Turn

A Quick Turn is when there is a very short duration between move-out and the next move-in. In this situation, skip the two-step process and ask the vendor to complete all repairs in one shot.

**Key considerations:**


1. **Scope must be well-understood** -- confirm the turn over scope is clear and cost will not escalate unexpectedly
2. **Coordinate closely with the** [**Leasing Lead**](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md) -- ensure cleaning is scheduled immediately after turn over completion
3. **Expedite all communication** -- call instead of text, text instead of email. Get confirmation on timing from every party
4. **Follow up daily** -- check in with the vendor every day until work is complete

Proactively communicate with incoming tenants and the leasing agent about any repairs that may still be pending, so there are no surprises at move-in.

:::
