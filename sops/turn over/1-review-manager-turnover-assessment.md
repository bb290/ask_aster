* **Assignment:** [Turn Over Coordinator Lead](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-staff-directory.md)
* **Initial due date:** 1 day after the move-out

### Purpose

Review the parent turn over task and look for:

* **Signs of vendor delay** -- Is the vendor behind schedule or unresponsive?
* **Signs of issues needing escalation** -- Are there complications that require management attention?
* **Delayed notification to owner or vendors** -- Has outreach been timely, or are communications stalled?

Make note of eviction status, keys, etc. in the Asana task comments.
