Once the vendor reports the turn over work is complete, update the following fields on the Asana task:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Turn Over Vendor** | Which vendor was dispatched for the turn over |
| **Turn Over Cost** | Total cost of the turn over work |
| **Turn Over Completion** | Date the vendor **first reports** turn over is complete |


:::info
**Clarification on Turn Over Completion date**

When the vendor reports work is complete, we send the leasing team to verify the property. The verification often reveals punch list items that need to be resolved. The **Turn Over Completion** date we track is the date the vendor first reports completion -- **before** any punch list resolution begins.

This gives us an accurate measure of vendor turnaround time, separate from the time spent resolving punch list items afterward.

:::
