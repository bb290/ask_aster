Paper checks are a last resort for vendors without electronic payment options.

## Procedure


1. Confirm vendor address is correct as stated on the invoice
   * If vendor is in the directory, address should already be confirmed from previous payments
2. Transfer funds in BOA
   * Transfer from Owner Operating Account to "00 SAGAREUS BXN" Account
3. Transfer funds in Chase
   * Transfer from Owner Operating Account to BXN Account (BOA)
4. Record the fund transfer in Buildium
   * Accounting > Banking > 101.SAGAREUS | BXN
   * Select "Record other transaction"
   * Select "Transfer"
   * Enter property address
   * Transfer from: owner's operating account
   * Transfer to: 101.SAGAREUS | BXN
   * Transfer Amount: Amount due on bill
   * Memo: INVOICE # or property address
5. Go back to vendor invoice under Bills
   * Click on Make a Payment
   * Change Bank Account from Owner's Operating Account to 101.SAGAREUS | BXN
   * Check "Queue check for printing" box
   * Click confirm payment
6. Go back to Accounting > Banking > 101.SAGAREUS | BXN
   * Select "Print Checks" button
   * Select the corresponding check for the invoice you are paying
   * Click "Generate and print checks"
