:::info
Tax filing Overview:

* First filing must be completed by January 31st (push to get majority of documents sent out by this date)
* Unable to access current year tax service until January 1st
* Property Owner 1099s
  * Electronically sent to the email on file
  * Paper copy is sent to the office and stored
* Vendor 1099s
  * Electronically sent to the email on file
  * Paper copy is sent to the address on file

:::

## Step 2: Obtain Missing Information

* Reach out to rental owners
  * First communication should be done through owner reports
  * Followed up every 3-5 days until received
  * If rental owner is missing email address; verify if we have it on file in a different location before requesting (look for duplicate owner accounts for different LLC's)
* Reach out to vendors missing TIN's
  * Place stop payment on vendor's account until W9 is received


:::warning
Adjust vendor name with **ONBOARDING** \[Vendor\]; this should inform the billing team to not pay any bills until Onboarding note is removed.

:::

* Follow up every 2-3 days
* Refer to Accounting Set Up for email templates

## Step 3: Process tax e-file batch


:::warning
Must be complete by January 31st.

:::

:::
