> **Situation:** A vendor uses billing@sagareus.com for non-billing communication (estimates, scope of work, access, scheduling, etc.).


---

## Key Points

* **Redirect to mgmt@** — Non-billing vendor communication should go through mgmt@sagareus.com.
* **Reply all** — Include mgmt@sagareus.com when responding so the management team has visibility.


---

## Procedure


1. Reply all and add mgmt@sagareus.com
2. Redirect the vendor to use mgmt@sagareus.com for non-billing topics:
   * Estimates
   * Scope of work
   * Access requests
   * Other non-A/R related questions
