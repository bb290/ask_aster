For vendors who do not accept EFT or check.

## Procedure


1. Log into [paypal.com](http://paypal.com)
2. Send
3. Select vendor
4. Enter bill amount
5. Memo is property address
6. Send money from BXN account (2066 account)
7. Record the fund transfer in Buildium
   * Accounting > Banking > 101.SAGAREUS | BXN > Record other transaction
   * Select transfer
   * Enter property address
   * Transfer from: owner's operating account
   * Transfer to: 101.SAGAREUS | BXN
   * Transfer Amount: Amount due on bill
   * Memo: INVOICE # or vendor name
8. Transfer funds in bank account (BofA or Chase) to match Buildium
9. Go back to vendor invoice under Bills
   * Click on Make a Payment
   * Change Bank Account to 101.SAGAREUS | BXN
   * Under Check Number enter payment type or transaction id
