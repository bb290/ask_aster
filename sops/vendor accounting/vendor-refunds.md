## VENDOR Refund Policy


:::warning
All refund procedures must follow the specific steps outlined below based on the payment method and refund type. Do not skip steps or reorder operations.

:::


---

## Void Check Payment - Reissue Check


1. Confirm check has not been deposited in BofA BXN account by searching for the check number
2. Locate check number and send to Cooper to void in BofA
3. Locate bill for the check and "Void"
4. Go back to original bill and resend through BXN account to issue new check


---

## Void Check Payment - Issue Different Payment Method


1. Confirm check has not been deposited in BofA BXN account by searching for the check number
2. Locate check number and send to Cooper to void in BofA
3. Locate bill for the check and "Void"
4. Record Other Transaction reversing transfer from BXN back to Owner's Operating Account
5. Go back to original bill and process as normal


---

## Issuing Vendor Credit To A Different Invoice


1. Go to the vendor's financials page
2. Select "record refund"


   1. Deposit Bank Account: \[Owner's operating account that is being reimbursed\]
   2. Payment Method: Direct Deposit
   3. Memo: Reference invoice that is being reimbursed/ credited
   4. Account: Match account from original invoice
3. Save refund
4. Accounting > Banking > Select the owner's operating account that is being reimbursed
5. Select "record deposit"
6. Select the refund transaction and click "Save"
7. Go back to adjusted invoice
8. Pay Bill > Make a "Check" payment matching the refund amount
9. Transfer funds between the two operating accounts


---

## Refund Owner From Vendor \[Credit Card\]


:::info
Credit card refunds may be processed through Chase or Bank of America depending on the account. The steps below apply to Bank of America credit card refunds.

:::

**CHASE**

**BANK OF AMERICA**


1. Confirm refund was received to the credit card
2. Process Vendor Refund


   1. Go to vendor's profile page
   2. Click on "Financials"
   3. Click on "Record Refund"


      1. Deposit Bank Account: Owner Operating Account
      2. Payment Method: Direct Deposit
      3. Memo: Vendor Refund
      4. Enter Property Address the invoice was associated with
      5. Match Account with what was stated on Buildium bill
      6. Description: Vendor Refund
      7. Enter the amount of the refund
      8. Click "Save Refund"
3. Process Withdrawal From Sagareus


   1. Accounting > Banking > Sagareus > Record Other Transaction


      1. Withdrawal
      2. Property: Sagareus Real Estate
      3. Offsetting Account: Bank Fee
      4. Withdrawal Amount: Match the refund amount
      5. Memo: Vendor Refund | Property Address
      6. Click "Save"
4. Process Deposit in Buildium


   1. Account > Banking > Owner Operating Account > Record Deposit
   2. Select the deposit payment from the vendor
   3. Click "Save"
5. Transfer funds in Bank of America between Sagareus account and Owner Operating


---

## Refund Via Zelle From Vendor


1. Click on the Bill that is being refunded
2. Find Vendor's name and click on it to access their vendor profile


   1. Financials > Record Refund


      1. Account | Owner's operating account
      2. Enter identical information from the refunded bill
3. Go to Bank of America - all zelle transactions are processed through the BXN account


   1. Verify funds were received
4. Transfer funds from BXN account to the owner's operating account
5. Go to the owner's account in Buildium


   1. Click on record deposit
   2. Select the refund line item and save


---

## Request Refund for Overpayment to Vendor


:::tip
Use the email template below to request a refund from a vendor for an overpayment. Zelle refunds should be directed to admin@sagareus.com.

:::

### Email Template — Request Refund for Overpayment

> Hi \[Vendor Name\],
>
> There is an overpayment for Invoice #\[Invoice Number\] that has been sent out in the amount of $\[Amount\].
>
> Can you please confirm that is correct on your end? If so, we can accept a refund via Zelle to admin@sagareus.com.
>
> Let me know if you need anything else from us.
>
> Thank you,

## Correct Address on a Paid Invoices


1. Adjust original bill to reflect the correct property address
2. Create an "Other Transaction" to reflect transfer of funds between operating accounts


   1. Property: Original property address associated with the charge
   2. From account: The correct owner account
   3. To account: The incorrect owner account
   4. Memo: Misallocated funds // \[description or invoice\]
3. Initiate actual transfer of funds between operating accounts
