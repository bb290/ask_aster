> **Situation:** A vendor asks about the status of a submitted invoice.


---

## Key Points

* **Check Buildium first** — Look up the invoice status before responding.
* **Missing invoice** — If not found, request the vendor to resend to billing@sagareus.com.
* **Recent bills (< 2 weeks overdue)** — Respond with standard Net 30 reminder.
* **Overdue bills (> 2 weeks overdue)** — Escalate by creating a task in [Finance & Office Asana Project](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1206627942064331/list/1206627972779236), assign to [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) with immediate due date.
* **Paid bills** — Confirm payment details including method and date.


---

## Response — Recent Bill (< 2 Weeks Overdue)

> Thank you for your inquiry. Your invoice has been received and is recorded in our system for payment processing.
>
> As per Sagareus policy, our standard payment terms are Net 30, and payment will be issued within 30 days of invoice entry.
>
> Please let us know if you have any other questions.
>
> Thank you,

## Response — Overdue Bill (> 2 Weeks)

> Thank you for your inquiry. Your invoice has been received and is recorded in our system for payment processing.
>
> I have escalated the bill to the accounting for expedited payment.

## Response — Paid Bill

> This is to confirm that payment for your invoice has been issued. Please find the details below:
>
> **Amount:** \[Amount\]
> **Payment Issued Date:** \[Date\]
> **Payment Method:** \[ACH / Check\]
>
