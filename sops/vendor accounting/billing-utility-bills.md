> **Situation:** A utility bill is sent digitally to billing@sagareus.com.


---

## Key Points

* **No utility bills should be sent digitally** — Utility bills should arrive by mail, not email. Digital delivery means paperless billing was turned on and needs to be reversed.
* **Still process the bill** — Even though the delivery method is wrong, the bill is valid and must be entered.


---

## Procedure


1. Add the bill to Buildium
2. Reply to billing@sagareus.com to indicate the issue is processed
3. Create a task in the [Finance & Office Asana Project](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1206627942064331/list/1206627972779236) and assign to [Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md) with immediate due date to remove paperless billing
