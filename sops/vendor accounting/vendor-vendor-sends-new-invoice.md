> **Situation:** A vendor submits a new invoice for completed work.


---

## Key Points

* **All invoices to billing@sagareus.com** — Vendors must send invoices to billing@sagareus.com. Do not accept invoices via other channels.
* **Proof of work required** — Invoice must include photos or description of completed work. Invoices without proof of work are not eligible for payment.
* **Net 30 payment terms** — Standard payment terms are Net 30 from invoice entry date.
* **ACH recommended** — ACH payments arrive within 3 business days. Paper checks take up to 12 days. Encourage vendors to enroll in ACH.


---

## Response

> Your invoice has been approved and entered for payment processing.
>
> While we aim to pay as soon as possible, our standard terms are Net 30.
>
> **Payment info:**
> • ACH: arrives within 3 business days from payment issuance
> • Check: up to 12 days from payment issuance
