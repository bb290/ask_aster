EFT is Sagareus's preferred payment method. Buildium can batch process EFT payments to vendors.

## Procedure


1. Select a bill
2. Select pay by EFT
3. On the new screen, adjust to all properties and all vendors
4. This will reflect all payments available by EFT for all bills
5. Work through the bill selection process by cross-referencing each bill with funds available in each designated bank account
6. If there are not enough funds available, unselect the bill for payment
