For vendors set up with direct deposit in Buildium.

## Procedure


1. Click on "Make a Payment"
2. Refer to Bank Account to confirm which owner operating account the payment is coming from
3. Reference the owner's bank account in BofA or Chase to confirm they have enough funds to cover the invoice payment
4. Payment Method: Select EFT
5. Check the "I authorize this transaction" button
6. Click "Confirm Payment"

**Notes:**

* Payments can take a few days to clear
* The payment amount can be adjusted by changing the "Total Payment Amount" before issuing EFT
