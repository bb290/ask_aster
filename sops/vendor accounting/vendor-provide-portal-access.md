> **Situation:** A vendor needs access to the Buildium vendor portal to view their bills and payments.


---

## Key Points

* **Self-service transparency** — The portal gives vendors real-time visibility into submitted bills and payment status, reducing back-and-forth inquiries.
* **Portal is not perfect** — If a vendor can't find an invoice or payment, they should still contact billing@sagareus.com.

## Response

> **Subject: Sagareus Vendor Portal Access -- Quick Guide to View Your Bills & Payments**
>
> Dear \[Vendor Name\],
>
> Thank you for partnering with Sagareus! We're excited to provide you with access to our accounting platform, Buildium, through a dedicated **vendor portal**. This gives you real-time visibility into the bills we've received from you and the payments we've made -- helping increase transparency and reduce back-and-forth questions.
>
> Here's a simple step-by-step guide to using the portal effectively:
>
> **Log In**
> Use the invitation link or credentials we sent you to access the portal. (If you haven't received the login details yet, please let us know.)
>
> **What You'll See First**
> After logging in, you'll land on the **Vendor Ledger**. This screen shows all transaction types related to your account.
>
> **View Bills We Have Received**
> At the top of the ledger, change the filter from "All Transaction Types" to **Bill**. This will display only the invoices we have received from you.
>
> **Quickly Check a Specific Invoice**
> Once on the Bill list, press **Ctrl + F** (or **Cmd + F** on Mac) to open the search bar. Type your invoice number (e.g., 137). If the invoice appears in the results, we have received it.
>
> **Check Payment Status**
> Change the filter from "Bill" to **Payments** (or **EFT Payments** if you are paid via electronic transfer). Use **Ctrl + F** again and search for the same invoice number. If it appears, you'll see the payment date (e.g., "Paid on December 1, 2025").
>
> Example: If your invoice #137 shows up under Bills -- we received it. If it also appears under Payments -- it has been paid, and you'll see the exact date.
>
> This portal is a helpful self-service tool, but it's not perfect. If you don't see an invoice you sent, don't see a payment you expected, or have any other questions, please don't hesitate to reach out to us directly -- we're always happy to assist.
>
> You can reply to this email or contact [billing@sagareus.com](mailto:billing@sagareus.com) as you normally would.
>
> Thank you again for your partnership. We appreciate your work and look forward to continuing our collaboration.
>
> Thank you,
