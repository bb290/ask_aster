> **Situation:** An owner requests that Sagareus terminate the lease — typically because the owner plans to sell, move in, or has another reason to end the tenancy.


---

## Key Points

* **Owner-requested terminations are handled by LtServices** (legal process server), not through the standard renewal termination procedure. This increases the likelihood of successful service.
* [**Operations Manager**](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md) **must review and approve** the termination before proceeding. Create a subtask in the Renewal task to track.
* **Required documents for LtServices:** Lease, most recent renewal, roommate addendum (if any), and resident ledger — all uploaded to a shared Google Drive folder.
* **This is different from the auto-termination at 40 days.** The standard "Terminate if not renewed" procedure in [6 — Resident Follow Up & Lease Termination](6-resident-follow-up-lease-termination.md) is triggered by resident non-response. Owner-requested termination follows the LtServices intake process below.


---

## Procedure


1. Create subtask in the Renewal task to track the termination
   * Assign to [Operations Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md) to review and approve
2. **IF Approved**, prepare the LtServices intake:


:::info
**Prepare a shared Google Drive:**


1. Update folder share option so it is accessible by all
2. Fill out the [LtServices Intake Form](https://drive.google.com/file/d/13ZoJnG0ApHW4xHWIWW4JYCJqAClsZOM5/view?usp=drive_link)
   * Fill out all other questions
   * For the question, "Please prepare and serve the following documents:", select **Terminate**/Non-Renewal
3. Include the following files, rename to reflect the file:
   * [LtServices Intake Form](https://drive.google.com/file/d/13ZoJnG0ApHW4xHWIWW4JYCJqAClsZOM5/view?usp=drive_link)
   * Lease - [Year] to [Year]
   * Renewal - [Year] to [Year]
   * Roommate addendum if any
   * Resident Ledger - [Date]

:::


3. Send intake using the email template below
