:::info
Key Points:

* **IF renewal was previously approved by owner** — Confirm with owner
* **ELSE** — Auto approve based on policy below
* Sagareus only offers **fixed-term leases**
* Leases only end on the 15th or last day of the month
* Leases **cannot end between Sept 16 and March 31** (winter vacancy policy)
  * Sept 15th is the latest end date we'll accept; March 31st is the next available date after Sept 15th
  * Examples:
    * Can my lease end November 30? — No. Sept 15th or March 31st
    * Can my lease end April 5th? — No. March 31st or April 15th works.
* Invite Resident to confirm preferred end date if within approved window
* If outside the window, offer an alternate end date

:::

## Email Template — Resident Requests Month to Month

> **Subject: Lease Renewal Options**
>
> Hi [Resident First Name],
>
> Thank you for reaching out about your lease term. We do not offer month-to-month agreements. Any change to your lease duration must be requested and approved in advance.
>
> We can review shorter fixed-term options within our policy (leases cannot end between Sept 16 – Mar 31). Please reply with your preferred end date, and we'll confirm if it can be approved. Approval is not guaranteed, but we'll do our best to accommodate your request where possible.
>
> Thank you,
