> **Situation:** An owner emails asking about the status of their lease renewal before the notice deadlines have arrived.


---

## Key Points

* **Renewal is a two-step process with jurisdiction-specific deadlines.** Rent increase notices go out first (120–180 days before increase), followed by the renewal offer (60–90 days before lease end). See [Renewal // Important Deadlines](renewal-important-deadlines.md).
* **Rent increase notices do not require resident response.** The increase takes effect automatically on the specified date.
* **Renewal offers require resident response.** Residents must respond by 30 days before lease end, or Sagareus terminates the lease.
* **The owner will receive proactive communication** at each milestone — rent increase approval request, then renewal approval request.

## Owner Inquiry

Reply to the original email thread.

> Renewal is a two step process with deadlines on when housing provider should communicate with the residents.
>
> **Rent Increase Notice**
> Seattle & Tacoma: 180 days prior to the increase
> Other WA jurisdictions: 120 days for all other jurisdictions
> Notice does not require resident response
>
> **Lease Renewal Offer**
> Renewal offers are served between 60-90 days prior to end of lease. Residents are required to respond by 30 days from the end of the lease or the lease is terminated by Sagareus
>
> You will receive additional communication from Sagareus when it is time to set the rent increase amount and offer renewal.
