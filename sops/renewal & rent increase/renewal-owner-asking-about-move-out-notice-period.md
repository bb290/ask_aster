> **Situation:** An owner asks how much notice the resident must give before moving out, or wants to understand the move-out timeline.


---

## Key Points

* **Residents must provide notice at least 30 days before the end of the lease term.** This is the minimum required period.
* **If no notice is received, Sagareus terminates the lease** to prevent it from converting to month-to-month. Once a lease converts to MTM, the housing provider loses the ability to end the tenancy.
* **Owners with plans to terminate** (selling, moving in, etc.) should notify Sagareus as early as possible. Different jurisdictions have different legal requirements for owner-initiated terminations.
* See [Renewal // Owner requesting to terminate lease](renewal-owner-requesting-to-terminate-lease.md) if the owner wants to end the lease proactively.
