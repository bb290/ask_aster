## Rent Increase (Seattle)

* Rent increase notices to be served **180 days** prior to rent increase

## Rent Increase (Tacoma)

* **First notice:** Rent increase notice to be served **180 days** prior to rent increase
* **Second notice:** A second rent increase notice to be served **90 days** prior to rent increase

Both notices are required by Tacoma municipal code.

## All other jurisdictions in WA

* Rent increase notices to be served **120 days** prior to rent increase (Sagareus standard)


:::warning
The WA legal minimum is 90 days, but several local jurisdictions require 120 days if the rent increase exceeds 5%. Sagareus serves all non-Seattle/non-Tacoma jurisdictions at 120 days to ensure compliance.

:::

## Renewal

* Renewal offer must be served to the Resident **between 60-90 days** to the end of the lease
* Serving the renewal earlier than 90 days will **NOT** meet the WA requirement (Confirmed by attorney)
