## 45–0 Days from end of lease


:::info
Sagareus leases are fixed term with no conversion to MTM. If the resident does not sign the renewal, the lease terminates — it does not revert to month-to-month.

:::

* Send text + email every week
* More assertive email template:

### Email Template — Lease Renewal Final Notice (45–0 Days)

> Can you please confirm whether you plan to renew or move out?
>
> Your current lease ends on **[Lease End Date]**. If you plan to stay, your lease must be renewed no later than 30 days from the end of your lease. Please note: your lease does not automatically convert to month-to-month. If we do not receive a signed renewal by that date, your lease will terminate by default and move-out procedures will be initiated. Let us know as soon as possible so we can assist with next steps either way.
>
> Thank you,
>
> —
>
> ¿Podría confirmar si planea renovar o mudarse?
>
> Su contrato actual vence el **[Lease End Date]**. Si planea quedarse, deberá renovar su contrato a más tardar 30 días antes de su vencimiento. Tenga en cuenta: su contrato no se convierte automáticamente en mes a mes. Si no recibimos una renovación firmada antes de esa fecha, su contrato se dará por terminado y se iniciarán los procedimientos de mudanza. Por favor infórmenos lo antes posible para poder asistirle con los próximos pasos en cualquiera de los casos.
>
> Gracias,

## Terminate Lease if not renewed (40 days to end of lease)


1. Create [End of Tenancy Notice with Cause](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1X31DhDXuCul3g0TNR_31MW81hRsC1Ado/view?usp=drive_link)
2. Attach cover letter — [40-Day End of Tenancy Cover Letter](https://app.pandadoc.com/a/#/templates/4uqNQx8icAo5PCTNs24pFC)
3. Certify mail to the Resident
4. Email Resident:

### Email Template — End of Tenancy Notice

> **Subject: End of Tenancy Notice // [Property Address]**
> **Attachment: End of tenancy notice with cause**
>
> We've attached a formal End of Tenancy Notice as we have not received a signed renewal. Your current lease will terminate on **[Lease End Date]**.
>
> However, the original renewal offer is still available. If you wish to stay, you may sign and return the lease at any time before the lease ends. Let us know if you have any questions.
>
> Regards,
>
> —
>
> Adjuntamos un Aviso de Terminación de Contrato debido a que no hemos recibido la renovación firmada. Su contrato actual finalizará el **[Lease End Date]**.
>
> Aún puede renovar. Si desea quedarse, puede firmar y devolver el contrato de renovación en cualquier momento antes de esa fecha. Quedamos atentos si tiene alguna pregunta.
>
> Atentamente,
