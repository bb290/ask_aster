> **Situation:** An owner requests a market analysis or rental comparable for their property, often in the context of setting a rent increase amount.


---

## Key Points

* **Sagareus rent increase recommendations do not follow market rent.** Our standard heuristic is based on maximizing renewal likelihood while maintaining owner ROI — see the impact table below.
* **Comparables are pulled from Rentometer and Zillow.** Set the correct room count and property type in Rentometer; export the detailed report.
* **Rent increase limits still apply.** Even if market data suggests a higher rent, the increase is capped by jurisdiction rules. See [Renewal // Rent Increase Limits](renewal-rent-increase-limits.md).

| Increase | Impact |
|----------|--------|
| **1-3%** | Maximizes renewal chance |
| **4-7%** | Standard increase exceeding inflation |
| **7%+**  | Increased likelihood of Resident vacating |


---

## Owner Inquiry


1. Pull comparable from **Rentometer** for the property — set the room count and property type, export the detailed report
2. Pull comparable from **Zillow**
3. Reply to the original email thread with attachments:

> Market analysis and comparable attached. For rent increases, our recommendation does not consider market rent and follows this heuristic:
>
> 1-3%: Maximizes renewal chance 4-7%: Standard increase exceeding inflation 7%+: Increased likelihood of Resident vacating
