> **Situation:** An owner asks to change how utilities are billed (e.g., from flat fee to percentage pass-through) at the time of renewal.


---

## Key Points

* **Single family + 1-to-1 meter mapping:** Yes, switching from flat fee to % pass-through is possible.
* **Multi-family:** No — Sagareus policy is flat utility fee for multifamily. See [Sagareus Owners FAQ](https://www.sagareus.com/owner-faqs).
* **Utility fees count toward total rent in WA.** When calculating rent increase limits, utility charges are included in the total rent figure. If the owner wants more utility cost recovery, the solution is to add more to the rent increase amount rather than changing the billing method.


---

## Owner Inquiry

If the property is multi-family, reply to the original email thread.

> For multi-family, our policy is to bill as flat fee — See [Sagareus Owners FAQ](https://www.sagareus.com/owner-faqs).
