> **Situation:** An owner requests a lease renewal term of 6 months or less.


---

## Key Points

* **Renewal offers must exceed six months.** If the lease term is six months or shorter, the housing provider loses the ability to terminate the lease at the end of the term.
* **Short-term renewals require a mutual termination agreement.** The lease must be accompanied by a mutually executed termination agreement to preserve the landlord's right to regain possession.
* **No further renewals are allowed** after a short-term renewal with a termination agreement. This is a one-time arrangement.
* **Owner must confirm understanding** before Sagareus proceeds with a sub-6-month term.
