1. Send email to the owner, requesting approval for rent increase
   * **[Current Rent]** and **[Proposed Rent]** are the TOTAL combined rent, including utilities, pet rent, parking and other rent.
2. If owner specifies a different number, make the corresponding changes to the "New Rent" field
3. Once approval is received or 1 week has passed, Assign the **"Rent Increase // Asana Bot // Create Notice"** task to Asana Bot.
4. Take screenshot of owner instruction and attach to Renewal task

## Email Template — Owner Approval // Rent Increase

> **Subject: Renewal [Year] // [Address]**
>
> We are preparing for the next scheduled rent increase for your property. **Please reply to confirm your approval, or let us know if you would like to discuss a different amount.** This is not a renewal decision, this is approval for the proposed rent increase amount only.
>
> Rent increase date: **[Date]**
> Proposed Increase: $**[Current Rent]** to $**[Proposed Rent]**
>
> Sagareus standard policy:
> Tacoma/Olympia: 4.99% increase recommended
> All other jurisdictions: 6% increase recommended
>
> Rental cap limits by law:
> Tacoma/Olympia: 5% maximum
> All other jurisdictions: 9.683% maximum (2026)
>
> If we do not hear back from you within one week, we will proceed with our proposed amount.
