> **Situation:** An owner asks whether the resident has responded to the rent increase notice or the renewal offer.


---

## Key Points

* **Rent increase notices do not require a response.** The increase takes effect automatically on the specified date. The resident only needs to respond if they choose to appeal the amount.
* **Renewal offers require a response.** Residents must sign by 30 days before lease end, or Sagareus terminates the lease automatically.
* **Sagareus follows up proactively.** The [6 — Resident Follow Up & Lease Termination](6-resident-follow-up-lease-termination.md) procedure reminds residents up to 4 times per month in both English and Spanish.
* **The owner will be notified** once the resident renews or provides notice to vacate.


---

## Owner Inquiry — Rent Increase

> The rent increase notice was served as scheduled. No response is required from the Resident unless they choose to appeal the rent increase amount. Sagareus will check in as we near the lease expiration date to review your preferences for renewal or next steps.


---

## Owner Inquiry — Renewal

> The renewal offer was served as scheduled. Resident has until 30 days to the end of the lease before Sagareus automatically terminates the lease. Our renewal procedure will remind the Residents up to 4 times per month in both English and Spanish of the renewal offer. You will receive additional communication if the Resident renews the lease or provides notice to vacate.
