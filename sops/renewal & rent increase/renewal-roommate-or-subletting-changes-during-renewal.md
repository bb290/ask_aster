> **Situation:** During a renewal, there is a change in the resident lineup — a roommate is leaving, a new person is moving in, or the resident wants to sublet.


---

## Key Points

* **If some residents are staying, Sagareus does not execute a new lease.** The security deposit is tied to a move-in report, and since the remaining residents are not moving, the landlord won't have the opportunity to reset the condition of the home for withholding purposes.
* **This is treated as a roommate change, not a new lease.** Use the [Subletting or Roommate Change](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-customer-service.md) template and procedure.
* **The renewal still proceeds.** The existing lease is renewed with the updated tenant roster via a roommate addendum.


---

## Owner Inquiry

If the owner asks about the process:

> During renewal, if a roommate is leaving or a new person is being added, we handle this as a roommate change rather than a new lease. This preserves the existing security deposit and move-in condition report. We'll process a roommate addendum alongside the renewal to update the tenant roster.
