## Tacoma & Olympia

* Rent increase must be less than **5%**
* If rent increase exceeds 5%, landlord has to pay Resident relocation fund to move out

## All other jurisdictions in WA

* Rent increase is limited to **7% + CPI** or **10%**, whichever is less (9.683% maximum for 2026)

## Setting Rent Increase Amount

* **Olympia/Tacoma** — set to **4.99%** (safety margin — exceeding 5% triggers mandatory relocation assistance to the Resident)
* **All other jurisdictions** — set to **6%**
