In-person notice servicing for rent increase and renewal notices.

**Asana Project:** [Notice Servicing](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1212998012800658/list/1213034139918485)


---

## How It Works

* Notice servicing tasks are created automatically by the [Automation](renewal-automation.md) worker and multi-homed into the Notice Servicing project
* Each task includes the address, deadline, and notice type (Rent Increase, Renewal, TC 180-day, TC 90-day)
* Staff must serve the notice in person between the **Task Due Date** (first allowed day) and the **Deadline Date** (last allowed day)
* Follow the instructions under [Serving Notice - In Person](../resident relations/notices.md)

## Progress Tracking

* Monthly notice servicing progress is tracked on the [Renewal // Dashboard and Metrics](renewal-dashboard-and-metrics.md)
* The dashboard shows pending vs. completed counts and staff-level breakdown
