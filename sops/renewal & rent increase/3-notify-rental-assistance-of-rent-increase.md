1. Check the Resident's ledger if there are payments from rental assistance — Housing Authority, Section 8, etc
2. If Yes, reach out to the rental assistance program and update them of the rent increase
   * Some require email
   * Some require filling out forms

   1. ### **Seattle Housing Authority (SHA)**

      1. **Email / Phone**
         * Email: [**HCVRentIncrease@seattlehousing.org**](mailto:HCVRentIncrease@seattlehousing.org)
         * Phone: **(206) 615-3300**

         **Forms / Links**
         * Rent increase info: <https://www.seattlehousing.org/landlords/how-request-rent-increase>

         **Timeline Requirements**
         * **180 days' notice to tenant** (Seattle law)
         * **At least 60 days before effective date to SHA**
         * Cannot increase during initial lease term
   2. ### **Tacoma Housing Authority (THA)**

      1. **Email / Phone:** **(253) 207-4400**

         **Forms / Links**
         * Rent change page: <https://www.tacomahousing.org/landlords/request-rent-change/>
         * Required:
           * **Landlord Rent Change Request Form**
           * Written notice of rent increase

         **Timeline Requirements**
         * **Submit to THA: at least 60 days before effective date**
         * **Tenant notice (Tacoma law):**
           * Typically **120 days minimum**
           * May require **180 days** depending on increase
           * Some cases involve staged notices (180 / 120 / 90 days)
   3. ### **King County Housing Authority (KCHA)**

      1. **Email / Phone**
         * Email: [**rentrequest@kcha.org**](mailto:rentrequest@kcha.org)
         * Phone: **(206) 574-1100**

         **Forms / Links**
         * Online Rent Change Form:

           <https://onbase.kcha.org/AppNet/UnityForm.aspx?key=UFKey>
         * Info page: <https://www.kcha.org/landlords/rent-change>
         * Required:
           * Online submission (OnBase form)
           * Rent increase notice to tenant + KCHA

         **Timeline Requirements**
         * **Submit to KCHA: at least 60 days before effective date**
         * **Minimum 90 days' notice** to tenant and KCHA
         * Local rules may require:
           * **120 days** (moderate increases)
           * **180 days** (larger increases)
