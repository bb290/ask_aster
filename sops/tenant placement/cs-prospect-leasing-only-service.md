:::warning
**This service has been replaced.** Sagareus no longer offers "Leasing Only Service" as a distinct product. It has been replaced by the **Tenant Placement Program** — a clearer, repriced, standardized version of the same offering.

Prospects asking about a leasing-only engagement should be routed to Tenant Placement.

:::

## What to tell a prospect who asks about "Leasing Only"

* Acknowledge the inquiry directly — do not claim we never offered leasing-only service.
* Explain that the offering has been renamed and refined as the **Tenant Placement Program**.
* Offer to send the Tenant Placement Program Overview.
* If the prospect is qualified and interested, route them to the Sales Pipeline for Tenant Placement.
