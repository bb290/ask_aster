> **Situation:** HOA sends notification requiring interior access to a unit for pest treatment, fire inspection, or other maintenance.


---

## Key Points

* **Forward notice to Resident** — Resident is required to provide access per their lease agreement
* **Acknowledge to owner** — If forwarded by the owner, confirm the notice has been sent to the Resident
* **Track follow-up** — Create tracking task to confirm Resident can provide access on the scheduled date


---

## Response — Forward to Resident

> We have received the below notice from the Homeowners Association (HOA) requiring access to your unit for **\[Reason\]**.
>
> Per your lease agreement, you are required to provide access for such mandatory inspections and maintenance. Please reply to this email to confirm you will be available to provide access on the scheduled date.

## Action


1. Forward the notice to the Resident using the email template above
2. If email forwarded from owner, acknowledge the notice has been forwarded to the Residents
3. Create a tracking task in [Resident Relations 2.0 Ticket](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1201903129380625/list/1205103326230069) project, assigned to [Admin Team Lead](../../docs/role-profiles/admin-team-lead.md)
   * Follow up with the Resident to get confirmation they can provide access
   * Document communication
   * Close task after the access date
