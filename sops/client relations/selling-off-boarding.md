# Offboarding


:::info
Owners may choose to off board for many reasons: selling the property, moving back in, redevelopment, or dissatisfaction with our service.

It is Sagareus' responsibility to make the transition as smooth and straightforward as possible, regardless of the reason for departure.

:::


---

## Owner provide Notice to terminate service


1. Create a task in the Client Relations 2.0 project using the "Off Board // \[Address\] // Owner" template, assigned to Office Manager (Hannah)
2. Set the termination date in the task body
   * Sagareus require 30 days written notice to terminate


---

## Send Initial Offboarding Email

### Email Template, Initial Offboarding Email

> **Subject:** Offboarding Property // \[Property Address\]
> 
> We have received your notice to terminate management services. Per your notice, the management agreement will terminate effective \[Termination Date\], which is set to 30 days from the notice date. The off boarding process will typically require about 30 days to complete from this date.
> 
> **Offboarding Roadmap**
> 
> Our team will prepare all required documents over the next week.
> 
> Once you provide the new management contact information below, we will send you a Change of Management notice for your signature.
> 
> Our accounting team will reconcile final bills and send out the final statement(s) within approximately 30 days after the termination date.
> 
> **New PM Contact**
> Name:
> Address:
> Phone:
> Email:

## Prepare & Email Owner Off boarding package


:::warning
This needs to be completed within the first 5 days of initial notice to buy Sagareus more time to finish the remaining steps.

:::


---

## Prepare change of management notice, get owner signature & Send to Residents


1. Continue working with the owner until they provide the New management contact, Default rent payment instructions and security deposit transfer info requested in the initial email.
2. Once provided, use PandaDoc's **Transfer in Ownership / Management to the owner** template
3. Once the document is signed:
   * Email the notice to all residents
   * Mail a copy of the notice to all residents using mailform.io


---

## Move-out Resident in BD to stop future rent

* Navigate to each lease in BD, set the lease end date to **The Termination Date**

## Remove Sagareus from Utilities


1. Identify all utilities for the property by inspecting the Bills in BD for the past year
2. For each utility, notify the utility Sagareus PM is no longer managing the property
