> **Situation:** Owner is requesting a change to account details that affects ownership, new LLC, adding/removing an owner, divorce/marriage-triggered change, or similar structural change.


---

## Key Points

* **Security-sensitive**, ownership structure changes require the secure update form, not email.
* **Addendum to PMA required**, a PandaDoc Revised PM Agreement Addendum must be created from the "Addendum to Property Management Agreement" template, with "Ownership / Entity Information" checked, sent, and signed before any system updates.
* **Buildium profile updated after signed addendum**, only update the Buildium Rental Owner profile once the signed addendum is received.
* **Scope check**, mailing address / phone / email changes are not sensitive and can be handled over email. This scenario is for ownership/W-9/entity changes only.

## Procedure

Once the Account Update Form is received:


1. **SEND | Revised PM Agreement Addendum**
   * PandaDoc -> Templates -> BizDev -> Addendum to Property Management Agreement -> Create Document
   * Fill in variables and check the **"Ownership / Entity Information"** option
   * Send to the owner for signature
2. **UPDATE | BD Owner Profile**, once the signed copy is returned, update the Buildium Rental Owner profile to reflect the new ownership / entity details.
