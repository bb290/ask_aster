:::warning
A non-conforming multifamily property is defined as a property in which individual units are not recognized as separate addresses in official tax records. As a result, the United States Postal Service (USPS) and internet service providers (ISPs) do not recognize or support individual units as separate, serviceable addresses.

:::


:::info
These policies exist to:

* Prevent predictable service failures
* Reduce tenant frustration and complaints
* Provide clear move in instructions
* Maintain a functional living environment
* Protect owners and Sagareus from avoidable operational issues

:::


---

## Internet Service for Non-Conforming Multifamily Properties

### Owner Responsibility


:::warning
For non-conforming multifamily properties, the property owner is responsible for ensuring that each unit has reliable, functional internet access.

The owner is responsible for:


1. Establishing reliable internet access for **all units**
2. Ensuring the service provider's unit labels exactly match the unit designations used by ownership and management
3. Ensuring service is active prior to tenant move-in
4. Working directly with the service provider for initial installation

:::

### Default Requirement: Shared Internet Service


:::info
If the owner is unable to successfully establish reliable, unit-specific internet service for all units, then the default requirement applies: Property owners of non-conforming multifamily properties establish and maintain a single, shared Wi-Fi service for the property.

:::

### Prohibited Configuration


:::warning
Tenants may not be instructed or expected to independently establish internet service for individual units within a non-conforming multifamily property.

:::

## Mail Service for Non-Conforming Multi-Family Property Policy

### Required Mail Configuration


:::warning
To ensure consistent and functional mail delivery, Sagareus requires tenants to establish a private mailbox with a local mail service provider (USPS, UPS, or FedEx). The recurring cost of the mailbox service will be credited to the tenant ledger upon receiving proof of cost from the tenant, not to exceed $30 / mo.

:::
