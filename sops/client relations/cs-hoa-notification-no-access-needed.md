> **Situation:** HOA sends a notification that does not require interior access (e.g., exterior work, community notice).


---

## Key Points

* **Forward to Resident** — For informational purposes
* **Acknowledge to owner** — If forwarded by the owner, confirm it has been sent to the Residents
* **No tracking needed** — No follow-up task required


---

## Action


1. Forward the notice to the Resident
2. If email forwarded from owner, acknowledge the notice has been forwarded to the Residents

### Example Third Party Questions

* "Please share this notice with the residents regarding upcoming exterior work"
* "Attached is a community notice for owners and residents"
* "Reminder: scheduled chimney/gutter/roof inspection for the building"
* "Notice to residents regarding upcoming maintenance in the common area"
