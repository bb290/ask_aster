> **Situation:** Owner is requesting a change to the bank account used for owner distributions.


---

## Key Points

* **Verbal verification required**, banking information must always be verified via phone call before being updated.
* **Confirm last 4 digits** on the call, confirm the owner initiated the update AND have them read back the last 4 digits of the new bank account number.
* **Test draw after update**, send a $17 EFT test draw with memo "test draw" to verify the new account works.
* **Security-sensitive**, use the secure update form for submission, not email.


---

> - Ownership Entity
> - W-9 Information
> - Bank Account Details
> - Other account-related information
> 
> Best regards,


---

## Procedure

Once the Account Update Form is received:


1. **Verify by phone call**
   * Call the owner using the phone number listed on their Buildium profile.
   * Confirm they initiated the bank account update.
   * Confirm the **last 4 digits** of the new bank account number.
2. **Update banking information in Buildium**
   * Buildium -> Owner's profile -> Financial Tab -> Owner draw ePay settings -> Edit EFT account
3. **SEND | Test Draw**
   * Set Amount to Pay = $17
   * Enter "test draw" in the Memo field
4. **Follow up via email**, confirm successful receipt of the test draw with the owner.
