## Goals

* **Maintain a Reliable, High-Quality Vendor Network** — Ensure all vendors meet Sagareus's standards for professionalism, responsiveness, pricing, and service quality.
* **Create a Structured, Transparent Selection Process** — Standardize how vendors are evaluated, onboarded, and categorized.
* **Improve Efficiency in Dispatching & Coordination** — Equip staff with an up-to-date, well-organized vendor list for faster, smarter decisions based on service type, capacity, location, and performance history.
* **Document Vendor Performance & Mitigate Risk** — Track vendor outcomes, communication, and incidents to identify high-performing partners and flag those who do not meet expectations.
* **Support Compliance, Accountability, and Cost Control** — Ensure all vendors meet legal, insurance, and licensing requirements while controlling costs through thoughtful selection and performance-based retention.


---

## Policies

* All vendors must be **licensed**, **insured**, and have **active tax information** (W-9) on file before receiving work orders.
* All electrical and plumbing work must be performed by **licensed professionals** — see [Maintenance Policies](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-maintenance.md).
* Standard payment terms are **Net 30** from invoice entry date.
* All invoices must include **proof of work** (photos, description of completed work) to be eligible for payment.
* All invoices must be sent to **billing@sagareus.com**.
