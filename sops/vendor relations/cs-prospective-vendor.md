> **Situation:** A vendor or contractor inquires about working with Sagareus.


---

## Key Points

* **Direct to Vendor Intake Form** — sagareus.com/vendor-onboarding
* **Onboarding requirements** — W-9, Certificate of Insurance, ACH enrollment, vendor expectations acknowledgment
* **Review process** — Once form received, reviewed for fit based on service area and trade
* **Full onboarding process** — See [Vendor // Onboard](vendor-onboard.md) for the complete vendor lifecycle


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Thanks for Reaching Out – Vendor Opportunities with Sagareus
>
> Hi \[Vendor Name\],
>
> Thanks for reaching out—we appreciate your interest in partnering with Sagareus Property Management. We're always looking to connect with reliable vendors who take pride in their work and provide great service.
>
> To be considered for upcoming work orders, please complete our [**Vendor Intake Form**](http://www.sagareus.com/vendor-onboarding) here:
>
> As part of our onboarding process, we ask all vendors to:
> • Submit a W-9 and Certificate of Insurance
> • Enroll in ACH payment for faster processing
> • Review and acknowledge our vendor expectations
>
> Once we receive your form, we'll review your submission and follow up if we have a fit based on your service area and trade. Approved vendors may be added to our Onboarding Phase for initial job assignments.
>
> Thanks again—we look forward to reviewing your info and possibly working together!
>
> Best regards,
