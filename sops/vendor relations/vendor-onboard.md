## Vendor List 2.0 Asana Project

### Sections


1. **Intake / Vet** — New vendor (outreach from vendor to Sagareus, Sagareus to vendor, or owner-selected vendor). Vendor agrees to Sagareus Policies, submits documentation. Online reputation reviewed. Price negotiation if needed.
2. **Trial Run** — Vendor has performed less than 3 jobs for Sagareus.
3. **Active** — After 3rd job complete, Operations Manager evaluates performance. Moves to Active or Do Not Contact. Vendor remains Active unless Ops Manager removes.
4. **Do Not Contact** — Operations Manager has identified vendor no longer meets expectations.

### Fields

* **Area** — Service areas vendor indicated they will travel to
* **Services** — Vendor's primary service; additional services listed in task description
* **Cost** — 3 options: $ / $$$ / $$$$$ — indicates skill level / capacity
* **Contact** — Vendor's preferred contact method

### Vendor List Task Template


1. Onboarding Form?
2. Review Pricing | Accept or Decline
3. Vet Online Reputation
4. Accounting Set Up
5. Assign Trial Job #1
6. Assign Trial Job #2

9. Write Google Review

## Lifecycle

### Intake / Vet

Vendors come from 1 of 3 places:


1. Vendor reaches out to Sagareus
2. Owner hires a vendor, Sagareus pays them
3. Sagareus reaches out to vendor

**Vetting Process:**


1. Send Onboarding Form to establish expectations & obtain License + insurance
2. Online reputation is decent
3. Pricing is equal to or less than our current pricing
4. 3 Trial Jobs for increased review
