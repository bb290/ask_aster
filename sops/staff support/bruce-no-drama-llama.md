Meet **Bruce**, the No Drama Llama: official internal mascot of the [Sagareus Values](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/sagareus-values-gfLVdbGiFf). All four of them.

Bruce wears sunglasses because he is never surprised. He is never surprised because he already checked.

His photo: the image above, or `assets/bruce-no-drama-llama.jpg` in this repo, or `clients/sagareus/assets/mascots/no-drama-llama.png` for the full-size art.

## Bruce Background

Bruce was born in the Sacred Valley of Peru, at about 11,000 feet, into a long line of working pack llamas. His family has been hauling gear up the Andes for generations. Where there is no hardware store at 14,000 feet, you pack right the first time or you have a very dramatic afternoon. Bruce's family does not have dramatic afternoons.

**Previous work.** Eight seasons as lead pack llama on the Inca Trail: know every load, every strap, and every weather front before the humans wake up. In eight seasons his teams lost zero tents, zero boots, and exactly one granola bar, which Bruce maintains was an inside job. He finished his Peru career on the sunrise shift at Machu Picchu, standing calmly in front of one of the wonders of the world while tourists panicked about their camera batteries.

**Education.** Self-taught meteorologist; right about tomorrow's rain more often than the app. Informal degree in load balancing, earned the hard way.

**The move north.** Bruce came to the Puget Sound in 2026. A llama built for Andean weather finds the Pacific Northwest extremely reasonable, and he liked that Sagareus was founded by people who also believe the best problems are the ones that never happened.

**Outside of work.** Weekend hikes in the Cascades with the ten essentials, always back before dark. A quinoa patch at the West Seattle P-patch community gardens. Amateur photography, minimum two photos of everything, more when anything looks off. And a standing recurring Tuesday walk he has never once missed.

## Bruce's Voice

Bruce is calm, dry, and economical with words. When you write for Bruce:

* **Short sentences. No urgency.** Bruce has never rushed anywhere. He left early instead.
* **Direct, never bossy.** He states the move and trusts you to make it. "Walk every vacant home, every week. I'll wait."
* **Dry humor, lightly applied.** One raised eyebrow per appearance, maximum. He is funny because he underreacts.
* **Never panicked, never preachy, never punitive.** Bruce does not scold.
* **Sagareus voice rules apply.** No em dashes, no jargon, specific over clever.

If a line sounds like an alarm, it is not Bruce. If it sounds like a guy leaning on a fence who happens to be right, that is Bruce.

## What He Stands For

Bruce represents all four Sagareus Values, practiced the way a trail llama would:

* **Proactive > Reactive, his home turf.** Catch it before it blows up. Walk the property, run the appliance, turn the key. His habit: when you finish a task, ask "what is the next thing that could go wrong here, and can I get ahead of it?"
* **Think on Your Feet.** The worst decision is no decision. Make the call you can explain afterward and keep walking.
* **Data is King, Context is Queen.** The number tells you where to look; the story tells you what to do. Never one without the other.
* **Continuous Improvement.** Bring the problem, the fix, and how to do the fix. Keep it, delete it, or improve it.
* **And underneath it all: boring is the goal.** Bruce considers boredom a five-star review.

## When He Shows Up

Any time we teach, roll out, or train on the values or the systems built from them: values sessions, onboarding, initiative briefs, training decks, SOP rollouts, checklists. [Operation: No Drama](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/operation-no-drama-1KiDctsybm) was his first assignment.

**The rule: when Bruce talks, that is your action item.** His speech bubbles carry the instruction in his voice. If he said it, do it.

## Usage Rules

* Speech bubbles carry directives, written in Bruce's voice.
* Lower-right corner of slides, one appearance per slide, only on slides with an action item.
* The sunglasses stay on. Always.
* Coaching only, never punitive. Consequences are a human conversation, not a llama's job.
* **Internal only.** Bruce lives in team materials. He does not appear in owner, resident, or public-facing content.
