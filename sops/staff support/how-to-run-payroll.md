## Payroll Cadence


:::info
Sagareus runs **Semi Monthly payroll**.

:::

This means we make payments to employees on the 16th and the 1st of each month. The pay reflects the preceding two weeks of work. The two payroll runs differ slightly from each other.

## Mid-Month Payroll

There are no reimbursements, miles, or bonuses on the mid-month run.

* Log in to ADP.
* Select **Run Payroll**.
* You will be prompted to import the latest time cards — do so.
* Do a sanity check:
  * Check for a lot of overtime.
  * Missed time punches.
  * Missed meal breaks.
* All employees enrolled in health care will have a deduction included on each pay stub. Confirm settings are consistently accurate.
* For mid-month, you can simply process the payroll.

## Month-End Payroll

Follow all of the steps for mid-month payroll, plus:

* Manually add any **reimbursements** requested by employees and tracked in Asana.
  * Column for these additions is **Misc Amount**.
* Manually add all **bonuses** from the Leasing Spreadsheet updated by the Leasing Manager.
  * Column for these additions is **Bonuses**.
* Manually add all **MilesIQ** reimbursements.
  * Log into MilesIQ.
  * Run report for previous month.
  * Upload report to CSV-readable format and manually enter into payroll.
  * Column for these additions is **Mileage Amount**.

## For Accounting

* Each employee is issued a check on the books for the Net Pay.
* Each employee is issued a check on the books for their Employee tax withholding.
* This second entry is combined with a one-time other bank transaction for Employer tax withholding under Sagareus Expense.
* Due to the nature of how the transactions post to the bank account, the net pay and the employer/employee tax withholdings are reconciled together. It is a non-intuitive entry but necessary for accounting purposes.
* For the deduction for healthcare: this is described in detail under benefits administration. There are additional check entries entered for each employee's deduction amount and a corresponding credit to healthcare company expense to net zero and report the funds more accurately.
