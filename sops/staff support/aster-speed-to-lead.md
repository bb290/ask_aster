Scans your Gmail for new Zillow leads and drafts a personalized reply for every one, per the Speed to Lead SOP. Draft-only: nothing auto-sends.

## When to Use

Say "check Zillow leads," "respond to my new leads," or invoke /speed-to-lead. Several times a day: the first responder usually wins the showing.

## How It Works

1. Invoke it; no questions asked. Aster searches Gmail for unread Zillow lead emails immediately.
2. For each lead, Aster looks up the property in Asana for listing URLs and preferred showing slots.
3. Aster creates a personalized Gmail draft per lead following the Speed to Lead SOP; you review and send.

Requires Gmail and Asana connectors.
