> **Situation:** Someone expresses interest in working at Sagareus or asks about open positions.


---

## Key Points

* **Direct to job board** — All applications go through CareerPlug
* **Acknowledge interest** — Thank them and provide the link


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Thanks for Reaching Out
>
> Hi \[Applicant's First Name\],
>
> Thanks so much for your interest in joining the team at Sagareus Property Management! We're always looking for great people, and appreciate you reaching out.
>
> To be considered for any of our open roles, please review open position & submit an application through our job board here:
> https://sagareus-group-llc.careerplug.com/account
>
> This ensures your information is reviewed by the right members of our team, and that you'll be notified about relevant openings as they come up.
>
> We look forward to reviewing your application!
>
> Best regards,

### Example Questions

* "Are you hiring?"
* "I'm interested in working for Sagareus"
* "Do you have any open positions?"
