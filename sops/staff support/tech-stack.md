## Asana

Our internal **project and task management system** used for operational transparency.

* Listings enter Asana during the **Pre-Listing stage**.
* The **main task is assigned to the Leasing Agent**.
  * Subtasks may be assigned to you or others as needed.
* **Due Date = next action date**, not lease signed date.
  * Update as needed to reflect the actual follow-up timeline.


:::warning
**Task Rules:**

* **Overdue tasks are bad.**
* Always take the next action, **update the comment section**, and **adjust the due date**.
* Reassign to another team member if their action is required next.

:::


:::tip
**Use the "My Tasks" tab:**

* Check this daily.
* Take action or reassign for proper load balancing.

:::

## 1Password

* Our secure password manager used to store, protect, and share company credentials.
* Staff access keybox information from 1Password before every showing or field appointment to ensure accuracy and safety.
* 1Password ensures all Sagareus accounts, tools, and platforms are accessed safely without employees creating or storing passwords on personal devices.


:::warning
**Usage Expectations**

* **Never save company passwords in Chrome, Notes, screenshots, or personal password managers.**
* All shared credentials (e.g., Zillow, Apartments.com, Jotform Admin, Buildium API, marketing logins) must live **only** in the appropriate 1Password Vault.
* When you create a login for any Sagareus-related tool, **store it immediately** in 1Password.
* Use the built-in **password generator** for all new credentials (no personal passwords, no re-use).
* Keep your **Master Password** private. Sagareus will never ask for it.

:::


:::warning
**Keybox Policy (Non-Negotiable)**

* **1Password houses ALL keybox codes, combinations, serial numbers, and access notes.**
* **We do not send keybox codes internally via email, text, Asana, or RingCentral.**
* Any update to a keybox (code change, location change, replacement, reassignment) must be made **directly inside 1Password**.

:::


:::info
**Functional Vaults**

Access levels are determined by role:

* **Property Access Vault** – lockbox codes & locations - All Staff
* **Property Management** – Utility / vendor accounts - Admin Team
* **Finance** – Company level accounts - Accounting Team

:::


:::warning
**Security Compliance**

* 1Password is required for all staff.
* Any password outside 1Password is considered a security violation.

:::
