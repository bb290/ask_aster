The onboarding skill: the first thing a new leasing team member runs when meeting Aster, and the refresher for anyone who forgets what is available.

## When to Use

Invoke /meet-aster, or any first-contact phrase: "hey Aster," "what can you do," "help," "I'm new here." Any time it is not obvious which skill to use.

## How It Works

1. Aster introduces itself in plain leasing-team language.
2. Checks the Claude.ai connectors (Asana, Gmail, Google Calendar) and walks through setup for anything missing.
3. Offers a choose-your-own deep-dive on the available skills.
