The self-audit for Operation: No Drama. One command checks every vacancy you hold: weekly site visit documented (checklist comment + photos in the last 7 days), every flagged problem has a real subtask (assignee + due date, per the Accountability Rule), and Preferred Showing Slot 1 is set with its recurring calendar event.

## When to Use

Say "no drama check," "check my vacancies," "am I covered," or invoke /no-drama. Monday or early Tuesday before weekly reports: run this first and /weekly-report has everything it needs. Team Leads can check an agent's portfolio or the whole team.

## How It Works

1. Zero questions: Aster pulls your vacancies and runs the checks immediately.
2. First message is the finished report: suggested subtasks to create, or confirmation your documentation is current.
3. Empty Slot 1: Aster asks your preference, then fills the field and creates the recurring calendar event after approval.

Requires Asana; Google Calendar for the calendar checks and writes.
