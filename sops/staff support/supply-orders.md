# Office Equipment / Supply Policies

## Office Supplies Policy + Supply List

**Purpose** — To ensure employees have the essential supplies needed to perform their work efficiently, Sagareus provides access to standard office supplies. This policy outlines eligibility, request procedures, and accountability standards for company-issued office supplies.

**Eligibility** — After successfully completing the 90-day probation period, employees may request approved office supplies as needed for their role.

**Frequency of Issuance** — Office supplies are provided on an as-needed basis, within reasonable use. Excessive or repeated requests for the same item may require supervisor approval.


:::warning
**Ownership and Return Requirements**

All supplies remain the property of the firm. Employees are expected to:

* Use supplies responsibly and only for Sagareus business purposes.
* Request only the items necessary for their role.
* Return any reusable items (such as phone stabilizers, keyboxes, or ozone deodorizers) within 5 business days if they resign, are terminated, or transition to a role that no longer requires the item. Failure to return reusable supplies may result in final paycheck deductions or legal action to recover the firm's property or its value, as permitted by law.

:::


:::tip
**Office Supplies List**

Employees may request the following items after completing the 90-day probation period:

* Phone Stabilizer
* Toner Refill
* Printer Paper
* Blue Painter's Tape
* Ozone Deodorizer
* Gloves
* Keyboxes
* Key Delivery Envelope Pack
* Miscellaneous Cleaning Supplies

:::

## Office Equipment Policy + Equipment List

**Purpose** — To ensure that all team members have the tools they need to succeed while maintaining accountability for company property, this policy outlines the terms under which firm-supplied equipment is requested, issued, and returned.

**Eligibility** — After successfully completing the 90-day probation period, each employee may request the following company-issued equipment:

* 1 desk top computer + accessories
* 1 printer

**Frequency of Issuance** — Each item may be requested **once every 3 years**, unless an earlier replacement is warranted due to verified malfunction or job-related damage. Upgrades and replacements outside this cycle may be approved on a case-by-case basis.


:::warning
**Ownership and Return Requirements**

All equipment remains the property of the firm. Employees are expected to:

* Use the equipment responsibly and only for business purposes unless otherwise approved.
* Maintain the equipment in good condition, reporting any damage or malfunction immediately.
* **Return all company-issued equipment** in working condition **within 5 business days** if they resign, are terminated, or transition to a role that no longer requires the equipment. Failure to return equipment results in final paycheck deductions or legal action to recover the firm's property or its value, as permitted by law.

:::

**Lost, Stolen, or Damaged Equipment** — Employees must report lost, stolen, or significantly damaged equipment immediately to HR or their direct supervisor. The company may require reimbursement in cases of gross negligence or unauthorized personal use.


:::tip
**Equipment List**

After completing the 90-day probation period, employees may request up to **one of each** of the following items:

* MacMini
* Monitor w/ built in webcam
* Keyboard + Mouse
* Adapter
* Home office printer

:::

## Laptop Policy


:::info
At this time, the company does not issue laptops as standard equipment. We've made this decision intentionally for the following reasons:

* **Risk & Liability** — Laptops present a higher risk of **damage, loss, or theft**, particularly when used outside a controlled work environment. This increases both replacement costs and data security concerns.
* **Productivity & Ergonomics** — Full-size monitors with built in webcam and dedicated workstations provide a significantly more **ergonomic and efficient setup** for most job functions. Laptops often reduce productivity due to smaller screens, limited input options, and distractions in non-office settings.
* **Work Focus & Structure** — We emphasize the importance of **dedicated desk work time** to promote focus, accountability, and consistency. Having a fixed workspace helps reinforce healthy boundaries between work and personal life.

:::
