## Missed Time Card Punches

When an email notice is received regarding missed punches:

* Navigate to ADP and log in.
* Select **Time** in the sidebar.
* Select **Manage my team** in the title bar.
* Review the **Things to do** tile on the top left.
  * Select **Missed Punches**.
  * Review the subject missed punches.
  * Send the subject employee the templated email below.
* Edit the employee's time card to match the response to the email.
