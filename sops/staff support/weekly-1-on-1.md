## Weekly One on One Check Ins


:::warning
Every team lead & manager meets with their direct reports **weekly** via Google Meet. Record every 1-on-1 with Gemini.

:::


1. Every team lead & manager meets with their direct reports **weekly** via Google Meet

   
   1. Record 1 on 1 meeting with Gemini
2. Identify & Train on 1 SOP

   
   1. Remain with the SOP until sufficient performance
   2. Train, review, review, review
3. Copy / Paste Gemini Summary in 1 on 1 meeting asana task

   
   1. Update due date to next week's meeting date
