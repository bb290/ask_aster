## Who Has Access?

Admin, Controller and Lead accountant have access to all necessary accounts to perform the day to day operations within banking.

The Admin accounts specifically can only access Zelle platform and view the Certificate of Deposit accounts that house liability funds.


:::warning
In worst case scenario, company owners (and signers) will need to contact bank representatives to establish Admin account access. No passwords can be stored as they specifically route to phones using a credentialing app or 2FA authentication.

:::
