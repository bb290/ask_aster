Staff-facing capture skill for situations the playbook did not cover cleanly: the SOP was missing, unclear, or felt off.

## When to Use

Say "I have an edge case," "this isn't covered in the playbook," or invoke /edge-case. Also whenever you ask Aster an operational question and the answer is not in the SOPs.

## How It Works

1. One batched intake: the situation, property, which SOP felt unclear or missing, what you decided or need to decide, any deadline.
2. At most one follow-up round; Aster confirms the summary.
3. Files an Asana task in Roll Out, New captures, for Brittany's review. Reviewed captures become SOP updates via Decision to Rollout.

Requires Asana.
