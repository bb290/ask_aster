## Approval of PTO

An automated email is received from ADP when an employee requests time off.

To review and approve/deny:

* Log in to ADP.
* Navigate to the **Time** sidebar.
* Select **Manage my team**.
* The time-off request is immediately apparent in the **Things to Do** tile.
* Select the time-off requests and approve or decline.
* TOOL: **Time off this week** provides insight into current time off approvals.
* TOOL: The **Team Schedule** link provides additional insights.

## Cash-Out Option

ADP confirmed that they do not currently see companies using PTO cash-out frequently. If we decide to offer this option, it will likely need to be managed on the payroll side, with PTO hours manually reduced from accrual balances. A PTO Cash-Out Request form has been drafted for future use.

## Email Template — Cash-Out Announcement

> **To:** All Employees
> **Subject:** **PTO Cash-Out Benefit Announcement**
>
> Good morning,
>
> I'm excited to share that this year, Sagareus is opening the Paid Time Off (PTO) "cash out" benefit in **\[November\]** to help employees plan for the holiday season!
>
> On **November 7th, 2025**, eligible employees will receive the payout for their approved PTO cash out request. Employees who are interested must complete the Cash Out Request Form by **Friday, October 10th, 2025**.
>
> **Guidelines and Eligibility:**
>
> • Employees have a minimum of One (1) Year of Service
> • Request Eight-hour increments (Eight-hour minimum).
> • Request no more than Five (5) days (40 hours)
> • Must have a reserve of 24 PTO hours remaining after using any leave. (This reserve ensures employees have sufficient leave available for unexpected absences.)
>
> Thank you,
