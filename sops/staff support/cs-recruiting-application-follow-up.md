> **Situation:** Job applicant follows up on an application they already submitted.


---

## Key Points

* **Forward to** [**Business Development Manager**](../../docs/role-profiles/business-development-manager.md) **internally** — Do not respond with hiring details
* **Acknowledge the follow-up** — Let applicant know the message has been forwarded to the hiring manager


---

## Response

> Thank you for the follow up, the extra effort is appreciated! I've forwarded directly to the hiring manager.
>
> Best regards,

## Action


1. Forward the follow-up to [Business Development Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/business-development-manager.md) internally
2. Let applicant know message received and forwarded

### Example Questions

* "I applied last week, any update?"
* "Just checking on the status of my application"
* "I submitted my application, when will I hear back?"
