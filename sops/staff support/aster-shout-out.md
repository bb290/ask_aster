Capture recognition for a teammate so it lands publicly instead of evaporating in a group chat. Brittany pulls from these for the weekly company email.

## When to Use

Say "shout out to [name]," "kudos to," "[name] crushed it," or invoke /shout-out.

## How It Works

1. One batched ask: who, what they did, why it stood out.
2. Aster files an Asana task in Roll Out (New captures or Shout outs section) for Brittany's review; recognition gets broadcast in the weekly company email.

Requires Asana.
