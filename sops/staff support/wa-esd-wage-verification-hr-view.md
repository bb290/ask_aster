## Washington State Inquiries/Responses

### Responding to WA ESD Wage Verification (Fraud Prevention Audit)

**Purpose** To ensure Sagareus responds timely and accurately to the Washington State Employment Security Department (ESD) when it requests verification of employee weekly wages as part of unemployment fraud prevention audits.

**Scope** Applies to HR and Payroll staff responsible for verifying and reporting employee wage and hour information.

### Procedure

#### 1. Receipt of Request

* ESD will send a **Wage Verification Request** (by mail, fax, or electronically).
* The request is not an unemployment claim but an **audit to confirm weekly wages paid** to a specific employee during a specific period.
* **Log the request** in the HR/Payroll records (employee name, period requested, date received, and response deadline).

#### 2. Initial Review

* Confirm the notice specifies:
  * Employee name and identifying information.
  * Specific weeks and wage data being requested.
  * Submission deadline.

#### 3. Gather Payroll Records

* Retrieve payroll registers, timesheets, or other supporting documents for the requested weeks.
* Verify:
  * **Gross wages** earned (before taxes/deductions).
  * **Hours worked** (if requested).
  * Any **leave pay (PTO, sick, vacation)** included in wages.

#### 4. Complete the Wage Verification

* Enter the exact weekly wages as shown in payroll records.
* Double-check for accuracy — discrepancies can trigger penalties or further audits.
* If the employee did not work or was unpaid for a week, note "$0 wages" clearly.

#### 5. Submit Response to ESD

* Return the completed form by the stated deadline via the method provided (mail or fax).
* Retain a copy of the completed form and supporting wage documents in the payroll audit file.

#### 6. Notify HR Manager

* Send a summary of the request and response to the HR Manager for oversight.
* Escalate any unusual findings (e.g., inconsistencies between wages reported by the employee and payroll).

#### 7. Recordkeeping


:::warning
Maintain a copy of the request, completed form, and supporting wage documentation for **at least 4 years** in accordance with WA state recordkeeping and ESD audit rules.

:::

* Maintain a copy of the request, completed form, and supporting wage documentation for **at least 4 years** in accordance with WA state recordkeeping and ESD audit rules.

### Responsibilities

* **HR Manger/Payroll:** Completes wage verification using payroll data and submits response.
* **HR Manager:** Logs request, maintains records, ensures deadlines are met. Reviews complex cases, ensures compliance, communicates with ESD if clarification is needed.
