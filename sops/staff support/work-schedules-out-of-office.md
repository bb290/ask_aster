## Floating Holidays

All employees are eligible for **11 floating holidays per calendar year** for personal, cultural, or religious observances, or other occasions of the employee's choosing.

* Taken in **full-day (8-hour) increments**
* Details in the [Employee Handbook](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1cFfW9yOZ3wyuBLxFuNgK3G9zv8vsnwBm/view)

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

All employees accrue Paid Time Off (PTO). Hours may be used for trips, sick leave, personal appointments, or any purpose.

* Can be taken in **1-hour increments** or up to **5 consecutive days**
* Accrual rates detailed in the [Employee Handbook](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1cFfW9yOZ3wyuBLxFuNgK3G9zv8vsnwBm/view)

## Team Calendar


:::info
The **Team Calendar** is our shared Google Calendar — the single source of truth for all scheduled absences across the company.

:::

Each team member is responsible for keeping it up to date:

* Recurring regular days off
* Approved PTO / Out-of-Office dates

### Event Label


:::tip
Always label events as: **\[Your Name // OOF\]**

:::

* OOF = Out of Office
* The reason you are out of office is personal and not required to share

### Enter Recurring Days Off


1. Google Calendar → Create Event → Choose Day Off (all day) → Label \[Name // OOF\] → Set to recurring weekly → Choose "Team Calendar" → Save

## Extended Leave

### Washington State Paid Family & Medical Leave (PFML)


:::warning
PFML is a **state-administered program** that provides paid leave for qualifying medical or family reasons. Employees apply directly through the [WA Employment Security Department](https://paidleave.wa.gov/).

:::

* [Official Website](https://paidleave.wa.gov/)
* [Updates](https://paidleave.wa.gov/updates/)
* [2026 Required Poster](https://paidleave.wa.gov/app/uploads/2025/12/Mandatory-poster-2026.pdf)
* [Employee Notice to Employer](https://paidleave.wa.gov/app/uploads/2019/12/30-day-notice-to-employer.pdf)
