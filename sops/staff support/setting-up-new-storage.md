:::info
**Dimensions:** 5x10

**Access Requirement:**

* Easy access from I-5
* Coded entry to property or building
* 24 hours access

**Property/Unit Security:**

* Exterior and interior cameras
* Lock for the unit (most storages provide them, depending on lock mechanism) and ability to put our own keybox on lock/slide mechanism (keybox needs to be big enough to fit at least 1 key and a cylinder lock for the door lock)
