## Core Processes

Core Process Training provides a high-level, end-to-end overview of the foundational workflows that operate simultaneously across Sagareus.

The objective of this training series is to ensure all staff:

* Understand the full scope of work occurring across the organization
* Recognize how their individual role contributes to shared outcomes
* Gain context for the functions, dependencies, and responsibilities of adjacent teams
* Operate with greater accountability, efficiency, and cross-departmental awareness


:::info
This training is intentionally broad-stroke. It is not a replacement for role-specific SOPs, but a foundation that allows staff to work more effectively within a complex, interconnected operation.

:::
