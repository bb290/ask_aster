## Accounts Structure

Bank accounts are a mirror of the recorded accounts housed in Buildium. Bank of America and Chase platforms are both used. All bank accounts are identified by their three digit numerical code. Chase bank accounts are specifically designated with a C at the end of their numerical code.

## Attaching New Bank Accounts to Onboarded Properties

Newly acquired properties are initially allocated to the **901. TEMP/ONBOARDING ACCOUNT**.

The lead accountant is responsible for daily monitoring of this account. The receipt of new property funds in this account triggers the comprehensive onboarding process.

Upon receiving new funds, identify their source by accessing the 901 account and reviewing the balance breakdown to determine the responsible property. Subsequently, adhere to the Asana onboarding process, which involves:

* Assigning a dedicated bank account to the property.
* Assigning a unique numerical code to an available checking account.
* Navigating to the property profile and setting the newly assigned account, with the new unique code, as the default.
* Updating liability funds to route to the 9990 account.
