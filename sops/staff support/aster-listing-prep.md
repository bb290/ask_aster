The full prelisting workflow in one guided conversation: 30-45 minutes of manual prep in about 5. Exists to help you determine market rent, not to ask you for it.

## When to Use

Say "prelisting package for [address]" or invoke /listing-prep when a new property is about to go live. The PreListing tool on sagareus.com/field is the standard path; this skill is the conversational alternative. For copy only use /listing-copywriter; for comps only use /market-rent-analysis.

## How It Works

1. Aster pulls owner info and property metadata from Asana custom fields.
2. Comp analysis runs first to surface a market rent recommendation; pricing decisions anchor on it.
3. Generates listing copy, assembles the PreListing email in Sagareus voice, drafts in Gmail, and posts a timestamped copy on the matching Asana subtask.

Requires Gmail and Asana connectors.
