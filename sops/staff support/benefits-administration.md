## How Benefits are Paid

Sagareus initially covers the full cost of the employee healthcare benefits package, paid directly to our vendor, KAVI.

* **Vendor Payment:** The entire benefit package amount is paid to KAVI.
* **Vendor Access:** KAVI login credentials are stored in 1Password and secured with 2FA to cooper@sagareus.com.
* **Payment Method:** The payment to KAVI is an automatic withdrawal from the operating account.
* **Accounting:** This initial expenditure is logged on the books as a lump sum under the **Employer Healthcare** line item.

This process concludes the monthly payment portion of the healthcare workflow.

## How Benefits are Recorded

The subsequent step involves tracking and recording the employee and employer contributions within the payroll system.

* **Employee Contribution Recoupment:** Each enrolled employee has a specific contribution portion that must be recouped.
* **Payroll Deductions:** These employee contribution amounts (deductions) are reconciled annually during the enrollment period (November) and updated in the employee's ADP payroll settings.
* **Employer Contribution:** The employer's "contribution" is reflected on the employee's pay stub as pay, but is not disbursed to the employee, as the funds were paid directly to KAVI.
* **Payroll Processing:** When payroll is run, both the deductions and contributions are automatically included. Process payroll as usual.

## Post-Payroll Recording

After the payroll run, the following accounting entries are made:


1. Record each employee's net pay.
2. Record each employee's tax withholding as a payment.
3. Enter the overall employer tax withholding for the entire payroll cycle.
4. **Healthcare Reconciliation:**
   * For every enrolled employee, enter their **Deduction** amount as a direct payment back to them (to zero out the earlier deduction entry).
   * Enter a single, overall credit to the **Employer Healthcare** line item via an "other bank transaction." This action decreases the total expense in the Employer Healthcare line item and appropriately allocates the withholding costs to the relevant team expense line.
