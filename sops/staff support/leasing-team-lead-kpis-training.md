## Leasing Team Lead – Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)


1. Personal listing performance meets Leasing Agent KPIs, adjusted for assigned listing volume.
2. Weekly 1:1 coaching sessions are conducted and documented for each assigned Leasing Agent.
3. All assigned Leasing Agents meet minimum Leasing Agent KPIs within 90 days of assignment.
4. SOP adherence is consistently maintained across the assigned team with no material compliance violations.
5. Routine leasing issues are resolved at the Team Lead level, and any escalations to the Leasing Manager include clear context, supporting documentation, and a recommended solution.
