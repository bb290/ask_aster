## Rental Properties

* Once deployed, the asset stays at the rental property
* During deployment, a subtask must be created to track pickup, assigned to [Assistant Property Manager (Asset)](../../docs/role-profiles/assistant-property-manager-asset.md)
* During move-out, any asset at the rental property must be reclaimed — check the [Asset Check Out](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1n2k7oY9oGUadTmFZWtDe7DN47BAhI0L9YnF-Mri5K1U/edit?usp=sharing) spreadsheet for deployed assets

## Held by Staff

* Staff may temporarily hold on to assets for convenience, but not exceeding 2x of each item
