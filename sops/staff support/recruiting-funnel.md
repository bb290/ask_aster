## People Lifecycle

**Recruiting:** Market and sell the job to attract top talent.

**Onboarding:** Integrate new hires into the culture, systems, and expectations so they feel confident, supported, and ready to contribute quickly.

**Training:** Equip people with the clarity, skills, and systems to succeed without constant supervision.

**Retention:** Create an environment where top performers feel valued, challenged, and aligned with the company's vision, so they choose to grow with us.

## Capacity Planning

* Best applicants want to provide *at least* 2 weeks notice, ideally 1 month
* Want advancement opportunities / clear plan
* Want health benefits + 401k

## Reviewing Resumes


1. As applications come in, utilize ZipRecruiter's sort function, sorting resumes into: Not a fit, Maybe, Good Fit

## Questions Applicants Ask


1. What is a typical day?
2. What is training like?
3. What are advancement opportunities?
4. Benefits?

## Reasons Leaving Jobs

* Inflexible hours
* Feeling under appreciated
  * New hires paid more
  * No follow up on training / advancement
* Feeling stuck / no growth opportunities

## Least Favorite Aspect of PM

* Getting yelled at
