Staff-facing capture skill for incidents: something happened that needs management attention, filed properly so it does not get lost in a text thread.

## When to Use

Say "I have an incident report," "I need to report something," or invoke /incident-report for any real event affecting operations, a property, a Resident, an owner, or a vendor.

## How It Works

1. One batched intake: what happened, where, when, who, what you have done, photos or links.
2. At most one follow-up round; Aster confirms the summary.
3. Files an Asana task in Roll Out, New captures, for Brittany's review.

Edge Case is for playbook gaps rather than events; Decision to Rollout turns reviewed captures into SOP work. Requires Asana.
