## Employee Handbook

The [Employee Handbook](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1i1RQXle3k7bqsEfDlCbs8uYiYYzULXyZTr7Yz42lxBM/edit?usp=sharing) serves as the central guide for all Sagareus team members, outlining company policies, procedures, expectations, and benefits. It ensures that all employees have access to the same information and resources needed to perform their roles effectively and in alignment with Sagareus' values and standards.

The Employee Handbook exists to:

* Provide a single source of truth for company policies and procedures
* Reduce reliance on informal knowledge and ad hoc explanations
* Ensure consistent execution across all teams
* Protect Sagareus from compliance risks, legal issues, and miscommunication
* Foster a clear understanding of expectations, responsibilities, and company culture

### Playbooks Prevent Problems

A maintained Employee Handbook helps prevent:

* Misunderstandings about policies, benefits, or expectations
* Inconsistent management of employees across teams
* Compliance violations, labor disputes, or HR escalations
* Confusion during onboarding, promotions, or role changes
* Last-minute scrambling to answer employee questions

### Ownership & Responsibility

**Primary Owner:** HR Manager **Secondary Owner:** Accounting Director

The HR Manager is the primary owner of the Employee Handbook and is responsible for ensuring all content is accurate, complete, and up to date. The Accounting Director provides backup oversight and ensures any financial or benefits-related policies are current.

## Employee Handbook Updates

HR Manager updates the Employee Handbook whenever new policies, procedures, or benefits are introduced or new regulations are implemented.

### How to Edit & Update Employee Handbook

[Video Overview - Edit / Update Employee Handbook](https://www.loom.com/share/0cf1ad86e727414daf0cf4b0a1e32094)


1. Edit Directly in the Existing Document

   
   1. Open the current [Employee Handbook](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1i1RQXle3k7bqsEfDlCbs8uYiYYzULXyZTr7Yz42lxBM/edit?usp=sharing) in Google Docs.
   2. Do **not** create separate draft addendums or duplicate files.
2. Use Suggestion Mode

   
   1. Switch to Suggestion Mode: **View → Mode → Suggesting**
   2. Make edits directly in the document
   3. Suggested edits appear in-line and can be accepted or rejected by reviewers.
3. Collaborate and Review

   
   1. Tag relevant team members for feedback using comments
4. Apply Approved Changes

   
   1. Once suggestions are reviewed and approved, accept the edits in Suggestion Mode.
5. Update Employee View

   
   1. Download Updated Version as PDF
   2. Rename: "Sagareus Employee Handbook (revised Month Day Year)
   3. Upload to Human Resources Shared Drive
   4. Copy Share Link
   5. Replace Hyperlink on [Employee's HR SOP](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1k7dKfmzUu98MEfsbkQliodkSnQpAxHE5tsFZiaawSko/edit?tab=t.ih8hgrz2t4o5#heading=h.nbzmgp16m92h)
   6. Move outdated Handbook to 'Employee Handbook Archive' file
