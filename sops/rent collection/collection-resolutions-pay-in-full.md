Residents with outstanding balances have until the **20th of each month** to resolve with 1 of 3 options: **Pay in Full**, Payment Plan, or Early Termination. Once the 22nd has passed & eviction proceedings have begun, only 2 options remain: **Pay in Full** or Early Termination.


---

At any point, Resident may pay balance in full. This may not be communicated by Resident.

**Before making any phone call/text:** Check ledger to ensure balance is still owed.

**If paid in full:**


1. Click Paid in Full subtasks
2. Screenshot ledger, showing paid in full
3. Send "Balance Paid" Owner Update email
4. Unenroll Resident from HubSpot sequence
5. Delete Lease ID from task field
6. Mark Parent task complete

**Resident communicates a date they intend to pay in full:**

Recommend payment plan instead if:

* Amount is over $1,000
* Rent has been late in the past

## Phone Script, Recommending Payment Plan

> We received your message that you will make payment on \[Date\].
> 
> My concern is that you will then be late again for April, constantly paying the late fee. \[PAUSE\]
> 
> We'd like to set up a payment plan so you can:
> - Get caught up
> - Avoid the monthly late fee
> - Avoid monthly phone calls and texts

## Continue with Pay in Full Resolution


1. Screen capture email, online form, or other communication
2. Upload to parent task comment
3. Update status to Pay In Full
4. Move Parent Task to Pay in Full Section
5. Update due date to the day after Resident indicates they will pay

## Pay in Full Default

On task due date, confirm payment has been made as planned.

**If not paid in full:**


1. Send "Payment Plan / Pay in Full Default Notification" via Buildium
2. Document in Parent task comments
3. Update Status to Default
4. Update due date to the **22nd**
