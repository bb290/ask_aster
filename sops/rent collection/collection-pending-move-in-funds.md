---

## Key Points

* **Cross-reference the ledger**, if the lease was created within the past 10 days, this is likely a false positive from normal move-in payment timing
* **Move task** to the Move In / Move Outs section in the Collection project
* **Monitor every 2 days**, the balance will likely resolve as move-in funds clear
