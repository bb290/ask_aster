Communication with the Resident should be done via call, text and email simultaneously until one channel reaches the Resident. Each attempt to communicate with the Resident should be documented in Asana, calling out the actions taken.

## Frequency & Time of Day

* Outreach to the Resident must be done weekly
* Mix up the time of day, sometimes Residents cannot pick up calls at work. PM can consider making calls over the weekend or between 5PM - 7PM
* If Resident does not respond, continue weekly follow up

## Call / Text Protocol

* Always call twice in a row to punch through do not disturb
* Follow up with text if no answer

## Phone Script, Outstanding Balance Resolution

> This is \[Name\] calling from Sagareus Property Management regarding the outstanding balance on your account.
> 
> , Wait for response,
> 
> Pay in full? Or a payment plan?
> 
> **Push toward Payment Plan if making this month payment will result in next month being late as well**

## Text Template, Outstanding Balance Follow-Up

> Hi \[Resident Name\], this is \[Name\] from Sagareus Property Management. We've been trying to reach you regarding an outstanding balance on your account. We'd like to help you resolve this, you can pay in full or set up a payment plan. Please call or text us back at your earliest convenience. Thank you.
