> **Situation:** Resident has been making regular payments, but account still shows overdue. The cause is a duplicate charge at lease creation.


---

## Key Points

* **Check lease creation entries first**, scroll all the way to the bottom of the ledger and confirm the first entries
* **Look for duplicate charges**, example: full rent charged on May 1, 2024 AND prorated May rent due on May 21, 2024. May rent is double entered.
* **Fix by deleting the duplicate**, in this case, delete the full rent amount due on May 1, 2024

## Procedure


1. Scroll to the bottom of the ledger
2. Confirm the first entries on the ledger
3. Identify the double entry
4. Delete one of the duplicate charges
