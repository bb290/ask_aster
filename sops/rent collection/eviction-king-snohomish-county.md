LtServices is the eviction attorney for **all regions outside of Pierce County**.

## Prepare Intake


1. Complete LT Services Intake Form, Fill out intake using the latest LT Services Intake Template. **DO NOT use local copy.**
2. Prepare LtServices Google Drive folder:
   * Completed LT Services intake form
   * Deed
   * Signed PMA
   * Latest ledger
   * Backing utility bills since last zero balance
