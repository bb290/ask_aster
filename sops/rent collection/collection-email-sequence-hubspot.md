Email sequence is a series of 3 emails sent over 6 days.

If Resident responds to an email OR completes the form on Late Rent webpage, they are automatically unenrolled from the sequence.

**Team must manually un-enroll when:**

* Resident communicates resolution via text, phone call or responds to a different staff member email
* Resident pays balance with no communication

Be sure to enroll **all Residents on the lease**.

## To Enroll in Sequence


1. Open HubSpot
2. Automations -> Sequences -> Eviction Prevention -> Enroll Contacts
3. Paste email address to search
4. Most Residents are already in HubSpot

## Sequence Timeline


1. **Initial Payment Reminder**, Auto sent from Buildium
2. **Let's work together**, 2 days later, after enrolled in HubSpot sequence
3. **We're Here to Help Email**, auto sent 2 days later if no response
4. **Please Respond to Prevent Eviction!**, auto sent 2 days later if no response
