## Post Eviction

### Limited Dissemination Order

Many evictions include a Limited Dissemination Order, which means Landlord cannot collect outstanding balance.

To ensure balance is not sent to 3rd party collection:

* Credit existing ledger the outstanding balance to $0
* Memo = Eviction | Limited Dissemination Order


---

## Vacated | Payment Plans or 3rd Party Collections

Residents continue payment plan post move out.

* Move task to "Vacated | Payment Plan or 3rd Party Collection"
* Status = Payment Plan **or** 3rd Party Collection

**Follow same Payment Plan Procedures:**

* Check payment made on the **21st** of every month
* **If payment made:** Note comment, update due date to next month
* **If no payment made:** Send default notice, then 3rd party collections

## 3rd Party Collection with Hunter Warfield

### Balance Due? Send to 3rd Party Collections Subtask

Subtask of Process Deposit. When a Resident vacates, Operations has 3 weeks to send final statement.

* Task assigned to Admin Team - Collections Specialist the 1st Monday of the following month
* Typically allows Residents ~2 weeks to pay debt in full

**If no payment or communication is received -> Initiate 3rd Party Collections:**


:::warning
Lease must be ended in Buildium. When you do this correctly, Residents do NOT show as active and their info shows up on Hunter Warfield website.

:::


1. Log into Hunter Warfield website (Check 1Password for Login details)
2. Click Claim Center
3. Click first option "Resident Search and Placement"
4. Search the Resident or property to find the record you want to send to collections
5. Click "Place Account" button
6. In pop up screen, upload Lease Agreement
7. Check certification box & click "Place for Collections"
8. It will say pending for a few days; team receives confirmation email when the account has been placed
