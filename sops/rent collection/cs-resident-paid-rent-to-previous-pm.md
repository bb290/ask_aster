> **Situation:** Resident claims they already paid rent to the previous property manager before Sagareus took over management.


---

## Key Points

* **Verification required** — Get proof of payment (date, amount, receipt, bank statement) from the Resident
* **Confirm with owner** — Cross-reference with the owner to validate the Resident's claim
* **If confirmed** — Add credit to ledger; set Credit Action to "Issue credit to waive unpaid charges" with memo "Rent received by previous PM"
* **If not confirmed** — Escalate


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Request for Proof of Payment // \[Address\]
>
> Thanks for your message. To help us verify your claim of rent payment to the previous manager, can you please provide the following details:
> • Date(s) of payment
> • Amount(s) paid
> • Who the payment was made to
> • Proof of payment (receipt, bank statement, or screenshot)
>
> Once received, we'll confirm with the property owner and add the credit to your ledger.

## Action


1. Verify Resident's claim
   * Get proof of payment — date and amount
   * Confirm with the owner if Resident's claim is correct
2. **IF claim is confirmed:**
   * Add credit to the Resident's ledger
   * Set Credit Action to: "Issue credit to waive unpaid charges"
   * Memo should indicate "Rent received by previous PM"
3. **ELSE:**
   * Escalate
