> **Situation:** Resident appears in collections but has already moved out of the property. Admin Team missed updating Buildium when they processed the move out.


---

## Key Points

* **Admin Team missed the update**, Buildium wasn't updated when the move-out was processed, so charges continued
* **Confirm the move-out**, check Leasing Project for the Move Out Subtask confirming the move-out date
* **Remove incorrect charges**, delete all charges after the confirmed move-out date


---

## Procedure


1. Check Leasing Project, there should be a Leasing Task with Move Out Subtask, confirming the move-out date
2. If unsure, confirm with Admin Team Lead (Katrina)
3. Remove incorrect charges, example: Resident moved out April 30, Delete May charges
