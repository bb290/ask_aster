> **Situation:** Resident or team member asks about rent check pick-up service, or a team member is asked to pick up a rent check from a Resident.


---

## Key Points

* **Discontinued** — effective June 1, 2025, Sagareus no longer offers staff pick-up of rent payments
* **Late fee reminder** — rent is due on the 1st, considered late after 5:00 PM on the 5th. Late fees auto-applied at 5:01 PM on the 5th.
* **Alternative methods available** — EFT (free), credit/debit card (fees apply), in-person drop-off, or mail

## Resident Inquiry

### Email Template — Rent Check Pick Up Discontinued

> Effective June 1 2025, Sagareus will no longer be offering staff pick-up of rent payment. All rent payments must be made using one of the available methods outlined below.
>
> As a reminder, rent is due on the 1st of each month and considered late after 5:00 PM on the 5th. Late fees are automatically applied at 5:01 PM on the 5th.
>
> **Rent Payment Options**
>
> **Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT)**
> - Login to the Resident Portal
> - No additional cost
> - Most secure and reliable method
> - Option to set up recurring automatic payments
>
> **Credit/Debit Card**
> - Login to the Resident Portal
> - Additional fees apply: $25 + 2.5% processing fee
> - Option to set up recurring automatic payments
>
> **In-Person Drop-Off**
> - Deliver check, cashier's check, or money order to: Sagareus Real Estate, 2265 116th Ave NE, Suite #200-8, Bellevue, WA 98004
> - 24/7 drop-off available via the labeled exterior mailbox
> - Must be received on or before the due date to be considered on time
> - Residents are responsible for transportation and money order costs
>
> **Mail Payment**
> - Send check or money order to the address above
> - Must be received on or before the due date to avoid late fees
> - Mail delays or losses are the Resident's sole responsibility
> - We do not recommend this method due to high risk and potential late fee
>
> Sincerely,
