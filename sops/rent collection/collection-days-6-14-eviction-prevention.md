## Days 1-5 | Catch Up + 3rd Party Collections

Light week:

* Convert lingering accounts to Payment Plans
* Send accounts to 3rd Party Collections
* Finish / Follow Up on Eviction Paperwork
* Review SOPs

## Days 6-14 | Eviction Prevention

**6th of month**

* Auto reminder email goes out via Buildium
* Asana / Buildium Sync + Initial Task Assignments

**7th of month**

Once Buildium is synced with Asana, Collections team will be assigned a batch of tasks:

* Click thru each task to double check ledger / communication updates
* Check all Payment Plan accounts have paid on time
* Process task influx, identify / remove issues
* Update tasks with notes

**IF balance is unpaid & no communication is documented:**

* Enroll in Collection // Email Sequence (HubSpot)
* Assign F/U contact task to Collections Assistant
* Begin Collection // Follow Up Calls & Texts

## Eviction Prevention Documentation

Document every communication & communication attempt in the Collections **Parent task** (not the individual subtasks).
