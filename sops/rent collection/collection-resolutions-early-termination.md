At any point, Resident may communicate they will vacate property voluntarily. This is the best option for all parties if Residents simply cannot pay balance. Security deposit applied to balances at move out.


---

## If Early Termination Option is Decided

Click Early Termination subtasks:


1. **Send Early Termination Addendum** to Resident via PandaDoc, Assign to self and send Addendum
2. **Receive Early Termination Addendum**, Assign to self, follow up daily until addendum is signed
3. **Send Notice to Vacate** to mgmt@sagareus.com, This will initiate Leasing Project to include standard move out, owner communication, turn over, lease up & security deposit
4. Mark Early Termination task complete

## Update Parent Task

* PENDING EARLY TERMINATION
* Leave in Eviction Prevention Section
* Update due date to 1 day after scheduled move out

## Confirm Resident Vacated as Planned

* If yes, mark task complete
* If no, begin Eviction Subtask
