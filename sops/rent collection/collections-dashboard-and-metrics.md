This document explains the collections metrics tracked on the analytics dashboard.

:::

## What the Dashboard Tracks

* What is the total outstanding balance across all properties?
* How many active collection tasks are in progress?
* How are balances distributed across collection stages?
* Is the total balance trending up or down compared to last year?


---

## KPI Definitions

### Total Outstanding Balance
Sum of all outstanding tenant balances across managed properties.

### Active Collection Tasks
Count of open tasks in the Collections 2.0 Asana project.


---

## Collection Stages

| Stage | What It Means |
|-------|---------------|
| **Need Resolution** | New or unresolved balances awaiting initial contact |
| **Move In/Out** | Balances associated with move-in or move-out transitions |
| **Pay in Full** | Tenant has agreed to pay the full balance |
| **Payment Plans** | Tenant is on an active payment plan |
| **Evictions** | Balance has escalated to eviction proceedings |
