At any point, Resident may communicate they would like to enroll in payment plan to get caught up.

When a Resident agrees to a payment plan, it's very important they clearly understand the exact amount we expect them to pay by when:

* They need to pay their regular rent / utilities / etc on time each month **&** the additional payment by the **3rd Friday** of each month
* Eviction Prevention team verifies payment plan payments on the **21st** of each month


---

## If Payment Plan Option is Decided

Click Payment Plan subtasks:


1. Prepare + send Payment Plan Agreement
2. Follow up until signed
3. Receive signed Payment Plan
4. Remove Resident from HubSpot sequence
5. Send "Payment Plan Agreement Signed" Owner Update Email

**Once payment plan has been signed:**


1. Mark Payment Plan Task Complete
2. Update Status Field to Payment Plan
3. Move Parent Task to Payment Plan Section
4. Update due date to the **21st** of each month

## Preparing Payment Plan

Buildium -> Lease Profile -> Add Addendum -> Choose Payment Plan Template

## Modify Lease Ledger to Reflect Payment Plan

* Enter a credit for total plan amount
* Enter corresponding charges on the payment due dates on the ledger
* Credit and charges net zero
