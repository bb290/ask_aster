## 21st, Payment Plan Default

On the **21st** of each month, confirm payment has been made as planned.

**If payment has been made:**

* Note comment, Payment paid
* Remove late fee if applied, Account is compliant
* Update task due date for the 7th of the month

**If payment not made:**


1. Send "Default Notification | Payment Plan / Pay in Full" via Buildium
2. Document in Parent task comments
3. Update Status to Default
4. Move to Evictions Section
5. Update due date to the 22nd


---

## 22nd, Start Evictions

On or after the **22nd** of each month, every account should be in Pay In Full, Payment Plan, or Early Termination.

**All accounts labelled Default or Needs Resolution:**


1. Check ledger for any last minute payments
2. Update status to Eviction
3. Move to Evictions Section
4. Execute Eviction subtasks
5. Send to Manager for Review
6. F/U with attorney every 2 days until Formal POV served to Residents
7. Add the tenant to the do not deposit list


---

## Block Partial Payment

Once eviction is started, landlord can no longer accept partial payments.


1. Notify accounting@sagareus.com to not deposit check from Resident
2. Update Resident portal to only allow full payment


---

## Clean Up the Ledger

Eviction proceedings prevent certain charges from being passed through. We need to scrub the ledger so it is ready for eviction:

* Remove all late & insurance fees
* Confirm all the backing utility pass-throughs have been paid
* Confirm pass-through amount actually matches the backing utility bills


---

## Eviction File Review

Before sending to the attorney, the Manager reviews every eviction file:

* Verify tenant ledger
* Verify collection efforts exhausted
* Confirm no pending responses from the tenant that require action (DV, Abandonment, Litigious response)
* Special consideration from the owner
