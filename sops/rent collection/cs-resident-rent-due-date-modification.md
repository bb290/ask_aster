> **Situation:** Resident requests to change their rent due date.


---

## Key Points

* **Good payment history required** — Check if Resident has a good payment record before accommodating
* **Maximum extension** — Can accommodate up to the 10th of each month
* **Poor payment history** — Rent is due on the 1st with a grace period until the 5th; no modification available


---

## Procedure


1. Check if Resident has good payment record
2. **IF YES** — OK to accommodate up to 10th of each month
3. **IF NO** — Let Resident know rent is due on the 1st, they have until the 5th to pay

### Example Resident Questions

* "Can I change my rent due date?"
* "I get paid on the 10th, can I pay then?"
* "Is there flexibility on when rent is due?"
