## Annual Inspections Lifecycle

### To be Assigned


:::info
Sagareus automation will run daily to inspect the [Property Maintenance](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1211134623744906/list/1211134624931958) project. Any unit with **Annual Inspection Completed** >= one year will trigger creation of an Annual Inspection task in the [Annual Inspection](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1205841857464078/list/1205843370399109) project.

:::

* Assignment: (Annual Inspection Lead)
* Annual Inspection Lead will review weekly to assign inspector by setting the **Inspector** field:
  * Leasing team members - 8 annual inspections assigned
  * Ops team members - 15 annual inspections assigned

### Assigned

* Inspector will:
  1. Coordinate day / time for inspection directly with Residents
  2. Complete their assigned inspections by the last day of the month
  3. Find coverage if overloaded or unavailable
  4. Or document reasons for failure to complete inspection
* Monitors progress, ensures Annual Inspections are being completed

### Post Inspection

* (Annual Inspection Coordinator)
  1. Review annual maintenance record
  2. Provide summary for approval by (Annual Inspection Lead)
  3. Input needed repairs / maintenance
  4. Send owner debrief email
  5. Update maintenance record
* Admin Team
  * Bills for inspection

## Schedule / Confirm / Complete Annual Inspection


:::info
**Assignment:** Inspector

* Leasing team members
* Field team members

:::

### Schedule


1. Contact all the residents using email, group text and/or calling
2. Always **log a quick update in the task's comment section**
3. Once appointment date/time confirmed with Resident:
   * Document scheduled appointment
   * Update due date to the task to T-1 day from inspection date to send reminder
   * Block the time on your calendar


:::warning
**IF resident does not respond after 5 outreaches:**

* IF you are a leasing agent, re-assign the task to Ops Manager
* Task will be re-assigned to a Field Tech to follow Inspection with notice of entry

:::

#### Email Template — Scheduling Annual Inspection with Residents

> **Subject: (Response Required) Time for Your Annual Inspection // [Property Address]**
>
> Hi [Resident Name],
>
> My name is [Your Name], I'm reaching out on behalf of Sagareus Group, your property management team, to schedule your **annual property inspection**. It takes **15-20 minutes**.
>
> Please reply with your preferred time slot, or propose 2 alternatives that work for your schedule.
>
> **Scheduling Options**
> [Day / Time]
> [Day / Time]
>
> **Why we inspect (once per year)**
> Identify maintenance needs, ensure lease compliance, and protect the property's long-term condition.
>
> **What we check**
> - Plumbing fixtures (including under-sink leak check)
> - Appliances
> - Safety features (smoke & CO detectors)
> - Exterior (roof, gutters, landscaping, large trees/overgrowth)
>
> **Annual Inspection Photography Policy**
> We respect your privacy: no photos of personal items or irrelevant areas. Photos are internal documentation only and never used for marketing.
>
> Thank you,

#### Text Template — Scheduling Annual Inspection with Residents

> Hi [Resident Name], this is [Your Name] with Sagareus Group. It's time to schedule your annual property inspection at [Property Address]. It only takes about 15-20 mins. Please reply with your preferred time, or send 2 options that work best for you.

### Reminder

* Set to T-1 day from inspection
* Send a text to Resident reminding them of scheduled appointment day / time

### Complete Annual Inspection


1. Open Happy Inspector App on Phone
2. Click the "+" icon
3. Choose Start a New Inspection
4. Search Property Address from list
5. Choose Annual Inspection
6. Conduct Inspection by following Template

#### Section 1: Resident Interview

* Explain purpose of inspection & photo policy

#### Section 2: Exterior

* Exterior Conditions, photos optional
* Landscaping | Photos Required
* Roof / Gutter Condition | Photos Required

#### Section 3: Interior

* Photos of the labels inside all appliances
* Single photo of cabinets underneath sinks
* Test **all ventilation fans**
* Test **all** smoke alarms and carbon monoxide detectors

#### Generating Annual Inspection Report


1. Click the paper+ icon in the upper righthand corner
2. Choose Standard Report
3. Click Finalize & Sign

## Annual Inspections Policies (Owner Facing)


:::warning
Sagareus conducts a **required Annual Inspection** of each property under management to ensure proactive maintenance, verify lease compliance, and protect the long-term value of your investment.

* **Inspection Fee:** Fee Schedule
* **Frequency:** Once per year
* **Scheduling:** Based on the **move-in date of the most recent lease**
* **Scope:** Includes a visual review of interior condition, safety features, and exterior areas
* **Reporting:** Owners receive a detailed report

:::

## Annual Property Inspection Policy (Resident Facing)


:::warning
To maintain the quality and safety of your home, Sagareus conducts a **required Annual Inspection** of every property we manage.

:::

**What to Expect:**

* **Timing:** Scheduled once per year, based on the most recent lease's move-in date
* **Who:** A Sagareus team member will complete the inspection
* **Resident Presence Required:** Residents must be present for this inspection to ensure access and transparency


:::warning
Per **RCW 59.18.150**, Residents may not unreasonably withhold consent for a landlord to enter the unit for inspection purposes.

:::
