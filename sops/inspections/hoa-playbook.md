Sagareus manages properties governed by a variety of Homeowners Associations (HOAs). Each HOA operates independently and maintains its own rules, move-in procedures, approval requirements, and amenity policies. Because HOA requirements vary by property, they must be reviewed and followed on a case-by-case basis.

The purpose of this HOA Playbook is to centralize HOA-specific requirements and provide clear, consistent guidance for managing HOA-governed properties.


:::info
This playbook:

* Serves as a single source of truth for HOA requirements
* Reduces reliance on informal knowledge and one-off explanations
* Ensures consistent execution across all teams
* Protects Sagareus from compliance violations, fines, and reputational risk

:::


:::warning
**Playbooks Prevent Problems**

* Missed or delayed move-in approvals
* Tenants being denied access to amenities due to incomplete registration
* Violations of HOA rules resulting in fines or owner complaints
* Inconsistent messaging across teams
* Last-minute scrambles caused by missing HOA forms, fees, or approvals

:::

## Ownership & Responsibility

**Primary Owner:** Administrative Coordinator

**Secondary Owner:** Administrative Team Lead


:::info
**Initial Setup**

* Initial HOA Playbook input is completed during New Client Onboarding.
* All available HOA documents, contacts, move-in procedures, and amenity requirements are captured at onboarding and linked in the HOA Docs shared drive.

:::

**Ongoing Maintenance Procedure**

* HOA requirements change from time to time
* Any team member who becomes aware of outdated, missing, or incorrect HOA information is responsible for initiating an update by creating a subtask.

**Asana Subtask Requirements**

* **Title:** HOA Playbook Update
* **Assignee:** Administrative Coordinator
* **Due Date:** Same day
* **Description:** Include all relevant details, source of the update, and any urgency or downstream impact


:::warning
Ownership of accuracy does not transfer simply because information was shared verbally or via email. If it is not updated in the HOA Playbook, it is not considered resolved.

:::

## Playbook Input & Maintenance

**Adding a Property to the HOA Playbook**


1. Copy and paste the HOA Playbook template to create a new property entry
2. Create a subfolder and link it to the shared **HOA Docs** drive containing all applicable forms, rules, and regulations

**Obtain HOA Requirements**


1. Contact the HOA directly to request move-in instructions and amenity registration details
2. Follow up up to 3 times if no response is received
3. If the HOA remains unresponsive, loop in the property owner for assistance

### Update & Maintain HOA Information


:::warning
It is critical that the Playbook reflects the most current information available.

:::

**When Updates Are Required**

* A new rule, fee, form, or approval requirement is identified
* Move-in procedures or amenity access rules change
* HOA contact information is updated
* An issue arises that reveals outdated or incorrect HOA information

**How Updates Are Made**

* Update the property's HOA Playbook entry immediately upon confirmation
* Upload revised documents to the **HOA Docs** shared drive

## Email Template: HOA Intro

> **Subject:** <<Building>> HOA – Leasing Details Needed for <<Property Address>>
>
> Hi there,
>
> Sagareus Property Management represents the owner of <<Property Address>> (PMA attached). We're reaching out to confirm the HOA's leasing requirements and amenity access for this unit.
>
> **Move-In & Leasing Requirements**
> Please provide the following:
>
> - Any rental restrictions, rules, or required HOA forms
> - Lease approval or registration process
> - Move-in fees and instructions for submitting payment
> - Move-in procedures, including any orientation steps or elevator reservations
>
> **Amenities Available to Tenants**
> Please confirm which amenities tenants may access, such as:
> - Parking or garage details
> - Storage lockers
> - Fitness center / community rooms
> - Package lockers or concierge services
> - Roof deck, courtyard, or common outdoor areas
> - Any additional building-specific amenities
>
> For your records, here is our contact information for this unit:
>
> **Sagareus Property Management**
> mgmt@sagareus.com
>
> Thank you in advance for your help.
