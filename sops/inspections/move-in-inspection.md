## Prevent & Resolve Disputes


:::warning
The Move In Inspection is critical for reducing disputes & fostering a positive Resident relationship.

:::


:::info
Move In Inspections are completed on Move In Date with at least 1 Resident (or designated representative) are present.


1. Only Leasing Team is trained to complete Move In Inspections
2. Resident must sign the Move In Condition Report

:::


---

## Establish Property's Condition at Move In


:::info
The inspection provides a detailed record of the property's condition before a Resident moves in. This helps differentiate between pre-existing issues and Resident-caused damage when they move out.


1. Accurate records protect the Owner from costly repairs / replacements that are directly Resident damage (beyond normal wear & tear)
2. Accurate records protect the Resident from unnecessary deductions from their security deposit.

:::


---

## Reduces Security Deposit Disputes


:::tip
* A clear and documented inspection helps justify deductions or full refunds of security deposits.
* Without it, Residents may dispute charges, leading to potential legal issues.

:::


---

## Protects the Property Owner's Investment


:::tip
* Keeping accurate records ensures accountability and helps maintain the long-term value of the property.
* Proper documentation can support claims in case of legal disputes.

:::
