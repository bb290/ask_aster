# What is Multifamily Inspection?


:::info
Multifamily inspection is about:

* Deep understanding about the inner working of our buildings
* Frequent inspection to assess condition of the buildings
* Processing the inspection report & take action
* Communicate the inspection to the owner

:::

**Which properties are in the program?**

* Any building exceeding 4 units or has shared common amenities

## Frequent Inspections


:::info

* The last multifamily inspection date is tracked in the [Property Maintenance](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1211134623744906/list/1211134624931958) project in Asana:

## Send Report to Owner


1. Prep an email to the owner using the sample below
2. Attach the draft to the task and assign for review
3. Send report to the owner with the following email template:

> **Subject: Quarterly Inspection Completed // [Address] // [YYYY-MM]**
> **Attachment: Inspection report**
>
> Hi Owners,
>
> Sagareus has completed the quarterly inspection at [Address]. The report is attached for your records. Our team is tracking the few routine items noted and will coordinate any follow-up maintenance.
>
> Thank you,
