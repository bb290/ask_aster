## Income Verification Policies + Procedures

**All income verification documentation must be sent to leasing@sagareus.com.**

To qualify for a Sagareus-managed home, applicants must demonstrate stable, recurring, and legally sourced income that meets the minimum income threshold for the specific home they are applying for. Total household income must be verifiable and must collectively meet or exceed the required income tier for that property.

### Sagareus Fair Housing Policy

Sagareus Real Estate does not discriminate based on Race, Color, Creed, National Origin, Sex, Sexual Orientation, Gender Identity, Disability, Marital Status, HIV/AIDS or Hepatitis C Status, Families with children, Use of Dog Guide or Service Animal, Honorably Discharged Veteran or Military Status, Retaliation, Immigration or Citizenship status.

### Income Requirement Tiers

**Lenient: 2.0x Monthly Rent** — Low income / Accessible housing.

**Standard: 2.5x Monthly Rent (Default)** — Most Sagareus properties.

**Stringent: 3.0x Monthly Rent** — High-end properties, premium neighborhoods.

### Documentation Standards & Verification

All income documents submitted must meet Sagareus' verification standards. Documents must:

* Be clear, legible, and complete
* Match the applicant's full legal identity
* Reflect authentic, unaltered records
* Be verifiable through an employer, agency, financial institution, or official platform
* Include sufficient detail for income calculation

### Acceptable Documents for Income Verification

#### Employment Income

Sagareus uses an average of the last 2 months' gross income.

Sagareus accepts the following forms:

* Paystubs from the last 2 months
* Signed offer letter for new or pending employment

#### Housing Vouchers & Subsidies

Sagareus accepts all lawful sources of rental assistance, including federal, state, and local housing subsidy programs:

* Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers
* Tenant-Based Rental Assistance (TBRA)
* Rapid Rehousing (RRH)
* VA VASH vouchers
* Sound Families / Solid Ground programs
* Catholic Community Services rental assistance

##### Calculating Income Ratio

**Formula:** Gross Monthly Income ÷ Tenant Portion of Rent = Income Ratio

#### Self-Employed / Small Business Owner

Self-employed or 1099 applicants must submit:

* Previous year's full federal tax return
* Business tax returns, if applicable
* Bank statements may be requested

#### Variable / Gig / Contract Work

Sagareus uses 70% of the average gross income from the most recent 2 months.

#### Assets in Lieu of Income

Applicants who fall short of the required income multiplier may qualify using liquid assets.

#### Court-Ordered Income

Sagareus accepts verified court-ordered payments:

* Child Support
* Spousal Support / Alimony
* Structured Settlement Payments

#### Education-Related Income

* Graduate Researcher (RA) Stipends
* Teaching Assistant (TA) Stipends
* Fellowship or Academic Scholarship Stipends
* Vocational training stipends

#### Trust Fund Disbursements

Sagareus accepts recurring trust fund income when it is verifiable, liquid, and guaranteed for a minimum of 12 months.

#### Long-Term Disability (LTD) Income

Sagareus accepts LTD benefits as income when they are verifiable, recurring, and expected to continue for at least 12 months from the date of application.
