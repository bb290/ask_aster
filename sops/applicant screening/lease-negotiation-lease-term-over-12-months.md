> **Situation:** An applicant requests a lease term longer than 12 months.

:::warning
Per Washington State law ([RCW 59.18.210](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.210)), any residential lease that exceeds one year must be signed in the presence of a notary.
:::

## Key Points

- **Allowed, but not automatic** — a longer term requires Leasing Manager approval. The Leasing Manager determines whether it is in the client's best interest.
- **Notary required** — leases over 12 months must be signed before a notary, per RCW 59.18.210.

## Procedure

1. Request approval from the Leasing Manager.
2. Communicate the result to the applicant using the matching template below.
3. **IF approved** — send a revised lease: change the dates to the new term and add the Notary Page via the PandaDoc Content Library.

### Verification Before Drafting

- Reference the most recent listing and LU Task notes to confirm lease terms.
- If the property has been de-listed and the latest listing is unavailable:
  - Send a Lease Term Confirmation email to verify details before drafting (prevents unnecessary revisions). Use the Lease Confirmation Email Template in the Email Templates tab.
  - Include all Residents on the lease and CC the Leasing Manager, Team Lead, and Leasing Agent on all communications.

## Email Template — Not Approved

:::warning
**Subject:** Lease Term Request – [Property Address]

Hi [Applicant Name],

Thank you for your request for a [XX]-month lease at [Property Address].

We've reviewed your request with the property owner, and unfortunately, they are not able to approve a lease term longer than 12 months at this time.

We'd be happy to proceed with the standard 12-month lease term if you'd still like to move forward. Please confirm, and we'll prepare the lease documents right away.

We appreciate your understanding and look forward to finalizing next steps with you.
:::

## Email Template — Approved

:::info
**Subject:** Confirmation of Lease Term for [Property Address]

Hi [Applicant Name],

Our Leasing Manager has approved your request for an [XX] month lease.

Per Washington State law ([RCW 59.18.210](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.210)), leases longer than 12 months must be signed before a notary.

Please confirm if you would like to proceed with the [XX]-month lease term. Once confirmed, we will update the lease documents to reflect this term and include a notary page for signing.

We look forward to your confirmation so we can finalize next steps.
:::
