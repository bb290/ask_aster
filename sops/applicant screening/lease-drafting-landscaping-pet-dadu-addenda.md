The smaller lease sections and addenda to check on every draft.

## Landscaping Check Box

1. The landscaping policy is whatever was advertised on the listing.
2. Check the appropriate box: Landlord responsible or Resident responsible.

## Pet Addendum

1. Even if the Resident has no pet at move in, the addendum must reflect the pet policy for the property.
   - This tells the Resident whether pets are allowed, the weight limit, and the pet rent if they get a pet later.

## DADU Addendum

:::info
DADU means "Detached Auxiliary Dwelling Unit." Occasionally a property has plans to build an additional unit, primarily in Seattle and Burien. These are advertised with HouseHack Seattle, a brokerage team specializing in these properties.
:::

[Video: DADUs + DADU Addendum Explained](https://www.loom.com/share/26849774289f43c8b9006e64f6e6d58a)

1. Residents moving into the existing unit will lose a portion of the property and experience a construction period during the tenancy.
2. The Leasing Team discloses these plans on the listing and during showings.
3. A signed DADU Addendum is required so the Resident acknowledges the plans.
   - It includes a rent reduction during construction, usually 6 to 8 weeks.
   - The rent credit offered is whatever was advertised in the listing.
4. Add the DADU Addendum to the lease via the PandaDoc **Content Library**.
