How to complete the Utility Addendum so every account is assigned and billed correctly.

## Assigning Accounts

1. **Accounts paid by Resident directly to the utility company** — check off the accounts the Resident will pay to the company directly.
2. **Accounts paid by Resident to Landlord** — these are the accounts managed and paid by Sagareus.
   - **Sagareus always manages water and sewer accounts.**

Residents are billed for Sagareus-managed accounts in one of two ways:

## Billing Practices

:::info
**Pass thru** — the actual cost is added to the Resident's ledger as received.
:::

:::info
**Flat fee** — the Resident is charged the same amount every month regardless of the actual bill cost.

If a utility is included in rent, choose the flat fee option and enter $0 for the amount.
:::

## Examples

### Resident Responsible for All Utilities

1. Paid directly to utility company: electricity, garbage, gas, internet.
2. Paid to Landlord: water, sewer.
3. Billing practice: pass thru.

### Resident Responsible for Electricity/Gas; $120 Flat Fee for Water/Sewer/Garbage

1. Paid directly to utility company: electricity, gas, internet.
2. Paid to Landlord: water, sewer, garbage.
3. Billing practice: flat fee, utility charge $120.

### All Utilities Included in Rent

1. Paid directly to utility company: internet.
2. Paid to Landlord: electricity, garbage, sewer, water, gas.
3. Billing practice: pass thru, utility charge $0.
