> **Situation:** A lease includes a move in bonus, or an applicant asks to apply their rent credit toward the security deposit or last month's rent.

:::warning
**Move In Bonus = Rent Credit.** Rent credits may only be applied toward rent. They cannot be applied to the security deposit or last month's rent deposit.
:::

## Key Points

- **Trust funds are not rent** — both the security deposit and last month's rent deposit are tenant-held trust funds under Washington State law. They must be held in a separate trust account and remain the legal property of the Resident until properly disbursed at move-out.
- **A rent credit is a temporary rent reduction** — not a transfer of trust funds, so it cannot legally or operationally be applied to deposits.
- **Overflow rolls forward** — if the move-in credit exceeds the first month's prorated rent, the remaining credit applies to the next month's rent.
- **Deposits are still due in full at move-in** — Residents pay the full security deposit and last month's rent deposit on the normal schedule.
- **Payment plans remain available** — Sagareus may approve up to six (6) equal installments for the security deposit and/or last month's rent deposit if requested and documented before move-in. See [Lease Negotiation // Payment Plan for Move In Costs](lease-negotiation-payment-plan-for-move-in-costs.md).
