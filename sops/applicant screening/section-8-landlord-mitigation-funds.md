The [Landlord Mitigation Fund](https://www.commerce.wa.gov/landlord-fund/landlord-damage-relief/) is administered by the Dept of Commerce. Sagareus Statewide Vendor (SWV) Number is **SWV0281476-00**.

:::

**Documents required for damage and/or rent loss claims:**

* **Sagareus Vendor Number: SWV0281476**
* **An executed written Rental Agreement between the landlord and the Resident(s).**
* **A Rental Assistance Agreement (or adequate proof of housing assistance).**
* **A completed** [**Move-In Condition Report (PDF)**](https://deptofcommerce.box.com/s/furnk92dsyrm7ifewv1r7t92bfbf9a2a) **(this is not a Rental Assistance Inspection Report) signed and dated by both Landlord and Resident(s).**
* **A Resident ledger showing any unpaid portion of rent and other charges.**
* **Copies of all repair invoices for damages that are included on the claim.**
* **Complete the entire** [**claim submission form**](https://secureaccess.wa.gov/myAccess/saw/select.do)**.**
