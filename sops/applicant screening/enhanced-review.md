## Enhanced Applicant Review


:::warning
The purpose of Enhanced Applicant Review is to further verify applicant identity.

:::


:::info
If any flags are identified on application or supporting documents, assign enhanced review subtasks.

:::


1. If any flags are identified on application or supporting documents:
   1. Assign enhanced review subtasks:
      1. Send Email to Applicant <Leasing Assistant>
      2. Upload Copy of Photo ID <Leasing Assistant>
      3. Upload copy of last 2 month bank statement <Leasing Assistant>
      4. Social Media Audit <Leasing Manager>


---

### Send email to applicant requesting additional documents


1. This email initiates Enhanced Application Review with applicant & requests additional documents
   1. Includes the reason for the enhanced review
   2. Document this email to avoid discrimination claims


:::warning
Document this email to avoid discrimination claims.

:::

##### Enhanced Application Review Email Template

> Hi <Applicant Name>,
>
> Your application was flagged due to <Choose reason(s)> Mismatched SSN, Mismatched name / DOB, 1 or more accounts in collections, Mismatched Paystub, lack of rental history verification.
>
> For these reasons, we are requesting the following documents to complete your application:
>
> Copy of Photo ID - Driver's License, Passport, Military ID
>
> Last 2 month bank statements
>
> Please send these documents at your earliest convenience so we may continue processing your application.
>
> Best,


---

### Upload Copy of Photo ID / Work Visa


1. This provides additional confirmation of applicant identity
2. Acceptable documents include:
   1. Driver's License, State Issued ID, or Passport


---

### Upload Copy of last 2 Bank Statements


:::info
Applicants often push back on this item. Purpose is to verify previous address & further confirm identity.

:::


1. Applicants often push back on this item
2. Purpose is to verify previous address & further confirm identity


---

### Social Media Audit


:::info
Social media is not a determining factor in the application process, rather just an informal identity verification.

:::


1. Leasing Manager will attempt to find applicant on social media in effort to further confirm identity / explanation of flags on application.


---

### Assign for Approval


:::tip
Once all tasks are complete, assign Application Task to Leasing Manager for approval / lease negotiation.

:::
