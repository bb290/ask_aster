> **Situation:** An applicant asks whether Sagareus will accept a payment plan for move in costs.

:::warning
The answer is always **YES**. Accepting a payment plan for move in costs is required by law.
:::

## Key Points

- **Always yes** — this is a legal requirement, not a judgment call.
- **Up to 6 monthly payments** — the applicant is entitled to as many as 6 payment months, but Sagareus prefers to collect in fewer. Ask what terms work for them and negotiate down where possible.

## Procedure

1. Confirm yes, and ask what payment terms would work for them.
2. Set the plan:
   - Prorated first month rent is due within 48 hours of lease signing.
   - Payment 1 is due on the following 1st of the month.
3. Draft the lease with the Move In Funds Payment Plan: [video walkthrough](https://www.loom.com/share/fa03e87ac49f4b6593821a6c6e9b2a28).
4. Enter the payment plan into Buildium:
   - Split each monthly payment into 2 entries — one toward the security deposit, one toward last month rent.
   - A 6-month plan is therefore 12 separate Buildium entries.
