### Leasing Team Lead Procedures


1. Initial Scan → Review all documents
   * Look for inconsistencies or potential fraud; Anything that seems off
   * Proof of Income
     * Verify dates represent the most recent pay period
     * Critically examine → Do these documents seem suspicious at all?
   * Credit Screening
     * Confirm Experian Score
       * Disregard AI Score
