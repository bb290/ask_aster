> **Situation:** Applicant requests a refund of their rental application fee.


---

## Key Points

* **Non-refundable** — Application fee is non-refundable as disclosed on the first line of the application
* **Do not email Leasing Manager** — The answer is no; do not escalate
* **Persistent follow-up** — If they continue, direct to the Customer Service Request Form for management review


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Request for Application Fee Refund
>
> As disclosed on the 1st line of the [application](https://sagareus.managebuilding.com/Resident/rental-application/new/apply); The Application Fee is charged immediately and is non-refundable.

## Response — If They Follow Up Again

> **Subject:** RE: Request for Application Fee Refund
>
> I have explained the Application Fee Policy; I am unauthorized to make an exception to this policy. You may complete a [Customer Service Request Here](https://www.sagareus.com/contact-us) for management review.

## Action


1. **Do not email** it to the Leasing Manager — the answer is no
2. If they continue to follow up, use the second response template above

### Example Applicant Questions

* "I want my application fee back"
* "Can I get a refund on my application?"
* "I didn't get the apartment, refund my fee"
