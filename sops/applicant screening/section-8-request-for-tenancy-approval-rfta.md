## Overview

The Request for Tenancy Approval (RFTA) is the formal paperwork submitted to the housing authority to request approval for a Section 8 tenant to move into a specific property. Each housing authority has its own version of this form, and requirements vary.


---

## Required Information

The RFTA packet requires:


1. **Tenant Information:**
   * Names and birthdates of **all adults and children** who will be residing in the property
2. **Property Information:**
   * Address, unit details, proposed rent amount
3. **Owner / Management Company Information:**
   * Property Management Agreement between Owner & Sagareus
   * Sagareus W-9 Form
   * W-9 Form with Owner Name, address & SSN or EIN
     * Complete form on behalf of owner
     * Info available on Buildium, under Owner profile

## Submission Process


1. Complete the RFTA packet thoroughly — double-check all fields
2. Email the complete packet directly to the Housing Authority
3. **Do not send the packet directly to the Resident** — it contains sensitive owner and firm information
4. Instead, update the Resident that the packet has been sent to the Housing Authority
5. Upload a copy to the Section 8 subtask in Asana for records


:::warning
Due to sensitive owner & firm information, **do not** send the RFTA packet directly to the Resident. Always send directly to Housing Authority.

:::
