Where the lease template lives and the defaults to use unless the Asana task says otherwise.

## Lease Template

1. PandaDoc → Documents → Templates → (Folder) Lease + Move In Instructions
2. 2 Residents — **MAIN Sagareus Lease 2025**

## Default Move In Costs

Unless otherwise noted in Asana:

1. **Prorated first month rent** — due within 48 hours of lease signing.
2. **Security deposit** — equal to 1 month rent less $100.
3. **Last month rent.**
4. Remaining balance (after the prorated rent) is due at move in via cashier check or money order.

:::warning
**Tacoma has different rules:**

- Prorated rent due within 48 hours of lease signing.
- Security deposit (less $100) due at move in via cashier check or money order.
- **Sagareus cannot collect last month rent in Tacoma.**
:::

## Default Lease Dates

Unless otherwise noted in Asana:

1. **Start date** — as approved by the Leasing Manager.
2. **End date** — 12 months out, rounded down to the 15th or the last day of the month, so the lease is exactly or just under 12 full months.
   - Start March 11 → end February 28.
   - Start March 30 → end March 30.

Longer than 12 months requires Leasing Manager approval and a notary: [Lease Negotiation // Lease Term Over 12 Months](lease-negotiation-lease-term-over-12-months.md).

## Calculating Prorated Rent

1. **Determine the total monthly cost** — rent plus utility, pet, parking, and any other monthly fee.
2. (Total monthly cost × 12) ÷ 365 = daily rate.
3. Daily rate × number of days = prorated rent.
