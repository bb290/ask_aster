:::info
The **General Addendum** is a controlled, standardized method for documenting **property-specific, non-standard, or situational lease terms** that do not warrant a standalone addendum but must be disclosed and acknowledged in writing.
:::

It exists to ensure consistency across leases, reduced legal and compliance risk, clear Resident acknowledgment of unique conditions, and prevention of ad-hoc or informal lease modifications. It captures miscellaneous terms, property-specific disclosures, temporary permissions or restrictions, and clarifications not otherwise addressed in standard lease addenda.

## When to Use It

Use the General Addendum **only** when:

- A property has unique characteristics that must be disclosed
- Owner-provided items need clarification or limitation of liability
- Utilities or services require clarification beyond standard addenda
- There are limited-use permissions or restrictions
- Temporary agreements are being granted
- Non-standard conditions are acknowledged by the Resident

:::warning
**Do NOT use the General Addendum to:** override standard lease terms, introduce major policy changes, replace required disclosures, or address issues that require a dedicated addendum (Utilities, HOA, Mold, Lead-Based Paint).

If it is more than **one paragraph** or applies broadly across many properties, it likely needs its **own addendum**.
:::

## Examples by Category

### Property-Specific Disclosures

- Known building limitations (low water pressure, shared utilities, limited electrical capacity, non-conforming use)
- Seasonal conditions (flooding risk, snow removal limitations, leaf accumulation, coastal corrosion)
- Special access considerations (steep driveway, alley access only, limited street parking)

### Utilities & Services Clarifications

- Shared utility acknowledgment
- Owner-provided services (Wi-Fi, water, garbage, landscaping, snow removal, pest control)
- Resident responsibility clarifications for utilities or services not separately metered

### Owner-Provided Items (Not Warranted)

- Security cameras / doorbells, smart home devices
- Extra appliances (second fridge, garage freezer, wine fridge)
- Window AC units, portable heaters, fireplace accessories

Items listed are provided as a convenience and are not guaranteed, maintained, or replaced unless required by law.

### Use Restrictions or Permissions

- Limited use of garage, shed, basement, attic, or outbuildings
- Restrictions on commercial activity, storage, or equipment
- Approved but limited uses (home office only, no client traffic)

### HOA / Community Rules (Supplemental)

- Parking limitations, amenity use restrictions, move-in / move-out rules
- Fines passed through to the Resident if caused by the Resident

### Maintenance & Care Clarifications

- Yard care specifics (watering schedules, leaf removal, irrigation limitations)
- Septic / well usage guidelines
- Specialty surfaces (hardwood care, stone counters, specialty flooring)
- Drain, garbage disposal, or plumbing use restrictions

### Temporary or Time-Bound Agreements

Include start and end dates. Always.

- Temporary rent concessions
- Delayed repairs with disclosure
- Phased move-in access
- Short-term accommodations or exceptions

### Acknowledgment of Non-Standard Conditions

- Resident acknowledges acceptance of disclosed conditions
- Resident agrees conditions do not constitute a habitability issue
- Resident waives claims arising from disclosed limitations
