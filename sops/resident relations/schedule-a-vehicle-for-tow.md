> **Situation:** A vehicle needs to be towed from the property after failing to comply with parking rules or after enforcement deadlines have expired.


---

## Key Points

* **Create Asana task first**, Use the Tow Vehicle task template
* **Assign to Field Tech**, Northend = Christian, Southend = Dezy
* **Notice of Removal required**, Posted to vehicle window, then 3-day waiting period before towing
* **Document everything**, Photos, notices, and communications uploaded to Asana


---

## Procedure


1. Create Asana task
   * Task Name: Tow Vehicle // \[Address\]
   * TASK TEMPLATE: Tow Vehicle
2. Due Date = Today or following day
3. Assign to Field Tech
   * Northend = Christian
   * Southend = Dezy
4. Fill in description
5. Upload photos of vehicle for Field Tech + towing vendor
6. Convert to subtask of existing parking parent task
7. Update parent task due date to 7 days out to follow up

## Field Tech Subtasks


1. Post Notice of Removal to vehicle
   * Write date / time on Notice
   * Tape to Window with painters tape
   * Take picture / upload proof of notice posted
2. 3 days later
   * Call tow truck company / schedule vehicle to be towed
