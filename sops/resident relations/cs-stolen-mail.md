> **Situation:** Resident reports stolen mail or packages in the community.


---

## Key Points

* **File police report** — Advise Residents to file a police report for stolen mail/packages
* **Secure delivery alternatives** — Recommend UPS Access Point, Amazon Locker, or other package-holding services
* **Community notice** — Send notice to all Residents in the building if multiple reports received


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Important Notice | Mail Theft in the Community
>
> Dear Residents,
>
> We have received multiple reports of **recent mail theft** within the community. Please be aware that stolen mail and packages are unfortunately a common target, and we want to ensure you are informed and prepared.
>
> **Recommended Actions:**
> • If you believe any mail or packages have been stolen, please **file a police report** as soon as possible.
> • For future deliveries, we encourage you to explore secure delivery options such as **UPS Access Point, Amazon Locker, or other package-holding services** available in our area. These services allow you to pick up deliveries at a secure location and reduce the risk of theft.
>
> Your vigilance and prompt reporting help us protect the community. If you notice suspicious activity near mailboxes, please report it immediately to local authorities.
>
> Thank you for your attention and cooperation.
>
> Sincerely,
