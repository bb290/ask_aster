> **Situation:** Resident in a single-family property asks if they can repaint their unit.


---

## Key Points

* **Owner approval required** — Request must be forwarded to the property owner for review
* **Details needed** — Proposed paint colors (brand and color code), areas to paint, whether a professional painter will be used
* **Restore original color** — Tenant is required to restore walls to original color upon vacate; restoration costs will be passed through to the resident if done by the housing provider


---

## Resident Inquiry

> Thanks for checking in! We'll forward your request to the property owner for review and approval. Please include:
> • The proposed paint color(s) - brand and color code
> • The areas you'd like to paint
> • Whether you'll use a professional painter
>
> Please note that if approved, you will be required to restore the walls to the original color before move-out. If restoration is completed by the housing provider, the cost will be passed through to you.
>
> We'll follow up once we receive direction from the owner.
