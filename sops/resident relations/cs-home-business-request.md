> **Situation:** Resident asks if they can run a business from their rental unit.


---

## Key Points

* **Home offices always allowed** — Remote work, freelancing, consulting, and standard office work require no prior approval
* **High-impact businesses require written approval** — Daycares, pet sitting, catering, auto repair, hair salons, massage therapy, or similar client-based services
* **Why approval is required** — Affects neighbor parking/traffic, wear and tear on property systems, and liability/insurance coverage
* **Escalation** — If approval is required, escalate to [Leasing Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/leasing-manager.md)


---

## Response

> Thank you for checking in. Below is Sagareus Home Based Business Policy, pasted from our [Resident FAQ Page](https://www.sagareus.com/resident-faqs):
>
> **Home-Based Business Policy**
> We understand that many residents work from home. However, operating a business from your rental property must not disrupt the residential nature of the community or cause undue wear on the home.
>
> **What's Allowed**
> Home offices are always permitted—this includes remote work, freelancing, consulting, and other setups that don't involve regular visitors or unusual property use. No prior approval is required for standard office work.
>
> **What Requires Written Approval**
> Businesses that involve increased traffic, noise, or high-impact use of the property do require prior written approval from Sagareus. Examples include:
> • Home daycares or child care operations
> • Pet sitting or pet daycare services
> • Catering or food production businesses
> • Auto repair or detailing
> • Hair salons, massage therapy, esthetician services, or similar client-based services
>
> **Why Approval Is Required**
> These activities can affect:
> • Neighbor parking and traffic
> • Wear and tear on the property systems
> • Liability and insurance coverage

## Action


1. If office-based business: "Your Home Based Business is approved!" — Close associated tasks
2. If approval required: Escalate to [Leasing Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/leasing-manager.md) for approval
