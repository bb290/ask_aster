:::tip
Two notice templates for parking lot audits, one for properties where spaces are **not yet labeled**, and one where spaces **are already labeled**.

:::


---

## NOTICE: Parking Spaces Not Labelled


:::info
Use this template when the parking lot has **no labeled spaces** and you need resident input to create assignments from scratch.

:::

**Subject:** Parking Lot Organization, Please Review Map & Confirm Your Space

Dear Residents,

We're currently reorganizing the parking lot to create clearly marked, assigned parking spaces for each unit.

**Action Requested by** **\[Date\]**

Please review the attached parking map:

* **The space number you are currently parking in** (as shown on the map)
* **Your vehicle information**, Make, model, and color

Please email Leasing@sagareus.com or Call/Text: 425-390-8122 to claim your space!

Best Regards,
Sagareus Leasing Team


---

## NOTICE: Parking Spaces Labelled


:::info
Use this template when the parking lot **already has labeled/numbered spaces** and you are auditing current assignments.

:::

**Subject:** Parking Lot Audit, Please Confirm Your Space & Vehicle Information

Dear Residents,

We're conducting a parking lot audit to ensure all parking spaces are correctly assigned and being used by the appropriate vehicles.

**Action Requested by** **\[Insert Date\]**

Please review the map & assignments enclosed to confirm the following information:

* **The numbered space(s) you are currently using**
* **Your vehicle information**, Make, model, and color

Please email us at **Leasing@sagareus.com** or call/text **425-390-8122** with this information.


:::warning
Once all responses are collected, we'll update our records accordingly. Beginning Sept 15 2025, any unauthorized vehicles will be subject to tow at owner's expense.

:::

Best Regards,
Sagareus Leasing Team
