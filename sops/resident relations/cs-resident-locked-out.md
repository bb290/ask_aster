> **Situation:** Resident is locked out of their unit and contacts management for help.


---

## Key Points

* **No lockout service** — Management does not provide lockout service
* **Resident's responsibility** — Resident must contact a locksmith at their own expense
* **Provide local locksmith** — Include locksmith vendor servicing the area if available


---

## Response

> Management does not provide 'lock out' service. If Resident(s) misplace keys or otherwise lock themselves out of the rented premises, Resident(s) are to contact a locksmith to allow entry at their own expense. \[Insert locksmith vendor servicing the area\]

### Example Resident Questions

* "I'm locked out, can you let me in?"
* "I lost my keys, can management open my door?"
* "Is there an emergency lockout service?"
