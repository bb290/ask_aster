> **Situation:** Resident asks if their security deposit or last month's rent can be applied to an outstanding balance before moving out.


---

## Key Points

* **Deposit applied after move-out only** — Security deposit is only applied to the ledger after the Resident has fully vacated
* **Last month's rent** — Only applied during the final month of the lease term
* **Payment plan available** — If Resident is having difficulty, offer to establish a payment plan through the [Resident Account Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/resident-account-manager.md)


---


:::warning
* Security deposit is only applied to the ledger after they vacate
* Last month rent is only applied to the ledger in the last month of their lease

:::

## Response

> Per Sagareus policy, the security deposit can only be applied to your account after you have fully vacated the property. Similarly, last month's rent—if collected—is only applied during the final month of your lease term.
>
> If you are having difficulty paying the outstanding balance, we are happy to establish a payment plan. I've created a follow up ticket for our Resident Account Manager to reach out.

## Action

   1. Title: "Collections // \[Address, Unit\]"
   2. Project: Collections
   3. Section: Untitled
   4. Assigned to: [Resident Account Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/resident-account-manager.md)
   5. Due: Same or next day
   6. Document interaction and/or upload screenshots
