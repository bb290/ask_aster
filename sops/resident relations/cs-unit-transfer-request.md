> **Situation:** Resident wants to transfer to another unit under Sagareus Management.


---

## Key Points

* **Complex procedure** — Involves new security deposit, lease termination addendum for current unit, and new lease agreement
* **New deposit required** — Collected and due at move-in for the new unit
* **Current deposit refunded** — Within 30 days of move-out per standard procedure
* **Route to** [**Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator**](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md) — To confirm eligibility and coordinate


---


:::info
Unit Transfer is a Complex Procedure.

:::

## Response

> **Subject:** Request to Transfer Units
>
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
>
> Thank you for reaching out about your interest in transferring to another unit under Sagareus Management. We're happy to help you explore this option. I've assigned your request to our [Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md), who will confirm eligibility and coordinate the next steps with you.
>
> **Important Notes:**
> • A new Security Deposit will be collected and due at move-in for the new unit.
> • Your current Security Deposit will be refunded within 30 days of move-out, per standard procedure.
> • A Lease Termination Addendum will be required if ending your current lease early.
> • A new Lease Agreement will need to be signed for the new property.
>
> We appreciate you choosing to continue your tenancy with Sagareus and look forward to supporting your move.
>
> Warm regards,
