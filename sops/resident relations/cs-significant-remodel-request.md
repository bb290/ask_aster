> **Situation:** Resident asks about doing electrical, drywall, plumbing, or other structural modifications to the property.


---

## Key Points

* **Not permitted** — Residents may not perform or coordinate structural, electrical, plumbing, or heating modifications
* **Licensed contractors required** — These changes require licensed professionals and owner authorization
* **Maintenance suggestions welcome** — Encourage Resident to submit improvement ideas for owner review


---

## Response

> Thanks for reaching out about your project idea. Unfortunately, residents are **not permitted** to perform or coordinate structural, electrical, plumbing, or heating modifications. These types of changes require licensed contractors and owner authorization.
>
> We appreciate your care for the home and encourage you to submit maintenance or improvement suggestions anytime. We will review them with the property owner as appropriate.

### Example Resident Questions

* "Can I add an outlet in the bedroom?"
* "I want to update the bathroom fixtures"
* "Can I finish the basement?"
