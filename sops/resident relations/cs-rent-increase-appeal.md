> **Situation:** Resident wants to re-negotiate or appeal the rent increase amount they received.


---

## Key Points

* **Approvals are rare** — Based on documented market data only
* **Maintenance issues handled separately** — If Resident mentions maintenance items, those are processed separately from rent increase procedures
* **Route to** [**Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator**](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md) — Convert to subtask of existing Renewal & Rent Increase task


---

## Response

> Thank you for reporting your concerns about the upcoming rent change. We've opened an appeal ticket and will review your appeal in detail. Approvals are rare and based on documented market data.
>
> If you noted maintenance items, our maintenance team will contact you separately to schedule service. Maintenance issues are processed separately from the rent increase procedures.
>
> While we can't guarantee an adjustment, we'll weigh your feedback carefully and get you a clear answer.
