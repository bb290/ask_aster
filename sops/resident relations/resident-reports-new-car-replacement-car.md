> **Situation:** Resident proactively notifies management of a change in their vehicle information (new vehicle, vehicle sold, license plate update).


---

## Key Points

* **Thank the resident**, Proactive notification keeps records accurate and avoids parking enforcement issues
* **Update Buildium**, Navigate to the tenant's profile and update vehicle information
* **Check building SOP**, If the property has a vehicle/parking log, update it with the new details
* **No approval needed**, Vehicle changes are informational; no owner or management approval required


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Vehicle Registration Updated
> 
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
> 
> Thank you for letting us know about your new vehicle. We've updated your records with the following details:
> 
> Vehicle: \[Year / Make / Model\]
> License Plate: \[Plate #\]
> 
> No further action is needed on your part at this time.
> 
> Best regards,

## Action


1. Update Resident Vehicle in Buildium
2. Check the building SOP, if the property has a vehicle/parking log, update it with the new vehicle details
