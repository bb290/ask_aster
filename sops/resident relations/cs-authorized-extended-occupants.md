> **Situation:** A non-leaseholding individual needs to reside in the unit beyond the guest limit, but is not intended to be a tenant (e.g., adult child, au pair, live-in caregiver).


---

## Key Points

* **Not a tenant** — Authorized Extended Occupants are not responsible for rent and are not a party to the lease
* **Documented via addendum** — Extended Occupant Addendum signed by all leaseholders and the occupant (acknowledgment only)
* **No application required** — Income verification not required unless specifically requested due to risk, insurance, or owner instruction
* **Common scenarios** — Adult children (recent graduates), au pairs, live-in nannies/caregivers, temporary medical/family needs


---


:::info
An **Authorized Extended Occupant** is a non-leaseholding individual approved by Sagareus to reside in the unit beyond the guest limit, without being added to the lease. They are:

* **Not responsible for rent**
* **Not a party to the Lease**

Authorization is documented via the **Extended Occupant Addendum**.

:::

## Occupancy Category Decision Logic

* **14 days or less in any 8-week period** — Guest (No action required)
* **Over 14 days, not paying rent, not intended as tenant** — Authorized Extended Occupant (this SOP)
* **Paying rent or permanent** — Roommate / Lease Addendum required (different SOP)

## Response

> **Subject:** Extended Occupancy – Next Steps
>
> Hi \[Resident Name(s)\],
>
> Thank you for letting us know; We appreciate the proactive communication.
>
> Per the lease, guests may stay up to 14 days within any 8-week period without approval. When a guest is expected to stay longer than that, we simply need to document the additional occupant for our records. This does not add them to the lease or make them responsible for rent.
>
> In these situations, we classify the individual as an Authorized Extended Occupant (commonly used for adult children, live in caregivers, or similar arrangements).
>
> To proceed, please reply with the following information:
> • Full legal name of the individual
> • Email address for the individual
> • Anticipated start date of occupancy
>
> Once received, we'll prepare a brief **Extended Occupant Addendum** for e-signature. This addendum:
> • Documents their presence in the home
> • Does not modify your lease terms
>
> Once completed, no further action is required unless the situation changes.
>
> Please let us know if you have any questions.
>
> Kind regards,

## Action


1. Once information received, prepare Extended Occupant Addendum:
   * PandaDoc — Templates — Leasing — **Extended Occupant Addendum**
   * Signed by all Leaseholders
   * Signed by Extended Occupant (acknowledgment only)
2. Upload executed Addendum to Buildium Lease Profile
