> **Situation:** Resident reports smoke exposure from a neighboring unit.


---

## Key Points

* **Collect details** — Ask for time, date, and unit/location of the smoke source
* **Document and escalate** — If details already provided, issue formal notice to the neighboring unit per lease and property policies
* **Encourage documentation** — Ask Resident to document timestamps, photos of smoke entry points


---

## Response

**If details are needed:**

> Thank you for reaching out — we're sorry to hear about the disruption you've experienced. We understand how frustrating and unpleasant it can be to deal with smoke exposure in your home.
>
> To help us address this complaint, could you please provide the following details:
> • The time and date of the incident
> • If known, which unit or location the smoke was coming from
>
> Please continue to notify us if the problem persists. If you're able to safely document any details (timestamps, photos of smoke entry points, etc.), that can help support further action.
>
> Thank you for your cooperation.

**If details are already provided:**

> Hi \[Resident's First Name\],
>
> Thank you for reaching out — we're sorry to hear about the disruption you've experienced. We understand how frustrating and unpleasant it can be to deal with smoke exposure in your home.
>
> We've documented your concern and will be issuing a formal notice to the neighboring unit in accordance with our lease and property policies. If the issue continues, we will escalate enforcement as appropriate.
>
> Please continue to notify us if the problem persists. If you're able to safely document any details (timestamps, photos of smoke entry points, etc.), that can help support further action.
>
> We appreciate your patience as we work to maintain a healthy and respectful living environment for all residents.
