> **Situation:** During turn over, the team identifies missing or damaged window coverings (blinds, curtains, etc.) and the owner asks whether they need to be replaced. Also arises when a prospect requests window coverings during lease-up, or a current resident submits a maintenance ticket for window coverings.


---

## Key Points

* **Not legally required** — Window coverings are a common courtesy many landlords offer, but there is no legal obligation to provide them.
* **Lease terms** — Per the lease, residents can be held responsible for damage to window coverings during their tenancy.
* **Vacant unit appearance** — Window coverings, along with outlets, switches, and cover plates, tend to stand out in a vacant unit. They can affect showing quality.
* **Recommendation** — Leave them out unless the owner instructs otherwise. If the property does not rent quickly, blinds can always be added later.
