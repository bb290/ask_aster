> **Situation:** Resident notifies that they no longer have a pet and wants it removed from their lease.


---

## Key Points

* **Details needed** — Type of pet, effective date of change, ESA documentation if any
* **Addendum required** — Sagareus prepares addendum to formalize pet occupancy change and corresponding pet rent adjustment
* **Pro-rated adjustment** — Last month pet rent is pro-rated; pet rent removed going forward after addendum is signed


---

## Response

> We have received your notice to remove the pet from your lease. Please provide:
> • Type of pet to remove from the lease
> • Effective date of the change
> • ESA documentation if any
>
> A ticket has been created and assigned to our team: Sagareus will prepare addendum to formalize the change of pet occupancy and corresponding change in pet rent. Once the addendum is mutually signed, we will adjust your ledger to reflect the pro-rated last month pet rent and removal of pet rent going forward.
