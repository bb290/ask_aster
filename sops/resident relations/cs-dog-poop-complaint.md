> **Situation:** Resident reports pet waste left in common areas, near entryways, or on the property.


---

## Key Points

* **Community notice** — Send notice to all Residents reminding of pet waste responsibilities
* **Ask for details** — If Resident can identify the offending unit, document for enforcement
* **Lease enforcement** — Ongoing violations may result in escalated enforcement


---

## Response

> **Dear Residents,**
>
> We have received reports regarding pet urine and waste being left near the front entry area of the building. Please be reminded that all pet owners are responsible for properly cleaning up after their animals at all times, including any accidents that occur inside or outside common areas.
>
> Allowing pets to relieve themselves near entryways or failing to clean up waste creates sanitation and odor issues that affects everyone in the community. We kindly ask all residents to be considerate of their neighbors and help keep our shared spaces clean and welcoming.
>
> If you witness ongoing violations, please report details (date, time, and description if possible) to management so we can address the issue appropriately.
>
> Thank you for your cooperation and attention to this matter.
>
> Sincerely,
> **Sagareus Property Management**
