> **Situation:** Resident in a multifamily property asks if they can repaint their unit.


---

## Key Points

* **Allowed with conditions** — Colors must be restored to original neutral color before move-out
* **Professional quality** — Any patching or repainting at move-out must meet professional standards
* **Keep photos** — Recommend photographing original wall color for reference
* **Move-out billing** — Touch-ups or wall repair will be billed if original color is not restored


---

## Response

> Thanks for reaching out. You're welcome to paint the interior, provided:
> • Colors are restored to the original neutral color before move-out.
> • Any patching or repainting at move-out must meet professional quality standards.
>
> We recommend keeping photos of the original wall color for easy reference. Please note that touch-ups or wall repair after move-out will be billed if the original color is not restored.
