## Lifecycle Detailed

### Complaints / Questions

#### Noise Complaint / Disruptive Activity


:::info
**Process: Issue Notice of Disruptive Activity**

Follow the Buildium mailing workflow, then follow up one week later.

:::


1. Issue Notice of Disruptive Activity via email + mail
2. Inform reporting Resident notice has been sent
3. Update task due to 1 week out
   * Follow up with the original complaining Resident; Has the issue improved?
     * **If yes:** Mark task complete & close BD ticket.
     * **If no: Prepare 10 Day Notice to Comply or Vacate**

#### Resident moved out then wants to move back in


:::warning
If a Resident vacates a unit, either through formal notice or by abandoning the premises, and later expresses interest in returning to the property, they will be required to **submit a new rental application** and undergo full screening.

:::

* Any return request will be treated as a **new lease**, subject to updated rent, deposit, and lease terms
* Applications will be reviewed based on current qualifications, availability, and rental history, including the circumstances surrounding the prior move-out
* Prior tenancy does **not guarantee re-approval**

### Resident transferring units / moving units


:::warning
Unit Transfer is a Complex Procedure.

:::


1. TASK TEMPLATE: "Unit Transfer // Current Address to New Address"
2. Check rent payment history
   * If 1 or less late payments, proceed with transfer
   * If 2 or more, send Declined for Unit Transfer template

#### Email Template, Approved for Unit Transfer

> **Subject:** Unit Transfer - APPROVED!
> 
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
> 
> Your request to transfer to another unit under Sagareus Management has been **approved**.
> 
> Please confirm your desired **move-out date** for your current address and **move-in date** for the new property.
> 
> **Important Reminders:**
> A new Security Deposit will be collected and due at move-in for the new unit.
> Your current Security Deposit will be refunded within 30 days of move-out, per standard procedure.
> 
> Best,

#### Email Template, Declined for Unit Transfer

> **Subject:** Unit Transfer Request, Decision
> 
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
> 
> After review, your request to transfer to another unit under Sagareus Management has been **declined** due to your rental payment history.
> 
> For your reference, I've attached a copy of your payment history.
> 
> You are still welcome to apply for the property if you'd like.
> 
> Regards,

### Add Pet to Lease

#### If No Pet Allowed

**Email Template, No pet allowed**

> Thank you for your inquiry. We reviewed your lease, and per the property's pet policy, animals are not permitted in the home. We appreciate you checking in with us before moving forward and are here if you have any other questions.

#### If Pet Allowed

**Email Template, Adding pet to the lease**

> Thanks for checking with us. Pets are allowed at your home with prior approval. Here is a quick overview of the pet policy:
> 
> $50/month pet rent per pet
> Only dogs and cats under 30 lbs are permitted
> Other animals may be considered on a case-by-case basis
> Aquariums, fish tanks, and other aquatic animals are not permitted under any circumstances
> 
> To proceed, please reply with the following details for review:
> Pet move-in date
> Pet type
> Weight
> 
> Once approved, we will send you the Lease Addendum for signing
