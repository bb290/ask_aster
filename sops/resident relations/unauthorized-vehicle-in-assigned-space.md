> **Situation:** Resident reports another vehicle parking in their assigned parking space.


---

## Key Points

* **Get vehicle details first**, Need make, model, and/or license plate to identify the owner
* **Check the parking log**, Determine if the vehicle is registered to another resident
* **24-hour removal window**, If owner is identified, they must move immediately
* **5 business day identification window**, If no owner found, all residents are contacted
* **Tow if necessary**, Vehicle towed at owner's expense if not moved


---

## Email Template, Request Vehicle Information

> **Subject:** Reported Vehicle
> 
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
> 
> Could you please provide the make, model, and/or license plate number of the vehicle in question? This will help us determine whether it belongs to a registered resident.
> 
> Thank you,

## Procedure

### Step 1, Check parking log & identify registered owner

### If Vehicle Owner is registered

> **Subject:** Parking Space Assignment, Immediate Action Required
> 
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
> 
> We have received a report you are parking in the incorrect space. Please be advised that parking space **#\[fill in blank\]** is assigned to another resident and may not be used.
> 
> Your assigned parking space is **#\[assigned spot, if applicable\]**.
> 
> Please move your vehicle from space **#\[fill in blank\]** immediately. If the vehicle is not relocated, it will be towed at your expense.
> 
> Best regards,

### If No Owner identified, Contact all residents

> **Subject:** Unidentified Vehicle on Property, Owner Identification Requested
> 
> Hi Everyone,
> 
> We've received a report of a vehicle parked in a Resident's assigned space that is not registered with management.
> 
> If this vehicle belongs to you or a member of your household, please notify us within 5 business days.
> 
> Best regards,
