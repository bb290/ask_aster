> **Situation:** Resident asks about renter's insurance requirements or how to get a policy.


---

## Key Points

* **Required by lease** — All Residents must maintain an active renter's insurance policy during the lease term
* **Portal purchase option** — Residents can buy a policy directly through their Resident portal
* **Proof required** — Resident must provide proof of coverage


---

## Response

> Thank you for reaching out. Please provide proof of renter's insurance, or you may purchase a policy directly through your resident portal.
>
> The lease requires all Residents to maintain an active renter's insurance policy during the term of the lease. This requirement helps ensure that there is adequate protection in place for your personal property and liability, and it also aligns with our standard risk management practices.
