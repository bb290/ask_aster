> **Situation:** Resident reports they will have a guest staying for an extended period, or asks about the guest policy.


---

## Key Points

* **Under 14 days** — Thank the Resident for notifying; no additional work needed
* **15–90 days** — Allowed with notice; track to confirm guest has departed; add occupant to BD lease; send template below
* **Over 90 days** — Guest must be added to the lease as a roommate; follow Subletting/Roommate Change procedures
* **No additional pets** — Guest must not add pets beyond what is listed on the lease
* **Resident is responsible** — For guest's conduct and lease compliance

## Procedure


1. Confirm the number of days the guest is staying

**IF less than 14 days:**

* Thank the Resident for their notification; no additional work needed

**IF 15–90 days:**

* Create a tracking task in [Resident Relations 2.0](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1201903129380625/list/1205103326230069) project to check with Resident after duration of stay and confirm guest moved out
* Add the occupant to BD lease as Resident
* Send template below

**IF over 90 days:**

* Guest needs to be added to the lease as a roommate — follow Subletting/Roommate Change procedures

## Response (15–90 day stay)

> Thank you for reporting. Please let us know your guest's full name, email, and phone number before their stay begins.
>
> Guests may stay up to 90 days with prior notice, provided:
> • The guests do not add pets beyond what is listed in your lease.
> • Ensure your guest follows all lease terms and rules.
> • You are responsible for their conduct.
> • Guests shall not receive mail or use the address for official registration unless approved.
>
> If your guest will stay beyond 90 days, they must be screened and added to the lease as an approved occupant.
