> **Situation:** Owner reports a potential pet lease violation in their rental unit (e.g., pet equipment seen, neighbor reports, photos). Management must investigate and determine whether the pet is unauthorized, an approved pet, or an approved ESA.


---

## Key Points

* **Acknowledge the owner promptly** — Thank the owner for reporting, let them know we will investigate
* **Request evidence** — Ask the owner to forward any photos or documentation they have (gently — don't burden them)
* **Investigate before acting** — Audit the lease and application for any mention of pet or ESA approval before contacting the tenant
* **Three possible outcomes** — The investigation will lead to one of three branches:

**IF ESA:**

* Verify ESA documentation is current and valid
* Notify owner that this is an approved ESA — attach ESA documentation

**IF Approved Pet:**

* Verify pet rent is being charged correctly in Buildium
* Notify owner that this is an approved pet with active pet rent

**IF Unauthorized Pet:**

* Contact tenant to ask about the presence of a pet
* Inform tenant that their lease does not currently allow a pet
* Outcome: comply or vacate notice, OR lease amendment to add pet + pet rent

## Procedure


1. Receive owner report — acknowledge via Owner Inquiry template above
2. Audit lease and application in Buildium for pet or ESA records
3. **Branch based on findings:**
   * **ESA on file** — Verify documentation is current. Send Owner Notification — Approved ESA template. Close ticket.
   * **Approved pet on file** — Verify pet rent is active in Buildium. Send Owner Notification — Approved Pet template. Close ticket.
   * **No pet/ESA on file** — Send Tenant Inquiry — Unauthorized Pet template. Continue to step 4.
4. Wait for tenant response (follow up after 3 business days if no reply)
5. **Based on tenant response:**
   * **Tenant provides valid ESA documentation** — Update file, send Owner Notification — Approved ESA. Close ticket.
   * **Tenant wants to add pet** — Route to [Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md) for lease amendment + pet rent. Send owner update once resolved.
   * **Tenant denies or does not respond** — Consult with owner on next steps (comply or vacate notice).
