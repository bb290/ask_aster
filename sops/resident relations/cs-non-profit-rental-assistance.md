> **Situation:** A non-profit or rental assistance agency contacts Sagareus requesting information or documents for a Resident.


---

## Key Points

* **Route to** [**Office Manager**](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) — Handles document requests and is the point of contact for payments
* **Include** [**Office Manager**](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) **on the response** — So the agency communicates directly with them going forward


---

## Response

> Thank you for reaching out. I've included our Office Manager on this message; She will follow up with the requested documents & be your point of contact regarding payments.
>
> Best,

## Action


1. Include [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md) on the response
2. Create Asana Task


   1. Title: "Non-Profit Requesting Docs // \[Resident Names\]" or "\[Address\]"
   2. Project: Finance & Office
   3. Section: Untitled
   4. Assigned to: [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md)
   5. Due Date: Same or Next Business Day
   6. Status Field: Section 8 / Agency
   7. Upload Screenshot Email response for reference
