> **Situation:** A former Resident who has already moved out wants to return to the property.


---

## Key Points

* **Tenancy concluded** — Once a Resident vacates, the tenancy is considered concluded
* **Must reapply** — New rental application and full screening required
* **No guarantees** — Re-approval is not guaranteed; reviewed based on current qualifications, rental history, and availability
* **Standard terms apply** — If approved, standard lease terms, deposit, and current market rent apply


---

## Response

> **Subject:** Re: Request to Return to Property
>
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
>
> Thank you for reaching out. Per our policy, once a resident has vacated a unit the tenancy is considered concluded. If you're interested in returning to the property, you'll need to submit a new rental application and undergo full screening. Please note:
> • Re-approval is not guaranteed
> • Applications are reviewed based on current qualifications, rental history, and availability
> • If approved, standard lease terms, deposit, and current market rent would apply
>
> You can apply online here:
> https://sagareus.managebuilding.com/Resident/rental-application/new
>
> Let us know once submitted, and we'll keep you updated on the review process.
>
> Best regards,

## Action

* No further action needed — Leasing Team will receive application and process per normal procedures
* Security Deposit will be processed and returned per normal procedures
