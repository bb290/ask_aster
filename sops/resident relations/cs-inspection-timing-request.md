> **Situation:** Resident receives a notice of entry for a scheduled inspection and requests specific timing or accommodation due to their schedule.


---

## Key Points

* **Identify the inspection task** — Search for the property in Asana and find the corresponding inspection task (Annual Inspection, Sprinkler Inspection/Repairs, RRIO/Rental Inspections)
* **Task found: loop in the assignee** — Screenshot the resident's request to the inspection task, set the task due date to today so the assignee sees it, and reply to the email adding the task assignee to the thread
* **Task not found: escalate** — If no inspection task can be identified, create a new task in the Ops Team project assigned to the Ops Manager
* **Accommodation not guaranteed** — Building-wide inspections or inspections involving third-party inspectors may not allow individual scheduling


---

## Resident Inquiry

Reply to the resident's email and add the inspection task assignee to the thread.

> Hi \[Resident's First Name\],
>
> Thank you for reaching out. We've forwarded your request to the inspector assigned to your property. They will review whether it's possible to accommodate your preferred timing.
>
> Please note that for building-wide inspections or when third-party inspectors are involved, individual scheduling may not be available.
>
> We'll do our best to work with your schedule, and the inspector will follow up with you directly.
>
> Thank you for your cooperation.

## Action

* Search Asana for the property's inspection task:
  * Annual Inspection project
  * Sprinkler Inspection/Repairs
  * RRIO/Rental Inspections
* **If task found:**
  * Screenshot the resident's email to the task comments
  * Set task due date to today
  * Reply to resident's email, CC the task assignee
* **If task NOT found:**
  * Create task in Ops Team project
  * Title: "Inspection Timing Request // \[Address\]"
  * Assigned to: Ops Manager
  * Due: Today
  * Include resident's request in task description

### Example Resident Questions

* "Can the inspection be scheduled for the afternoon instead?"
* "I work during the day — is there a way to reschedule the inspection?"
* "I received a notice of entry but I won't be home at that time"
* "Can the inspector come after 3pm?"
* "Is it possible to reschedule my unit's inspection?"
