> **Situation:** Resident notifies management of the birth of a child or addition of a minor to the household.


---

## Key Points

* **Thank the resident** — Congratulate them and thank them for keeping us informed
* **No lease modification needed** — Dependents and minors do not need to be added to the lease or documented via addendum
* **No system action required** — Minors are not tracked in Buildium; no task or record update needed


---

## Response

> Hi \[Resident's First Name\],
>
> Congratulations — and thank you for letting us know!
>
> No action is needed on your end. If you have any questions, don't hesitate to reach out.
>
> Thank you,

### Example Resident Questions

* "We just had a baby, do we need to update our lease?"
* "I need to add my child to the lease"
* "We have a new baby, what do we need to do?"
* "My partner's child is moving in, do they need to be on the lease?"
* "Do I need to notify you about a new baby?"
* "We're expecting a baby, is there anything we need to do for the lease?"
