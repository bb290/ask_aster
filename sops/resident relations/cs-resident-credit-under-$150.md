> **Situation:** Resident made a small repair to the home instead of submitting a maintenance request, and is asking for reimbursement.


---

## Key Points

* **Up to $150 reimbursement** — Maintenance team can approve if it passes the "good stewardship of owner's money" test
* **Materials only** — Labor is not covered; Resident must provide the invoice/receipt
* **Eligible categories only:**
  * Furnace filter
  * Fridge water filter
  * Specialty light bulb (normal light bulbs do not apply)
  * Shower head
  * Smoke/CO detectors
* **Must be something landlord would normally dispatch vendor for**


---

## Procedure


1. Verify the item falls within eligible categories
2. Verify Resident has provided invoice/receipt
3. Apply credit to the Resident's ledger:
   * Memo: "Landlord credit for \[item\]"
   * Category: Rent

### Example Resident Questions

* "I replaced the furnace filter myself, can I get reimbursed?"
* "I bought a new shower head, will you pay me back?"
* "I replaced the smoke detector batteries"
