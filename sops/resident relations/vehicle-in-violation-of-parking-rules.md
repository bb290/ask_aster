> **Situation:** A vehicle on the property is reported in violation of Parking Rules & Regulations, abandoned, junk car, unlicensed, untagged, working on car in lot, or taking up multiple spaces.


---

## Key Points

* **Check the parking log first**, Identify if the vehicle is registered to a current resident before taking action
* **10 business day compliance window**, Identified owner gets 10 business days to fix or remove the vehicle
* **5 business day identification window**, If no owner found, all residents are contacted and given 5 business days to claim
* **Tow as last resort**, Only after all deadlines expire with no action taken
* **Document everything**, All notices and communications logged in Asana comments


---

## Common Violations

* Abandoned or junk car
* Unlicensed or untagged vehicle
* Working on car in parking lot / making a mess
* Taking up 2 spaces

## Procedure

### Step 1, Check parking log & identify registered owner

### If owner is identified

> **Subject:** Action Required: Vehicle Not in Compliance with Parking Rules
> 
> Hi \[Resident Name\],
> 
> We've identified that your registered vehicle is currently not in compliance with the property's Parking Rules & Regulations.
> 
> Please take corrective action within **10 business days** **(by \[Insert Date\])** to either bring the vehicle into compliance or remove it from the property.
> 
> If no action is taken, we may need to proceed with enforcement, including towing at the vehicle owner's expense.
> 
> Best regards,

### If No Owner identified, Contact all residents

> **Subject:** Unidentified Vehicle on Property, Owner Identification Requested
> 
> Hi Everyone,
> 
> We've received a report regarding a vehicle on the property that appears to be abandoned or inoperable.
> 
> If this vehicle belongs to you or someone in your household, please notify us **within 5 business days** so we can document ownership and discuss next steps, if needed.
> 
> If we do not hear from anyone, the vehicle may be tagged for removal in accordance with our parking policies.
> 
> Best regards,
