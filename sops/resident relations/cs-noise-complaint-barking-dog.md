> **Situation:** Resident reports noise issues from a neighbor, including barking dogs, loud music, or other disturbances.


---

## Key Points

* **Collect details** — Ask for time, date, and unit/location of the noise source
* **Document and escalate** — If details already provided, issue informal notice to the neighboring unit
* **Encourage documentation** — Ask Resident to record timestamps and brief recordings if the problem persists
* **Escalation path** — If the issue continues after informal notice, escalate enforcement per the lease


---

## Response

**If details are needed:**

> Thank you for bringing this to our attention. To help us address this complaint, could you please provide the following details:
> • The time and date of the incident
> • If known, which unit or location the noise was coming from
>
> Your report has been received and a ticket has been created for our team to review and follow up.
>
> Thank you for your cooperation.

**If details are already provided:**

> Hi \[Resident's First Name\],
>
> Thank you for reaching out — we're sorry to hear about the disruption you've experienced. We understand how frustrating repeated noise can be, especially during late hours.
>
> We've documented your concerns and will be issuing an informal notice to the neighboring unit. If the issue continues, we'll escalate enforcement in accordance with the lease.
>
> Please continue to notify us if the problem persists. If you're able to safely document the noise (timestamps, brief recordings, etc.), that can help support further action.
>
> We appreciate your patience as we work to maintain a peaceful living environment.
