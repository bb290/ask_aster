# Notice of Entry


:::warning
The housing provider **may not** enter the unit without proper notice and reasonable cause except in cases of emergency or abandonment.

Housing providers have the legal right to enter an occupied property to inspect, repair, show a unit, or in an emergency situation provided the proper notice is given.

:::

## Housing Provider Rights

* May enter the property without giving notice if invited to enter the property by all occupants.
* Otherwise, must give proper notice to enter an occupied unit. (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(6))
* May enter an occupied unit to inspect, repair, show unit. (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(1))
* May not abuse the right of entry.
* May enter without permission in the case of an emergency. (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(5), (6))
* May enter without permission if the unit is abandoned. (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(5))


---

## Resident Rights

* Have rights to the enjoyment of the dwelling and may object to unreasonable entry.
* May object to entry if they believe a request to enter is unreasonable (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(6))
* May request to reschedule entry (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(6))
* May not unreasonably deny entry to the housing provider (RCW [59.18.150](https://app.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=59.18.150)(1), (6))

### Get Written Confirmation (IF Single Family)


:::info
For single family homes or condo, we attempt to enter by Invitation first before serving notice of entry. Because:

* Notice of entry feels oppressive, we are demanding access. We respect the Resident's enjoyment of the home and attempt to work with them first.
* Does not require physical servicing.

:::

To enter by invitation, we must have:

* Written approval from the Residents allowing access
* Confirmation if they will provide access or Sagareus will provide access


:::warning
Sagareus will make **3 documented attempts** to schedule with the Resident. If no agreement is reached, Sagareus will proceed to serve notice of entry.

:::

# 10 Day Notice to Comply or Vacate


:::warning
10 Day Notice to Comply or Vacate is a formal Notice issued to Resident for a violation of their lease terms (other than Non-Payment).

Typically, 10 Day Notice to Comply or Vacate is issued after an initial informal notice.

:::


:::info
Common Violations requiring 10 Day Notice are Noise/Disruptive, Parking, Landscaping, etc.

:::


---

## Serving Notice, In Person

### Prepare the package for servicing


:::info
Download the corresponding cover letter:

* Rent Increase Notice Cover
* Renewal Offer Cover
* Declaration of Service
* Abandoned Notice Cover
* Abandoned Property Storage Notice Cover
* Notice of Entry Cover
* Notice To Comply Cover

:::


:::warning
* Combine the cover letter with the document with staple
* DO NOT put in envelope
* Make at least one copy of the notice for each adult occupant and one additional copy for "all other occupants".

:::


---

### Deliver the notice in person

* Go to the Premises, knock on the door/ring the doorbell and wait 30 seconds
* If Resident opens the door:

#### Scenario 1, all adults are home/available

#### Scenario 2, not all adults are home/available

* Hand all copies to the adult

#### Scenario 3, no one answered


:::warning
* Tape **all** package to the door using painter's tape on ALL sides
* Ensure other people cannot flip through the pages as they walk by
* Still take a picture

:::


---

### Fill out the declaration of delivery

* Download Declaration of Service
* Fill out in its entirety and attach to the Asana task


---
