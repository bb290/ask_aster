## Access & Layout

### Building & Unit Access

* Maps brings you to the front of the unit which shows a business not the building of the units.
* There is an alley on the right side northwest facing; upon walking down, it'll bring you to steps at the end of the alley walking up into access to all units.
* Directly beside the steps on the left is the parking for the units.
* At the top of the stairs, there are three doors located on the left side. First door located on left has units 1 and 2.
* 2nd door located on the left has units 3 and 4.
* 3rd doorway has units 5 and 6; directly on the right side is 5 and 6 at the end of the hallway.
* Access to roof can be granted on the east side of property — three-story ladder.
