*4-plex*

If you're trying to locate keys to enter units, they are located in the laundry room — please see **Shared Laundry** below (under Amenities & Common Areas > Laundry).

## Access & Layout

### Building & Unit Access

* All units can be accessed through the front door — all one story
* Units 2, 3, and 4 are all west facing
* Unit 1 is south facing
* Access into the backyard of Unit 1 can be entered by the side gate and would have to reach over to pull the lever. West facing.
* Access to backyards of Units 2, 3, and 4 can be accessed on a south-facing fence, closest to Unit 4 around back
* Access into the laundry room can be gained through lockbox code (available in 1Password). Door to the laundry room is between Units 2 and 3.
* Additional lockbox inside of laundry room directly above fire extinguisher
