How a Leasing Agent locks in the weekly slots for a vacant property, from move-out assignment until move-in. Part of [Operation: No Drama](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/operation-no-drama-1KiDctsybm).

Companion docs: [Weekly Site Visit](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/weekly-site-visit-yjZFdeB9EC) (what happens at Slot 1 every week) and [Email | Owner - Weekly Activity Report](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/email-owner-weekly-activity-report-VDE6GnaYef) (the weekly report the visits feed).

:::info
The **Preferred Slot 1** and **Preferred Slot 2** custom fields exist on the Lease Up task. The "Schedule Showing Slots" subtask is a new Asana task as of July 21, 2026.
:::

## Set the Slots (once, when the move-out is assigned)

When a move-out is assigned to you, you get a subtask: **Schedule Showing Slots**.

1. Pick the day and time you will be at the property **every single week**. This is **Preferred Slot 1**.
2. Pick a second weekly window as your backup. This is **Preferred Slot 2**.
3. Enter both in the Lease Up task custom fields **Preferred Slot 1** and **Preferred Slot 2**.
4. Create a **recurring calendar event** for Preferred Slot 1: weekly, default **6 weeks** recurring. Extend it if the vacancy runs longer.

:::warning
**Preferred Slot 1 is the Weekly Site Visit. It is required.** You are at that property every week at that day and time, no matter what: showing booked or not, listed or not, turnover in progress, lease signed, move-in pending. It ends at move-in.
:::

## Booking Showings into the Slots

* Offer **Preferred Slot 1 first** when scheduling showings. A showing during your site visit is the double whammy: one trip, both jobs.
* **Preferred Slot 2** is the backup window for prospects who cannot make Slot 1.
* This pairs directly with **Ask Aster's speed to lead skill**: the two standing weekly windows are exactly what the speed to lead response offers every inquiry, instantly.
