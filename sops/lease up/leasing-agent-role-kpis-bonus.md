What the Leasing Agent owns, how performance is measured, and how the PreLease bonus works.

## Key Responsibilities

### Leasing Process

- Establish market rent per [Market Rent & Listing Strategy](market-rent-listing-strategy.md) using the PreListing tool on the Team Hub (https://www.sagareus.com/field).
- Publish rental listings that meet all Sagareus Listing Standards: approved marketing copy, professional photos, required disclosures, consistent formatting.
- Review listing performance weekly and update as needed per [Updating Active Listings](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/updating-active-listings-aytdYiWQoa).
- Conduct Move Out Property Inspections to document condition and inform turnover work orders.
- Conduct [Weekly Site Visits](weekly-site-visit.md) on every vacancy, move-out to move-in, via the Site Visit tool.
- Create Property Tour video for the marketing team.
- Respond to all incoming inquiries via email, messenger, text, or phone; schedule and conduct showings.
- Communicate lease terms between owner and prospective Residents in compliance with company policies and local regulations.
- Explain the Application, Lease Signing, and Move-In processes; meet Residents for Move-In.
- Create the Move-In Condition Report; collect move-in funds; provide keys; mail keys to the main office; deposit move-in funds at Bank of America as needed.

### Documentation and Record-Keeping

- Maintain accurate records of leasing activities, showing feedback, and prospect communications.
- Send the [Weekly Activity Report](email-owner-weekly-activity-report.md) by Tuesday at 12pm via the Activity Report tool.
- Track and report personal hours, mileage, and receipts for reimbursements.

### Collaboration

- Weekly 1:1 Google Meet syncs with the Leasing Team Lead.
- Coordinate Move-In with the Leasing Assistant.

### Operations Team Support

- Conduct Annual Inspections as assigned; assist with field tasks (legal notices, vacant unit checks, supplies, retrievals).

## KPIs

1. Move Out Inspections complete within 48 hrs of assigned move out date.
2. 100% of Activity Reports delivered by Tuesday at 12pm.
3. 6 Move Ins per month per Leasing Agent.
4. 9+ average customer rating (post Move In).
5. 1 five-star Google review per month per Agent ($50/review).

## PreLease Bonus

1. 21 days or less vacancy = $200 bonus.
2. Add to the Sagareus KPI Sheet Bonus Tab under the Move In Month: name, $200 bonus, property address linked to the Asana task.
