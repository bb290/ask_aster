Scope: execution of a Tenant Placement (TP) engagement from the moment the TP Service Agreement is signed through close-out of the file and handoff to the post-placement nurture workflow. Does NOT cover the sales pipeline (pre-signature) or post-placement nurture. Primary reader: Approved Leasing Agents. Supporting: Biz Dev Agent, Leasing Assistants, Brittany French (Leasing Manager).

## Program Summary

Tenant Placement is a fee-for-service, one-time leasing transaction. Sagareus handles listing through move-in; the owner self-manages after close-out. See the [Tenant Placement Program Overview](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/tenant-placement-program-overview-XjjeNDcRtv) for pricing, deliverables, and positioning. TP is both a standalone product AND a sales stage: every TP file ends in the HubSpot "TP Complete — Nurture" stage for future conversion to full PM.

## Role Split — Biz Dev Agent vs Approved Leasing Agent

Duties are assigned by role, not person; one person may hold both roles.

* **Biz Dev Agent** — commercial and administrative side: TP Service Agreement, onboarding (welcome email, JotForm follow-up, Buildium / Drive / Asana setup, Welcome Home Letter creation), and all invoicing (50% onboarding invoice, final invoice, showing overage billing).
* **Approved Leasing Agent** — leasing side: pre-listing prep, marketing, showings, applications, lease review, move-in, and file ownership through close-out.
* **Leasing Assistants** — lease drafting, Welcome Home Letter delivery, Underwriting File assembly, admin close-out.

## Tenant Placement vs Standard Lease Up

Screening, showing, and inspection standards are identical. The differences are who Sagareus is to the prospect and where the money and paperwork flow.

| Area | Standard Lease Up (full PM) | Tenant Placement |
|---|---|---|
| Answering prospect questions | Sagareus is the long-term property manager; ongoing maintenance and rent questions stay with Sagareus | Sagareus handles leasing only; prospects are told the owner manages the property directly after move-in, including maintenance and rent |
| Lease template | Sagareus lease documents; Sagareus administers the lease | RHAWA Lease Template, no Sagareus branding; signed by Owner and Tenant only; Sagareus is not a party |
| Move-in procedures | Sagareus collects and holds funds (Buildium deposit, BofA, Resident Center) | Cashier's check or money order payable to the Owner only; funds shipped FedEx overnight to the Owner; Sagareus never holds funds; inspection procedure identical |
| Turnover vendors | Sagareus coordinates | Owner coordinates; the Agent may only recommend work |
| Photos | Uploaded to Buildium | Property's Google Drive folder only |
| After move-in | Sagareus manages the property | Owner self-manages; file moves to post-placement nurture |
