:::info
**Scope:** This SOP covers execution of a Tenant Placement (TP) engagement from the moment the TP Service Agreement is signed through close-out of the file and handoff to the post-placement nurture workflow.

**Does NOT cover:** Sales pipeline (pre-signature) or post-placement nurture (future PM conversion). Those are separate SOPs.

**Primary Reader:** Nicole (Leasing Agent — Business Development in training)

**Supporting Team:** Onboarding Team, Leasing Assistants, Brittany French (Leasing Manager)

:::


---

## Program Summary

Tenant Placement is a fee-for-service, one-time leasing transaction. Sagareus handles listing through move-in; the owner self-manages the property after close-out. See the [**Tenant Placement Program Overview**](tenant-placement-program-overview.md) for pricing, deliverables, and strategic positioning.

Tenant Placement is both a standalone product AND a sales stage. Clients can later convert to full-service Property Management. Every TP file ends in the HubSpot "TP Complete — Nurture" stage for future conversion.

# Phase 1 — TP Onboarding

**Trigger:** Tenant Placement Service Agreement signed in PandaDoc **Owners:** Onboarding Team (lead execution) + Nicole (handoff recipient) **Phase Complete When:** JotForm submitted + 50% invoice paid + Asana TP Task Template applied + Buildium record created + Google Drive folder auto-created by JotForm + HubSpot deal moved to "TP Onboarding" stage + Nicole notified

## Workflow

### 1.1 — PandaDoc Signature Received *(Day 0)*

**Owner:** Onboarding Team Monitor PandaDoc signed-notification for the Tenant Placement Service Agreement. Client is automatically redirected from PandaDoc to the [JotForm Tenant Placement Onboarding Form](https://form.jotform.com/260593354075157) immediately after signature.

### 1.2 — Send Welcome Email + 50% Invoice *(Day 0, within 2 hours of signature)*

**Owner:** Onboarding Team Send the Welcome Email to the owner using the **TP — Welcome + Onboarding Invoice Email Template**. Email must include: thank-you/confirmation of signed TP Service Agreement; link to the [JotForm Tenant Placement Onboarding Form](https://form.jotform.com/260593354075157); the 50% invoice (manual send — Onboarding Team creates and sends); brief note that listing prep begins once the form is complete and the 50% is received.

### 1.3 — Follow-Up Cadence if JotForm Not Submitted

If the owner has not submitted the JotForm by the times below, the Onboarding Team sends the corresponding follow-up.

| Day | Action | Owner | Template |
|-----|--------|-------|----------|
| Day 2 | Gentle nudge — "Just making sure you received the onboarding form, happy to clarify anything" | Onboarding Team | TP — Day 2 Nudge |
| Day 5 | Second nudge — listing prep cannot begin until form is complete | Onboarding Team | TP — Day 5 Nudge |
| Day 8 | Nicole calls the owner directly | Nicole | N/A (phone call) |
| Day 14 | Escalation to leadership — file flagged for engagement decision | Onboarding Team | TP — Day 14 Leadership Flag |

### 1.4 — Process JotForm Submission *(When submitted)*

**Owner:** Onboarding Team


1. Review submission for completeness; follow up with owner on missing fields
2. Migrate property and owner data from JotForm into **Buildium** (create property + owner records)
3. Verify the Google Drive folder was auto-created by JotForm
4. Save the signed TP Service Agreement PDF and JotForm submission PDF to the Drive folder
5. Create the Asana task within the **Leasing Project** and apply the **TP Task Template**
6. **Create the Welcome Home Letter** for this property, populated with property-specific data from the JotForm: rent payment platform and instructions, move-in fund payment instructions (cashier's check or money order only), maintenance contact, utility provider info, any owner-specific property instructions

### 1.5 — Confirm 50% Payment Received

**Owner:** Onboarding Team Verify the 50% invoice has been paid. **Phase 1 cannot close until both the form is submitted AND the 50% is received.**

### 1.6 — Update HubSpot

**Owner:** Onboarding Team Move the HubSpot deal to the **"TP Onboarding"** stage. The deal remains in HubSpot post-placement for future nurture and full PM conversion.

### 1.7 — Handoff to Nicole

**Owner:** Onboarding Team → Nicole Send handoff notification to Nicole. Handoff package: link to the Asana project, link to the Drive folder, Buildium property record link, completed Welcome Home Letter (ready for delivery in Phase 5), summary of any flags or unusual owner requests from the JotForm.

**Phase 1 complete. Phase 2 (Pre-Listing Prep) begins.**

## TP-Specific Rules for Phase 2

### Labeling

Asana task is labeled `**TP — [Property Address]**` so the team visually distinguishes TP files from full-service PM leasing.

### No Vendor Coordination


:::warning
Sagareus does NOT schedule, coordinate, or manage any turnover vendors (cleaning, repairs, maintenance) for TP clients.

* Leasing Agent may **recommend** work
* Owner is **responsible** for executing and coordinating all vendors

:::

### Owner-Driven Turnover Timeline

Phase 2 begins the turnover cycle only when the owner informs the Leasing Agent the property is ready. The Leasing Agent then conducts the Turn Over Complete confirmation. If not ready, Leasing Agent informs the owner; the cycle repeats until the Leasing Agent deems the property ready.

### Photography Trigger

Leasing Agent schedules the professional photographer **only after** confirming turnover is complete.

# Phase 4 — Showings & Applications

**Trigger:** Phase 3 complete. Listing is live. **Owner:** Nicole (Leasing Agent)

## Showings

Showing procedures inherit from the standard Lease Up SOP — see [Conduct Showings](conduct-showings.md), [Speed to Lead](speed-to-lead.md), [Follow Up & Secure Application](follow-up-secure-application.md), and [Lease Up Policies](lease-up-policies.md) for conduct rules. The following TP-specific rules apply:

### Showing Limit

* Each TP engagement includes **25 showings**
* Additional showings are billed to the owner at **$50 per showing**
* Overages are **batched** and invoiced at close-out (Phase 7)

### Showing Documentation


:::warning
**Every showing must be logged.** Because overages generate billable charges, accurate showing documentation is mandatory.

Showings are tracked via the **Showings Log** (separate task list per property in Asana).

**Note:** Showings Log structure is being built as a follow-up to this SOP. Until built, Leasing Agent must manually track showing count on the master TP task in a dedicated field or comment thread.

:::

### Showing Count Reporting

The owner receives the running showing count as part of the Weekly Activity Report — see [Email | Owner - Weekly Activity Report](email-owner-weekly-activity-report.md).

### Approaching the 25-Showing Limit

At showing #20, Leasing Agent notifies the owner that the property is approaching the included-showings limit. *(Automated notification to be built as part of the Showings Log follow-up work.)*

## Applications

Application procedures inherit from the standard Lease Up SOP. The following TP-specific rules apply:

* Applications processed using the **TP Application Task Template** in Asana (not the standard LU Application Template)
* Screening procedures (credit, income verification, rental history, AI Summary) are identical to full PM standards

**Phase Complete When:** Application approved, applicant confirms intent to move forward, and the file is ready for lease preparation.

# Phase 5 — Lease Preparation & Signing

**Trigger:** Phase 4 complete. Application approved; ready to prepare lease. **Owners:** Leasing Assistants (execution) + Nicole or Brittany (Leasing Manager — internal review)


:::warning
**TP Lease Requirements — Non-Negotiable:**

* Must use the **RHAWA Lease Template** — do NOT use Sagareus-branded lease documents
* Lease is signed by **Tenant and Property Owner only** — NOT signed by the Leasing Manager
* Sagareus is not a party to the lease

:::

## Workflow

### 5.1 — Populate the TP Lease Template

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Leasing Assistants populate the TP Lease template (RHAWA base) in PandaDoc using data from Buildium, the JotForm submission, and the screening file.

### 5.2 — Internal Review

**Owner:** Nicole or Brittany (Leasing Manager) Nicole or Brittany reviews the lease before it is sent for signature. Confirm: correct property address and legal description; rent amount, lease term, and start date align with the application and owner's instructions; all owner-specific clauses from the JotForm have been incorporated; no Sagareus branding appears on the document.

### 5.3 — Send for Signature

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Send the lease via PandaDoc with **sequenced signing**:


1. **Owner signs first** (owner's signature = approval of lease terms — no separate approval step)
2. **Tenant signs second**

### 5.4 — Confirm Full Execution

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants + Nicole When PandaDoc confirms the lease is fully executed, both Leasing Assistants and Nicole are notified automatically.

### 5.5 — Deliver Welcome Home Letter to Tenant

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Leasing Assistants send the Welcome Home Letter (created by the Onboarding Team in Phase 1) to the tenant upon full lease execution.

### 5.6 — Assemble the Underwriting File

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Leasing Assistants create a Google Drive file containing: Application; AI Summary of applicant; credit report; rental history (if available); income verification.

### 5.7 — Deliver Underwriting File to Owner

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Send the complete Underwriting File to the owner via email using the **Underwriting File Delivery Email Template**. This happens **after** the lease is fully signed.

**Phase Complete When:** Both parties have signed in PandaDoc, Welcome Home Letter delivered to tenant, and Underwriting File delivered to owner.

# Phase 6 — Move-In

**Trigger:** Phase 5 complete. Lease fully signed. **Owner:** Nicole (Leasing Agent)


:::warning
**Financial Distinction — Critical:** Sagareus does NOT hold or deposit any owner or tenant funds. All move-in funds flow directly from the tenant to the owner via FedEx.

:::

## Workflow

### 6.1 — Coordinate Move-In

**Owner:** Nicole Nicole coordinates the move-in date and time **directly with the tenant**. Owner is not involved in scheduling; owner already knows move-in is being handled.

### 6.2 — Retrieve Keys

**Owner:** Nicole Nicole retrieves keys from the onsite property lockbox.

### 6.3 — Collect Move-In Funds


:::warning
**Move-In Fund Requirements:**

* **Cashier's check or money order ONLY** — no personal checks, no online payments
* Made payable to the **OWNER** (legal name as indicated on the JotForm Onboarding Form) — NOT to Sagareus
* Amount must be equal to or greater than the required move-in amount

:::

**Triple-confirm** the check/MO is made out correctly before accepting.

### 6.4 — Complete Move-In Inspection Report

**Owner:** Nicole Complete the Move-In Inspection Report with the tenant present using the standard inspection procedure — see [Move In](move-in.md) for walk-through steps, photo standards, and signature requirements.


:::warning
**Do NOT follow the LU Move-In payment flow.** The LU Move-In SOP assumes Sagareus holds funds (Buildium deposit, BofA, Resident Center). For TP, fund handling is cashier's-check/money-order + FedEx to owner — see Phase 6.3 and 6.6 in this doc. Only the inspection procedure itself is inherited from LU Move-In.

:::

### 6.5 — Documentation (CYA)

Nicole must document:


1. Photo of funds on the Inspection Report (serves as tenant's receipt)
2. Photo of funds + key + FedEx tracking receipt together
3. FedEx tracking number recorded in Asana and provided to owner

### 6.6 — Ship Funds to Owner via FedEx

Nicole ships the funds to the owner via **FedEx overnight** personally.

* **Same day** if time permits
* **Next business day morning at the latest** if same-day shipment is not possible due to time of day Goal: prevent lost checks or delays. Nicole goes to FedEx herself; this is not delegated.

## Error Protocols

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| Funds made out to wrong name | Postpone move-in. Tenant must return with corrected check. |
| Funds less than required amount | Postpone move-in until funds are accurate. |
| Funds more than required amount | Acceptable — proceed with move-in. |
| Personal check presented | Reject. Cashier's check or money order only. |

**Phase Complete When:** FedEx tracking confirms delivery to owner.

# Phase 7 — Close-Out

**Trigger:** Phase 6 complete. FedEx tracking confirms move-in funds delivered to owner. **Owners:** Nicole (final invoice + file ownership) + Leasing Assistants (admin close-out)

## Workflow

### 7.1 — Send Final Invoice

**Owner:** Nicole Nicole sends the final PandaDoc invoice. Invoice includes:

* Remaining **50% of the TP fee** ($1,000 of the $2,000 flat fee)
* Any additional showing charges beyond the 25-showing limit ($50 per overage showing, batched)

Nicole reviews the final showing count against the 25-limit before invoicing.

### 7.2 — Send Close Out Email

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Leasing Assistants send the **TP Close Out Email** to tenant and owner. Email includes the fully signed Move-In Condition Report (attached for records) and a 1-question survey link for client feedback.

> **Subject:** Closing Out // [Property Address]
>
> Hi [Tenant Name] & [Owner Name],
>
> Attached, please find the fully signed Move-In Condition Report for your records.
>
> It's been my pleasure working with you! Please tell me about your experience by taking this 1-question survey: [Survey Link]
>
> Thank you,

### 7.3 — Update HubSpot

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Change the HubSpot deal stage to **"TP Complete — Nurture"**. Deal remains in HubSpot for future nurture toward full PM conversion.

### 7.4 — Confirm Final Invoice Paid

**Owner:** Nicole Confirm final invoice (50% + any showing overages) has been paid before true file close.

### 7.5 — Close Asana File

**Owner:** Leasing Assistants Mark the TP master task complete in Asana. The Asana file is closed.

## What Happens After Close-Out (NOT part of this SOP)

Following close-out, the owner assumes full responsibility for: rent collection, maintenance coordination, lease enforcement, tenant communication.

The file enters the separate **Post-Placement Nurture Workflow** *(not yet written — see Asana)* for future full PM conversion opportunities.

**Phase Complete When:** Final invoice paid + Close Out email sent + HubSpot stage updated + Asana file closed.
