The weekly documented coaching call with each assigned Leasing Agent.

## Agenda

- How's it going? Issues or concerns up front?
- Review current listings / weekly updates (support, encouragement, feedback sandwich).
- Review recent and upcoming Move Ins / Move Outs / Turn Over confirmations.
- Review upcoming listings / PreListing emails sent.
- Review the team member's current task list: zero overdue tasks.

## Setup + Documentation

1. Recurring Google Meet on Monday or Tuesday each week; Leasing Manager and Leasing Lead as optional attendees.
2. Prep 10-15 minutes: compile the week's notes and observations.
3. Conduct the call with Gemini recording; the summary goes to all attendees.
4. After the call, copy the Gemini summary into Asana: Team Development Project, task "[Employee Name] // Weekly 1 on 1 - Development", due the date of the check-in, assigned to the Team Lead.
5. Call out exceptional and/or poor performance: subtask for the Leasing Manager with a summary and links to supporting tasks. Criteria: [Coaching & Escalation](leasing-team-lead-coaching-escalation.md).
