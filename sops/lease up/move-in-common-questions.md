Common questions and misunderstandings on move-in day. Part of [Move In](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-f0sLMcj1VF).

## Unit is not clean at Move In

1. Clean the unit as best you can for a more positive experience.
2. If no time, call Residents and offer 2 options: postpone move in by 1 day with a $150 rent credit for the inconvenience, or a cleaner comes post move in (date/time TBD).
3. Create subtask "Move In Not Clean - [Address]": make a list, lots of pictures, assign and tag the Leasing Assistant (M-F Mary, Sa-Su Jody), due today.

The specific label "Move In Not Clean" matters: at the start of each month we search it for the prior month to report which vendors are doing the best jobs, prioritize them for upcoming turnovers, and give feedback to the rest.

## Unit needs repairs at Move In

1. Create subtask "Post Move In Repairs // [Address]": make a list, lots of pictures.
2. Assign to the Turn Over Coordinator (Bart), tag Priority Dispatch.
3. Due date today. Reassure the Resident these repairs are top priority.

The specific label "Post Move In Repairs" matters for the same monthly vendor-quality report.

## Cannot receive Move In Funds in Buildium at Move In

1. Move In amounts have not posted yet; they post on the actual move in / lease start date.
2. If moving in early, hold funds until the following day to receive.

## Owner asks when they will receive funds from Move In

The owner is really asking when they will receive their Owner Distribution. Use the standard response.

## Unable to obtain Resident signature on Move In Report

1. Phone connections are sometimes unstable and the Inspector App will not capture the signature.
2. Tell Residents we will send the Move In Report for e-signature (same way as the lease) within 2 days.
3. Signing protects their security deposit: they are not charged for damages or conditions present at move in.

## Paid on Portal - Not approved for online payment

Cashier's check required unless portal payment is pre-approved (check the Move In task description). If a tenant pays on the portal without authorization: portal payments are pending, not cleared. Do NOT hand over the keys. They must either cancel the portal payment and obtain a cashier's check, or wait 1 to 2 business days for funds to clear and schedule another appointment. Keys are released only once funds are confirmed in Sagareus' account.
