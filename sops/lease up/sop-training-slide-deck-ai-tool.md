SOP Training Slide Deck – AI Tool


1. Google Apps → NotebookLM → Create new notebook → Add title
2. Choose 'Copied text'
3. Copy / paste directly from the relevant SOP in Outline
   1. *Only the portion you'd like to convert to a training*
4. NotebookLM will display output format options — select **Slide Deck**
5. Click the **Edit Pencil Icon** on the Slide Deck card to configure:
   1. Set format to **Presenter slides**
   2. Set length to **Short** (AI tends to be extra)
   3. Paste the SOP Training Slide Deck Prompt below into the custom instructions field


---


:::info
**Slide Deck Prompt** — Copy everything below this line into NotebookLM's custom instructions field.

:::

**Context**

Sagareus is a property management company focused on creating long-term value through Exceptional Property Management. Our mission is to give owners peace of mind, residents a positive living experience, and our team the tools to succeed. We operate with a business-first mindset and are guided by three core values: **Proactive > Reactive**, **Data is King**, and **Continuous Improvement**.

**Objective**

Create a **professional, instructional slide deck** for internal or external use that educates the audience on a specific topic, process, policy, or framework related to Sagareus operations or property management. The deck should support clarity, consistency, and alignment with Sagareus's mission and values.


---

**Style**

**Understated, clean, and functional** Strong hierarchy with minimal on-screen text. One core idea per slide. Designed to stand alone without verbal explanation. No decorative visuals or filler slides.

**Tone**

Approachable and educational. Slides should guide the viewer with clarity and confidence, positioning Sagareus as a disciplined, thoughtful operator.

**Audience**

Internal team members (training, onboarding, retraining). Property owners or partners (educational presentations). The experience should feel consistent regardless of the audience.


---

**Branding & Visual Design (mandatory)**

**Color Palette**

| Color | Hex |
|-------|-----|
| White | #FFFFFF |
| Light Grey | #CCD1D1 |
| Dark Grey | #616A6B |
| Emerald | #0B4F42 |
| Maroon (accent) | #4F190B |
| Gold (accent) | #D4AF37 |

* Accent colors (Maroon, Gold) are used sparingly for emphasis or alerts
* Neutral tones dominate all slides

**Typography**

* Approved fonts: **Inter** and **Lexend**
* Use size and weight — not excessive color — for hierarchy
* Maintain readability for both screen and projection


---

**Rules & Constraints**

**Content Rules**

* Use clear slide titles that state the takeaway
* Break complex information into steps or sections
* Include summary or "Key Takeaways" slides where appropriate


:::warning
* Reflect Sagareus values throughout:
  * Proactive thinking and ownership
  * Data-backed decisions and accountability
  * Continuous improvement of systems and outcomes

:::


---


:::tip
**Recommended Slide Structure**

* Title slide with topic and purpose
* Context or "Why This Matters" slide
* Section divider slides for major topics
* Process, framework, or policy slides
* Key takeaways / expectations slide
* Closing slide reinforcing mission and accountability

:::


---


:::info
**Output Expectations** Produce a **slide-by-slide outline** including:

* Slide titles
* Bullet or content structure per slide
* Notes on emphasis or hierarchy
* Clear guidance that allows direct build-out in PowerPoint, Google Slides, or Keynote

:::
