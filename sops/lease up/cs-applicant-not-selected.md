> **Situation:** Applicant is upset they were not selected for a property, or were beat to a unit by another applicant.


---

## Key Points

* **First complete application wins** — Applications are processed in the order they are marked complete (application form + fee + documents + follow-up responses)
* **Send from Leasing email** — Respond from leasing@sagareus.com
* **Formal complaint option** — Direct to [Customer Service Request Form](https://www.sagareus.com/contact-us) for management review within 72 business hours
* **Document response** — Print sent email as PDF and upload to applicant's Asana task


---

## Response

Send from [Leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:Leasing@sagareus.com):

> Hi \[Applicant Name\],
>
> Thank you for your interest in the property and for taking the time to apply. We understand how disappointing it can be to learn a property has been leased, especially after submitting an application and supporting documents.
>
> We often receive multiple applications for a single property, and we process them in the order they are marked **complete**. An application is considered **complete** when all required applicants have:
> • Submitted the application form
> • Paid the application fee
> • Provided all supporting documents
> • Responded to any follow-up questions to clarify discrepancies
>
> If another application is completed and approved while others are still in review, we are required to move forward with the first qualified applicant. Once a lease is signed, we are no longer able to proceed with additional applications for that unit.
>
> We truly appreciate the time and effort you invested. If you would like to be notified of similar listings, we're happy to keep your information on file. If you'd like to submit a formal complaint regarding the application process, [you may do so here.](https://www.sagareus.com/contact-us) This feedback form will trigger management to review the issue and respond to you with findings within 72 business hours.
>
> Thank you again, and we wish you the best in your housing search.
>
> Best regards,
