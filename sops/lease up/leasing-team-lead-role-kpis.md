What the Leasing Team Lead owns and how leadership performance is measured.

:::info
The Team Lead function: develop team members into confident and capable Leasing Agents; champion Sagareus SOPs and policies (prevent chaos); document weekly calls; on-call support for real-time issues; report exceptional and subpar performance.
:::

## Key Responsibilities

- **Leasing production** — maintain an active portfolio of assigned listings; meet or exceed all Leasing Agent KPIs (lead by example).
- **Team leadership** — coach, train, and support 1-4 Leasing Agents with weekly documented [1:1 check-ins](leasing-team-lead-weekly-1-on-1s.md); assign and balance workloads; ensure agents meet KPIs and manage listings Pre-Move-Out through Move-In in full compliance.
- **Quality control** — reinforce SOPs and expectations; spot-check files, communications, and documentation; identify gaps and coach ([Coaching & Escalation](leasing-team-lead-coaching-escalation.md)), requiring agents to correct and resubmit rather than taking tasks over.
- **Support and escalation** — help with complex scenarios, owner communication, and edge cases; assist SOP training; escalate unresolved performance, compliance, or risk issues to the Leasing Manager with context and documentation.
- **Collaboration** — primary liaison between Leasing Agents and the Leasing Manager; leadership meetings; coordinate with Operations, Customer Success, and Accounting; calm presence under pressure.

## KPIs

- Personal listing performance meeting Leasing Agent KPIs, adjusted for volume and leadership scope.
- Weekly 1:1 coaching sessions conducted and documented per assigned agent.
- All assigned agents meet minimum Leasing Agent KPIs within 90 days of assignment.
- SOP adherence maintained across the team with no material compliance violations.
- Routine issues resolved at the Team Lead level; escalations include context, documentation, and a recommended solution.
