Steps for verifying and fixing Zillow listings. Referenced during the weekly listing check.

## Check the Zillow Listing


1. Leasing Assistant checks the Zillow listing **2 days after posting** to confirm it syndicated correctly.
2. Leasing Team Lead reviews the Zillow listing weekly as part of the Weekly Activity Report.


---

## Common Issues

### Not Posted


1. Verify the property address is correct in Buildium — unit number must be on the second line of the address field.
2. Confirm listing details are accurate (price, description).
3. **If the property is also listed for sale** — the for-sale listing overrides the rental. Ask the owner to de-list the property.
4. **If the property has 25+ units** — see the 25+ Unit Properties section below.
5. Email [priorityrentalshelp@zillowgroup.com](mailto:priorityrentalshelp@zillowgroup.com) for fast resolution.

### Wrong Property Type

Most common: showing as a townhouse when it's a condo.


1. In Buildium, update Property Type to **"Multi-Family"** instead of "Condo/Townhouse."
2. The unit will then appear as an apartment on Zillow.

### Duplicate Listing

Contact Zillow support to consolidate or remove duplicates.
