How to verify a Zillow listing and get it fixed when it is missing or wrong. Used in step 12 of the Lease Up Asana SOP (2 days after posting) and weekly from the Activity Report tool's **Verify Listings** buttons.

:::info
**The fix path is simple: check the common issues below, correct what you can in Buildium, then email Zillow. Zillow fixes syndication problems on their end; you do not need to keep tinkering. Email [priorityrentalshelp@zillowgroup.com](mailto:priorityrentalshelp@zillowgroup.com) and they will resolve it, usually within 1 business day.**
:::

## When to Check

1. **2 days after posting** — confirm the listing syndicated from Buildium ([12 — Confirm | Zillow Listing Active](12-confirm-zillow-listing-active-2-days-after-listing.md)).
2. **Every week** — tap the Verify Listings buttons in the Activity Report tool (https://www.sagareus.com/field) and confirm price, photos, description, and availability are live and accurate.

## Procedure

1. Open the listing from the Verify Listings buttons (or search the address on Zillow).
2. **Listing is live and accurate** — done. Mark Zillow / Redfin Verified in the Activity Report tool.
3. **Listing is missing or wrong** — run the common-issues check below and fix what is on our side in Buildium.
4. **Then email Zillow.** Whatever is left after step 3 is a syndication problem on Zillow's side, and only Zillow can fix it. Send the email below and move on; check back the next business day.

:::info
**To:** priorityrentalshelp@zillowgroup.com

**Subject:** Syndication issue | [Property Address]

Hi Zillow team,

Our rental listing at [Property Address] is [not appearing / showing incorrect details] on Zillow. It is posted and accurate in Buildium ([listing rent], available [date]). We have verified the address format and listing details on our side.

Please correct the syndication for this listing.

Thank you,
[Name], Sagareus Property Management
:::

## Common Issues Check

### Not Posted

1. **Address format in Buildium** — the unit number must be on the second line of the address field.
2. **Listing details** — price and description accurate in Buildium.
3. **Property also listed for sale?** The for-sale listing overrides the rental. The owner must de-list the sale before the rental can appear.
4. **25+ units?** Syndication does not apply; see the section below.

### Wrong Property Type

Most common: showing as a townhouse when it is a condo. In Buildium, set Property Type to **"Multi-Family"** instead of "Condo/Townhouse"; the unit will then appear as an apartment on Zillow.

### Duplicate Listing

Nothing to fix in Buildium. Email Zillow (template above) and ask them to consolidate or remove the duplicate.

## 25+ Unit Properties (Stone Court, Adams Apartments, Holly Apts)

Properties with 25+ units are covered by a Zillow marketing agreement and **do not syndicate from Buildium**. Manage these listings manually on Zillow.com.
