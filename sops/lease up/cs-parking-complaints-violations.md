> **Situation:** Resident reports a parking complaint, violation, or needs to change their parking assignment.


---

## Key Points

* **Route to Leasing team** — Leasing manages parking lot assignments and enforcement
* **Common issues include:**
  * Abandoned or junk car
  * Unlicensed or untagged vehicle
  * Working on car in parking lot / making a mess
  * Taking up 2 spaces
  * Someone parking in their assigned space
  * Wants to add/remove parking from lease
  * Requests a different spot / parking reassignment


:::info
**Vehicle change (new car, sold car, plate update)?** This is not a parking complaint — handle directly using the procedure in [Parking + Storage 2.0 > Resident reports new car / replacement car](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-parking-storage.md).

:::
