Part of [Move In](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-f0sLMcj1VF). Post Move In is the final set of subtasks in Move In and the final tasks in the entire Lease Up Process. Completed by Admin and Accounting Teams: closes out the Leasing 2.0 parent task, ensures Residents and property are in compliance, ledgers are balanced, and owners are invoiced for the Leasing service.

## Review Move In Report; Share with Resident + Owner

* Admin Team double checks the Move In Report is uploaded to Buildium; if missing, locate it in HappyCo.
* Ensure Resident signature. If signed: download from Buildium, upload and share with Residents and owners, send email to each. If not signed: download and send to Residents via PandaDoc for e-signature (add signature for one Resident, backdate to move in date), and follow up until signed. Template: "Upon reviewing your recent move in, I realize we failed to obtain your signature on the Move In Condition Report. Could you please review and e-sign at your earliest convenience?"

## Enter Appliance Details

1. Enter appliance model numbers into the Buildium Appliance Inventory section and mailbox detail into the Custom Fields section.

## Add Resident to Hubspot (Post Move In)

Customer Service Specialist adds Residents to HubSpot for ongoing Resident feedback surveys.

1. Copy/paste resident details from Asana into HubSpot.
2. HubSpot: Contacts, Add Contact, paste name, email, phone ("create + add another" for multiple).
3. From the contact profile, Add Deal: address as the Deal Name, Deal Pipeline = Residents, Deal Stage = Post Move In, add all residents to the Deal, Create.
