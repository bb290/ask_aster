The six-month ramp from new hire to independent Leasing Agent.

## Training Milestones

### Month 1

- Demonstrates SOP comprehension; references the SOP doc **before** asking.
- 100% of Weekly Activity Reports delivered to the Team Lead by 12pm Tuesdays.

### Month 2

- KPIs within acceptable variance.
- Manages basic Lease Up tasks independently: showings, applicant FAQs, weekly reports via the Activity Report tool, updating listings.

### Month 3

- Move Ins / Move Outs executed without deviation from SOP.
- **Approved for Owner Communication | Weekly Activity Report.**
- Consistent KPI performance; Annual Inspections as assigned.

### Month 4

- Complete Lease Up Advanced Training.
- Complete 10 practice listings using the PreListing tool, sent to the Team Lead: draft listing, market rent, PreListing email.

### Month 5

- 10 more practice listings sent to the Team Lead.
- Post + assign a Lease Up Task to self.

### Month 6

- **Approved for Owner Communication | PreListing Email Template.**
- Monitor and post listings to minimize delays.
