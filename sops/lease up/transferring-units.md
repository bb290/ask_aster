:::warning
A unit transfer is not a shortcut. It's two clean transactions executed back-to-back, with zero financial overlap and zero legal blur.

Our goal is to manage inter-portfolio moves without breaking leases, breaking trust, or breaking compliance.

* Approval must be obtained
* A new lease must be signed
* Move Out / Move In are scheduled per normal procedures
* New security deposit is paid; previous deposit is refunded per normal procedures

:::


---

## Unit Transfer Policies


:::warning
**Sagareus DOES NOT transfer security deposit between units**

* A new Security Deposit will be collected and due at move-in for the new unit, per standard procedure.
* Current Security Deposit will be refunded within 30 days of move-out, per standard procedure.

**Sagareus DOES NOT revise existing lease**

* A Lease Termination Addendum will be required if ending current lease early.
* A new Lease Agreement will need to be signed for the new property.

:::

## Transfer Procedures

### [Initial Inquiry - All Staff Standard Response](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-customer-service.md)


:::info
If a current resident inquires about transferring to another unit within their property or another Sagareus managed property, all staff members should use the following standard response.

:::

**Response**

> **Subject:** Request to Transfer Units
>
> Hi [Resident Name],
>
> Thank you for reaching out about your interest in transferring to another unit under Sagareus Management. We're happy to help you explore this option.
>
> I've assigned your request to our [Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md), who will confirm eligibility and coordinate the next steps with you.
>
> **Important Notes:**
> A new Security Deposit will be collected and due at move-in for the new unit. Your current Security Deposit will be refunded within 30 days of move-out, per standard procedure. A Lease Termination Addendum will be required if ending your current lease early. A new Lease Agreement will need to be signed for the new property.
>
> We appreciate you choosing to continue your tenancy with Sagareus and look forward to supporting your move.
>
> Warm regards,

**Action**


1. Create Asana Task
   * Project: Resident Relations 2.0
   * TASK TEMPLATE: "Unit Transfer // Current Address to New Address"
   * Section: Negotiations
   * Assigned to: [Renewal & Rent Increase Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/renewal-rent-increase-coordinator.md)
   * Screenshot relevant email communications
   * Include any notes

## [Transfer Tasks Executed by Renewal Specialist](../resident relations/resident-relations.md)


:::warning
Transfer is approved if **ALL** conditions are met:

* **NOT** between Sept 16 and March 31 (winter vacancy policy)
* [Operations Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/operations-manager.md) approval to address owner specific concerns
* Tenant has 1 or less late payments or bounced payments
* Tenant has lived in the same unit for over 2 years

:::

### IF Approved for Unit Transfer


1. Send Approved for Transfer Email
2. Draft Lease Termination Addendum, if applicable
3. Start Standard Notice to Vacate Procedures
4. Create Asana Task
   * Project: [Leasing 2.0](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1208297375044026/list/1208297386032994)
   * Section: Pending Applications
   * TASK TEMPLATE: "Application // <ADDRESS>"
     * RENAME → Approved for Transfer // <New Address>
     * Convert to Subtask of existing "LU | New Address" task
   * Description:
     * Input Resident Contact Details
   * Assigned to: Leasing Assistant (Mary or Bryan)
   * Due: Today
   * Screenshot communication, upload to comments

#### Email Template - Approved for Unit Transfer

> **Subject:** Unit Transfer - APPROVED!
>
> Hi [Resident Name],
>
> Your request to transfer to another unit under Sagareus Management has been **approved**.
>
> Please confirm your desired **move-out date** for your current address and **move-in date** for the new property.
>
> Once we have these dates, we will:
> Send a Lease Termination Addendum for your current lease.
> Send a Lease Agreement for the new property.
> Assign an agent to schedule your move-in once the lease is signed.
>
> **Important Reminders:**
> A new Security Deposit will be collected and due at move-in for the new unit. Your current Security Deposit will be refunded within 30 days of move-out, per standard procedure.
>
> We look forward to supporting your move and making this transition as smooth as possible.
>
> Best,

## [Approved for Unit Transfer Executed](../applicant screening/approved-lease-negotiation-signing.md) by Leasing Assistant (Mary/Bryan)


:::info
When a current resident is approved for unit transfer:


1. Leasing Assistant receives standard application task, renamed to "Approved for Transfer // Property address"
   * This should be converted to subtask of existing LU | Task per usual
2. These residents are already approved for Lease
   * Skip initial screening tasks
   * Proceed from "Execute Lease" task
   * Follow standard Lease Signing / Move In procedures
     * Residents **ARE APPROVED** for online deposit payment

:::
