Capture a walk-through video of the unit and post it to Google Drive. The Brandtegic YouTube version is handled separately in [13 — Request | Video from Brandtegic](lease-up-asana-sop.md).


:::warning
Do not record the listing video until the unit is clean and presentable. Push the task due date until it is ready.

:::

## Record the Video

**Prep the unit:**


1. Turn on all lights and open blinds.
2. Put all toilet lids down.
3. Open all interior and closet doors.

**Shooting tips:**

* Start outside the front door and walk through naturally, as if giving a tour.
* Hold phone in **vertical** orientation.
* Use a **stabilizing gimbal** for smooth footage. [How to use gimbal](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1zN79n61NKKKQVFEWtDlXyI3KgQiUEJ_r/view?usp=drive_link)
* Use **wide-angle** camera setting (0.5x on iPhone).
* Move slowly and smoothly. Walk in straight lines; pause and pan before moving to the next room.
* Target **2–3 minutes** maximum. Match the Move Out Video walkthrough path where possible.


---

## Upload to Google Drive


1. Open the LU Asana task.
2. Click the blue **File Link** in the task.
   * If the link is not blue (not yet created), assign to Leasing Assistant with a comment to create the link.
3. Clicking the link opens the Google Drive folder for the property.
4. Tap **+** in the lower right corner → Upload File → Photos & Videos → select your video → Upload.
5. Mark task complete.
