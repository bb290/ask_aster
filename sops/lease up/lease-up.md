:::warning
A vacant property earns nothing. Every day between a move-out and a signed lease is lost revenue for the owner and a missed placement fee for Sagareus. Rushed or poorly documented listings result in unqualified applicants, extended vacancy, and owner frustration.

:::

* Owners depend on Sagareus to minimize vacancy while placing a qualified Resident who will protect their investment.
* Prospective Residents deserve accurate listings, responsive communication, and a professional showing experience.
* Lease-up fees are a direct revenue source for Sagareus — fast, high-quality placements demonstrate competency and drive referrals.


---

## Policies


:::warning
* A Sagareus Leasing Staff member must accompany every showing. Unattended showings are never permitted.
* Professional photos are required for every listing. No exceptions. Escalate to the Leasing Manager if an owner refuses.
* Do not stop showing a property until a fully executed lease is signed. Verbal commitments, approved applications, and pending paperwork do not secure a unit.
* Properties may be listed no more than 4 weeks prior to Move Out.
* Facebook Marketplace listings are not part of Sagareus's standard marketing strategy.

:::

## Lifecycle Overview


:::info
The Lease Up process begins as soon as a Move Out date is confirmed. Pre-leasing starts immediately if photos are available; the listing activates the moment the unit is rent-ready and Turn Over is confirmed complete.

:::

### PreLease Flow (Unit Still Occupied)

All steps above apply. Additionally:

* Steps 1–2 begin up to **4 weeks before Move Out**, as soon as photos are available.
* Set the **availability date** in Buildium to the expected move-in date — do not set to today.
* Step 7 (Go Live) is delayed until the unit passes Turn Over.
* See [Lease Up Policies](lease-up-policies.md) for occupied showing and sandbag approach guidelines.
