Update the Lease Up funnel metrics on the Asana task so the team and owners see accurate conversion data.

## Purpose

The Lease Up funnel — inquiries → showings → applications — is the core leasing KPI. Accurate counts let us diagnose conversion problems (Respond/Speed to Lead vs. Follow Up) and inform owner recommendations in the Weekly Activity Report.

## When This Runs

1. **Weekly** — right after building the Weekly Activity Report. You just gathered this week's numbers for the report; add them to the running totals on the task.
2. **At lease signing** — one final update so the closed task carries the complete funnel.

## Fields to Update

On the LU Asana parent task, each field is the **cumulative total since list date**:

* **# of Inquiries**
* **# of Showings**
* **# of Applications**

## Procedure

1. Take this week's counts from the same sources you used in the Activity Report tool: inquiries from the Buildium inbox/leads, showings from the showing calendar, applications from the Buildium applicants list.
2. Add them to the current values on the LU task fields (running totals, not weekly snapshots).
3. Owner: the **Leasing Agent** who builds the Weekly Activity Report.
