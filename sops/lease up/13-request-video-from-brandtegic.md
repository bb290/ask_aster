Submit the Brandtegic YouTube Video Request Form once the listing video has been captured and uploaded.


---

### Purpose

Brandtegic produces a polished YouTube version of the walk-through video that we reuse across current and future listings of the property. Posting on YouTube maximizes search discoverability and gives owners a professional asset for their listing.


---

### When This Runs

After [10 — Record | Listing Video](10-record-listing-video.md) has uploaded the raw video to Google Drive. Assigned to Leasing Assistant as a +5 subtask during [8 — Assign | Task to Agent & Move to Active Section](lease-up-asana-sop.md).


---

### Procedure (Leasing Assistant)


1. Open the [Brandtegic YouTube Video Request Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScxN3fbLIoEI_i9tsb9H_44L9IrOO-VN3TS8G0muSG-gV5X6g/viewform).
2. Open the LU Asana task → find the **Listing Video File** link → use those assets when filling out the form.
3. Submit the form.
4. Once you receive the YouTube link from Brandtegic:
   * Open the unit in Buildium.
   * Upload the YouTube link to the Unit Photo section (keep it even if the unit is later de-listed).
5. Send the owner notification email below.


:::info
**Email Template — YouTube Video Live**

Hi [Owner Name],

We're excited to share that the property tour video for your rental is now live on our YouTube channel!

Watch it here: [INSERT LINK]

This video will be reused for future listings of the property, maximizing exposure and keeping marketing efforts streamlined.

:::
