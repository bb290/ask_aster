:::info
Follow these steps in order from top to bottom — they map to the subtasks in the Asana Lease Up task.

Pre-listing steps (1–2) are handled by the Leasing Team Lead. Active listing steps (3–14) are handed off to the Leasing Agent at activation.

:::


 1. [1 — Prepare | BD Listing](1-prepare-bd-listing.md) — gather all property details and fill in the LU task description and rental policies template
 2. [2 — Email | Owner - Market Rent & PreListing](2-email-owner-market-rent-prelisting.md) — set data-backed pricing, establish listing strategy, and send PreListing Email to owner
 3. [3 — Decide | Schedule photo? (y/n)](3-decide-schedule-photo-y-n.md) — decide whether to schedule a new photo shoot (TODO criteria)
 4. [4 — Upload | Photos to Buildium & Google Drive](4-upload-photos-to-buildium-google-drive.md) — confirm photos are ready and upload to both platforms
 5. [5 — Prepare | 📐 Floor Plan](5-prepare-floor-plan.md) — prepare floor plan graphic for the listing (TODO)
 6. [6 — Decide | Occupied showings? (y/n)](6-decide-occupied-showings-y-n.md) — decide whether to allow occupied showings (TODO criteria)
 7. [7 — Go Live | Buildium Listing & Update list_date metric](7-go-live-buildium-listing-update-list-date-metric.md) — post the listing and record list_date
 8. [8 — Assign | Task to Agent & Move to Active Section](8-assign-task-to-agent-move-to-active-section.md) — assign parent task and subtasks to the team
 9. [9 — Email | Owner - Property Listed Notification](9-email-owner-property-listed-notification.md) — formally notify the owner the listing is live
10. [10 — Record | Listing Video](10-record-listing-video.md) — capture walk-through video and upload to Google Drive
11. [11 — Respond | ⚡ Speed to Lead](11-respond-speed-to-lead.md) — respond to every inquiry within minutes
12. [12 — Confirm | Zillow Listing Active (2 days after listing)](12-confirm-zillow-listing-active-2-days-after-listing.md) — verify Zillow syndication 2 days after Go Live
13. [13 — Request | Video from Brandtegic](13-request-video-from-brandtegic.md) — submit the Brandtegic YouTube Video Request Form
14. [14 — Update | # of Inquiries, # of showings, # of applications metrics](14-update-of-inquiries-of-showings-of-applications-metrics.md) — update funnel metrics on the Asana task (TODO cadence)

### Active Listing Playbook

These docs cover ongoing activity during the active listing period. They are not numbered subtasks in the Asana template -- they span the full listing lifecycle.

* [Conduct Showings](conduct-showings.md) — prepare the unit, run a professional showing, leave with a next step confirmed
* [Follow Up & Secure Application](follow-up-secure-application.md) — guide every prospect to a submitted application
* [Email | Owner - Weekly Activity Report](email-owner-weekly-activity-report.md) — keep owners informed every Tuesday by 12pm until lease is signed
