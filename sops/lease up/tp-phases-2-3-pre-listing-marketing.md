## Phase 2 — Pre-Listing Prep: TP-Specific Rules

* Labeling: the Asana task is labeled "TP — [Property Address]" so the team visually distinguishes TP files from full-service PM leasing.
* No vendor coordination: Sagareus does NOT schedule, coordinate, or manage any turnover vendors (cleaning, repairs, maintenance) for TP clients. The Leasing Agent may recommend work; the owner is responsible for executing and coordinating all vendors.
* Owner-driven turnover timeline: Phase 2 begins only when the owner says the property is ready. The Leasing Agent then conducts the Turn Over Complete confirmation; if not ready, inform the owner and repeat until the Agent deems it ready.
* Photography trigger: schedule the professional photographer only after confirming turnover is complete.

Standard LU procedures inherited: rent comps / pricing analysis (Market Rent & Listing Strategy), Turn Over confirmation, Pre-Listing Email, photography scheduling, listing prep materials.

Phase 2 complete when: Turn Over confirmation passed, photography captured, listing materials prepared.

## Phase 3 — Listing & Marketing

Fully inherits from the standard Lease Up SOP. One TP-specific note: photos live in the property's Google Drive folder; skip the Buildium photo upload step (full-service PM only).

Phase 3 complete when: listing is live across all standard marketing platforms and showings can be scheduled.
