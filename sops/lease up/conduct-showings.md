Prepare the unit, run a professional showing, and leave with a next step confirmed.

## Before the Showing


1. **Confirm the appointment** a few hours before:

   > *"Hi! Just confirming our appointment at [Address] at 6pm today. Please let me know if you're unable to make it, as I'll be driving from Bellevue."*


:::tip
   Personalizing the message reminds prospects your time is valuable and increases the chance they'll cancel rather than no-show.

   :::
2. **Arrive 10–15 minutes early** and prep the unit:
   * Turn on all lights
   * Open all closet doors
   * Adjust climate: heat on, or windows/ceiling fans open
   * Check for any last-minute cleaning or staging needs


---

## When Prospect Arrives

**Open house style (multiple prospects):**


1. Greet each guest at the main entrance.
2. Invite them to walk through freely.
3. Be available to answer questions; maintain an approachable presence.

**Individual appointment:**


1. Greet the prospect and ask: *"Would you like me to show you around, or would you prefer to take a look on your own?"*
2. If touring together — walk through and highlight key features (kitchen layout, natural light, storage, upgrades).
3. If self-touring — wait in the entry or kitchen while they look around.

**On multi-unit properties**, also point out:

* Parking areas
* Storage units
* Trash and recycling locations
* Fitness rooms or shared amenities


---

## Before They Leave


1. Make sure they have the **application link**.
2. Ask: *"How does this home compare to others you've looked at?"*
3. Thank them and walk them out.


---

## After the Showing


1. **Log the interaction in Asana:**
   * Record feedback, questions, and any upgrade requests.
   * Use notes to inform owner updates and justify future price changes.
2. **Schedule a follow-up task** for the next 1–2 days.
3. If the unit isn't a fit, **suggest other Sagareus listings** that better match their needs.


---

## Secure the Property Before Leaving


1. Turn off lights and heat.
2. Close all windows and blinds.
3. Lock the door.
4. Return key to the key box.
