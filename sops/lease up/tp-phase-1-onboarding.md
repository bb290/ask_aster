Trigger: Tenant Placement Service Agreement signed in PandaDoc. Owners: Onboarding Team (lead execution) + Nicole (handoff recipient). Phase complete when: JotForm submitted + 50% invoice paid + Asana TP Task Template applied + Buildium record created + Google Drive folder auto-created by JotForm + HubSpot deal moved to "TP Onboarding" + Nicole notified. Part of the [Tenant Placement SOP](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/tenant-placement-sop-0Ho4i49zol).

## Workflow

1.1 — PandaDoc signature received (Day 0). Monitor the signed notification. The client is automatically redirected from PandaDoc to the JotForm Tenant Placement Onboarding Form after signature.

1.2 — Send Welcome Email + 50% invoice (Day 0, within 2 hours). Use the TP Welcome + Onboarding Invoice template. Must include: confirmation of the signed agreement, the JotForm link, the 50% invoice (manual send), and a note that listing prep begins once the form is complete and the 50% is received.

1.3 — Follow-up cadence if JotForm not submitted: Day 2 gentle nudge (Onboarding Team); Day 5 second nudge, listing prep cannot begin until the form is complete; Day 8 Nicole calls the owner directly; Day 14 escalation to leadership, file flagged for engagement decision.

1.4 — Process JotForm submission: review for completeness and chase missing fields; migrate property and owner data into Buildium; verify the Drive folder auto-created; save the signed agreement PDF and JotForm PDF to the folder; create the Asana task in the Leasing Project with the TP Task Template; create the Welcome Home Letter populated from the JotForm (rent payment platform, move-in fund instructions - cashier's check or money order only, maintenance contact, utility info, owner-specific instructions).

1.5 — Confirm 50% payment received. Phase 1 cannot close until BOTH the form is submitted AND the 50% is received.

1.6 — Update HubSpot: move the deal to "TP Onboarding". The deal stays in HubSpot post-placement for nurture and full PM conversion.

1.7 — Handoff to Nicole: Asana link, Drive folder link, Buildium record link, completed Welcome Home Letter (delivered to tenant in Phase 5), and a summary of any flags or unusual owner requests from the JotForm.
