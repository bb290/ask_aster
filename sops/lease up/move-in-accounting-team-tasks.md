The Accounting Team's move-in tasks, each owned by the role in angle brackets. Part of [Move In](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-f0sLMcj1VF).

## Set up utilities for the Resident (Utility Manager)

* Check the move-in date. Reach out to utilities on the day of move in to confirm setup.
* Check the lease to confirm which accounts should be taken over by the Residents directly (attach a screenshot of the utility takeover specification to the task). Usually: electricity, gas, garbage.
* Set up each utility on behalf of the Resident starting on the lease start date. For PSE, SCL, TPU this can usually be done via the utility company website, or call in.
* Send the utility setup notification email to the Resident: accounts opened in their name starting on the official move-in date; no further action needed.

## Add Move In Funds to Dashboard (Office Manager)

* Tracks that move in funds are received and ensures security deposits are transferred to the liability account.

## Charge Lease Up Fee to Owner (Accounting Manager)

* Accounting Team invoices the owner for the Lease Up Fee.

## Unblock tenant online payment (Resident Account Manager)

* Once move-in funds have been received and deposited, the tenant may submit ongoing payments online.
* If online payments were blocked at move in, unblock: Buildium, Lease Profile, Financials tab, Payment Settings subtab, Payment Methods, "Override payment methods", check "EFT / eCheck" and "Credit Card", save.

## Verify Resident Insurance + assess fee (Resident Account Manager)

1. Verify the Resident has provided renter's insurance.
2. If not, request proof and add the monthly fee until the Resident provides proof of insurance. Template: request a copy of the current renters insurance policy or certificate of insurance; if none, they must obtain a policy promptly and provide proof.

## Verify rent paid next month (Resident Account Manager)

1. Prioritize rent collection the following month to prevent new Residents from falling behind.
