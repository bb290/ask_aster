:::info
Approximately 6 months in the role, Leasing Agents are expected to operate independently and consistently meet Leasing Agent KPIs, fully capable of managing a rental listing from **Pre-Move-Out through Move-In**.
:::

Leasing Agents may continue as **individual contributors** or pursue a **Team Lead** role overseeing a pod of 1-4 Leasing Agents. Compensation for the two paths is intentionally comparable: advancement into leadership is driven by a desire to coach, develop, and support others, not by compensation. Both paths are meaningful, respected ways to grow.
