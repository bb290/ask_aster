:::info
Approximately 6 months in the role, Leasing Agents are expected to operate independently and consistently meet Leasing Agent KPIs. At this stage, agents should be fully capable of managing a rental listing from **Pre-Move-Out through Move-In** with proven strategies to minimize vacancy, maximize rental income while maintaining high standards for applicant quality and resident experience.

:::

Leasing Agents may continue as **individual contributors** or choose to pursue a **Team Lead** role, overseeing a pod of 1–4 Leasing Agents. Compensation for these two paths is intentionally comparable. Advancement into leadership is driven by a desire to coach, develop, and support others, not by compensation.


:::tip
Sagareus values high-performing individual contributors equally to team leaders. Both paths represent meaningful, respected ways to grow within the organization, based on strengths, interests, and long-term career goals.

:::

## Leasing Agent

### Key Responsibilities

**Leasing Process**

* Establish market rent using Sagareus pricing guidelines, neighborhood comps, property condition, turnover timelines, and seasonal trends.
* Publish rental listings and ensure they meet all Sagareus Listing Standards, including approved marketing copy, professional photos, required disclosures, and consistent formatting.
* Review listing performance weekly and make updates as needed (pricing adjustments, feature highlights, photo updates, or copy revisions) to maximize visibility and application flow.
* Conduct Move Out Property Inspections to document condition, inform turnover work orders, and become familiar with property features and amenities.
* Create Property Tour video for marketing team.
* Respond to all incoming inquiries via email, messenger, text or phone.
* Schedule showings for self and/or showing team.
* Conduct property tours, highlighting key features and amenities.
* Communicate lease terms and conditions between property owner & prospective tenants while ensuring compliance with company policies and local regulations.
* Provide exceptional customer service throughout the leasing process.
* Explain entire Application, Lease Signing, and Move-In processes.
* Meet tenants for Move-In.
* Create Move-In Condition Report; collect move-in funds; provide keys to tenants; mail keys to main office; and deposit move-in funds at Bank of America as needed.

**Documentation and Record-Keeping**

* Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of leasing activities, showing feedback, and prospective tenant communications.
* Send Weekly Leasing Activity Report by Tuesday at 12pm
  * To property owners and Leasing Team Lead including # of inquiries, # of showings, showing feedback, market insights, and recommendations on pricing or property issues.
* Track & report personal hours, mileage & receipts for reimbursements.

**Collaboration**

* Collaborate with colleagues to address operational issues and improve processes.
* Participate in weekly 1:1 Google Meet syncs with Leasing Team Lead for ongoing training, support, and process improvement.
* Coordinate Move-In with Leasing Assistant for a seamless transition.

**Operations Team Support**

* Conduct Annual Inspections as assigned.
* Assist with miscellaneous field tasks as needed/assigned, such as posting legal notices, checking on vacant units, delivering supplies, or retrieving items from the field.

### Leasing Agent | KPIs


1. Move Out Inspections complete within 48 hrs of assigned move out date
2. 100% Activity Reports delivered by Tuesday at 12pm
3. 6 Move Ins per Month per Leasing Agent
4. 9+ average customer ratings (Post Move In)
5. 1 five star Google review per month per Agent ($50/review)

## Leasing Team Lead

### Key Responsibilities

**Leasing Production**

* Maintain an active portfolio of assigned rental listings
* Meet or exceed all Leasing Agent KPIs (Lead by example)

**Team Leadership & Development**

* Coach, train, and support 1–4 Leasing Agents, providing weekly documented 1:1 check-ins focused on performance, development, and accountability.
* Assign and manage agent workloads, including listing assignments, to ensure balanced coverage and efficient execution.
* Ensure assigned agents meet or exceed Leasing KPIs and properly manage listings from Pre-Move-Out through Move-In in full compliance with Sagareus standards.
* Reinforce SOPs, workflows, compliance requirements, and performance expectations across the team.
* Review listing performance and agent activity regularly and spot-check files, communications, and documentation for accuracy and compliance.
* Identify performance gaps and provide clear, timely coaching, requiring agents to correct and resubmit work rather than taking tasks over.
* Support agents with complex leasing scenarios, owner communication, and edge cases.
* Assist with training on new or updated SOPs and support Leasing Manager initiatives related to scaling, consistency, and risk reduction.
* Escalate unresolved performance, compliance, or risk issues to the Leasing Manager with appropriate context and documentation.

**Collaboration & Communication**

* Serve as the primary liaison between Leasing Agents and the Leasing Manager
* Participate in leadership meetings and communicate updates to assigned agents
* Coordinate with Operations, Customer Success & Accounting Team as needed
* Maintain a calm, professional presence during high-volume or high-pressure periods
* [Assign Leasing 2.0 Subtasks in Asana](https://www.loom.com/share/81142e49893d4b7eaeee67b13578482e?sid=eb325db6-431e-46b6-8336-d212d9041fa1) (video)

### Leasing Team Lead | KPIs

* Personal listing performance that meets Leasing Agent KPIs, adjusted for assigned listing volume and leadership scope.
* Weekly 1:1 coaching sessions are conducted and documented for each assigned Leasing Agent.
* All assigned Leasing Agents meet minimum Leasing Agent KPIs within 90 days of assignment.
* SOP adherence is consistently maintained across the assigned team with no material compliance violations.
* Routine leasing issues are resolved at the Team Lead level, and any escalations to the Leasing Manager include clear context, supporting documentation, and a recommended solution.

### Entropy


:::info
* The measure of disorder, randomness or energy spread in a system
* Second Law of Thermodynamics - Entropy naturally increases over time unless work applied to maintain order
* **Things fall apart unless someone actively holds them together.**

:::

Leadership translation:

**Without structure, standards, and enforcement, any team will drift toward disorder.**

How to:

### Observe → Diagnose → Prescribe → Practice → Follow-Up

**Observe**

* Throughout the week, observe Leasing Agent performance via:
  * Questions they ask you throughout the week
  * Spot Checking calendar, listings, inspection reports & client / tenant / team interactions
  * Spot Check Asana tasks, due dates, comments & notes

**Diagnose**

* Document examples of issues you'd like to discuss
  * Where are SOPs being misapplied / misinterpreted?
* PICK 1-2 to focus on at a time

**Prescribe + Provide Feedback**


:::warning
* "Kind not nice."
  * Provide honest, but kind feedback to help them grow and develop as Leasing Agents.
  * Holding back to be "nice" is actually more hurtful. If they consistently underperform, they will be fired.
* *Link to Actual SOP they are not executing to standard correctly.*

:::

**Practice**

* The coming week, they are assigned a specific SOP to focus on

**Follow Up**

* At the next 1:1, evaluate improvements
  * Repeat for up to 3 weeks
  * If performance is lacking on the 4th week: Escalate

**Examples**

* (Diagnosis) Low Inquiry to Showing Conversion Rate
  * → 10+ inquiries AND ALSO 5 or less showing = less than 50%
  * → Retrain & (Prescribe) Agent focus on:
  * → Respond to Inquiries / Schedule Showings SOP
* (Diagnosis) Weekly Activity Report was sent late
  * → (Prescribe) Agent focus on:
  * [Calendar Blocking / Time Management](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1NO4CNmtM-qnisdBwUaeXNni4anfPpngYRjdpJLNp3G4/edit?usp=sharing)
* (Diagnosis) **Broke Policy – Handed off keys before Move In Funds received**
  * → Retrain & (Prescribe) Agent focus on:
  * Move In Policies
  * Emphasize the WHY behind the policies → when people understand why the policy is in place; They are more likely to respect
    * Create subtask to [Leasing Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/leasing-manager.md)
* (Diagnosis) Listing is 30+ on market
  * → Review Recommendations
  * Sagareus Rental Listing Template
    * Photo quality, order

### Escalating Employee (+/-) Performance Issues


1. To escalate the issue to [Leasing Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/leasing-manager.md):
   1. Create a Asana Task
   2. Indicate the violation / issue
      1. Provide links / screenshots / etc documenting the issue
   3. Assign to [Leasing Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/leasing-manager.md)
   4. Due today


:::warning

1. **Poor Performance Issues to Escalate Everytime (Kind not nice)**
   1. Documented, recurring performance issue that have persisted for 3 weeks with little to no improvement
   2. Weekly Activity Report sent after Tuesday at 12pm
   3. Move Out Report completed *after* 48 hours (with no documented explanation)
   4. No call / no show to an appointment
   5. Poor conduct / dress code violation

:::


:::tip

1. **Positive Performance Issues to Escalate Everytime (Praise in Public)**
   1. Kind words from client, tenant, vendor, prospect → Anyone. Any positive feedback that was shared with you that we could broadcast to the team.
   2. Examples of Exceptional Work on → Listing, Inspection, Weekly Report, etc
   3. Appreciation for going above and beyond in a unique situation

:::
