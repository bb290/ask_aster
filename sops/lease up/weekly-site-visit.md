Every vacant property gets an on-site visit **at least once a week from move-out to move-in**, at your Preferred Slot 1. Sometimes coupled with a showing, sometimes not. Part of [Operation: No Drama](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/operation-no-drama-1KiDctsybm).

Companion docs: [Scheduling Preferred Slots](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/scheduling-preferred-slots-pa5dv5TXHb) and [Email | Owner - Weekly Activity Report](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/email-owner-weekly-activity-report-VDE6GnaYef) (the weekly report your visit feeds).

## Conducting the Visit

1. Walk the property. Inside and out.
2. Take photos, at least 2 per visit; more when anything looks off.
3. **Create subtasks for anything needing maintenance attention**, the day you see it. Every subtask gets an **assignee and a due date**; per the Asana SOP, if it is not in Asana, it does not exist. The subtask is what protects you and what gets it fixed.
4. Document the site visit in the comment thread. The comment proves you were there and feeds the weekly report; it implies no action is needed. Anything needing action gets a subtask too.

## Common Move-In Issues to Look For

These are the misses that generated real post-move-in tickets in the last 90 days.

* **Appliances: actually run them.** Washer, dryer, dishwasher, fridge and ice maker, disposal, oven, microwave. "Looks fine" is not tested.
* **Heating and A/C: actually test them.** Turn the heat on and feel warm air. Run the A/C and feel cold air. Check the thermostat responds and remotes are installed. No smells, no random shutoffs.
* **Keys and access.** 3 unit keys, garage remotes. Locks lock, and no two units share a key.
* **2 mailbox keys: actually test them.** Put the key in the mailbox lock and turn it. A key that exists but does not open the box is a missing mailbox key.
* **Cleanliness/Smell.** Still clean since the last clean? Fridge, oven, cabinets, window sills, floors.
* **Pests.** Ants, roaches, spiders, droppings. Kitchen and bathrooms first.
* **Water.** Leaks under sinks, running toilets, water pressure, hot water, caulk and seals around tubs.
* **Electrical and safety.** Outlets, lights, smoke and CO detectors, handrails, deadbolts.
* **Doors, windows, blinds, screens.** Open, close, latch, lock. Blinds intact, screens present.
* **Landscaping and exterior cleanliness.** Lawn mowed, walkways and entry clear of overgrowth, no trash or debris in the yard, curb appeal showing-ready. If it looks neglected from the street, fix it before the resident sees it.
* **Garage and grounds.** Garage actually empty, remotes present and functional.
* **Signs of entry or squatters.**

## Site Visit Notes

**Every time you visit the site, there is a note.** Comment directly on the **Turn Over or Lease Up task** with notes and pictures from the visit. The comment is the record.

Which task gets the comment depends on where the property is in the process:

* **Turnover Completion Inspection not yet complete:** comment on the **Turn Over task**.
* **Turnover Completion Inspection complete:** comment on the **Lease Up task**.

:::warning
**Document or it didn't happen.**
:::

Include:

* Anything you are seeing at the property
* Notes from showings
* Any subtasks you have created

These comments are your running notes for the upcoming Weekly Activity Report.

:::info
**The Accountability Rule applies in full:** anything you identify and document **as an Asana subtask, assigned and with a due date, 72 hours before move-in** does not count against leasing agent performance. The comment communicates you were there; the subtask communicates you've identified an issue requiring action.
:::
