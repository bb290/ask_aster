Confirm photos are ready and upload them to both Buildium and Google Drive so the listing can go live.


:::warning
Do not post the listing until photos are uploaded and the unit is in showable condition. Confirm Turn Over is complete or has a confirmed completion date before setting the availability date.

:::


---

### When This Runs

Run this step after [3 — Decide | Schedule photo? (y/n)](lease-up-asana-sop.md) confirms photos are needed and/or available. This is a gating step: downstream steps (Go Live, Assign, etc.) cannot proceed until photos are uploaded.


---

### Upload Photos


1. If photos are already available in Buildium and Google Drive — mark this section complete.
2. If photos are not yet available:
   * Schedule a photographer. See approved vendors in [Lease Up Training & Resources](lease-up-training-resources.md).
   * Once photos are received, upload to both Buildium and Google Drive.
