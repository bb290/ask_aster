> **Situation:** Resident requests to add or remove a roommate, sublet their unit, or transfer their lease to another person.


---

## Key Points

* **Route to Leasing team** — Add leasing@sagareus.com to the thread
* **Application required** — Incoming roommate must submit a rental application and proof of income
* **Roommate Addendum** — All current Residents must sign; prepared once incoming roommate is approved
* **Seattle exception** — In the City of Seattle, immediate family members are automatically approved
* **Security deposit stays** — Deposits and last month's rent remain with the property; financial arrangements between roommates are their responsibility
* **Change in Occupancy Fee** — $200 administrative fee applies in all jurisdictions except the City of Seattle
* **Subletting** — Follows the same process as a roommate change

## Response


1. Add [Leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:Leasing@sagareus.com)
2. Respond with:

> Thanks for reaching out & I'm happy to assist! I've assigned this ticket to our Leasing Team (Mary + Bryan) +leasing@sagareus.com, who will follow up from here.
>
> In the meantime, to begin the process of adding or removing a roommate, please reply all with the following information:
>
> **Adding a Roommate**
> • Full name of the incoming roommate
> • Date the new roommate will begin occupancy
> • Incoming roommate must:
> - Submit a rental application [HERE](https://sagareus.managebuilding.com/Resident/rental-application/new/apply)
> - Upload proof of income to application or send to [leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:leasing@sagareus.com)
> • Note: As the property is not listed, please choose the "Not Sure - I'll choose later" option on the application.
>
> **Removing a Roommate**
> • Full name of the exiting roommate
> • Date the exiting roommate will vacate the property
>
> **Roommate Addendum**
> Once approved, we will prepare a Roommate Addendum for signature by all parties. In the City of Seattle, immediate family members are automatically approved. All current Residents must sign the Roommate Addendum in order for the change to be processed.
>
> **Security Deposit & Last Month's Rent**
> Security deposits and last month's rent remain with the property. Any financial arrangements between the incoming and outgoing roommates are solely the responsibility of the Residents to manage privately.
>
> **Change in Occupancy Fee**
> A one-time $200 Change in Occupancy Administrative Fee applies in all jurisdictions EXCEPT the City of Seattle.
>
> **Subletting / Lease Take Over**
> Subletting follows the same process as a roommate change. The original Resident(s) are treated as the "exiting Resident(s)", and the incoming Resident must complete the application and approval process outlined above.
