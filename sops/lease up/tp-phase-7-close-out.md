Trigger: Phase 6 complete, FedEx confirms move-in funds delivered to owner. Owners: Nicole (final invoice + file ownership) + Leasing Assistants (admin close-out). Part of the [Tenant Placement SOP](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/tenant-placement-sop-0Ho4i49zol).

## Workflow

7.1 — Send the final PandaDoc invoice (Nicole): remaining 50% of the TP fee ($1,000 of the $2,000 flat fee) plus any showing charges beyond the 25-showing limit ($50 per overage showing, batched). Review the final showing count against the limit before invoicing.

7.2 — Send the TP Close Out email to tenant and owner (Leasing Assistants). Tenant email: subject "Move In Condition Report // [Property Address]", with the fully signed report attached for their records. Owner email (cc Nicole@sagareus.com and bb@sagareus.com): subject "File Close Out // [Property Address]", with a link to all relevant documents (application and tenant details, signed lease, signed Move In Condition Report, move-in funds photo and FedEx receipt) and the 1-question survey link (giveratings.com/sagareus-property-management).

7.3 — Update HubSpot (Leasing Assistants): deal stage to "TP Complete — Nurture". Deal remains for future nurture toward full PM conversion.

7.4 — Confirm the final invoice is paid before true file close (Nicole).

7.5 — Close the Asana file: mark the TP master task complete (Leasing Assistants).

## After Close-Out (not part of this SOP)

The owner assumes full responsibility for rent collection, maintenance coordination, lease enforcement, and tenant communication. The file enters the separate Post-Placement Nurture Workflow (not yet written) for future full PM conversion.

Phase complete when: final invoice paid + Close Out email sent + HubSpot stage updated + Asana file closed.
