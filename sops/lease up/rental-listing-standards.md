Standards for photos, listing descriptions, rental policies, and applicant criteria that apply to every Sagareus rental listing.

## Photos

* Sagareus requires **professional photos** with proper lighting and angles. [See Photography Policy](https://www.sagareus.com/owner-faqs). No exceptions.
* If a client refuses to authorize professional photos, escalate to the Leasing Manager.
* The **first photo** should typically show the kitchen or the main living space.

**Standard photo order:** Kitchen → Living Room → Bathrooms → Laundry → Garage → Yard → Bedrooms → Exterior


---

## Listing Description

* Minimum **8 sentences**, maximum **14 sentences**.
* Describe the property accurately — highlight features **without overselling**.
* The final paragraph must include **specific** details on nearby amenities and commute options: parks, grocery, restaurants, gyms, freeways, transit.

**Structure:**


1. Strong opener — *"You'll love this…"* or *"Wait until you see this…"*
2. 2–4 sentences on unit features / highlights
3. 2–4 sentences on property highlights
4. 2–4 sentences on location highlights
5. Call to action — *"Don't wait — schedule your tour today!"* or *"Showings by appointment only, schedule now!"*

## Rental Policies

Rental policies are approved by property owners at onboarding. Copy/paste the template below and customize for the unit.


:::info
**Rental Policies Template** — Remove lines that don't apply.

**Pet Policy:** Pet Friendly! 2 pets maximum, dogs/cats allowed, 30lb weight limit; $50/mo/pet. ESA always allowed, no restriction. — or — Sorry, no pets. ESA always allowed, no restriction.

**Utilities:** Accounting Team completes Utility Details on Leasing Team's behalf. If unavailable: "Resident responsible for all utilities."

**Landscaping:** Resident responsible for landscaping. — or — Landscaping service included in rent.

**Parking:** 1-car garage / 2-car garage / 1 secure space (Included or $xx/mo) / Street parking only / Additional street parking available with Seattle's Restricted Parking Zone Permit.

**Laundry:** W/D In Unit / Onsite – Coin Operated / Onsite – App Operated / None On-Site (include closest laundromat).

:::

## Competitive Positioning

Every listing should highlight why Sagareus is a better experience than self-managed properties:

* Easy online rent payments and maintenance request submissions
* Below-market application fee
* No junk fees — no pest fee, no "Resident Benefits Package"
