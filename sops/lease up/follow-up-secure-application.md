Guide every serious prospect from showing to submitted application. Every lead should move forward — or be closed out.

## Follow-Up Funnel

Save every prospect's contact info as a subtask in Asana. Follow up via text or email on:

* Scheduled showings
* Submitted applications
* Proof of income (POI)
* Lease status and execution

> **"The Fortune is in the Follow-Up"** — prompt follow-up and Asana documentation keeps the process visible to the team.


---

## Answer Applicant FAQs


1. Provide link to [www.sagareus.com/leasing](http://www.sagareus.com/leasing).
2. Explain the full process: application, lease signing, and move-in.
   * Applications are first come, first served.
   * Complete application with proof of income sent to [leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:leasing@sagareus.com).


---

## Lease Term Negotiation

Only the Leasing Manager can approve terms that differ from the listing.


1. If a prospect wants to negotiate: *"I'd be happy to check with management to see if that's something we can accommodate."*
2. Ask every person after a showing: *"What would it take for you to submit an application?"* — find out their desired terms.
3. Comment on the application Asana task with bullet points of proposed terms and tag the Leasing Manager.
4. If approved, Leasing Assistant drafts the lease with the proposed terms.


---

## Report Maintenance Issues

When you find repairs or work needed during showings:


1. Create a **subtask** in the LU Asana task. Include detail and photos.
   * Do not use the comments section — it does not notify anyone to take action.
2. Assign to Maintenance Coordinator (Joy).
3. Set due date: **same day**.

[Video: How to Create a Subtask](https://www.loom.com/share/7a42e508d028464a88a886a9de649b37)

## When to Stop Showing


:::warning
Continue showing the property until a **fully executed lease is signed by all parties**. Do not stop for any of the following:

* One or more applications received
* An application has been approved (including Section 8 / voucher holders)
* A prospect states they "intend to sign" or verbally commits
* Paperwork is in progress or pending

Prospects change their minds. Timelines shift. Deals fall apart. Only a signed lease secures the unit.

:::
