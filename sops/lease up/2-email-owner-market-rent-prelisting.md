Set a data-backed rent price and listing strategy, then send the PreListing Email to gain owner alignment before the listing goes live.

See [Market Rent & Listing Strategy](market-rent-listing-strategy.md) for full pricing and strategy methodology.

## Determine Market Rent

Follow the five-step process in [Market Rent & Listing Strategy](market-rent-listing-strategy.md).

Once complete, input the recommended market rent in the Buildium unit listing draft.


---

## Establish Listing Strategy

**1. Confirm Move-In Costs**

Verify total funds due at move-in:

* First month's rent (pro-rated if applicable)
* Security deposit (per lease or city ordinance limits)
* Non-refundable fees (admin, cleaning, pet, or HOA move-in fees)

Double-check that the move-in total is comparable to other units on the market and affordable for the target demographic.

**2. Evaluate Incentives**

* Review comparable properties: days on market, inquiry volume, and incentives offered.
* If demand is soft, consider:
  * $500, $1,000, or 1 month rent credit
  * Reduced deposit
* All incentives must be disclosed in the PreListing Email.


---

## Send PreListing Email to Owner


1. Fill in the PreListing Email template with your findings.
2. Copy/paste the draft listing from Buildium so the owner can review details.
3. Send to owner; CC Team Lead.
4. **Document in Asana:**
   * Download sent email as PDF.
   * Upload email PDF and Rentometer report to the subtask.
   * Mark subtask complete.


:::info
**If owner responds** — make updates or answer questions. **If owner does not respond** — no follow-up needed. Task is complete; proceed.

:::
