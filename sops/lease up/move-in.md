## Leasing Agent Move In Procedures


:::info
* Move In task is assigned to Leasing Agent & moved to Move In Section in Leasing 2.0 parent task
* Pre Move In Quick Clean is scheduled by Leasing Assistant
* Leasing Agent schedules day / time for Move In with Resident; Calendar blocks to arrive 30 minutes to 1 hour before Resident is scheduled to arrive (depending on property size).
* Leasing Agent arrives early to complete Move In Inspection
* Resident arrives, reviews Move In Inspection, adds anything Leasing Agent missed, and signs Move In Inspection.
* Resident provides Cashier's Check or Money Order
* Leasing Agent records payment via Buildium App
* Leasing Agent provides keys to Resident & removes key box
* Move In task is moved to "Post Move In" section in Leasing 2.0 parent task
* Resident will receive Feedback Survey for their experience with Leasing Team / process

:::

### Schedule Move In with Resident


1. Schedule move in day & time with Resident directly
   * Allow yourself 30 mins to 1 hour before Residents arrive to complete Move In Condition Report, depending on size of the house.
2. Confirm with tenants ahead of time **how their funds will be paid. Almost always a cashier's check or money order.** If they show up after given directions to bring a check and they "paid on the portal" they will not receive the keys.


:::warning
**Cashier's Check Required (unless portal payment is pre-approved).**

* Tenants must bring a cashier's check unless they have been explicitly authorized to pay through the portal. You can check this under the Move In description task under the amount they owe.
* Clearly communicate this before meeting them.

**If a tenant pays on the portal without authorization:**

* **Do not hand over the keys.**
* Portal payments are still *pending* and do not mean the funds are in Sagareus' account.
* The tenant will need to either:
  * Cancel their portal payment, obtain a cashier's check from the bank, and meet you again, **or**
  * Wait 1–2 business days until the funds clear, then schedule another appointment to receive keys.

**Why this matters:**

* Keys should only be released once funds are confirmed in Sagareus' account.
* This ensures we are fully protected and avoids the risk of unpaid or bounced deposits.

:::

### Move In Inspection


1. Complete Move In inspection on Inspector App
   * Move In inspections take 30 minutes to an hour depending on size of property
   * Document any imperfections with comments & photos
   * Include pictures of every wall, floor, light fixture, kitchen cabinets & drawers - outside & inside, vanities, EVERYTHING.
   * Include pictures of all appliances SERIAL numbers
     * This allows for faster repair / replacement if appliances fail during occupancy
   * Get at least 1 resident signature on Move In Inspection
2. When residents arrive for move in, show them the imperfections you noted and show them all the photos you took.
   * Add in Resident for signature
3. Complete report
   * Immediately send to [leasing@sagareus.com](mailto:leasing@sagareus.com) from the app
4. If app is not working for some reason, inform Resident they will receive report for e-Signature within the next few days
   * Create Subtask "Get Signature on Move In Report"
     * Assign to Leasing Assistant to send for signature
5. Remove Lockbox

#### Lost Move In Cashier's Check or Money Order


:::warning

1. If Leasing Agent loses the Move In Funds prior to making the deposit at Bank of America.
   * First of all, don't do this.
   * Best Practices:
     * Place all checks & money orders in the Check Bag provided by Sagareus.
     * Take all checks / money order to the bank or Sagareus Office drop box as soon as possible
       * Same day or following morning, first thing.
2. If you do lose the check:
   * Don't panic or hide.
   * Document this issue under Move In Task
   * Also Document in the "Receive Resident Funds in Buildium" task
     * Assign to [Lead Accountant](../../docs/role-profiles/lead-accountant.md) or [Office Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/office-manager.md)
       * Accounting will need to delete the payment on the ledger so Residents can pay online.
   * Cashier's check
     * Contact Residents
     * Explain the situation;
       * Ask them to cancel the check
       * Pay their balance via their Resident Portal.
   * Money Order
     * You've essentially lost cash
     * Sagareus will have to pay this amount
     * Email [bb@sagareus.com](mailto:bb@sagareus.com) and [accounting@sagareus.com](mailto:accounting@sagareus.com) to explain the situation.

:::

## Move In Policies

### Move-In Payments


:::warning
* Residents must pay with a Cashier's Check or Money Order at move-in.
* No Cashier's Check or Money Order = No Keys. **No exceptions.**
* The only way around this is if they're pre-approved to pay online (see below).

:::

**Can This Resident Pay Online?**

* Before move-in day, Leasing Assistant needs to check if online payment is allowed.
* Check Their Credit Scores → Pull up the credit reports for everyone on the lease.
* They qualify for online payment ONLY if:
  * Every single person has a 700+ credit score
  * Not an average - each person individually needs 700+

**Examples:**

* Two residents with scores of 720 and 710? They can pay online
* Two residents with scores of 750 and 680? They need a cashier's check
* Three residents averaging 715, but one has 680? They need a cashier's check

**If They DON'T Qualify (someone has under 700 credit)**


1. Open the Move-In Asana Task
2. Answer "Approved to pay on portal?" with **NO**
3. In Buildium, block their EFT payment (Accounting will unblock it later)
4. Let the resident know they need to bring a Cashier's Check or Money Order

**If They DO Qualify (everyone has 700+ credit)**


1. Open the Move-In Asana Task
2. Answer "Approved to pay on portal?" with **YES**
3. Delete the line that says "NO CASHIER CHECK = NO KEYS"
4. Post a comment tagging @leasing agent that says something like:
   * "All residents have 700+ credit score - approved for online move-in payment"
5. Check off these 4 subtasks since you don't need to do them:
   * Receive Resident funds in Buildium
   * Deposit Move In Funds to BofA
   * Confirm Move In Funds received
   * Add Move In Funds to Dashboard


:::tip
**Quick Summary**

* All residents have 700+ credit?
* YES → They can pay online (as noted in Asana)
* NO → They must bring Cashier's Check or Money Order

**Who to ask if you have questions:**

* Credit score questions → Mary or Bryan (Leasing Assistants)

:::

### Who Needs to Be at Move-In?

* At least one resident on the lease must be present for move-in.
* If a resident can't be there, someone else can pick up the keys for them - but the resident needs to send us written permission first.
* The incoming tenant still needs to sign the Move In Condition Report
  * It will be sent via PandaDoc for e-signature

**How to handle this:**

* Have the resident email or text you saying "[Person's name] has my permission to pick up keys for [Address]"
* Save that message in Move In Asana
* Then you can hand over keys to that person
