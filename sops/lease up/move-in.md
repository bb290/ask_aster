Move Ins are assigned when: (1) approved applicant(s) signs lease; (2) the Leasing Agent who showed the property will meet the same people for move in (exception: covering for another team member).

## Objectives

1. Execute a controlled, compliant, and fully documented transition of possession from Sagareus to the Resident.
2. Average 9 out of 10 on Post Move In Tenant satisfaction survey.

## Policies

No Cashier's Check or Money Order = No Keys, unless pre-approved for portal payment. Full rules: [Move In // Policies](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-policies-aoMfZSfGI4).

## Reference

The move-in process by role, in order:

1. [Move In // Leasing Assistant Procedures](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-leasing-assistant-procedures-HoNlxQgPbc) — notify other applicants, owner notification, pre-move-in email, delist in Buildium, set move-in amount and portal approval.
2. [Move In // Leasing Agent Procedures](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-leasing-agent-procedures-nRsXtKdnmV) — schedule with the Resident, move-in inspection, receive and deposit funds, mail the key.
3. [Move In // Accounting Team Tasks](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-accounting-team-tasks-WuXjhOIN6h) — utilities, funds dashboard, lease up fee, unblock payments, insurance, next month's rent.
4. [Move In // Post Move In Subtasks](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-post-move-in-subtasks-IlTVKpJTFu) — report review and signatures, appliance details, HubSpot; closes out the Leasing 2.0 parent task.

Policy and reference:

* [Move In // Policies](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-policies-aoMfZSfGI4) — move-in payments (the 700+ credit rule for portal approval) and who must be present at move-in.
* [Move In // Common Questions](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-common-questions-IBELj0Jo8G) — unit not clean, repairs at move-in, funds not posting, signature problems, unauthorized portal payments.

Related: [Weekly Site Visit](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/weekly-site-visit-yjZFdeB9EC), the weekly visits that catch issues BEFORE move-in day, per [Operation: No Drama](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/operation-no-drama-1KiDctsybm).
