Keep owners informed and listings optimized with a weekly update sent every Tuesday by 12pm. Deadline: Tuesdays at 12pm.

What changed (2026-07-15): Leasing Agents visit the property at least weekly from move-out to move-in for a [Weekly Site Visit](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/weekly-site-visit-yjZFdeB9EC); every site visit gets a marked-up checklist comment on the Weekly Site Visit / Inspection subtask with notes and photos; the Weekly Activity Report closes at move-in, not at lease signing, and runs regardless of state. Companion docs: [Scheduling Preferred Slots](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/scheduling-preferred-slots-pa5dv5TXHb). Initiative brief: [Operation: No Drama](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/operation-no-drama-1KiDctsybm).

## Report Preparation

1. Leasing Agent prepares the report using the state-matched template from the Email Templates doc: Turnover, Leasing, or Pre-Move-In.
2. The report is emailed to the Leasing Team Lead or directly to the Property Owner, as designated by Team Lead.

## Review & Distribution

1. Leasing Team Lead reviews and revises the report as needed.
2. Leasing Agent and/or Team Lead updates the listing in Buildium based on current activity. See Updating Active Listings.
3. Finalized report is sent to the Property Owner.
4. Copy / paste email into Weekly Activity Report sub task.

## Task Documentation

After sending (the email itself is pasted into the Weekly Activity Report subtask in Review & Distribution step 4 above):

1. Update the task due date to the following week.
2. Repeat every week until a resident has moved in.
3. Do not mark the Weekly Activity Report subtask done at lease signing or when a move-in is scheduled; it closes at move-in. (Changed 2026-07-15; previously "until a lease is signed or a move-in is scheduled.")

## Standard Leasing Activity Report Content

* Zillow Link — click to verify the listing is live and accurate before sending.
* Number of inquiries received that week.
* Number of showings completed that week.
* Feedback from the Leasing Team and/or prospective Residents.
* Suggestions to improve the property (if applicable).
* Recommendations to improve the listing or strategy — use the Activity Thresholds & Low Activity Checklist to decide what to recommend.
