Speed to Lead is designed to ensure prospects feel acknowledged and prioritized.

We help them feel as special as they are.

**Situation Summary**
Use this when a new prospect inquiry is received and you need to respond quickly to maintain engagement and secure a showing.

**Steps to Follow**


1. Respond within 1 hour (max 4 working hours)
2. Use saved response template
3. Include listing link and video walkthrough
4. Ask if they have questions
5. Offer two specific showing times
6. Send via email, text, or phone
7. Save template for reuse (Gmail + phone notes)

**Decision Points**

* If within 1 hour → Ideal response
* If approaching 4 hours → Prioritize immediately
* If no template exists → Create one for listing



:::warning
Our objective is to respond to every lead within 1 hour.

The minimum standard is to respond within 4 working hours.

:::

**Handle Questions First**


1. If they respond with questions; Answer clearly and thoroughly
   1. If you're unsure, respond with: "That's a great question—I'm not sure, but I'll find out and follow up with you shortly."
2. First, Research the answer online, through internal notes, or the listing
3. If you can't figure it out,
   1. Assign Asana task to Team Lead for quickest response


---

##### Either/Or to Secure the Showing


1. Once their questions are answered (or if they're ready right away), offer **two showing time slots** based on your availability at the property.
   1. **"Great! Are you available Tuesday at 6pm or would Friday at 11am work better? I can also offer a FaceTime or GoogleMeet tour if that's more convenient."**
   2. **Connect the dots → How this ties to calendar blocking / effective time management**


---

**Group Showings**


1. The most effective and time-saving strategy is to **group showings together**
   1. Schedule multiple prospective residents on the **same day** and **around the same time**.
   2. This reduces your travel time and improves personal safety.
2. You can:
   1. Schedule multiple people for the **same time slot** and treat it like a quiet **open house**, or
   2. Schedule in **15-minute increments** back-to-back.
3. **Important:** Never tell prospects it's an "open house."
   1. Each person should feel they have a **dedicated appointment**.


:::warning

**Add a Showing to Your Google Calendar**


:::info
Why we do this

* **Safety**
* **Organization**
* **Accountability**

:::


1. Once a showing is confirmed:
   1. **Click "Create"** on your Google Calendar
   2. **Title:** Showing – [Property Address] // [Prospect Name]
   3. **Set the Date & Time**
   4. **Location:** Paste the **property address** in the "Add Location" field – this allows you to open the map with one click from your phone.
   5. **Add Notes:**
      1. Prospect's **name** and **phone number**
