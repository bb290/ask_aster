## Program Objectives

Tenant Placement (TP) is a fee-for-service, one-time leasing transaction for owners who plan to self-manage but want professional help securing a qualified tenant.

* **Support owners with limited cash flow** — provides a middle-ground option for owners who can't absorb full-service PM fees while still protecting leasing quality.
* **Downsell from full Property Management** — lower-barrier entry point for owners hesitant to commit to full-service.
* **Long-term conversion pipeline** — TP clients are intentionally nurtured post-placement with the goal of converting to Full Service PM.
* **Target higher-end single-family homes** — pricing is calibrated to discourage low-rent or small multi-unit properties that don't fit the program.

## Pricing

| Item | Terms |
|------|-------|
| Program fee | $2,000 flat |
| 50% due | At agreement signing |
| 50% due | Upon receipt of the tenant's security deposit |
| Included showings | Up to 25 |
| Showing overage | $50 per showing beyond 25 |

Sagareus **does not hold or deposit any owner or tenant funds** under TP. Because overages are billable, every showing must be logged in Asana.

## Deliverables

* **Pre-Listing Email** — kickoff communication confirming listing scope and timeline with the owner.
* **Link to Professional Photos** — professional photography is included in the flat fee.
* **Weekly Activity Report** — showing activity, inquiry volume, and market feedback delivered to the owner each week.
* **Up to 25 Property Showings** — in-person showings coordinated by the leasing agent; overages billed at $50 each.
* **Application Underwriting** — applicant screening using the same standards as Full Service PM.
* **Signed Lease Agreement** — RHAWA lease executed between tenant and owner.
* **Move In Inspection Report** — documented condition report at key handover.
* **Move In Funds** — cashier's check or money order collected at move-in and delivered to the owner via FedEx with tracking.

## How TP Differs from Full-Service PM

* **No vendor coordination** — Sagareus may recommend repairs, cleaning, or maintenance, but the owner coordinates and pays all vendors directly.
* **Owner holds all funds** — no Sagareus trust account involvement; move-in funds flow directly to the owner.
* **RHAWA lease template** — TP leases use the RHAWA template, never Sagareus-branded lease documents.
* **Lease signed by owner, not Sagareus** — the Leasing Manager does not sign the lease; tenant and owner sign directly.
* **25-showing cap with overage billing** — additional showings cost $50 each, which requires rigorous Asana logging.
* **Cashier's-check / money-order only move-in** — tenants cannot use Sagareus online payment systems for move-in funds.
* **One-time engagement** — service ends once the tenant has moved in and funds are delivered; owner assumes all ongoing rent collection, maintenance, lease enforcement, and tenant communication.
* **Separate agreement** — clients sign a Tenant Placement Service Agreement rather than a Property Management Agreement.

## Templates & Artifacts

* **RHAWA Lease Template** — customized for TP use; mandatory lease document.
* **Tenant Placement Service Agreement** — the client-facing agreement (distinct from the full PM agreement).
* **Tenant Placement Invoice** — billing document for the 50/50 fee schedule.
* **Tenant Placement Onboarding Form** — JotForm that collects property, listing, lease, and move-in instructions from the owner.
* **Welcome Home Letter Template** — given to the tenant with move-in payment instructions, ongoing rent instructions, maintenance contacts, and utility providers.
* **TP Application Task Template** — Asana template used to process applications.
* **Tenant Placement Task Template** — Asana onboarding task set inside the Leasing project.
