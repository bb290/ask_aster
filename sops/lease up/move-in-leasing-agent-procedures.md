The Leasing Agent's move-in day workflow, from scheduling with the Resident through banking the funds. Part of [Move In](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-f0sLMcj1VF).

Flow: Move In task assigned to Leasing Agent and moved to the Move In section; Pre Move In Quick Clean scheduled by Leasing Assistant; Leasing Agent schedules day/time with the Resident and arrives 30 to 60 minutes early to complete the Move In Inspection; Resident reviews, adds anything missed, signs; Resident provides Cashier's Check or Money Order; Agent records payment in the Buildium App; Agent provides keys and removes the key box; task moves to Post Move In; Resident receives a feedback survey.

## Schedule Move In with Resident

1. Schedule move in day and time with the Resident directly. Allow 30 minutes to 1 hour before Residents arrive to complete the Move In Condition Report, depending on the size of the house.
2. Confirm with tenants ahead of time how their funds will be paid: almost always a cashier's check or money order. If they show up having "paid on the portal" without authorization, they will not receive the keys.

**Cashier's Check Required (unless portal payment is pre-approved).** Check authorization in the Move In task description under the amount owed. If a tenant pays on the portal without authorization: do NOT hand over the keys. Portal payments are still pending and do not mean the funds are in Sagareus' account. The tenant must either cancel the portal payment, obtain a cashier's check, and meet you again, or wait 1 to 2 business days for funds to clear and schedule another appointment. Keys are only released once funds are confirmed in Sagareus' account.

## Move In Inspection

1. Complete the Move In inspection on the Inspector App (30 to 60 minutes by property size). Document any imperfections with comments and photos. Include pictures of every wall, floor, light fixture, kitchen cabinets and drawers inside and out, vanities, EVERYTHING. Include pictures of all appliance SERIAL numbers (faster repair or replacement later). Get at least 1 resident signature.
2. When residents arrive, show them the imperfections you noted and all the photos you took. Add the Resident for signature.
3. Complete the report and immediately send to leasing@sagareus.com from the app.
4. If the app is not working, tell the Resident they will receive the report for e-signature within a few days; create subtask "Get Signature on Move In Report" assigned to the Leasing Assistant.
5. Remove the lockbox.

## Receive Resident Funds in Buildium

1. In the Buildium App: More, Payments, search Resident or address, enter amount, select payment method (cashier check or money order), memo "move in funds", attach a photo of the funds, choose email receipt, confirm, Receive Payment.
2. If the outstanding balance is unavailable at move in (early move-in so charges have not posted, or a data entry error): take the photo of the check per usual, comment "Unable to input to BD" on the task, and assign to the Lead Accountant to resolve.

## Deposit Move In Funds to BofA

1. Go to any Bank of America branch. Fill out a deposit slip with the bank account information from your 1Password account.
2. Take a picture of the deposit slip and check to document the trip; upload to the Asana task. Deposit the funds.

## Mail Labelled Key to Main Office

The leasing kit box has index cards and addressed, stamped envelopes.

1. Tape the key to an index card. Write the house number and city, the type of key, whether it is a new key, the date, and your name.
2. Mail it in the orange bubble mailer (extra stamp for 2+ keys, second envelope for 4+).
3. Assign an Asana task to the Office Manager stating the key has been mailed, with a photo of the prepared envelope, full property address, key type, and whether it was rekeyed.
4. If rekeyed, the Office Manager disposes of the old office key.
5. Out of supplies? Create an Asana task for the Office Manager.

## Confirm Move In Funds Received in BD; Confirm Deposit Slip Uploaded

1. Confirm the check was entered into Buildium; if photoed but not entered, receive the payment yourself rather than chasing.
2. Confirm the deposit slip is uploaded; if marked complete with no picture, reassign to the Leasing Agent for the photo.
3. Once confirmed, assign the Add Move In Funds subtask to the Office Manager.

## Lost Move In Cashier's Check or Money Order

Best practice: all checks and money orders go in the Sagareus check bag and to the bank or office drop box the same day or first thing the following morning. If you do lose the check: do not panic or hide. Document in the Move In task AND the Receive Resident Funds task; assign to the Lead Accountant or Office Manager (accounting deletes the ledger payment so Residents can pay online). Cashier's check: contact Residents, ask them to cancel the check and pay via portal. Money order: that is lost cash; Sagareus pays this amount; email bb@sagareus.com and accounting@sagareus.com to explain.
