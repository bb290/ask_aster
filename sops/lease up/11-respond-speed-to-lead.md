Respond to every inquiry within minutes. Speed to Lead is the single biggest driver of showing conversion -- the first responder usually wins.


:::info
**The primary subtask is Speed to Lead -- the initial response.** Ongoing activities (conducting showings, follow-up, securing applications) are captured as references in the [Active Listing Playbook](lease-up.md) since they span the full listing lifecycle rather than a single subtask.

:::


---

### When This Runs

As soon as inquiries start arriving -- typically the same day the listing goes live. Most leads arrive via email; some by phone or text. Respond as soon as possible.

Create a personalized response template per listing to save time.


:::info
**Inquiry Response Template**

Hi there, thank you for your interest in [Address]!

Here is a link to the listing to review details and a video walkthrough to confirm it feels like a good fit: [Video Walk-Through Link] [Listing Link]

Do you have any questions about the property, or would you like to schedule a showing?

:::

After 1 day with no response, send a follow-up text via RingCentral with the same message.


---

### Handle Questions First


1. If they respond with questions, answer clearly and thoroughly.
   * If unsure: *"That's a great question — I'm not sure, but I'll find out and follow up shortly."*
2. Research the answer via online search, internal notes, or the listing.
3. If you can't find the answer, assign an Asana task to the Team Lead for the fastest response.


---

### Schedule the Showing

Once questions are answered (or if they're ready right away), offer **two time slots** based on your availability.

> *"Great! Are you available Tuesday at 6pm, or would Friday at 11am work better? I can also offer a FaceTime or Google Meet tour if that's more convenient."*

**Group showings for efficiency:**

* Schedule multiple prospects on the **same day at the same time** (quiet open house style), or
* Book in **15-minute increments** back-to-back.


:::warning
Never tell prospects it's an "open house." Every person should feel they have a dedicated appointment.

:::

**Add to Google Calendar:**

* **Title:** Showing – [Property Address] // [Prospect Name]
* **Date & Time:** confirmed appointment
* **Location:** property address (enables one-tap navigation from your phone)
* **Notes:** prospect's name and phone number
