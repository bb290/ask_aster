Trigger: Phase 5 complete, lease fully signed. Owner: Nicole (Leasing Agent). Part of the [Tenant Placement SOP](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/tenant-placement-sop-0Ho4i49zol).

Financial distinction, critical: Sagareus does NOT hold or deposit any owner or tenant funds. All move-in funds flow directly from the tenant to the owner via FedEx.

## Workflow

6.1 — Coordinate move-in date and time directly with the tenant. The owner is not involved in scheduling.

6.2 — Retrieve keys from the onsite property lockbox.

6.3 — Collect move-in funds. Requirements: cashier's check or money order ONLY (no personal checks, no online payments); made payable to the OWNER's legal name as indicated on the JotForm Onboarding Form, NOT to Sagareus; amount equal to or greater than the required move-in amount. Triple-confirm the check is made out correctly before accepting.

6.4 — Complete the Move-In Inspection Report with the tenant present using the standard inspection procedure from the [Move In](https://sagareus.getoutline.com/doc/move-in-f0sLMcj1VF) SOP (walk-through steps, photo standards, signatures). Do NOT follow the LU move-in payment flow: LU assumes Sagareus holds funds (Buildium deposit, BofA, Resident Center). For TP, fund handling is cashier's-check + FedEx to owner. Only the inspection procedure is inherited.

6.5 — Documentation (CYA): photo of funds on the Inspection Report (the tenant's receipt); photo of funds + key + FedEx tracking receipt together; FedEx tracking number recorded in Asana and provided to the owner.

6.6 — Ship funds to the owner via FedEx overnight, personally. Same day if time permits, next business day morning at the latest. Nicole goes to FedEx herself; this is not delegated.

## Error Protocols

* Funds made out to the wrong name: postpone move-in; tenant returns with a corrected check.
* Funds less than required: postpone move-in until accurate.
* Funds more than required: acceptable, proceed.
* Personal check presented: reject. Cashier's check or money order only.

Phase complete when: FedEx tracking confirms delivery to the owner.
