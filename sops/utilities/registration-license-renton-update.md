:::info
Renton rental registration renewal uses the same online form as initial registration. Renewal always expires 12/31 of the renewal year.

:::

## Trigger

* Annual renewal of Renton rental registration. Watch for reminder from `rentalregistration@rentonwa.gov` or track expiry via `Rental Registration Expiration` field.
* Executed by: [Customer Service Specialist](../../docs/role-profiles/customer-service-specialist.md).

## Prerequisites

* Confirm property is still under Sagareus management.
* **VPN connection** (if working remotely).

## Procedure


1. Go to [Renton Rental Registration Form](https://forms.rentonwa.gov/Forms/RentalRegistration).
2. Complete the `Checklist of Compliance` — answer **NO** to each item.
3. Complete the `Declaration of Compliance`.
4. Click `Submit`.
5. Get the reference number.
6. Send an email to [rentalregistration@rentonwa.gov](mailto:rentalregistration@rentonwa.gov) requesting a copy of the renewed license.
7. Update `Rental Registration Expiration` on [Property Settings](../../docs/section-indexes/sops-2-property-settings.md) to **12/31 of the new renewal year**.
