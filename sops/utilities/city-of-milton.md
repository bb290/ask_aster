Portal: <https://www.xpressbillpay.com/#/?org=milton10332>

Phone: 253-922-8733

**Account Transfer to Sagareus**

* The owner can never be taken off the account and will always receive City of Milton bills. They will always be ultimately responsible for any charges not paid. *I do not recommend it is placed into a tenant's name for this reason .*
* We can decide to have additional bills sent to either Sagareus or the tenant. 

\[Step by step — how to set up new account\]

* Send an email to [utilities@cityofmilton.net](mailto:utilities@cityofmilton.net) and attach the PMA and the completed Additional Bill Copies Request form (attached) to start or stop receiving the additional bills. These will always go to the owner. The additional bill can go to either Sagareus or the tenant. 

**Tenant Account Setup**

\[How to set up account on tenant behalf?\]

* Send an email to [utilities@cityofmilton.net](mailto:utilities@cityofmilton.net) and attach the PMA and the completed Additional Bill Copies Request form (attached) to start or stop receiving the additional bills. These will always go to the owner. The additional bill can go to either Sagareus or the tenant. 

**Disable autopay / paperless billing**

\[How to disable auto pay and paperless billing?\]

* Email [utilities@cityofmilton.net](mailto:utilities@cityofmilton.net) with PMA attached to request autopay and paperless billing are disabled. If they already have the PMA you can call 253-922-8733 to make this request. 

**Can tenant manage account?**

\[Yes/No\] 

Technically, yes they can be sent bills. However, the owner will always remain on the account and be financially responsible if the tenant does not make payments. I do not recommend they manage the account.
