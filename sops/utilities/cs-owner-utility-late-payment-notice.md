> **Situation:** Owner received a utility late payment or shutoff notice and is reaching out to Sagareus for resolution.


---

## Key Points

* **Immediate payment** — Pay outstanding balance and reimburse owner for any late fees
* **Full audit** — Audit all utility accounts for the property to verify billing and payment settings
* **Resident recovery** — Any Resident-responsible portions will be passed through for utility recovery
* **Loop in accounting** — Add accounting@sagareus.com to the email thread


---

## Response

> Thank you for bringing this to our attention. We apologize for the late utility notice you received. Our accounting team is taking the following immediate steps to resolve this:
>
> • We are paying the outstanding balance immediately and will have Sagareus reimburse you for any late fees.
> • A full audit of all utility accounts for the property is underway to verify billing and payment settings.
> • Any Resident-responsible portions will be passed through for utility recovery.
>
> We will confirm once the account is current and the audit is complete.
