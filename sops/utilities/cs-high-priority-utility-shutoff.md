> **Situation:** Owner or Resident reports that water or electricity has been shut off, or a shutoff notice has been received.


---

## Key Points

* **Immediate action required** — Water and electricity shutoffs are High Priority; same-day resolution needed
* **Dual escalation** — Tag both [Utility Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/utility-coordinator.md) and [Accounting Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/accounting-manager.md)
* **URGENT subtask** — Create subtask assigned to [Accounting Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/accounting-manager.md) with today's due date


---

## Response

> Received—thank you for reporting this. Your request has been marked as High Priority. Our team has been alerted and is actively working to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. You'll receive an update shortly.

## Action


1. Create Asana Task


   1. Title: "Issue // \[Address\]" (e.g., Electricity Shut Off // 3721 S Orchard #D Tacoma)
   2. Project: Finance & Office
   3. Section: Utilities
   4. Assigned to: [Utility Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/utility-coordinator.md)
   5. Due: Today / Next Day
   6. Screenshot communication, upload to comments
2. Tag both [Utility Coordinator](../../docs/role-profiles/utility-coordinator.md) and [Accounting Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/accounting-manager.md)
3. Create subtask titled "URGENT UTILITY"
   * Assign to [Accounting Manager](../../docs/role-profiles/accounting-manager.md)
   * Due Today

### Example Resident Questions

* "My water was shut off"
* "We have no electricity"
* "I received a utility shutoff notice"
