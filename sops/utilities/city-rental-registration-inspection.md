This doc covers procedures for **rental registration inspections**, dispatching third-party inspectors, coordinating access for single- and multi-unit properties, tracking failed-item repairs, and responding to owner inquiries about inspection notices.

## City Required Rental Inspection


1. Property registrations are done with mgmt@sagareus.com, Sagareus will be notified of inspection in the mgmt Google Group.
2. Maintenance Coordinator (Joy) receives task assignment when office receives inspection notification
3. Sagareus rely on 3rd party inspectors instead of city inspectors

## Inspection Dispatch, Single Unit


1. For single units, Sagareus will rely on the inspector to schedule with the Resident directly.
2. Maintenance Coordinator
   * Send an email to all the Residents + inspector with the email template
   * Update task due date a few days out to monitor scheduling progress
   * Move to Dispatched Section
   * Follow up with Inspector every 3 days until Inspection received

### Email Template, Schedule Inspection for Single Unit

> **Subject: Required rental inspection for city // \[Property Address\]**
> **Attachment: Original city request**
> 
> We have been notified by the city a mandatory rental inspection is required for your home.
> 
> This inspection will be conducted by a licensed inspector (CC'd on this email).
> 
> Lisa Lotus, Taurus Inspection
> lisa@taurushomeinspections.com
> (206) 676-0023
> 
> Please coordinate directly with the inspector to schedule a time that works for you to provide access to the property.

### Post Inspection | Single Family


1. For each failed inspection item, create a sub task to track the work.
   * IMPORTANT: vendors must provide proof of completed work. The proof will be sent to the inspector to avoid needing to pay for a re-inspection.
2. Maintenance Coordinator must be mindful of the deadline


---

## Inspection Dispatch, Multi-Unit


1. For buildings with multiple units, Maintenance Team Lead will provide access to all units to the inspector at a pre-determined time.
2. Maintenance Coordinator kicks off process with intro email between Inspector & Maintenance
