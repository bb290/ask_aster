> **Situation:** City sends a rental registration or business license renewal notice.


---

## Key Points

* **Sagareus handles it** — No action needed from the owner
* **Assign to** [**Admin Team Lead**](../../docs/role-profiles/admin-team-lead.md) — They process the renewal


---

## Response

If owner asks about the renewal:

> Thank you for reaching out regarding the city's renewal notice. We have also received the same notice and we will process the renewal. No action is needed on your end at this time. We will handle the entire process and keep you updated as we move forward. Thank you for checking in.

## Action


1. Create Asana Task


   1. Title: "RRIO / Biz License Renewal // \[Resident Names\]" or "\[Address\]" or "\[Owner Name\]"
   2. Project: Resident Relations
   3. Section: RRIO
   4. Assigned to: [Admin Team Lead](../../docs/role-profiles/admin-team-lead.md)
   5. Due Date: Same or Next Business Day
   6. Upload Screenshot or PDF of mail received
