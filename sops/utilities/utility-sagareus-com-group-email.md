# Approved Uses

* All utility-related email correspondence
* Account takeover procedures with utility providers
* Direct communication and inquiries with utility companies
* Receiving payment receipts, confirmations, and statements from utility providers
* Internal Sagareus team communication regarding utility account management

# Not Approved Uses

* Communication with Residents about utility matters (billing inquiries, service disruptions, etc.)
* Communication with property owners about utility matters (forwarding bills for owner-paid utilities, utility expense discussions, etc.)
* General property management inquiries or non-utility-specific issues

# Monitoring

utility@sagareus.com is monitored by the Utility Coordinator.

# Sending Email from utility@sagareus.com

All communication to utility companies for account takeover and utility audits should be sent from utility@sagareus.com. This keeps correspondence visible to all team members. To send email from this address, follow the steps outlined in *Sending Email with Team Alias*.
