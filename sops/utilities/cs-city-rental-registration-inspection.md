> **Situation:** City sends a rental registration inspection notice for a managed property.


---

## Key Points

* **Assign to Maintenance Coordinator (Joy)** — She handles scheduling the licensed third-party inspector
* **Check last inspection date** — If within the past year, reach back out to the city to ask if inspection is needed
* **Fill out task details** — Inspection deadline, number of units, last inspection date


---

## Response

* No direct response needed; Just assign follow up action

## Procedure


1. Check Last Inspection Date
   * **IF** last inspection is within a year — reach out to the city and ask if this inspection is needed since we already completed an inspection this year

## Action


1. Create Asana Task


   1. TASK TEMPLATE: "Rental Registration Inspection | \[Address\]"
   2. Project: [Resident Relations 2.0 Tickets](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1201903129380625/list/1205103326230069)
   3. Section: RRIO
   4. Assigned to: Maintenance Coordinator (Joy)
   5. Due: Same or next day
   6. Fill out the Task Name & Task Body:
      * Inspection Deadline: \[YYYY-MM-DD\]
      * Number of units: \[# units\]
      * Last inspection date: \[YYYY-MM-DD\]
   7. Upload Inspection Notice for reference

## Owner Inquiry Response

If the owner receives the same notice and reaches out:

> Thank you for reaching out regarding the city's inspection notice. We have also received the same notice and have already scheduled a licensed third-party inspector to complete the inspection. No action is needed on your end at this time. We will handle the entire process and keep you updated as we move forward. Thank you for checking in.
