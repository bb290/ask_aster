> **Situation:** A utility bill is marked as autopaid, meaning the owner likely set up autopay from their personal account. Billing preferences are configured correctly to receive the bill, but the owner's autopay may need to be removed so we can process payments appropriately. Some utility companies allow us to make this change; most do not.


---

## Key Points

* **Owner autopay conflict** — The owner's personal autopay can interfere with our ability to process and pass through utility bills correctly.
* **Utility company limitations** — Most providers will not let management remove autopay on the owner's behalf.
* **Early payment workaround** — Paying before the due date will usually override the scheduled autopayment.


---

## Procedure


1. **Verify account details** — Contact the utility company to confirm the bill is for the owner's account. If it pertains to a Resident, ensure they receive a copy at their address and discontinue paper billing to our office.
2. **Check autopay removal option** — If the bill is for the owner, ask the utility company whether we can remove the autopayment setting.
3. **Remove autopay (if possible)** — If allowed, proceed with removing the autopayment.
4. **Process current balance** — If a balance exists, process the bill.
5. **Prevent autopay (if removal not possible)** — If we cannot remove autopay, ask whether our payment will prevent the scheduled autopayment. In most cases, paying before the due date will override it.
6. **Process and notify owner (if autopay cannot be removed)** — If our payment will prevent the autopay, process the bill.
7. **Contact the owner** — Send the email below requesting the owner remove autopay.

## Email Template — Request Owner to Remove Autopay

> **Subject:** Action Required: Update Utility Autopay Settings // [Property Address]
>
> **To:** [Owner Name/Email]
>
> Hi [Owner Name],
>
> We recently received the attached utility bill from [Utility Id], which is currently set up for autopay from your personal account.
>
> Unfortunately, the utility company does not permit management to remove this setting on your behalf.
>
> We have already processed this bill from your operating account, and any applicable utility recovery will be collected from the Residents as per their lease agreement.
>
> Please contact the utility company at [Utility Phone Number] to update the account and remove the autopay settings.
>
> Thank you,
