:::info
Kirkland has **no rental-specific registration program**. Sagareus's obligation is a **WA State DOR Business License** with Kirkland endorsement, applied for and renewed through the WA DOR MyDOR portal.

:::

## Program Basics

* **Program type:** WA State DOR Business License with Kirkland city endorsement
* **Portal:** [MyDOR](https://secure.dor.wa.gov/home/) (WA State DOR Business Licensing Service)
* **City page:** [Kirkland Apply for a Business License](https://www.kirklandwa.gov/Government/Departments/Finance-and-Administration/Customer-Accounts/Apply-for-a-Business-License)
* **Payee:** State of Washington (not City of Kirkland directly)
* **Administering agency:** WA Department of Revenue — Business Licensing Service

## Credentials

* SAW ID: **Sagareus LLC**
* Credentials stored in 1Password


:::warning
Each MyDOR login requires Multi-Factor Authentication. The MFA code is sent to `mgmt@sagareus.com` — a shared inbox. Coordinate with whoever is monitoring that inbox when logging in.

:::

## Sagareus Business Details

* **Business type:** Sole Proprietorship
* **Business mailing address:** `2265 116th Ave NE #200-8 Bellevue, WA 98004`
* **Business activity:** Apartment Rental (Services)
* **Independent Contractor Endorsement:** No
* **Optional Coverage for Owners and Officers:** No

## Owner Information Lookup

* King County properties: [King County eRealProperty](https://blue.kingcounty.com/Assessor/eRealProperty/default.aspx) for owner name, address, first date of ownership.

## Payment


:::warning
Pay with **Vincent's SAGA CC** via MyDOR. Buildium bill entry memo must match verbatim: `Paid with Vincent's SAGA CC`.

:::

* Buildium coding:
  * `Pay To`: `State of Washington`
  * `Account`: `Licensing and Permits`
  * `Unit`: `Property Level`
  * `Description`: `Kirkland Business Registration`
