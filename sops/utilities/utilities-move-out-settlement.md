> **Situation:** A tenant with a utility-inclusive lease (flat fee or actual cost recovery) is moving out. Final utility charges must be settled and accounted for in the security deposit disposition.


---

## Key Points

* **Final meter reading required** — Contact the utility provider with the tenant's move-out date to request a final bill
* **Settle against flat fee collected** — Compare what was collected to date against the final bill to determine surplus or deficit
* **Applies to deposit accounting** — Any remaining utility balance (owed or credit) is factored into the security deposit disposition
* **Account transition** — After move-out, the utility account must be transitioned to the next tenant or back to Sagareus management


---

## Procedure

### 1. Request Final Meter Readings

* Contact the utility provider with the tenant's move-out date
* Request a **final bill** for the tenant's period of occupancy

### 2. Settle Utility Charges

* The final bill shows actual charges through the move-out date
* Calculate: **Flat Fee Collected to Date − Final Bill Amount = Surplus / (Deficit)**
* Add charges or apply credits to the tenant's security deposit accounting

### 3. Process the Final Bill

* In Buildium, select "Enter Move Out Date" in the utility tracking table
* Enter the information for the departing tenant
* This generates a final bill — add it to the tenant's ledger as **Utility Recovery**
* Email accounting to confirm the tenant and the amount collected by management for their final bill

### 4. Transition the Account

If the property needs a new tenant account or the lease transfers to a new tenant:

* Set up a new utility account in the new tenant's name, OR
* Transfer the account back under Sagareus management (per property-specific rules)
* Document the transition in Asana
