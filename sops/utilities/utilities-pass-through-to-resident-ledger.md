> **Situation:** A utility bill has been paid and the charge needs to be passed through to the resident's lease ledger. The resident must also receive a redacted copy of the bill.


---

## Key Points

* **Charge type is "Utility Recovery"** — always use this category when adding the charge to the lease ledger.
* **Due date depends on outstanding balances** — post as due on the 1st of next month unless the resident has outstanding charges, in which case post as due immediately.
* **Redact private information** — mark up the bill to remove any owner or management details before sharing with the resident.


---

## Procedure


1. Add a charge to the resident's lease ledger.
   * Set the charge type to **Utility Recovery**.
   * Memo format: utility type | service dates.
   * Set the due date to the 1st of the next month, unless the resident has outstanding charges — then post as due immediately.
2. Navigate to the resident's summary page.
3. Upload a redacted copy of the utility bill to the resident's summary and share it with the resident.
   * Mark up the bill to remove any owner or management private information before sharing.
