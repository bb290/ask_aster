> **Situation:** A late payment notice or shut-off notice has been received for a utility account. Late payments should never occur. Owners are typically frustrated, so treat this as an opportunity to go above and beyond. This is a quick-turnaround issue.


---

## Key Points

* **Zero tolerance for late payments** — Every late notice requires immediate investigation and resolution.
* **Asana tracking required** — Every late payment notification is tracked using the "Audit Utility & Resolve Late Notice" template in the Finance & Office Project, assigned to the Utility Coordinator.
* **Owner debrief** — After resolution, the owner must be informed with a full summary of findings and corrective actions.


---

## Procedure


1. **Add all missing/overdue bills for all utilities to Buildium**
   * Do NOT add the original late payment notice or shut-off notice to Buildium.
   * Look up existing utility bills for the property and identify the service period for the most recent bill.
   * Contact the utility company and obtain ALL bills for service periods after the most recent bill.
   * Add the bills to Buildium.
   * If there are late fees, break out that amount into the separate line item account: *Utility Late Payment/Penalty*.
2. **Add Bill Passthrough Form to trigger Asana Bot processing**
   * For each bill added to Buildium, fill out the [Bill Passthrough Form](https://form.asana.com/?k=p-JkNJ59a-ELyilzXoS_ZQ&d=706990140225747) to trigger Asana Bot processing for pass-through.
3. **Pay outstanding balance**
4. **Reimburse owner for late fee (if any)**
   * Resolve the fee as a Miscellaneous Expense for Sagareus.
   * Receive the fee as a deposit to the *Utility Late Payment/Penalty* line item.
5. **Complete full questionnaire and update property settings**
   * Contact the utility companies and answer ALL questions in the task body.
   * Once all questions are answered, replace the "Utilities" field for the [property setting](https://app.asana.com/1/706990140225747/project/1209863830500678/list/1209867411652141).
6. **Add root cause to the task**
   * Examples: USPS failed delivery, Paperless billing enabled, Auto-Pay enabled, Accounting team did not pay on time.
7. **Debrief owner on investigation**
   * Respond to the owner in the original email thread if possible. Use the template below.
