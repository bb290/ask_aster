> **Situation:** Resident's autopay covers base rent but not variable utility pass-through charges, resulting in an outstanding balance that may trigger collection protocol.


---

## Key Points

* **Why it happens** — For units with utility pass-throughs, the monthly balance fluctuates; autopay set to base rent only leaves the utility portion unpaid
* **Two solutions** — Adjust autopay "Maximum amount" setting to include estimated utilities, or make a separate utility payment between the 1st and 5th each month
* **First occurrence grace** — Delete late fee if this is the first time it happened
* **Utility billing timing** — Charges are always billed to the Resident's account on the 1st of the following month


---


:::info
For units with utility pass-throughs, the monthly balance may fluctuate based on variable utility charges added each month. While Residents may use autopay to cover base rent, this may result in an outstanding balance if the utility charges are not included, potentially triggering Sagareus Collection protocol.

:::

## Response

> Thank you for reaching out, and we understand the frustration that can come with fluctuating balances. For units with utility pass-throughs, the monthly balance may change due to variable utility charges, which are always billed to your account on the 1st of the following month. To help you avoid future outstanding balances, there are two ways to manage payment:
>
> **Adjust Your Autopay Amount:**
> When setting up autopay in the Resident portal, you can include an estimated utility amount in your "Maximum amount (Optional)" setting. The system will automatically apply the correct amount each month, up to your maximum.
>
> ![Autopay maximum amount setting](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXd1pJExFILMBy9ZhHnpWamBtJxoH_cdvEjuriHipSeABf_2vqOUsVsIEbTdWV-LvcFka8pGFz1o8R2rpem2PdihXJhE1gDcizrKoicwnd_y81B8iIKhPJsd8nREuSISynSrnFcl_SsqZ2wLtqsssZbZlOIC?key=nnhKEmcP1_mvGVmGiinqRQ)
>
> **Make a Separate Utility Payment:**
> Since utility charges are added to your balance on the 1st, you can log into the portal between the 1st and 5th of each month to pay the additional amount.
>
> If you have any questions or need assistance adjusting your payments, please let us know. We appreciate your understanding and are here to help.
>
> Best regards,
